# Button Making at the iLab

![](../img/resources/buttonmaker.jpg)

Want to make a button? All you need is to come in with a design, fix it to fit in our 2.25" button makers, and cut yours in the iLab today! We will provide you with all of the material. Just bring your own printed designs!
