# Learn about Business Research Panel with Short Video Clips

Consistent with the polytechnic, “learn-by-doing” approach used at Cal Poly Pomona, the Business Research Panel (a.k.a Business Sona Systems) provides a supportive environment for innovative teaching and research in the College of Business Administration.

To facilitate the process of getting enrolled and participating in studies, watch the videos below to get started and understand the steps to partake in studies at the Business Research Panel. Also, review the [Business Research Panel](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/resources/brp/business-research-panel.shtml) to learn more on specific topics like credits, new studies, time slots, and more.

Please contact the Customer Insights Analyst at (insights-lab@cpp.edu) if there are any questions.

To participate in the Business Research Panel, go to Business Sona-Systems at <https://cpp-cba.sona-systems.com> and register.

## Part 1: How to Get Started with Research Participation in the Business Research Panel

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
|  |  | The Center for Customer Insights and Digital Marketing presents this tutorial on how to get started with research participation in the Business Research Panel. In this workshop, we explain the goal and benefits of the business research panel, how to register, when and how much sona credits participants will receive, and the difference between sona credits and the actual score in the student’s course.  **Note that there is one important change made since the video was produced. That is about enrollment. Unlike what the video said about self-enrollment, we no longer provide a self-enrollment process. We will work with faculty to get their class roster and create everyone’s account to eliminate errors associated with self-enrollment. In short, ignore the account creation process. If your professor told you to participate in the BRP, the chances are that your account has already been created and you just need to log into the BRP system (aka, business sona) using CPP’s Single Sign-On procedure.**  If you have further questions, please email insights-lab@cpp.edu. |
