# Dr. H. Erkan Ozkaya discussed using Generative AI in Course Design at CAFÉ AI workshop series

October 17, 2024

On October 17th, Dr. Erkan Ozkaya led a workshop "Designing Courses with AI Awareness" as part of CAFÉ’s AI workshop series. Dr. Ozkaya shared his expertise and experiences in using AI and AI checkers in his teaching Marketing classes.
