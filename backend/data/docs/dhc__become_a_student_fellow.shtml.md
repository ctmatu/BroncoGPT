# Become A Student Fellow

## How To Become A Student Fellow

The DHC offers two paid student fellowships per year for rising sophomores through seniors.

|  |
| --- |
| **What are we looking for?** |

Students who have completed Digital Humanities coursework or club work here at Cal Poly Pomona, who are creative and thoughtful about Digital Humanities directions that will be useful to fellow students, who are patient and organized, effective communicators who can work with faculty to support student research and learning, are welcome to apply.

**Desired skills** may include video and audio recording, content creation, editing and post-production especially in Audacity, Camtasia, Capcut, and/or Adobe products.  May include Story Maps ArcGIS, GIS, Google Earth/Google Maps, Python. May include background with HTML, editing CPP websites (with Cascade), or other programming or graphic design experiences.

|  |
| --- |
| **What will you be up to?** |

* Assist in coordinating programming from pitch to implementation. You will be a member of our Steering Committee to help us address what programming and services would especially benefit our community
* Plan workshops, visit classrooms to discuss DH skills and/or experiences
* Mentor Student Experts (student workers who can help provide office hours and do classroom visits)
* Marketing, outreach

Approximately 4 hours/week expected, but we can work with your schedule.

**Applications for the 2025-26 DHC Student Fellows are due by Monday, April 7, 2025, at 11:59 PM.**

For additional details, please see [DHC Student Fellows 2025-2026 (PDF)](dhc-student-fellows-2025-2026.pdf). Should you have any questions, do not hesitate to **[Contact Us](https://www.cpp.edu/dhc/contact.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/dhc/contact.shtml")**.
