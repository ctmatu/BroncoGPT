## Office Hours & User Labs

Following P2P go-live, additional hands-on support will be available in February through Office Hours and User Labs.

### Office Hours

Office Hours are open Zoom sessions where users can log in, ask questions, and get help with specific P2P tasks or workflows.

**When:** Monday–Friday, Beginning the week of January 26 through February

**Where:** Zoom

**Time(s):** 9:00–11:00 a.m. and 2:00–4:00 p.m.

**Join via Zoom (Links Below):**
👉 [9:00–11:00 a.m.: P2P Office Hours (9am–11am)](https://cpp.zoom.us/j/84070191004)
👉 [2:00–4:00 p.m.: P2P Office Hours (2pm–4pm)](https://cpp.zoom.us/j/89395532586)

### User Labs

User Labs focus on specific P2P topics each week and provide guided, hands-on practice in the system. Participants are encouraged to bring their laptops.

**When:** Tuesday & Wednesday, Beginning in February

**Where:** Tuesdays - In person; Wednesdays - Zoom

**Lab Schedule:**
 - Week 1: Navigation, requisition form, shopping, and punchouts
 - Week 2: Requisition review and approval
 - Week 3: Purchase orders and receiving
 - Week 4: Voucher payment status and reporting
