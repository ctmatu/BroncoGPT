## About Me

Edward is the Principal Economist at a forensic economic consulting firm based in Southern California. He has testified hundreds of times as an economic expert witness in Federal Court, Superior Court, Hearings, Arbitrations, and Depositions. He is also passionate about serving his community, having served as the Mayor of the City of Sierra Madre and currently serving as a City Councilmember. He has been a delegate for State, Regional, County, and Local Associations and Districts. In his spare time, he enjoys camping and fly fishing with his family and dogs.
