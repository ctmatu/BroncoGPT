## Bio

Peter Hii, CPA, CGMA, MBA is an adjunct Professor of Accounting at Cal Poly Pomona.

He is currently a full time CPA/CFO in industry.  He has held CFO and Controller positions with Fortune 500, large and entrepreneurial companies spanning various industries. As a CFO, he provides overall organization planning and policy development. His responsibilities include (i) providing strategic leadership and direction as a Board of Directors member (ii) Managing treasury functions and banking relations to ensure compliance with loan covenants (iii) implement policies and procedures to improve overall operation, effectiveness and financial condition of the company (iv) Pinpoint opportunities and areas where capital can be better allocated (v) Advise senior management on financial and legal perspective of contracts and agreements.
