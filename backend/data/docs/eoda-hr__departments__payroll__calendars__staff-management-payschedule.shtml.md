## 2026 Staff/Management Pay Schedule

Staff/Management Pay Schedule

| Pay Period | Begin Date | End Date | Master Payroll Certification Report Due\* | Master Payday\*\* | Estimated Direct Deposit Posting Date\*\*\* |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| January | January 01 | January 29 | January 21 | January 29 | January 30 |
| February | January 30 | February 28 | February 19 | February 27 | March 02 |
| March | March 01 | March 31 | March 20 | March 30 | April 01 |
| April | April 01 | April 30 | April 22 | April 30 | May 01 |
| May | May 01 | May 31 | May 20 | May 29 | June 01 |
| June | June 01 | June 30 | June 18 | June 30 | July 01 |
| July | July 01 | July 30 | July 22 | July 30 | July 31 |
| August | July 31 | August 31 | August 21 | August 31 | September 01 |
| September | September 01 | September 30 | September 22 | September 30 | October 01 |
| October | October 01 | October 31 | October 22 | October 30 | November 02 |
| November | November 01 | December 01 | November 18 | December 01 | December 02 |
| December | December 02 | December 31 | December 21 | December 31 | January 04, 2027 |

\*Master Payroll Certification Report authorizes Payroll Services to process and release payment to employee. Certification may also include shift differential, which is paid separately on [Positive Payday](positive-pay-schedule.shtml).

\*\*Paychecks are available after 4:00 p.m. in your department.

\*\*\*Verify actual direct deposit posting date with your financial institution.

For additional information regarding direct deposit, refer to [Direct Deposit website](../cal_employee_connect.shtml).
