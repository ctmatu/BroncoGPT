## EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (EAP)

[MyLifeMatters by Empathia offers staff, faculty, and their households individual assistance and support services.](https://www.cpp.edu/faculty-affairs/eap-employees-assistance-program.docx)

When you or your family need helpful guidance, short-term counseling, financial and legal consultation, referral to local resources, your Life Matters program is just a phone call away, and it is available on a live basis, 24/7. Direct Assistance is free and confidential.

* Call at 1-800-367-7474
* Log on to [MyLifeMatters Website](https://www.mylifematters.com).  Our company password is available for use for the services covered by Empathia, please reach out to your campus benefits/HR office at benefits@cpp.edufor the company password.

## Fall 2025 RTP Workshops

**Fall Semester 2025 RTP Workshops**

RTP workshops are for faculty going through the RTP/Periodic Evaluation process and for evaluators at all levels of review including department RTP committees, college RTP committees, university RTP committee, department chair and dean. The workshops provide an overview of the applicable policies and Interfolio but allow for any questions you might have. Please see below for Fall 2025 workshop recordings.
