# The Lyle Center Presents:

## A Study in Energy Poverty in Madrid

![](../../)

**Date:** April 14, 2021
**Time:** 12:00pm to 01:00pm
**Location:** TBD

![Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies presents.  A Study in Energy Poverty in Madrid.  Research focused on the distribution of energy poverty in Madrid, Spain.](../img/study-in-energy-poverty-in-madrid.png)

*Architects Carmen Alonso and Fernando Martin-Consuegra discuss their findings on the lack of access to modern energy services in the Spanish capital*

Featured speakers **Carmen Alonso** and **Fernando Martin-Consuegra** will discuss the distribution of energy poverty — the lack of access to modern energy services — in Spain's capital (and most populous) city. Architects by training, Alonso and Martin-Consuegra share their expertise with the Spanish National Research Council. Read their works [here](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Carmen-Alonso-7 "Carmen Alonso's (Opens in new window)") and [here](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Fernando-Martin-Consuegra "Fernando Martín-Consuegra's(Opens in new window)").

**A Study in Energy Poverty in Madrid**
By Carmen Alonso, Ph.D., and Fernando Martin-Consuegra, Ph.D.
[Instituto de Ciencias de la Construccion Eduardo Torroja](https://www.ietcc.csic.es/en/ "Instituto de Ciencias de la Construccion Eduardo Torroja(Opens in new window)")
Wednesday, April 14 | 12 p.m. (PST)
Email crs@cpp.edu for the Zoom link.
