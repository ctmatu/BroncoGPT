# Veterans Enrollment

## After Admission

### Becoming A Bronco

Once you have been admitted to the University, your first step will be to participate in orientation where you will be introduced to the opportunities and services available and assist you in navigating the university.
The campus has also put in place services that are specific to your academic and personal needs as a student veteran. Click on the links below for more information.
