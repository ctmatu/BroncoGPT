CBA Alumni Chapter

Thanks to nearly 50,000 living alumni, Cal Poly Pomona's College of Business Administration and Singelyn Graduate School of Business offers a powerful community of successful business leaders. The best way to tap into this network is by joining the College of Business Administration Chapter of the Alumni Association.

With over 300 active members, the CBA Alumni Chapter comprises the largest alumni chapter at Cal Poly Pomona. Our members are some of the most successful people in Southern California and beyond.

The CBA Alumni Chapter provides excellent professional and social networking opportunities for its members, including the Executive Speaker Series and the annual Alumni Night at the Ballpark event. Our members are actively involved in professional development, mentoring, and community partnerships and are willing to share their expertise. Whether you are already at the height of your career or are working your way up the ladder of success, we encourage you to join our esteemed and fun group. [Visit their website to learn more](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/chapters/business.shtml).
