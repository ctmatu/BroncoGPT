The Kellogg Legacy Project Endowment is a multimillion-dollar philanthropic investment in the future of our students, our state, and our nation. This remarkable gift from one of the nation's leading foundations supporting education strengthens student success programs, improves faculty teaching and scholarly innovation, expands relations with the communities we serve, and supports our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion.

The Kellogg Legacy Endowment is held and invested by the Cal Poly Pomona Philanthropic Foundation. Working in partnership with the Presidential Cabinet and other campus leaders, the University President, considers a variety of targeted investments in projects and activities that strengthen the university and advance our mission to cultivate success through a diverse culture of experiential learning, discovery, and innovation.

Projects for consideration of funding should be directed to the division Vice President in the area most closely aligned with the project for consideration during the annual budget cycle of the university. Letters announcing the awarding of funds are typically sent each spring semester for programs beginning in the fall.
