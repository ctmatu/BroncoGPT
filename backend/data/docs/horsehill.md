# Horsehill Vineyards

Horsehill Vineyards is a collaborative wine project between Cal Poly Pomona and South Coast Winery that creates the perfect blend of scientific creativity, plant mastery, splendid hospitality, and innovative learning.

Students from the [Don B. Huntley College of Agriculture](https://www.cpp.edu/agri/) grow and harvest the grapes, which are then sent to the winery to be produced and bottled by [South Coast Winery’s](https://www.southcoastwinery.com/wine/) master winemaker, Jon McPherson. With the talents of students from the [College of Environmental Design](https://www.cpp.edu/env/), the wine label and bottle packaging is designed. The finished product is marketed and poured by students from [The Collins College of Hospitality Management](https://www.cpp.edu/collins/). It is sold at various locations on campus.

***“It’s been a great pairing to be working together,” says McPherson of the unique collaboration with Cal Poly Pomona. “It’s a natural marriage."***
