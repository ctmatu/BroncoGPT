# Scholarships & Financial Aid

## We’re Invested in Your Success

### Get a degree without piles of debt.

The Collins College awards $300,000 in scholarships every year — to both incoming students and current Broncos. These scholarships are made possible by our longtime industry partnerships and generous donors.

You may qualify for up to $1,000 in scholarship funds with the new Collins Incoming Freshman, Transfer or Change of Major Student Scholarship.
