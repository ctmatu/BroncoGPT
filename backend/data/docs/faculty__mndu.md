## Bio

Mengjiao Nancy Du joined Cal Poly Pomona as an assistant professor in 2018. She graduated from the Washington State University with a Ph.D. in Accounting. She also worked for Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu China in Auditing and Enterprise Risk Management. Her research has been primarily focused on using experimental approaches to explore topics in sustainability reporting, investors’ judgment and decision making, and cross-cultural differences. Her recent work has been published at Behavioral Research in Accounting and presented at American Accounting Association meetings.
