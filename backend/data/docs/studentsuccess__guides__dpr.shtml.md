# Degree Progress Report Tool

## Using the Tool

The Degree Progress Report, or DPR, is a tool in your BroncoDirect Student Center that provides information about the completion status of your degree requirements. It indicates which requirements you have satisfied and which ones you still need to completed.

The Registrar's Office has recently made updates to the DPR to make it easier for you and your advisor to read and understand. However, there are several scenarios that the Registrar's Office is aware of where things may not look right to you. If something does not look right to you, select the scenario from the tool linked below to learn more about your next steps.

[DPR Tool (Qualtrics Form)](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_ehfj03NnM4IOhTf)
