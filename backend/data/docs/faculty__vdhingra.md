## About Me

Vinita Dhingra (Ph.D., Claremont University) is Professor of Communication. She teaches in the Public Relations and Journalism options.

## Sample Publications:

Dhingra, V. (1998, May). *Making strides: The role of circumstantial serendipity in the diversity planning process*. Paper presented at the National Conference on Race & Ethnicity (NCORE) in American Higher Education.

Dhingra, V. (1998, May). *Designing the course: Structuring and writing a syllabus*. Panelist in Career Services College and University Teaching Symposium at Claremont Graduate University.

Dhingra, V. (1996). Winning public support for waterworks projects through effective communication techniques. *Proceedings of the American Waterworks Association (AWWA)*, Toronto, Canada.

Dhingra, V. (1978). *Personnel management in small business: Successful operating business survival report*.
