# Who We Are

## About the Centers for T.R.E.E.

The primary goal of cultural and identity support services through T.R.E.E. is to provide all students with resource assistance, advocacy, and services that provide exploration and celebration of all identities by building a sense of belonging.

**Key Offerings**

* Navigation assistance for campus resources
* Student wellbeing and mental health services
* Reflection and meditation room
* Career readiness and internship support
* Community outreach and engagement initiatives
* Educational workshops, programs and discussions on inclusive excellence
* Funding support for community-building celebrations and programs

**Retention Centers**

Retention centers through T.R.E.E. offer a variety of support aimed at supporting students' academic success, personal development, sense of belonging and overall well-being. These services are designed to address equity gaps related to student retention and graduation.

**Services Include:**

* Academic coaching and guidance
* Academic support services referrals
* Peer-to-peer tutoring and mentorship programs
* Student success workshops and event series
* Leadership development opportunities
* Student-centered celebrations and programs
* Community-building events and engagement
* Support and resources for student clubs and organizations
* Connections to peer support networks
* Networking opportunities and professional connections
