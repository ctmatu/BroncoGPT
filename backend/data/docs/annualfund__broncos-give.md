# Annual Giving & Stewardship

## Broncos Give

In March, Cal Poly Pomona’s annual 36-hour fundraising event Broncos Give unites the campus community to support students, scholarships, programs and more. This high impact giving campaign taps into personal networks and leverages matching gifts and challenges to maximize contributions and amplify donor impact.

If you’re interested in participating, fill out our [Fundraising Interest Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScAl4Php564OeR-Nfcv3FgWTRY1NylWeGHDBaKv3UaUQWRhVA/viewform). Approved applicants become Broncos Give Captains! You’ll lead the charge by organizing and driving your campaign with the help of volunteer fundraisers called “Champions” and a helpful toolkit.

**Key Dates:**

* December 2025-January 2026 – Information Sessions
* Dec. 8, 2025 – Applications Open
* Jan. 28, 2026 – Applications Close
* February-March 2025 - Momentum Meetings (Fundraising Training Sessions)
* Mar. 4, 2026 – Matching Gift Deadline and Page Edits
* March 18, 2026, at 9 a.m. PT – Broncos Give begins
* March 19, 2026, at 9 p.m. PT – Broncos Give ends
