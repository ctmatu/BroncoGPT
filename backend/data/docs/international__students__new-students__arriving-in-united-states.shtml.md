# Arriving in the United States

### Date of Entry to the U.S.

U.S. immigration regulations permit new F/J students to **enter the US up to 30 days prior to but no later than the program start date** printed on page one the CPP I-20/DS-2019. Please consider this date carefully when making travel plans to the U.S.

### Late Entry

**Entry to the U.S. after the program start date is not guaranteed** and is at the discretion of the Port of Entry Officer at the airport as well as the CPP academic department. In general, **a student cannot arrive more than one week after classes start** and must be enrolled full time for that semester.

## Planning Resources

### Transportation to CPP

Cal Poly Pomona is easily accessible from a number of local airports. You can rent a car or take a taxi to the campus. The closest international airports are Los Angeles Airport (LAX) and Ontario International Airport. You can catch an airport shuttle to Cal Poly Pomona with **[Prime Time Services](https://www.primetimeshuttle.com/?gclid=CjwKCAjwt52mBhB5EiwA05YKow11TH3KZhytw_C5QwqA02aT-4HWW708pVN4Y5NCvgSx4Cnxq8q6qBoCa1sQAvD_BwE#/search)** or [**Super Shuttle Express**](https://www.supershuttle.com/). Some students also use taxi or shared drive, such as Uber and Lyft.

[Learn More about Transportation to CPP](https://www.cpp.edu/international/students/living-pomona/transportation-options.shtml)
