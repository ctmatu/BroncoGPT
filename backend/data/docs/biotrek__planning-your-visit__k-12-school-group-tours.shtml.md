## About K-12 Group Tours

**We are fully booked for Fall 2025 and Spring 2026. If you would like to be placed on our waitlist, please click [here](https://www.cpp.edu/biotrek/planning-your-visit/tour-reservation.shtml). Thank you!**

### General Tour Information

Our own interpreters will lead walking tours with interactive activity stations through three of the following learning centers:

* Rain Bird Rainforest
* Rain Bird BioTrek Ethnobotany Learning Center and Garden
* Mesozoic Garden and Learning Center

Tours are conducted rain or shine. Please advise students to dress appropriately (good shoes and clothes for walking).  To schedule an in-person visit, please complete the Group Tour Reservation Form.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Expand/Collapse All Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does it cost to attend a field trip?

The cost per student for in-person tours is $8. Chaperones and parents are free. We require a minimum payment of $240 (equivalent to 30 students) for all groups. We accept checks made payable to BioTrek. Payment can be brought to campus on day of tour.

### Does BioTrek offer a stipened for field trips?

Unfortunately, we no longer have funding available for bus stipends.

### What will the students learn about during their field trip?

* California native plants and how the Gabrielino-Tongva native people use them (acorn grinding activity and brush making with dried yucca)
* California biomes and their unique characteristics
* Rainforest plants and their uses – cocoa butter demonstration, chocolate midge pollination, caiman talk
* The Mesozoic – “Age of the Dinos” and plants that existed during that time that still exist today (solar panel activity)
* Our animal station allows students to get an up close and personal with some of our reptiles

### What if I need to cancel a reservation?

If your plans change, please let us know immediately at biotrek@cpp.edu so we can cancel your field trip at no charge.
