# Academic Senate Referral Request Form

Please provide all information requested in this form. Incomplete referrals will be returned.

Referrals will be placed on the agenda of the next meeting of the [Executive Committee](../committees/meeting-times.shtml#q-1) (EC). The Executive Committee forwards the referrals to a standing committee that researches the proposal, contacts appropriate resources and proponents, and submits a report. The EC reviews the report, forwards it to the Senate or returns it to the standing committee for additional information, clarification, or review. After the EC accepts the report, it is placed on the agenda of the next Academic Senate meeting for a first reading and a month later for a second reading where voting takes place. The referral is then sent to the President for approval. Depending on the topic, the process may take from 1 to 3 semesters. For a summary of this process, please see the [Referral Flow Chart (PDF).](../documents/referral_flowchart_revised.pdf)

Complete the form below and click Submit when you are finished. All fields marked \* are required.
