# Alumni & Supporters

## Parents & Family

![A woman peers into a microscope while a man and woman look on at open house.](../img/2020-ag-open-house-920x570.jpg)

At Cal Poly Pomona, we provide a range of resources with the hope that you’ll become active members of our community. We truly believe family involvement is important to ensuring your student has a rewarding and fulfilling experience with the Huntley College.

Through the university’s [**Office of Parent and Family Engagement**](https://www.cpp.edu/family/index.shtml), it is our goal to keep you well-informed and connected as you partner with us in your student’s journey!

[Bronco Resource Hub](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/hub/index.shtml)

For more information, contact the Parent and Family Hotline at (909) 869-3009 or broncofamily@cpp.edu.
