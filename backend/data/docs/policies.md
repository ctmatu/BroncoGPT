# University Policies

Cal Poly Pomona formally approves, promulgates in a consistent format, and centrally maintains all official university policies.  Please refer to ***[Administrative Policy 001, University Policy Creation and Development](/policies/university/administrative/interim_administrative_policy_001.shtml)***.

University Policies are organized under the following main headings:

* [Administrative](/policies/university/administrative-policies.shtml)
* [Academic Manual](https://www.cpp.edu/academic-manual/)
* [Facilities](/policies/university/facilities-policies.shtml)
* [Financial](/policies/university/financial-policies.shtml)
* [Alumni and Donors](/policies/university/alumni-donors-policies.shtml)
* [Information Technology](/policies/university/information-technology-policies.shtml)

Please use the menu located on the left of this page and click on a heading for a listing of policies for that category.
