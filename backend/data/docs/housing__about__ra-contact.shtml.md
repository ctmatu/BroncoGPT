# Resident Advisor Contact Information

## Contact Information

### During Business Hours

Residence Life staff are available at all times. During business hours **Monday-Friday from 8:00am-5:00pm**, please contact your Area Coordinator (AC) or the main Housing Office for assistance.

[Contact the Housing Office](../contact-us.shtml)

## Contact Information

### After Business Hours

Resident Advisors (RAs) are available to assist outside of regular business hours. You can reach an RA during the following times:

* **Monday through Thursday:** 6:00 PM – 8:00 AM
* **Friday through Sunday:** 6:00 PM – 8:00 AM (on Monday)

Resident Advisors (RAs) are here to help with after-hours concerns and support when you need it most.
