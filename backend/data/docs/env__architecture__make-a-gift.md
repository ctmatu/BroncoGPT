## Supporters

The Department of Architecture would like to thank the professionals that support the Department through supporting topic studios, serving on design studio juries, providing lectures, hosting our students for office visits and project tours, and mentoring our students. Professional interactions truly expand student learn-by-doing opportunities. An especially big thank you goes out to our industry partnerd, offices and individuals that donate staff time and financial resources.

### MiTek Timber

We are grateful for MiTek's long lasting support of the Department's Timber Design Studios. MiTek supports the collaborative effort of the Departments of Architecture and Civil Engineering to provide interdisciplinary courses, in which students from both disciplines practice collaborative design and develop an appreciation for each other's art.

## Alumni

We invite you to join the Friends of Architecture. Your support will help us to continue our tradition of academic excellence and will provide funds for the Department’s initiatives.

Your annual subscription will fuel innovations in academic programs and student services and enhance the Department’s competitiveness in attracting the best students and faculty. The tax-deductible contributions raised through this network will enable the Department to fund scholarships, library acquisitions, cutting-edge research, lecture series, publications, conferences, and state-of-the–art instructional technology.

We look forward to welcoming you as a Friend of Architecture and thank you for your support of the Department.

Please contact Chair George Proctor to get involved.

## Make a Gift

The College of Environmental Design and its departments rely on the generosity of alumni and friends to help us sustain our standards of excellence as a *learn-by-doing* institution. Your investment helps:

* **Advance**innovation and applied research within the design disciplines
* **Create**learning environments that foster student, faculty and industry collaboration
* **Transform**student training through cutting-edge instruction

*We invite you to be a part of our future!*

For more information, please visit the College of Environmental Design, which maintains a section with detailed information on how to **[make a gift](https://cpp.thankyou4caring.org/pages/env "Make a Gift to ENV!")**.
