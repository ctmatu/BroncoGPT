## Hospitality Begins with Heart. Your Future Begins with Collins.

Experience what makes The Collins College of Hospitality Management one of the most inspiring places to learn the art and business of hospitality. During your visit, you’ll connect with our Hospitable Heart Leaders: students who chose hospitality management at CPP. It’s your chance to see how Collins brings hospitality to life — and how you can become part of it.

### What can I expect during a Hospitality Hour?

A Hospitality Hour is an engaging session led by our Hospitable Heart Leaders— current students who share their passion for The Collins College of Hospitality Management (CCHM). You will explore what makes CCHM one of the nation’s premier destinations for hands-on learning in hospitality, from student life and scholarships to career opportunities and program highlights. Each session includes an interactive Q&A and a guided walking tour of our learning spaces, including the Restaurant at Kellogg Ranch.

### What is a Hospitable Heart Leader?

A Hospitable Heart Leader is a hospitality management student who embodies the spirit of hospitality through kindness, professionalism and enthusiasm. These student leaders welcome guests, share their experience and help you feel at home.
