## Welcome, Broncos!

Welcome to the website of the Office of the Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs at Cal Poly Pomona.

The Provost is the chief academic officer of the University, reporting to the President, with responsibility for the academic enterprise of the university, including coordination of academic programs, administration of academic resources, and the planning and implementation of key initiatives. The Office of the Provost also works collaboratively with the Divisions of Student Affairs, Administrative Affairs, Instructional and Information Technology, and University Advancement to facilitate organizational success across the Cal Poly Pomona campus.

The faculty and staff in the Division of Academic Affairs take great pride in our continuing efforts to sustain excellence in undergraduate and graduate education, and we are especially proud of Cal Poly Pomona's rankings at No. 2 among the [Top Public Universities in the West](https://www.usnews.com/best-colleges/rankings/regional-universities-west/top-public) and No. 3 among the [Best Regional Universities West](https://www.usnews.com/best-colleges/rankings/regional-universities-west), in addition to [other accolades](https://www.cpp.edu/aboutcpp/why-cpp/well-ranked.shtml) for quality of education, academic innovation, student outcomes, and more.
