# ENTITLED OPINIONS

## Robert Harrison and Aishwary Kumar In Conversation

![headshots of Robert Harrison and Aishwary Kumar](../img/robertaishwary.jpg)

A series of galvanizing conversations between Robert Harrison (Professor of French and Italian, Emeritus; Rosina Pierotti Professor of Italian Literature, Emeritus) at Stanford University and Aishwary Kumar (Director of The Democracy Institute and Professor of History of Political Thought) at Cal Poly Pomona on questions of violence, nonviolence and the fate of the global democratic vision.​ With its high-powered, long-form conversations, Entitled Opinions has fans around the globe, from Australia to China, Mexico to Russia. Host Robert Pogue Harrison has interviewed some of this era's leading figures in literature, philosophy, science and cultural history for over a decade.
