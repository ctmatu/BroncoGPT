## Step-by-Step-Instructions

Follow Open University Payment Instructions below:

1. Once your petition is approved, you can pay
2. Pay on your BroncoDirect account
3. Email CPGEReg@cpp.edu with receipt

**More detailed step-by-step instructions:**

1. Go to my.cpp.edu and log in with your credentials
2. Choose "BroncoDirect for Students"
3. Confirm you are in "Student Center". Choose Make a Payment
4. Allow window to open. Note: this works best with Chrome browser
5. Confirm you are on "Overview". Choose "View All"
6. Choose CPGE - College of Professional and Global Education (CPGE Student Only). Choose "View Selected Items"
7. Calculate total; balance will always show zero for guest students
8. Reminder! Please email CPGEReg@cpp.edu with a screenshot of the receipt for enrollment.
