## Disclaimer

Online Learning programs are available through the **College of Professional and Global Education (CPGE)** at Cal Poly Pomona, in partnership with ed2go, a third-party provider. These programs are sponsored by **Cal Poly Pomona Enterprises**. Learn at your own pace, perfect for busy professionals. Click on each program to register for an online learning course, offered by one of our trusted providers.

These programs are in partnership with a third-party provider and are not taught by California State Polytechnic University, Pomona (CPP) nor College of Professional & Global Education (CPGE) faculty. The courses are not offered for academic credit and are not degree-eligible.
