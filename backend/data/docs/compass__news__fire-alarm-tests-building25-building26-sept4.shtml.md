# Fire Alarm Tests Scheduled in Building 25 and Building 26 on Friday, Sept. 4

**Update on disruption:** Fire alarm testing is scheduled in Building 25 (Drama Department/Theatre) and Building 26 (University Plaza) on Friday, Sept. 4 from 7:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.

**Reason for disruption:** Allow the university to conduct state-mandated annual testing of the fire alarm system in each building.

**How does this affect me?**Loud horns and flashing strobe lights will be activated in the buildings during testing. The HVAC system will undergo interruptions. Elevators will not be affected.

**What do I need to do?** Testing may interrupt work routines. Please share this information with others who may have not received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:**Facilities Customer Service at Ext. 3030.
