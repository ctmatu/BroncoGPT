# CalWorks

## CalWORKs

![calworks logo](../img/2025/calworks-logo.jpg)

The Care Center wants to make the process of transitioning to our campus as a CalWORKs student as easy as possible. We are here to develop, improve, and promote the advancement of supportive services in higher education for single parents and low-income families.

Please review the materials below for detailed information about how we can help you as a CalWORKs student at CPP. To get connected to the CalWORKs Outreach Coordinator, please submit a Student of Concern Referral Form below. You will receive outreach within three business days.

[Submit a Student of Concern Referral Form](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=7)
