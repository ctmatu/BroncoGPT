# Liberal Studies Professor Announces New Exhibit in Los Angeles

April 4, 2023

![1526](../../img/1526.png)

A new exhibit titled “Afro Atlantic Histories” at the Los Angeles County Museum of Art (LACMA) currently features neon sculptural research-based works of art by Liberal Studies Assistant Professor Maya Stovall (*Neon Theatre*). “Afro-Atlantic Histories” charts the transatlantic slave trade and its afterlives in the African diaspora to reexamine the histories and stories of enslavement, resilience and the struggle for liberation from a global perspective. The show is currently on view and will run through September 10, 2023. For more information, visit [www.lacma.org/art/exhibition/afro-atlantic-histories](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.lacma.org%2Fart%2Fexhibition%2Fafro-atlantic-histories&data=05%7C01%7Cashleyjones%40cpp.edu%7Caf95f7b9a0ab4b01b38a08dacd7c0316%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C638048229764911856%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C3000%7C%7C%7C&sdata=vcrFAHONyh2HAQ1oA8lRtDMvKHfwzIAopSmCdOUXhYA%3D&reserved=0).
