## Important Info

### Regarding Orientation

Admitted students for Fall 2026 will complete orientation over the summer. Orientation registration will open on April 2, 2026. Students should monitor their Bronco Applicant Portal for updates. Additional orientation information will be shared in mid-March. We look forward to welcoming you to the Bronco Family!

## Orientation Day

Orientation is just the beginning of your Cal Poly Pomona experience. Attending Orientation helps ensure a successful transition into the academic community and is designed for incoming freshmen, transfer and returning students, as well as their parents, family members and supporters.
