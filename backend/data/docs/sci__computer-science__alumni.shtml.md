# Alumni

The Computer Science Department invites alumni, faculty, and students to join our LinkedIn group called *Friends of CS @ Cal Poly Pomona*

This group is intended to be used for many things, for instance:

* Letting alumni know the latest happenings in the department.
* Reconnecting alumni with each other so they can keep up with where people are working.
* Reconnecting faculty with alumni so that we can change our courses as needs in industry change.
* Connecting our graduating students with our outstanding alumni network for internships and full time positions.
* Generally discussing the latest cool technologies and where the field is heading.

Go to our profile page to join! [Friends of CS @ Cal Poly Pomona](http://www.linkedin.com/groups?gid=4421525&trk=myg_ugrp_ovr)
