# CPP Department & Program Visits

BioTrek is a haven for a number of visitors, including campus personnel that wish to walk through our facilities. BioTrek’s outdoor spaces are open to the public for self-guided visits at your leisure. Be aware that scheduled tours have the right of way as they come through. When the glasshouse doors are open, you are welcome to walk through the rainforest. We encourage all departments and campus programs to complete a [Use of Facilities Reservation Form](facilities-reservation.shtml) prior to your self-guided visit.

## Things to Remember

* Be aware of parking restrictions and fees: [Parking Permit Pay Stations](https://www.cpp.edu/parking/permits/pay-stations.shtml).
* Please do not handle the plants. Never put any plant or plant part in your mouth. Some plants are poisonous and may cause adverse reactions to you. The information provided about BioTrek’s plants is historical and for academic purposes only, and it should not be interpreted as a recommendation for use or medical treatment. Picking or gathering fruits, flowers, or other plant parts is prohibited.
* During your visit you may encounter loose slippery surfaces, falling branches, and plants with thorns. Please stay on the paths at all times, do not climb on rocks or plants, and exercise caution around the stream, pond, and bridges.
