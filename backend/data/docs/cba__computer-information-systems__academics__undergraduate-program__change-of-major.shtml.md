# Change of Major

## Thinking about changing your major to CIS?

### Requirements

There are currently no change of major meetings. Please review [the most current curriculum of your year](cis-curriculum.shtml) and make sure CIS is the major you desire to change to. If you have further questions, you can send emails to the department chair. If not, you simply submit this [change of major form](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/forms.shtml) (under Registration/Record Forms) and inform the department chair in an email right after.

In order to change your major to CIS, you must have a GPA of 2.0 or better.

[**Process for Changing Your Major to CIS**](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/guides/change-of-major.shtml)
