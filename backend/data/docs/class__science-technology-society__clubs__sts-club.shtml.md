# Science, Technology, and Society Club

## Starting Fall 2025!

The Science, Technology, and Society (STS) Club is a dynamic forum where students explore the complex interrelationships between scientific advancement, technological innovation, and societal impact. Our club creates a space for interdisciplinary discussions that bridge the gap between STEM fields and the humanities.

![STS Club logo](../img/stslogo.jpeg)We welcome students from all academic backgrounds who are interested in understanding how science and technology shape our world and how society influences their development. Through guest speakers, workshops, field trips, and collaborative projects, we aim to foster critical thinking about the role of science and technology in addressing contemporary challenges.

Join us to become part of a community dedicated to understanding and shaping the future of science and technology in society!
