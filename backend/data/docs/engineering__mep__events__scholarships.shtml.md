# MEP Scholarships

![MEP scholars](/engineering/mep/img/scholarships--grants_photo.jpg)

All scholarships are administered through the university’s scholarship application site:

[Bronco Scholarship Portal](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/)

We encourage students to review all scholarship opportunities to determine which ones they are eligible for. To see a comprehensive listing of our scholarships (including hyperlinks to the application site and selection criteria), please visit [College of Engineering Scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/honors/coe-scholarship.shtml).

Below are scholarships that are set aside for MEP students. As new opportunities are added, students will be notified via email.

* + Boeing Scholarship
  + Eric Chun Scholarship
  + Leadership Scholarship
  + Larry Gates Scholarship
  + Southern California Gas Company Scholarship
  + William Randolph Hearst (Endowed) Scholarship
