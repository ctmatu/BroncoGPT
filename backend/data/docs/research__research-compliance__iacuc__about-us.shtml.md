# Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee

**PHS Assurance: A3344-01 | USDA Registration: 93-R-0421**

**Contact Us: IACUC-office@cpp.edu**

Cal Poly Pomona’s IACUC is dedicated to the humane care and use of animals in research and teaching activities conducted by the university community.

---

## Regulatory Framework:

CPP’s IACUC adheres to established federal regulations and recognized best practices governing the humane care and use of animals:

* [USDA Animal Welfare Act](https://www.nal.usda.gov/animal-health-and-welfare/animal-welfare-act)
* [NIH Public Health Service Policy](https://olaw.nih.gov/policies-laws/phs-policy.htm)
* [Guide for Care and Use of Laboratory Animals](https://olaw.nih.gov/policies-laws/guide-care-use-lab-animals)

## IACUC Membership

Cal Poly Pomona’s IACUC is composed of a diverse group of individuals. Membership includes:

* **Veterinarians**
* **Scientists**
* **Non-scientist members**
* **Unaffiliated community representatives**

The IACUC also collaborates with the following groups to maintain the safety and wellbeing of animals and researchers here at CPP:

* [**Environmental Health & Safety (EH&S)**](https://www.cpp.edu/ehs/)
* **Research Compliance**
* **Veterinary Services**

## IACUC Responsibilities:

* Review of Animal Use Protocols
* Inspection of Animal Use Facilities
* Evaluation of the Animal Care and Use Program
* Provision of Animal Use Training
* Address Animal Welfare Concern
* Authorized to suspend an activity involving animals
