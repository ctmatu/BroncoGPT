# McNair Scholars Undergraduate Research Symposium

![Student talking to person about his research at conference](../../img/scholars/kellogg-honors-college-wolf-min.jpg)

## About the Symposium

Every year at the end of Spring semester, The Ronald E. McNair Scholars Program brings our scholars together to present their culminating research projects at our annual research symposium. The McNair Scholars Research Symposium is a celebration of our scholars' research and the perfect opportunity for our students to showcase their hard work.

## 26th McNair Symposium

The 26th Annual Ronald E. McNair Scholars Undergraduate Research Symposium will take place on Thursday, May 8, 2025 at the Kellogg West Conference Center. Research presentations will take place from 10:00am to 12:30pm.
