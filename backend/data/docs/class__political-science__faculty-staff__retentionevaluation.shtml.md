# Tenure Track & Lecturer Guidelines

## RTP and TFEC Procedures

For tenure-track faculty, Cal Poly Pomona participates in a university-wide reappointment, tenure, and promotion (RTP) program. For lecturers, Cal Poly Pomona participates in a yearly evaluation program. All faculty evaluation is set in accordance with guidelines set forth by policies agreed upon by the administration, faculty, and the CFA.

**Tenure-Track Faculty**

**University Policy**Policy #1328 is intended to be a guide for the conduct of all reappointment, tenure, and promotion (RTP) matters. Every September before tenure and promotion, faculty are expected to submit periodic evaluation narratives for reappointment.

**Department Standards**The standards for RTP guidelines outline the reappointment, tenure, and promotion process and standards for tenure-track faculty in the Department of Political Science. Last updated 2023/2024 AY.

The previous standards for RTP had been enacted in 2022/2023 and prior years.

**Lecturer Faculty****University Policy**Policy #1336 is intended to be a guide for the conduct of evaluation and reappointment matters for temporary faculty. Every March, lecturers who teach in consecutive semesters are expected to submit perioidic evaluation narratives for reappointment.

**Department Standards**Our Department RTP Guidelines outline the appointment, evaluation, and re-appointment process for lecturers in the Department of Political Science. Last updated 8/1/2021.
