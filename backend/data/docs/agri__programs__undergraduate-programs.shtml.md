# Undergraduate Programs

## Become by Learning

![Two female students work with a dog in a vet-tech lab.](../img/2021-vet-tech-lab-960x450.jpg)

Discover the worlds of farming, food, and fashion through our undergraduate majors.

At the Huntley College of Agriculture, you'll receive a STEM-based curriculum and hands-on learning opportunities that will prepare you to enter your chosen profession.

Our majors are connected to major industries, and there are numerous career opportunities in them. Our Southern California location provides us access to industries through field trips, guest speakers, and internships.

Learn more about our majors below:

### Agribusiness & Food Industry Management

Combine your passion for growing plants or raising animals with the skills needed to run your own business, a startup, or a global conglomerate.](/agri/agribusiness-agricultural-science/programs/agribusiness-and-food-industry-management-bs.shtml)

### Agricultural Science

Get a strong foundation in agricultural basics that will prepare you for a wide variety of careers. Combine that with specialization in one of our many minors.](/agri/agribusiness-agricultural-science/programs/agriculture-science-bs.shtml)

[Card 3 of 8

![A woman in scrubs holds a dog while scribbling notes. ](../img/2021-programs-ahs-1124x684.jpg)

### Animal Health Science

With this degree, you are eligible to take the Veterinary Technician National Exam and become a registered veterinary technologist, the equivalent of a nurse.](/agri/animal-science/programs/animal-health-science-bs.shtml)

[Card 4 of 8

![A woman kneels and pets a goat.](../img/2024-programs-as-1124x684.jpg)

### Animal Science

Want to become a veterinarian? Study animal science. But this major also offers other options, working as a researcher, farm manager, or in the animal health or food production industries.](/agri/animal-science/programs/animal-science-bs.shtml)

Card 5 of 8

![A man cuts fabric on a table while another looks on.](../img/2024-programs-amm-1124x684.jpg)

### Apparel Merchandising & Management

Get a comprehensive education preparing you to work in the global apparel industry, whether it's in design and production, fashion retailing, or textiles.

[Card 6 of 8

![A woman in lab coat offers a food sample to another woman.](../img/2025-programs-fst-1124x684.jpg)

### Food Science & Technology

Employ science, statistics, engineering, and technology to create healthier foods that taste better, are easier to produce, and last longer.](/agri/nutrition-food-science/programs/food-science-and-technology-bs.shtml)

[Card 7 of 8

![A man in lab coat poses with a display about sodium in food.](../img/2025-programs-ntr-1124x684.jpg)

### Nutrition

Help people live healthier lives by becoming registered dietitians, health coaches, or researchers. You can work in health care, food service management, or community nutrition settings.](/agri/nutrition-food-science/programs/nutrition-b.s.shtml)

Card 8 of 8

![A man in a hat harvests crops on a farm.](../img/2025-programs-plt-1124x684.jpg)

### Plant Science

Learn the science behind farming and growing plants. Graduates have found work as farm managers, viticulturists, sports turf managers, pest control advisors, urban farmers, and arborists.
