# Safer Return 2021

## Planning Update on Safer Return

A spring Campus Conversation to be held via webinar on Thursday, April 8, from noon to 1 p.m. The purpose of this meeting will be an update and discussion on the comprehensive planning for the return to campus.

As vaccine distribution moves forward and the number of COVID cases continues to decrease statewide, Cal Poly Pomona remains focused on being fully prepared to repopulate our campus safely while prioritizing student success. The Campus Conversation will feature presentations covering the full range of planning — from budget considerations to teaching and learning to campus life — as well as time to consider feedback and questions from the campus community.

A video of the campus conversation, as well as a PDF of the presentation, will be available for download below.
