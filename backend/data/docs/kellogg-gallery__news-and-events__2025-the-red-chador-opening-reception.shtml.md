# "The Red Chador: Close Encounters"

January 7, 2025

![Opening reception promo image with date and time and an image of "The Rainbow Brigade"](../exhibitions/2025-the-red-chador-close-encounters/img/opening-reception-500x500.png)

## Artist Reception

Join us on **Thursday, February 20, 2025**, from **4:00–7:00 PM** at the **Kellogg University Art Gallery** for the artist reception of Anida Yoeu Ali’s *The Red Chador: Close Encounters*. A collection of photographs, videos, and installations exploring themes of identity, resilience, and resistance to Islamophobia and misogyny.
