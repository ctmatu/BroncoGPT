# Research Distinction

## Be recognized for your engagement in Research, Scholarship, and Creative Activities!

---

The **Research Distinction**, awarded by the [Office of Undergraduate Research](https://cpp.cascadecms.com/entity/open.act?type=page&id=1a74cc1b8647f71f27a1993cf2bff490&confId=020b75608647f71f74b7f89fbfafc67f), recognizes students who exhibit exceptional commitment to the research, scholarship, and creative activities communities and embrace the "Become by Doing" culture at Cal Poly Pomona. Students who fulfill all the requirements for the Research Distinction receive a [digital badge](https://cpp.badgr.com/public/badges/XMMbAjV2Se2Ry486Kw16hA) and burgundy-gold cord.

![Beverly Cotter profile](../../img/research-distinction/distinction-profile-1.jpg)

If I was not gtive the opportunity early on in my path to conduct research under my faculty mentors, I would not be able to start a Ph.D. program immediately after receiving my undergraduate degree. Betty Cotter 2020 Research Distinction Awardee. Office of Undergraduate Research Cal Poly Pomona

I took the things I loved about working at an equine therapeutic riding center, along with animal science and combined them into my research project. Rachel Christensen, 2020 Research Distinction Awardee. Office of Undergraduate Research Cal Poly Pomona
