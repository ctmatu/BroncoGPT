## Become an Orientation Leader

### The best job on campus

The Office of New Student Programs & Family Engagement employs knowledgeable, friendly, and helpful Cal Poly Pomona students as Orientation Leaders, who play an important role in the success of their fellow Broncos!

## About the Orientation Leader Position

Orientation Leaders play a significant role in the incoming Bronco’s journey at Cal Poly Pomona. As an Orientation Leader, you can directly impact student success, help create life-long connections among classmates, and foster a sense of belonging for incoming students. Become a Cal Poly Pomona expert while sharing your student experience with parents, family members, and other supporters. Reach out and email us with any questions about this opportunity.

Undergraduate students from all majors are encouraged to apply!

Compensation includes: $16.90/hour paid monthly, summer housing, free meals during orientation, and priority registration (upon Academic Senate approval).
