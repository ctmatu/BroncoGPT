## About Me

Alison Pearlman is Professor Emerita of Art History in the Department of Art at Cal Poly Pomona. She is also a McGinnis-Ritchie-award-winning author of nonfiction. Her books on contemporary art, design, and restaurants include *Unpackaging Art of the 1980s* (University of Chicago Press, 2003), *Smart Casual: The Transformation of Gourmet Restaurant Style in America* (University of Chicago Press, 2013), *May We Suggest: Restaurant Menus and the Art of Persuasion*(Agate Surrey, 2018), and the forthcoming *Meet Me at the West Beach Café: Art, Restaurants, and the Rebirth of L.A*. For updates, please see [www.alisonpearlman.com](https://www.alisonpearlman.com).
