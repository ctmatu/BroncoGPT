# Transfer Credit FAQ

## FAQs

### Does CPP accept electronic (PDF), official transcripts after completing the transfer course?

Yes, Cal Poly Pomona accepts electronic (PDF), official transcripts. Electronic Transcript Exchange (ETX) is also acceptable if the sender participates in this service.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to tga@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### Where do I send my transcripts for transfer credit?

You may send external transcripts to verify@cpp.edu. If you do not have access to e-transcripts, please mail the hard copy official transcript to the following address: **Cal Poly Pomona, Attn: Office of Admissions, 3801 W. Temple Ave, Pomona, CA 91768**.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to tga@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### How do I transfer credits from another college to Cal Poly Pomona?

A student who has attended an accredited two-year or four-year college will be given full credit for college-level courses completed successfully. Credit for courses taken at other institutions count toward fulfilling the curriculum requirements when applicable; other courses count as elective credit. Cal Poly Pomona does not accept credit for courses in religious practice. To submit transfer credit for evaluation, send your official transcript(s) to the Office of Admissions at verify@cpp.edu. For detailed information please refer to the [University Catalog, Transfer Credit policy](https://catalog.cpp.edu/content.php?catoid=53&navoid=4006#Transfer_Credit).

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to tga@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.
