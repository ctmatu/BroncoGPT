# Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS) Program

Cal Poly Pomona Risk Management is committed to supporting the safe usage of Unmanned Aircraft Systems (UAS)/Drones for instructional, research, and work related tasks while adhering to federal, state, and local regulations. The UAS/Drone Program ensures compliance to these laws/policies and protects personnel, animals, property and the environment on and off campus. All personnel, including faculty, staff, students and third parties/contractors wishing to operate drones on Cal Poly Pomona property, or as part of a Cal Poly Pomona activity (instructional, research, student organization), must request approval prior to flight.

[Risk and Safety Solutions (RSS) Drone Application](https://csu.risksafety.solutions/auth/login?originalUri=https%3A%2F%2Fcsu.risksafety.solutions%2Fdrones%2F): Manage UAS/Drone equipment, pilot information, flight requests & flight projects.
