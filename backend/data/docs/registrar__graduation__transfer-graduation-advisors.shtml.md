# Transfer & Graduation Specialists

## TGS Team

**Meet Your Transfer and Graduation Specialists (TGS)!**

Transfer and Graduation Specialists (TGS) are responsible for evaluating transfer coursework and clearing students for graduation.  They provide support to students and  campus partners by:

* **Interpreting and explaining academic policies** and **Title 5 requirements**
* **Providing guidance** on **General Education (GE)** and overall **degree requirements**
* **Assisting with academic petitions,** including course substitution or general academic petitions, to help students navigate the process
* **Serving as a key resource** to ensure a smooth and successful academic journey

**Find Your TGS**

TGS are assigned to students by major.  Click the [TGS List](tga-assignments-for-ro-website.pdf) to find your assigned TGS.

For further information, visit [Applying for Graduation](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/graduation/applying-for-graduation.shtml) or contact the TGS team via email at tga@cpp.edu or [Contact Us](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/contact-us.shtml).
