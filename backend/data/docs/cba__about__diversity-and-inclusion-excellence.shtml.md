## Diversity Statement

The College of Business Administration and Singelyn Graduate School of Business at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona take pride in offering a premier polytechnic business school experience. Our work in diversity, equity and inclusion is at the core of our educational mission, as these ideals fuel innovation and foster personal growth. We celebrate and value the many backgrounds, experiences, perspectives, cultures and identities of our Broncos. Students, faculty and staff are vital contributors to our welcoming Southern California campus.
Our affordable and accessible polytechnic experience fosters belonging for all, empowering our Broncos to proudly share their diverse perspectives. We inspire our community to be socially responsible and culturally sensitive leaders, prepared to make impactful, empathetic and purposeful contributions in the CBA and beyond.
