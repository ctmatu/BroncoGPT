# Program and Student Learning Outcomes

![students discuss projects](../img/students-discuss-projects.jpg)

*Students in Assistant Professor Robert Alexander's undergraduate studio discuss their projects.*

The Department of Architecture at the College of Environmental Design at Cal Poly Pomona has a unique set of learning outcomes related to the university's institutional mission and goals, and more importantly, critical objectives specific to the architectural discipline. The bachelor's program (B.Arch) PLOs and SLOs are designed to equip students with skills critical to ensuring their success for a future in the profession.
