# Welcome to the Ahimsa Center Programs and Events!

## Explore Recent Events

To facilitate the teaching and learning of nonviolence and nonviolent social change, the Ahimsa Center in the College of Letters, Arts and Social Sciences at Cal Poly Pomona offers a variety of programs and events.

## Recent Events

![ahimsa student leaders in a group holding certificates](../img/events/oxford-consortium-human-rights2.jpg)

### Oxford Consortium for Human Rights

Ahimsa student leaders—Everett Elias and Jenaveve Gregory—represented our work in human rights and political nonviolence at the Oxford Consortium for Human Rights held at Sonoma State University in January 2024.
