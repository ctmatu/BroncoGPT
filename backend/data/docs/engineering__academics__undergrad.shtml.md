# Undergraduate Programs

[College of Engineering Info Card (PDF)](infocards/coe_infocard.pdf)

The College of Engineering offers 11 Bachelor of Science Degrees in:

![](../img/academics/aro.jpg)

## Aerospace Engineering

[Aerospace Engineering Info Card (PDF)](infocards/aro-v6.a.pdf "Aerospace Engineering Info Card (PDF)")

This program trains students in aerodynamics, structures, propulsion, flight mechanics and controls, astronautics, and systems engineering. The department has nine laboratories, including a Subsonic Wind Tunnel, a Supersonic Wind Tunnel and an Uninhabited Aerial Vehicle (UAV) Laboratory.

[Aerospace Engineering](https://www.cpp.edu/~engineering/aro/ "Aerospace Engineering. ")

![CME students](../img/academics/cme.jpg)

## Chemical and Materials Engineering

[Chemical and Materials Engineering Info Card (PDF)](infocards/cme-v6.a.pdf)

This program offers a degree in chemical engineering, which includes work related to the design, construction and operation of plants for fuels, plastics, fibers, foods and pharmaceuticals. Because of their strong background in chemistry and materials processing, many chemical engineers also work for industry as environmental engineers.

[Chemicals and Materials Engineering](https://www.cpp.edu/~engineering/cme/ "Chemicals and Materials Engineering. ")

![CE surveying](../img/academics/ce.jpg)

## Civil Engineering

[Civil Engineering Info Card (PDF)](infocards/ce-v6.a.pdf)

*[Available Program Options: General, Environmental, or Geospatial]*

This program teaches students how to design, build, operate and maintain the structures and infrastructure that form our modern society. These facilities include buildings, bridges, highways, water supply systems, environmental protection systems, ports, railroads, dams, airports and many others.

[Civil Engineering](https://www.cpp.edu/~engineering/ce/ "Civil Engineering. ")

![ECE students](../img/academics/ece.jpg)

## Electrical and Computer Engineering

[Electrical and Computer Engineering Info Card (PDF)](infocards/ece-v6.a.pdf)

**[Electrical Engineering](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/ece/undergrad_programs/bsee.shtml)**This program trains students in the fundamentals of electrical engineering including communications, controls, instrumentation, electronics, illumination and power. At the junior and senior level students may specialize in power, electronics, or controls and instrumentation.

**[Computer Engineering](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/ece/undergrad_programs/bsce.shtml)**
This program trains undergraduate students in the fundamental electrical engineering principles, design of computer hardware and software, and embedded systems.

[Electrical and Computer Engineering](https://www.cpp.edu/~engineering/ece/ "Electrical and Computer Engineering. ")

![Dr. Norali Pernalete](../img/academics/emet.jpg)

## Electromechanical Engineering Technology

[Electromechanical Engineering Technology Info Card (PDF)](infocards/emet-v6.pdf)

[Electronic Systems Engineering Technology](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/et/eset/index.shtml)
Graduates from this program are technical members of the engineering team. They are well grounded in the principles necessary to apply current engineering methods and design procedures to solve practical technical problems currently found in industry.
**[Electromechanical Systems Engineering Technology](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/et/emset/index.shtml)**
The program's mechanical emphasis stresses the application and design of mechanical and thermal power systems utilizing strength of materials, metallurgy, statics, dynamics, fluid mechanics, thermodynamics and heat transfer.

[Electromechanical Engineering Technology](https://www.cpp.edu/~engineering/et/ "Electromechanical Engineering Technology. ")

![IME lecture](../img/academics/ime1.jpg)

## Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering

[Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering Info Card (PDF)](infocards/ime-v6.pdf)

**Industrial Engineering**
This program prepares students as problem solvers who can tackle the complex interactions associated with productivity of workers, capital and facilities. They design and implement quality improvement methods for a more efficient, safe, productive work place with proper working conditions.

**Manufacturing Engineering**
The program prepares students as experts in the design and analysis of complex computer-controlled manufacturing and automation systems. They determine the processes to use and sequence of operations required to produce the product.

[Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering](https://www.cpp.edu/~engineering/ime/ "Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering. ")

![ME student](../img/academics/me.jpg)

## Mechanical Engineering

[Mechanical Engineering Info Card (PDF)](infocards/me-v6.pdf)

This program provides a quality, well-rounded education that is based on imparting fundamental knowledge and skills in mathematics and pure science as well as engineering science and design.

[Mechanical Engineering](https://www.cpp.edu/~engineering/me/ "Mechanical Engineering. ")
