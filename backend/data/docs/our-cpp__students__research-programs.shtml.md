# Research Programs

## Campus Research Programs

#### *Be Part of a Scholar Network*

---

Each program is different and may offer additional opportunities beyond the research scholarship. You may apply for more than one program.

## Achieve Scholars Program

The Achieve Scholars Program (ASP) helps students in all majors connect to Peer and Faculty mentors to learn about research and career development opportunities.

[Learn more about Achieve Scholars Program](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/asp/index.shtml "Learn more about Achieve Scholars Program. ")

![Panel of professors sitting in a room with the cal-bridge logo in the upper right hand corner](../img/programs/calbridge-pic.jpg)

## Cal-Bridge

The mission of the Cal-Bridge program is to increase the number of California State University (CSU) students completing their bachelor's degree and successfully entering a PhD program to study physics, astronomy, computer science, computer engineering, or a closely related field.

[Learn more about Cal-Bridge](https://calbridge.org/ "Learn more about Cal-Bridge. ")

![Student at podium with a projected presentation behind them and campare logo on upper right hand corner](../img/programs/campare-pic.jpg)

## Cal-Bridge Summer (CAMPARE)

The Cal-Bridge Summer (CAMPARE) program recruits undergraduate students from any field of science or engineering to participate in research projects in the summer in physics, astronomy, and astrobiology at world-class research sites across the country.

[Learn more about Cal-Bridge Summer (CAMPARE)](https://calbridge.org/summer-program/ "Learn more about Cal-Bridge Summer (CAMPARE). ")

![student working in a lab and rise logo on upper right hand corner](../img/programs/b2d.jpg)

## CPP-UCR Master's Bridges to PhD Program (B2D)

The NIH Bridges from MS to PhD program is a partnership between CPP and UCR with a goal to provide Master’s of science students from diverse groups, including those from underrepresented racial and ethnic groups, low socio-economic backgrounds and those with disabilities, the technical, communication, and professional skills needed to successfully transition into PhD programs and develop careers as biomedical researchers.

[Learn more about B2D](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/bridges-masters-to-phd/index.shtml "Learn more about B2D. ")

![Students talking with ESP in the top right corner.](../img/programs/esp-pic.jpg)

## Emerging Scholars Program

 The Emerging Scholars Program is an initiative for early career students, that targets freshmen, sophomores, and first-year transfer students. The program's goal is to immerse students in research during a one semester period, through a blend of workshops, advising sessions, and community engagement activities students will build the essential skills needed to navigate the research processes.

[Learn more about ESP](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/emerging-scholars/index.shtml "Learn more about ESP. ")

![Student painting and Engage logo in the upper right corner.](../img/programs/engage-pic1.jpg)

## Engage

The Engaging and Graduating Hispanic Students Through Undergraduate Research Program, or Engage for short, aims to provide broad access to undergraduate research for historically underserved students by creating a flexible model of engagement and through multiple touchpoints while also supporting student success.

[Learn more about Engage](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/engage/index.shtml "Learn more about Engage. ")

![Student sitting alone in a computer lab with the LTD icon on the upper right hand corner](../img/programs/ltd-pic1.jpg)

## Projects Hatchery

Projects Hatchery allows students to turn a meaningful and innovative idea into a fully realized project. Students are guided through every phase of development, from conception to implementation to completion.

[Learn more about Projects Hatchery](https://www.cpp.edu/hatchery/index.shtml "Learn more about Projects Hatchery. ")

![Student in classroom looking through microscope and lsamp logo on upper right hand corner](../img/programs/lsamp-pic.jpg)

## LSAMP

LSAMP provides academic support, advising, opportunities for networking and UR and preparation for graduate school.

[Learn more about LSAMP](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/lsamp/students/index.shtml "Learn more about LSAMP. ")

![Student presenting their work with their presentation projected on wall and mcnair icon on upper right hand corner](../img/programs/mcnair-pic.jpg)

## McNair Scholars

The McNair Scholars Program prepares undergraduate students for Ph.D. programs by promoting student success through developing skills, research, and mentorship.

[Learn more about McNair Scholars](https://www.cpp.edu/mcnair/index.shtml "Learn more about McNair Scholars. ")

![Students on a research project and Pathways icon on upper right hand corner](../img/programs/pathways-pic.png)

## Pathways to STEM

Pathways to STEM is a collaboration between Cal Poly Pomona, the Oak Crest Institute of Science, and the Jet Propulsion Laboratory to engage Citrus College and Mt. San Antonio College students in STEM exploration.

[Learn more about Pathways to STEM](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/pathways.shtml "Learn more about Pathways to STEM. ")

![](../img/programs/stars-pic.jpg)

## STARS

The STARS: Student Success and Transfer Articulation through Research and Support Services Undergraduate Research and Mentorship Experience Program seeks to engage STEM undergraduate students from Cal Poly Pomona, Mt. SAC, and Citrus College to engage in a research experience and related wrap-around services (such as workshops, advising, etc.).

[Learn more about STARS](http://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/stars/index.shtml "Learn more about STARS. ")

![Student writing on whiteboard and sure icon on upper right hand corner](../img/programs/sure-pic.jpg)

## SURE

SURE is a summer research experience for undergraduates where partnering community colleges send STEM students to participate in high-level faculty-mentored research projects.

[Learn more about SURE](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/sure/index.shtml "Learn more about SURE. ")
