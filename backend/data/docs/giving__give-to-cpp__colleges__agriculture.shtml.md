# Give to Cal Poly Pomona

## Support the Don B. Huntley College of Agriculture

The [Don B. Huntley College of Agriculture](https://www.cpp.edu/~agri/) offers students a choice of eight majors that range from the traditional disciplines to current topics of sustainable agriculture, food science and apparel industries. The college operates with a philosophy of learn by doing and community engagement as a foundation for excellence in understanding and disseminating knowledge of the natural and human systems that underlie the agricultural sciences and their applications. Students become future leaders in the agricultural and related industries through innovative approaches to learning and student support from faculty, staff, and the community.

### **Funding Priorities**

* Urban & Community Agriculture related programs
* Student stipends for research and project-related work
* Student scholarships for domestic and foreign internships, educational trips, and conference experiences
* Update and maintain equipment for student learning experiences
* Create physical spaces for education and research
* Faculty Scholarships & Innovation
* Endowed Faculty Positions
* Support Recruitment, Retention and Development of Faculty
