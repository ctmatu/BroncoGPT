## Introduction

To promote safe and sanitary swimming pools and spa facilities, and to avoid the spread of waterborne diseases and physical injury, EH&S conducts routine inspections of campus pools and spas. These include the BRIC, Kellogg West Conference Center and University Village. EH&S ensures pools and spas are maintained according to the California Health and Safety Code regulations.

EH&S provides consultations and plan reviews prior to any modifications or construction of CPP pools and spas.
