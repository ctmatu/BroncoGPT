# Huntley Alumni Chapter

![Arabian horses grazing in a field](arabian-horses.jpg)

## Mission Statement

A good networking resource and campus reconnection outlet, this chapter will serve the professional and practical interests of our Don B. Huntley College of Agriculture alumni. Chapter activities benefit not only the members themselves, but also the university and current students through events, programs and scholarships.
