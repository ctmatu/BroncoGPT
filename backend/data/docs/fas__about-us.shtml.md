# About Us

## About Us

**Our Vision**

Financial Services will be a strategic partner recognized for exceptional customer service, financial integrity, and promoting fiscal and responsible stewardship.

**Our Mission**

We maintain the University's fiscal integrity through collaboration to support the strategic goals and objectives of all units on campus to enhance student success through the development of effective, ethical, and the best practices.

**Values:**

Collaboration \*Integrity \* Commitment \* Equity \* Competence \* Continuous Improvement \* Transparency
