# Join us!

![](cfalogo.png)

Welcome to the California Faculty Association (CFA) / Pomona Chapter. CFA is a vibrant union of 29,000 dedicated professors, lecturers, librarians, counselors, and coaches, all committed to enriching the lives of the 485,000 students within the California State University (CSU) system. Our members work tirelessly across 23 campuses, fostering critical-thinking skills, technical expertise, and cultural awareness in our students, preparing them to be thoughtful, productive, and creative contributors to society.

**Our Mission**: At CFA, we stand for quality education, fairness for educators, and policies that ensure access to higher education for all. We invite you to join us, stay informed, and advocate for your colleagues, students, and staff. Your membership is crucial in our mission to protect and enhance one of the world's greatest university systems. Together, we can make a difference.

**Join CFA today** and be a part of a movement that champions academic freedom, faculty rights, and the future of higher education.

**[Become a member now.](https://www.calfac.org/join-cfa)**
