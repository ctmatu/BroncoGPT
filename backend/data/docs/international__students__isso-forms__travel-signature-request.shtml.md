# Travel Signature Request

## How to Request a Travel Signature

1. [Complete Travel Signature Request](https://www.cpp.edu/international/students/isso-forms/index.shtml)
2. Allow **10 business days (weekends and holidays are excluded)** for processing
3. Check your CPP email
   1. An I-20 with new travel signature will be sent to you via CPP email

#### Please note, in-person pickup is currently unavailable.

## Important:

* **I-20s signed and sent as electronic scans can be used for all official purposes**, including visa applications and renewals, and entry to the U.S. For more information, please read the [guidance issued by Student and Exchange Visitor Program](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/I20-guidance.pdf).

* **Print a hard copy of your I-20 and sign** before appearing for your visa interview and/or attempting to enter the U.S. Electronic versions of your I-20 (for example, showing your I-20 on your mobile device to a visa or Customs officer) will not be accepted under any circumstances. Do not leave your I-20 in your checked luggage when traveling.
