# Mailing List Request

Exhibition Announcements are sent either by physical mail (via mailing address) and/or digital e-blast format (via mass email with MailChimp or Constant Contact digital platforms). If you wish to be on our regular mailing list please indicate your preference by providing either your mailing address or email address.

**For physical Exhibition Announcement
mailers please provide:**

Full First and Last Name(s)

Street Address

City, State, Zip Code

via e-mail address please provide:**

e-mail address:

## For Ink & Clay Artists...

If you are an artist interested in receiving our annual **Ink & Clay Prospectus** via email please indicate this as well by stating this in your email request:   **Add me to your Mailing List - Ink & Clay Artist.**

## To be Added to our Regular Mailing List...

Please send your Mailing List Request to University Art Galleries via email at: artgalleries@cpp.edu with " **Add me to your Mailing List**" written in the **Subject Line**.

![Students visiting Olga Lah’s Lucent Shifts.  navigate down for further details](https://www.cpp.edu/kellogg-gallery/engage/img/mailing-list-image1.png)

Students visiting Olga Lah’s *Lucent Shifts*, a site-specific installation made up of discarded material—hundreds of sheets of silver reflective plastic. The work ascends across the space, creating an illusion of floating midair. The surface appears active as the material mirrors any objects or figures that pass by. Installation was exhibited at the Huntley Gallery in 2018.
