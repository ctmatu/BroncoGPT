# Students

The goal of our collaborative project is to improve the pathway for PCC students interested in continuing their Environmental Science careers at Cal Poly Pomona, through enhanced advising and mentoring of PCC transfer students before and after the transfer process has been completed. The goal of our research projects is to foster mentoring relationships between students and faculty, expose students to 'real science,' enhance students' enthusiasm for continued education in the environmental sciences and increase their chances of success in undergraduate careers and beyond.
