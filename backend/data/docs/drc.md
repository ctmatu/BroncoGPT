## Important Information - On-Campus Housing

The Disability Resource Center (DRC) works in collaboration with University Housing Services (UHS) and Cal Poly Pomona Enterprises (CPPE) Student Housing to coordinate appropriate accommodations for students with documented disabilities or health conditions. Please note, the DRC reviews housing requests and approves accommodations as needed. DRC Housing accommodations are considered on a case-by-case basis and are determined by the nature of the student’s disability and the functional impact it has within the campus living environment. The DRC does not have the authority to prioritize students on housing waitlists. Approval of housing accommodations does not guarantee housing placement and is contingent upon a student’s standing with University Housing Services and/or CPPE Student Housing. To learn more, please read our [housing accommodations policies and procedures](https://www.cpp.edu/drc/accommodations-and-procedures/policies-and-procedures-links.shtml#0-q10).
