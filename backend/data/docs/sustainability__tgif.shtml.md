## Associated Students Inc. Green Initiative Fund

The Green Initiative Fund (TGIF) is a student-led program that supports sustainability projects on campus. It provides financial resources for initiatives that advance environmental stewardship, promote renewable energy, reduce waste, conserve resources, and foster a culture of sustainability among the student body. Funded through student fees, TGIF empowers students to take direct action on climate change, environmental justice, and sustainable development goals.
