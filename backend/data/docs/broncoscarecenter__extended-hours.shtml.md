# Tap In to Wellness

## Wellness Wednesdays at the Care Center. 5 p.m. to 7 p.m. on select Wednesdays this Fall

As part of the Tap In to Wellness campaign, the Care Center and campus partners will host special Wellness Wednesday events throughout the Fall semester! Each program offers a chance to relax, connect, and explore new ways to support your well-being.

[See full schedule on Tap In to Wellness Website](https://www.cpp.edu/student-affairs/tap-in.shtml)
