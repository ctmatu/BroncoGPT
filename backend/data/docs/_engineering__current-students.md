# Current Students

Links for various resources for current students. Need something not covered here? Contact engineering@cpp.edu.

## Academic and Campus Resources

[**Engineering Advising Center**](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/advising/index.shtml) - Our staff advisors assist you with long-term course planning, general education course information, and university policy and procedures.

[**Curriculum Sheets and Roadmaps**](https://www.cpp.edu/academic-programs/academic-advising/tools/sheets-roadmaps/index.shtml) - Use this to track your academic progress. These sheets list the degree requirements for your program.

[**University Catalog**](https://catalog.cpp.edu/) - Contains general information about the university, rules that affect all students, descriptions of graduate and undergraduate courses, and the degree requirements for each program.

[**Academic Calendar**](https://www.cpp.edu/~registrar/calendars) - A list of key dates and deadlines, like the beginning and end of a semester, finals and when tution fees are due.

[**Applying for Graduation**](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/graduation/applying-for-graduation.shtml) - Graduating? Congrats! Go here to learn to apply for graduation.

[**Career Center**](https://www.cpp.edu/~career) - Apply for job and internship opportunities, get career counseling and find out about our free-to-attend career fairs.

[**Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships**](https://www.cpp.edu/~financial-aid) - Find out about financial aid and scholarship options to make college more affordable.

[**CPP College of Engineering Scholarships**](../honors/coe-scholarship.shtml) - Up-to-date information on CPP Engineering scholarships. Review opportunities, award amounts and application information.

## College Technical Resources

Contact coe\_it\_support@cpp.edu for IT/tech questions not covered below.

**[Software for Students, Faculty and Staff](free-software.shtml)** - Learn how to obtain software like Microsoft Windows and the Adobe Creative Suite.

[**Engineering Student Computer Accounts**](student-account.shtml) - A primer on the policies and benefits on accessing computers in the College of Engineering.

[**College Remote File Services**](https://services.engineering.cpp.edu/files/Login.aspx) - Access files you stored in the College of Engineering's network

[**Reset Password**](https://services.engineering.cpp.edu/PasswordRequest/) - Reset your password. If you've lost your password and need to recover it, contact coe\_it\_support@cpp.edu.

[**Student Swipe Card Access Request (PDF)**](/documents/departments/student_access_form.pdf) / [**ECE/ECET Student Swipe Card Access Request (PDF)**](../documents/departments/ece_student_access_form.pdf) - Primarily for student workers who need swipe card access to rooms and facilities required to work.
