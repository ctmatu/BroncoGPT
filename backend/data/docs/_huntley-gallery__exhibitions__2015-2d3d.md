## PolyKroma 2015: 2D3D Juried Art Exhibition

May 18, 2015 to Jun 14, 2015

**Location:** Huntley University Art Gallery

![2d3d Juried Art Competition](img/11760294_692725417499425_919135391438555436_n.jpg)This student exhibition features art and graphic design by students at all academic levels. This year’s exhibition was juried by: **Peter Emmerich**, Art Director and Character Designer for Walt Disney Television Animation, who also studied illustration at the Fashion Institute of Technology; **Meriel Stern**, formerly the artist-in-residence at The Armory in Pasadena, and currently is an education design consultant, and fine artist sculptor exhibiting her work throughout the Southern California area. Of 257 submissions reviewed in three rounds of eliminations, the jurors deliberated at length to make the 52 selections of artworks exhibited.
