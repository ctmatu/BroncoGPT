## About me

Dr. Abdelfattah Amamra is currently an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. Dr. Amamra received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Information Security from University of Quebec School of Higher Technology, and a Master of Engineering degree in Computer Engineering from Concordia University.

## **Research Area**

Dr. Amamra research intersts include Cyber Security, Big Data Analysis, and Machine Learning.

## **Research Topics**

Smartphone Security
Network Security
Data Driven Cyber Security
Machine Learning
