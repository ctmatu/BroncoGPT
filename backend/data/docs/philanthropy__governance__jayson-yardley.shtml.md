Jayson Yardley

Jayson Yardley is chief executive officer of Knowtion Health, a healthcare revenue cycle management company focused on improving the patient financial experience. A seasoned executive, innovator, and author, Yardley has led multiple healthcare and consulting organizations, including Avadyne Health, Conifer Health Solutions, and Cymetrix, and holds a patent for the use of augmented reality in billing education. He is the author of *Rev Up! Bold and Disruptive Strategies to Rev Up Your Revenue Cycle Hero’s Journey* and has been featured on NPR and iHeartRadio.

Yardley earned his MBA from Cal Poly Pomona in 1998 and holds a bachelor’s degree in political science from UCLA. He is an active member of the Healthcare Financial Management Association and a frequent speaker on healthcare technology and innovation.

At Cal Poly Pomona, Yardley is a lifetime member of the Alumni Association and the CBA Dean’s Advisory Council. He has served as a guest lecturer, panelist, and alumni mentor, and will begin a two-year term as chair of the Dean’s Advisory Council in 2025.
