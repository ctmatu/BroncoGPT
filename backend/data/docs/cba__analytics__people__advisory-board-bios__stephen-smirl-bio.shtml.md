# Stephen Smirl

Envista - Director, Data and Analytics, Business Intelligence

Stephen builds and empowers data and analytics communities to drive business outcomes with data and analytics. Currently leads a global team of 18 analysts, data scientists, data engineers, and developers focusing on: Analytics, Business Intelligence, Cloud Data Warehouse, IoT, Data Governance and Master Data Management, Reporting and Visualization across sales, marketing, operations, and finance areas to improve business outcomes.

Created and lead corporate wide Analytics Center of Excellence (CoE) focusing on connecting and empowering the analyst community and increasing adoption of advanced analytics to improve business results. BS, Business Management, California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, ’95.
