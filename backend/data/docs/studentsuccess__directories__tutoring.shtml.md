## Maximizing Engineering Potential (MEP) Tutoring

### About MEP

Maximizing Engineering Potential (MEP) offers tutoring to all College of Engineering students. Tutoring supports lower-division math, physics, and chemistry classes for all engineering majors and selected upper-division engineering courses.

[MEP Website](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/mep/tutoring.shtml%20)

![Two students in MEP pose with Billy Bronco](../img/directories/tutoring-directory/mep-contact.png)

## Reading, Advising, and Mentoring Program (RAMP) Tutoring

### About RAMP

RAMP tutoring is free to students who are low-income, first-generation, or a student with a disability.

[RAMP Website](https://www.cpp.edu/ramp/%20)

## Science Educational Enhancement Services (SEES)

### About SEES

SEES has a series of opportunities within the program to help students progress and excel. Some of these opportunities are available to anyone in the College of Science. However, due to the financial limitations of offering these programs, some opportunities are for SEES members only.

[SEES Website](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/sees/index.shtml%20)
