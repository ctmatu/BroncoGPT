## Returning Admission FAQs

## Welcome Back!

Cal Poly Pomona knows that the admission process can be confusing and we want to help make it easier. Our FAQ below will help answer any questions our returning students may have.

## Returning Student Process

### I am not sure if I am in good standing with Cal Poly Pomona, can I still return?

In order to determine your academic standing and eligibility, your first step is to submit a [Returning Student Interest Form](https://engage.cpp.edu/register/welcome_back) which the Office of Admissions will use to determine your next steps.

Please allow 2-3 week after submitting for an update from an Admissions Counselor.

### I want to meet with a Returning Student Counselor; how do I schedule an Advising meeting?

Schedule a meeting with a Returning Student Admissions Counselor by [clicking here](https://engage.cpp.edu/portal/portal_returning_stu_advising)

If you have scheduled a meeting and need to reschedule, please email Returning@cpp.edu

### Can Graduate students submit a Returning Student Form?

No, the Returning student is strictly for undergraduate students. Graduate students will need to reach out to their Graduate Coordinator in the program they are pursuing.

### I completed 112+ units, can I return to earn my undergraduate degree.

Based on your overall competed units, you may be able to finish your remaining degree requirements and/or graduate without the need to reapply to the university. In order for our office to better determine this, please submit your [Returning Student Interest Form](https://engage.cpp.edu/portal/portal_returning_stu_advising) to begin the review process.

### Do I need to attend Orientation?

Yes, Orientation is required for ALL incoming students, regardless of returning status.

### When can I expect to hear back after I submit the Returning Student Interest Form?

After your submit your Returning Student Interest Form, you can expect an Admissions update within 2-3 weeks.
