# Training Bias and Stereotype

## Managing Implicit Bias and Stereotype Threat in the Fearless Classroom

This material introduces the concept of implicit bias, describes how it can manifest in both remote and face to face aspects of our classrooms, and discusses strategies to be more equitable despite our implicit biases.
