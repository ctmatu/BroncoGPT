# Alumni and Friends

Our alumni exemplify what is exceptional about our Bronco Engineers—people working together to assure future prosperity through technological advancement.

Here are several ways to stay connected with the college and support its everlasting endeavor of graduating world-class engineers who go on to solve the world's toughest challenges.

## Alumni Resources

![A group photo of two men and three women.](img/alumni-group.jpg)

### [Alumni Association](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/index.shtml)

Join thousands of alumni focused on engagement, networking and sharing the pride they have for their alma mater. Events include social mixers, community volunteer days, professional development, chapter events and many other ways to connect with the Bronco family.

![A blonde white woman in a grey blazer smiling at a students.](img/mentor-network.jpg)

### [Bronco Mentoring Network](../mentoring-network.shtml)

Shape tomorrow's engineers as an alumni mentor. Build connections, leadership skills and perspectives of future Bronco Engineers.

![A young Asian man presenting a a technical engineering PowerPoint slide. The text in the slide is illegible.](img/pss.jpg)

### [Project Symposium & Showcase](../activities/symposium.shtml)

The annual Project Symposium and Showcase presents the results of individual and team efforts to solve multidisciplinary, real-world engineering challenges. Projects require planning and most often use a research, prototype, test, and document strategy. Attend the event and provide crucial feedback to the presenting students.

![Nine individuals, seven men and two women, all with green blazers and posing as a group.](img/hof.jpg)

### [Hall of Fame](../giving/hall-of-fame/index.shtml)

The CPP College of Engineering Hall of Fame honors and preserves the outstanding accomplishments of our distinguished engineers.
