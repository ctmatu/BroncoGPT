# Types of Insurance

The links below are intended to provide basic information regarding the various insurance coverages that are either provided or available for purchase through the University. The information provided should not be relied on for policy interpretation.

For additional information regarding insurance requirements, please refer to California State University (CSU) [Executive Order No.1069](https://calstate.policystat.com/policy/6590643/latest/) and [Technical Letter RM 2012-01 CSU Insurance Requirements](https://www2.calstate.edu/csu-system/administration/business-finance/systemwide-risk-management/Documents/Insurance/RM2012-01.pdf) (PDF).

Any specific questions should be referred to Strategic Enterprise Risk Management, (909) 869-2495.

* [Foreign Travel Insurance](../forms/6-22-21-foreign-travel-iinsurance-requirements.pdf) (PDF)

* [Mexican Auto Liability Insurance](mexican-auto.shtml)
* [Special Event Insurance](special-event.shtml)
