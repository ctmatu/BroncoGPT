# Direct Deposit for Student Refunds

## What is Direct Deposit?

All students who expect to receive a refund from the university, whether from financial aid, tuition overpayment, or dropping or withdrawing from classes, should sign up for direct deposit. It’s the safest and fastest way to ensure you get your money without delays or the risk of lost checks.

 Don't wait for your refund to arrive by mail; enroll in direct deposit to get your funds faster. ## FAQs

Not sure how it works? Start with these common questions.

### How to Enroll in Direct Deposit

1. Log in to your BroncoDirect Student Center.
2. Go to the "Student Financials" section and select "Bank Accounts" or "Enroll in Direct Deposit".
3. Enter your U.S. bank routing and account number.
4. Select the account type (checking or savings).
5. Confirm your information and submit.
   More info at [Direct Deposit Instructions (PDF).](../forms-and-policies/ach-direct-deposit-instructions.pdf#Direct Deposit Instructions "Direct Deposit PDF Instructions")

### When to Expect Funds

Once your refund is processed by Student Accounting & Cashiering Services, it typically appears in your bank account within 2–3 business days. Processing time may vary based on bank policies.

### Updating or Cancelling Direct Deposit

To update your direct deposit information (such as if your bank account changes), simply log into [BroncoDirect](https://my.cpp.edu/ "BroncoDirect") and follow the enrollment steps again using your new account information. To cancel, remove your current bank account from your profile.

### What if I enter the wrong account number?

Contact your bank and Student Accounting immediately.

### Can I use an international bank?

No, only U.S. bank accounts are supported.

### What happens if my account is closed?

The refund will be returned to CPP and reissued by check.
