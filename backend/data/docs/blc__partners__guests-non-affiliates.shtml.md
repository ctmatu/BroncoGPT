# Guests (non-affiliates)

## What is a non-affiliate?

"**Non-Affiliate**" means any person who is *not* any of the following: a Student, Employee, officer, trustee, auxiliary employee, official University volunteer, or member of a household authorized to reside on University Property.

If you wish to sell something on campus, please refer to our [vendor process](vendors.shtml).

## Policies applying to non-affiliates

All non-affiliates must adhere and understand the policies posted on the [Free Speech @ CPP website.](https://www.cpp.edu/free-speech)

The [Free Speech @ CPP website](https://www.cpp.edu/free-speech) contains the CSU Systemwide Time, Place and Manner policy and the CPP Addendum.

## Requesting temporary structures

* An advanced reservation is required if you wish to have temporary structures.
  + Tables, chairs, temporary signs, canopies or "EZ-ups" or "Pop-up tents"
* The turnaround time to get the proper permits is 2 weeks (10 business days)
* Note: This is only a request, we will contact you if your date, and event layout are confirmed
  + We only schedule for the current academic term
    - Fall term (August-December)
    - Spring term (January-May)
    - Summer term (May-July)

## Information required for temporary structures

Please include in your email the below information. Email us at leadership@cpp.edu.

* Your name
* Your organization (if you are part of one)
* The requested date and times
* The outdoor location on-campus you wish to put up a temporary structure
  + Spots outside of our student center are available based on availability
  + If you wish to put temporary structure elsewhere there will be an additional fee
* The items that you are requesting to bring to campus
  + If you are requesting to bring temporary structures such as a table, chair, sign, table, or canopy/EZ-up, please include the dimensions (length, width, and height), of all items you wish to bring.
  + The Bronco Leadership Center will create a layout of your setup.
  + This will take 7-10 business days to get approval from all outdoor event approval departments.
    - Risk Management, Campus Facilities, Outdoor Space Approval, and others.
