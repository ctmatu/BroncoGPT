# Military Affiliated and Veteran Students

## Welcome to Cal Poly Pomona!

As part of your transition to campus, you will be participating in Orientation to prepare you for success at CPP. Orientation is a mandatory enrollment requirement for all new incoming students.

## **Veterans Resource Center Pre-Orientation Workshops**

Incoming military-affiliated or veteran students should plan on attending a virtual VRC Pre-Orientation Workshop hosted by the Veterans Resource Center, prior to their Orientation day. During this session, you will learn about the services offered by the Veterans Resource Center and receive documents which will be needed during orientation in order to begin utilizing VA Educational Benefits. These informational workshops are open to all incoming veteran and military dependent students and are held in addition to Orientation day.

**Please click [this link](https://cpp.zoom.us/meeting/register/g4AQpy1dRNWed5PnuuR-1w) to register for your Veterans Pre-Orientation workshop.**

For questions about the VRC workshop, or to schedule a time to meet outside of your workshop, please contact the Veterans Resource Center at (909) 869-6994 or vetquestions@cpp.edu.
