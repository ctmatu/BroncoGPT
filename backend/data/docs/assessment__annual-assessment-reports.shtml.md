# Annual Assessment Reports

## Snapshot 2024-2025

![annual assessment graphic ](img/snapshot_annual_assessment_graphic.png)Annual assessment reports allow academic programs to capture and describe the different ways programs engage in the various stages of a typical assessment cycle. These reports allow Cal Poly Pomona to better understand the state of academic program assessment with a snapshot of assessment activity across all academic programs.

Annual assessment reports are due mid-October with feedback provided to programs in early Spring. The feedback is aggregated from a combination of the College Assessment Liaison (CAL) from the program’s home college, and either a CAL from a different college or the Office of Assessment and Program Review. Feedback is intended to be formative to strengthen program-level assessment activity and capacity
