# Applicant Eligibility and Requirements

* Bachelor’s in a STEM discipline with a Science GPA of 3.0 or better.
* Admittance into the CPP MS program with full-time status.
* US citizen or student with permanent visa **seeking to pursue a PhD degree in the biomedical and related sciences**.
* Applicants should send the completed **application form**, their **personal statement and research statement,** a copy of their complete **transcript** (pdf format), along with **three** **letters of recommendation** electronically to the Program Administrator, Ms. Airan Alvarado Jansen (adalvarado@cpp.edu).
* One cohort of 4 students will be accepted per year. Check the application page for more information.
* Students on a pre-medical track (MD) are not eligible for this program, as indicated by NIH.
