# Scholarships

The IBM department encourages students to search for and apply for all scholarships they qualify for.

## Timeline

The CBA scholarship application process usually occurs in February for the next academic year, however timing may change and we encourage you to check he scholarship links below for the most up-to-date information.

**CSU Systemwide Scholarships:** Mid-January – Mid-April**General Scholarships:** October 1 – Mid-April
 **CPP Scholarships:** October 1 – March 2
**CBA Scholarships:** February 1 – March 1

## Scholarship links

* [College of Business Administration Scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/scholarships.shtml)
* [Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) Bronco Scholarship Portal](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/)
