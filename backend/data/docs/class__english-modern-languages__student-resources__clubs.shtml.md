# Student Clubs

## Get involved

Student clubs and activities are an important way of connecting, building community, and having fun. CPP uses [myBAR](https://mybar.cpp.edu/) for official student clubs—search there to find a club or activity to match almost every interest!

English & Modern Languages is home to three official student clubs and one informal gaming group.

### Sigma Tau Delta​

Sigma is both an international honor society and CPP's club for anyone who loves reading, writing, and language. Activities include open mic nights, short story and poetry contests, nerdy word games, writing workshops, learning from English & Modern Languages faculty, and more.

Learn more about the club on the [Sigma website](https://sites.google.com/view/cppsigmatau/), on [Sigma's myBAR page](https://mybar.cpp.edu/organization/sigmataudelta), and [@sigmataucpp on Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/sigmataucpp/).

### Spanish Language Association​

SLA celebrates and promotes the use of Spanish on the Cal Poly Pomona campus and in the community through cultural, social and community-service activities, scholarships, participation in campus festivals, tutoring, and other activities. The Association encourages Spanish-speaking and non-Spanish- speaking students alike to acknowledge the strength of the Spanish language as an ideal medium of comradeship, culture, inquiry, politics, and all other branches of human activity. The association promotes diversity on campus, encouraging its Latino members to embrace their linguistic heritage.

Find [SLA on myBAR](https://mybar.cpp.edu/organization/spanishlanguage) and [@cpp\_sla on Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/cpp_sla/).

### TESOL Club​

Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages Club (TESOL) Club introduces students to the rich potential of teaching English to non-native speakers locally and internationally. This club also gives students study strategies to help them understand, engage, and excel in their TESOL undergraduate and graduate courses. Members share and exchange ideas with students to put together compelling conference proposals, presentations, and teach techniques to deliver a clear and concise presentation.

Find [TESOL Club on myBAR](https://mybar.cpp.edu/organization/tesolclub) and [@cpptesolclub on Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/cpptesolclub/).

### Split Party Infinitives

If you've ever wished you could roll for a skill check in real life, love fantasy and live storytelling, or wondered what the deal is with Dungeons & Dragons, Split Party Infinitives is here for you! Dr. Aaron DeRosa leads this motley band of roving adventurers on quests for glory throughout the academic year. Email Dr. DeRosa for SPI information.
