# BroncoBytes

## What is BroncoBytes?

BroncoBytes is a biweekly newsletter from the Bronco Advising Center that informs Cal Poly Pomona students about important university-wide dates, deadlines and announcements.

## Submitting to BroncoBytes

Campus partners are invited to submit items for consideration in upcoming issues by using the form below. You must submit your announcement or event by Monday at noon of the week of publication.

The Bronco Advising Center will edit and compile all submissions for BroncoBytes biweekly distribution. The BroncoBytes team reserves the right to consider submissions for publications, and may edit submission style and content. For more information or questions, email broncobytes@cpp.edu.
