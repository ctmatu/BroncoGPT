# Native Grad

## Learn More

Native American Graduation Celebration celebrates students' academic accomplishments in the spirit and traditions of our Native community. The ceremony is open to all graduating students and provides them the opportunity to publicly thank those that have supported them along the way. We also have a keynote speaker and provide lunch for graduates' families as we celebrate in community.
