# Access Services

---

![](img/access-services-banner.jpg)

The primary function of Access Services is to facilitate physical access of library material for student learning through its four units: **Circulation**,**Course Reserves**, **Interlibrary Loan** (CSU+ and ILLiad), **Stack Management**. The primary functions of the Circulation Desk are: checking out and returning material, managing patron records, collecting fees and fines, paging materials from the Course Reserves collection or compact shelving. Stack Management ensures a quick turnaround time for shelving library materials to ensure the availability of library materials. Access Services also oversees areas crucial to student learning such as quiet study areas, study rooms, assistance with locating micro-form materials, and material from compact shelving.
