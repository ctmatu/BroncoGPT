# FYE PolyX Showcase

## Your First Step in Cal Poly Pomona’s Learn-by-Doing Journey

First-year students participate in a Signature Polytechnic Experience (PolyX) within their FYE courses and present their work at the PolyX Showcase. This early immersion in Cal Poly Pomona's become-by-doing approach and polytechnic advantage prepares students for success from their first semester.

## Spring FYE PolyX Showcase and Celebration

![](../img/fye-showcase-2.png)

Join us **April 30,2026 at the** **UHS Suites Multipurpose Room from 11:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.**  as first-year students present their PolyX projects. This event showcases the power of Cal Poly Pomona’s hands-on learning, giving students an early start on real-world experiences.

[Register on MyBAR](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12191520)
