# Requirements

## Graduation Requirements

**Graduation** refers to the term in which you have officially and successfully completed all of your graduation requirements. You must apply for the term in which you plan to graduate through your Student Center in [BroncoDirect](http://www.cpp.edu/~broncodirect/).  Once grades are posted, your record will be evaluated and if all requirements are met, your degree will be awarded.

Undergraduate and Graduate students expecting to graduate Fall 2025, Spring or Summer 2026 need to apply online via BroncoDirect for graduation **by March 13, 2026, to participate in ceremonies, and have name displayed in the Class of 2026 ceremony program**.

* [**Complete details for Graduation**](http://www.cpp.edu/~registrar/graduation/index.shtml)
* [**Applying for Graduation & Deadlines**](https://www.cpp.edu/~registrar//graduation/applying-for-graduation.shtml)

Please note diploma covers are provided at commencement, and diplomas are mailed separately once degree is processed with final official grades.
