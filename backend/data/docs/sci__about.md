# About

## Our Vision

**Fostering curiosity and a culture of scientific discovery**

## Our Mission

**Educate, mentor, and inspire students through scientific inquiry and hands-on learning**

## Core Values

**Curiosity, Integrity, Collaboration, Inclusivity, Innovation**

The College of Science (CoS) at Cal Poly Pomona is committed to providing a first-rate science education with an emphasis on creativity, discovery, and innovation. With the university’s "learn-by-doing" philosophy, our programs advance science and foster real-world education. Our professionally active faculty members prepare students to become qualified science professionals.

Preparing students for the global work force, the College of Science has seven departments that lead to the Bachelor of Science degree:

* [Biological Sciences](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/biological-sciences/)
* [Chemistry and Biochemistry](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/chemistry-biochemistry/)
* [Computer Science](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/computer-science/)
* [Geological Sciences](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/geological-sciences/)
* [Kinesiology and Health Promotion](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/kinesiology-health-promotion/)
* [Mathematics and Statistics](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/mathematics-statistics/)
* [Physics and Astronomy](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/physics-astronomy/)

Graduate programs leading to the Master of Science degree are offered in:

* Biological Sciences
* Chemistry
* Computer Science
* Geology
* Kinesiology
* Mathematics

All programs are fully aligned with the university’s polytechnic, "learn-by-doing" philosophy, organization, and mission. [Visit the university's website to learn more about the polytechnic advantage](https://www.cpp.edu/~polyadvantage/index.shtml).
