## Calendar at a Glance

This spring we have **11****EOP Events** planned, both in-person and via Zoom, we hope to see you at one of our many Community Building, Academic/Professional Development, and/or Financial Literacy related success seminars.

**You may access the program details by viewing our [Calendar at a Glance](eop-events-calendar-at-a-glance_spring-2026.pdf) or accessing the [EOP MyBAR](https://mybar.cpp.edu/organization/ssep-eop) website****.**We hope to see you at our virtual and in-person events!

For more information about our EOP events or if EOP Event Accommodations are needed, please contact the EOP Events staff at eopevents@cpp.edu.
