**Location:** Kellogg University Art Gallery

![College of Environmental Design, Art Department, Senior Show, Exhhibition Dates: May 18 - June 12, 2010, Reception: Saturday, May 22, 2010- 6 - 9 pm, W. Keith and Janel Kellogg University Art Gallery California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, 3801 West Temple Ave, Pomona, Ca 91768, (909) 869 4302](img/28563_384063303980_2376086_n.png)**The Senior Show**  showcases work by graduating fine art seniors. The students featured in this year's exhibition are: Lovelyn Anastacio, Courtney Blackburn, Alex Brown, Luis Canales, Carly Clarke, Rachel Ellett, Nahomi Favela, Randall Garrett, Romina E. Gonzalez, Emil (Troy) Ingram, Karen Jaime, Jean Leonard, Robin Martin, Pedro Martinez, Jessica Ortega, Molly Semexan, Janice Tria, Clarisa Valdez, and Micheal Veloria. A catalog of the  graduating students' work will be available free of charge, for those who attend the opening reception. **2D/3D**  is a juried exhibition featuring two and three-dimensional work by both fine art and graphic design majors.

The jurors for this year's **2D/3D**  are **Keith Crockett**, MFA for fine art submissions and **Albert Inyoung Choi**, PhD for Graphic Design.
