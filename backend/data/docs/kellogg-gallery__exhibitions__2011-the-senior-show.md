## 2011 Senior Show

May 7, 2011 to Jun 11, 2011

**Location:** Kellogg University Art Gallery

![Patrick Mao. Oil on wood, 18 x 24 inches, series of 4](img/320976_10150262291408981_7855124_n.jpg)The **Senior Show**showcases work by graduating fine art seniors. The students featured in this year's exhibition are: Luis Betancourt, Robert Braden, Shelley Bruce, Katie Chan, Eugene Estrada, Victoria Garcia, Randall Garrett, Jessica Holcomb, Patrick Mao, Chitman Rungseriruch, Ileana Tejada, Amy Wrigtstone, Xi Yao, and Eduardo Zermeno.

**2D/3D**is a juried exhibition featuring two and three dimensional work by both fine art and graphic design majors. The jurors for this year's **2D/3D**are Kat Fon and Bob Pece.
