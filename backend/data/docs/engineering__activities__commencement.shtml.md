# Commencement

Cal Poly Pomona’s commencement ceremonies will return to campus to celebrate the Class of 2023!

For more info, visit the university's [commencement page](https://www.cpp.edu/commencement/index.shtml). For questions, contact commencement@cpp.edu.

## Class of 2023 Engineering Commencement Ceremonies

![A black, male Cal Poly Pomona engineerng graduate smiling at the camera.](img/thumb-grad.jpg)

### [Mon., May 22, 2023](https://www.cpp.edu/commencement/index.shtml)

Two College of Engineering ceremonies will be held to allow for shorter ceremonies and meet safety guidelines. The ceremonies will be divided by major.

Location: University Quad

Commencement #1 at 3:30 p.m.

* Aerospace Engineering
* Chemical & Materials Engineering
* Industrial & Manufacturing Engineering
* Mechanical Engineering

Commencement #2 at 7:00 p.m.

* Civil Engineering
* Electrical & Computer Engineering
* Electro-Mechanical Engineering Technology
