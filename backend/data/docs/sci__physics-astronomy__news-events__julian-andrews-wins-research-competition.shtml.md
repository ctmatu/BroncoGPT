# Julian Andrews, a Physics and Astronomy Senior, wins the CSU Research Competition

![Julian Andrews ](../img/news/julian-andrews.jpg)

Two Cal Poly Pomona students earned first-place honors at the 30th Annual CSU Systemwide Student Research Competition, and one of them was one of our own, Physics and Astronomy Senior Julian Andrews. Mentored by [Prof. Matthew Povich](http://www.cpp.edu/~mspovich/ "Matthew Povich website"), Julian's presentation was titled "Discovering Massive Runaway Stars with Infrared Bow Shock Nebulae: First Results.”  [Read more on Polycentric](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2016/05/two-students-earn-first-place-at-csu-research-competition/#.Vzz4IFc0p2A "Polycentric News CSU research competition").
