# Furniture Moves

To request a furniture move, follow these steps:

1. Go to the [Service Request](https://www.cpp.edu/fpm/docs/forms/service_request_revised-april-2019.pdf) form.
2. Print the Service Request form.
3. Complete the form. Please ensure that an approving or authorized signature and funding source account number has been listed.
4. Fax the request form to (909) 869-4363.

Once the form is processed, you will be contacted to schedule a moving day and time. If you have any further questions, please contact Facilities Customer Service at Ext. 3030. We ask that you schedule two to three weeks in advance of your anticipated move date. Please contact the I&IT Help Desk at Ext. 6776 for phone and computer moves.
