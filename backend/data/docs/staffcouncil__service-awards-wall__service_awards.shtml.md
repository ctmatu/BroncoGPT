# CPP Years of Service Recognition

## CPP Years of Service Recognition (formerly Service Awards effective 2023)

Note: CPP Years of Service Recognition does not account for service at other CSUs or reflects CalPERS service credit. CPP Years of Service Recognition accounts for years of service at Cal Poly Pomona, ASI, and Enterprise (formerly Foundation).

For the most updated information, please refer to [EODA - Service Recognition](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/csc/service-awards.shtml)

[View the 2025 Service Awards Wall](index.shtml)

## Service Award Pin

Current recipients can stop by the Customer Service Center (CSC) at any time to pick up their pins Monday–Friday, 8 am – 5 pm. For questions regarding Service Pins, please contact the CSC.
