# Change of Major Guide

## Step 3: Meet with an Advisor

Now that you know what the requirements are for changing your [major](../a-z.shtml#major), you’ll want to visit [your academic advisor](../../directories/advising-directory.shtml) to talk about your degree planning. Here are some things to keep in mind:

* **Bring a list of questions with you.** You may want to ask what courses you’re allowed to take without being in the major, or how the classes you took before might count for your new major.
* **Know how close you are to registering for a new term.** If there are any courses you can start taking in the degree, you might want to register for them. You can check the [Academic Calendar](../../calendar/index.shtml) or [Registration Guide](../registration-page.shtml) for more information about upcoming registration periods.
