# Student Organizations

## AIGA Cal Poly Pomona

**AIGA** advances design as a professional craft, strategic advantage, and vital cultural force. AIGA Cal Poly Pomona is part of the largest community of design advocates in the country. We bring together practitioners, enthusiasts and patrons to amplify the voice of design and create the vision for a collective future.

AIGA is the place design professionals turn to first to exchange ideas and information, participate in critical analysis and research and advance education and ethical practice. The Cal Poly Pomona student group is affiliated with AIGA Los Angeles, and AIGA National. ( [http://losangeles.aiga.org/](https://losangeles.aiga.org/ "losangeles.aiga.org.  Click or tap if you trust this link.  (opens in new window)")) ( [https://www.aiga.org/](https://www.aiga.org/ "www.aiga.org. Click or tap if you trust this link.  (opens in new window)"))

The Cal Poly Pomona chapter of the AIGA takes advantage of it’s strategic proximity to Los Angeles and hosts numerous events including: guest speakers, the 24 hour Design for Good Marathon, workshops, travel to Austin Texas’s SXSW conference, studio tours, and more.
