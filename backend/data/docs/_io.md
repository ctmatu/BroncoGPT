# SIIL Innovation Orchard

**This website is no longer maintained**
To be redirected to our current website, click [here](https://www.cpp.edu/siil/our-spaces/innovation-orchard.shtml)

![](img/20180228_103425.jpg)

The Innovation Orchard provides an open-space environment for individuals and startup companies to ideate, create, collaborate, and more! Visitors have access to amenities that can assist them in building their projects, including a conference room, general office space, and equipment. Overall, the IO strives to maintain a supportive environment for startups and remain a creative outlet for those without access to creative equipment. Join us and see where your imagination will take you!

At the Innovation Orchard, visitors can utilize an array of resources, including a laser cutter, 3D printers, and many other tools, to help actualize ideas and products. Not only that, but the IO team also offers free workshops and hands-on training for the machinery, so sign up today!
