# Support the Department

You can support the Department of Kinesiology and Health Promotion by giving today. We have a variety of opportunities available, each of which can be tailored to meet your personal or professional needs. Please consider one of the following options:

* Make a one-time gift to support specific Department initiatives
* Become a corporate or business partner
