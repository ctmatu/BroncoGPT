Cal Poly Pomona's Chemistry & Biochemistry Department offers [three B.S. degree options](undergraduate-students/degree-options.shtml) and an [M.Sc. program](graduate-students/requirements.shtml) to prepare students for careers in chemistry. The faculty embrace Cal Poly Pomona's inclusive polytechnic identity and embody the teacher-scholar model by providing students with experiential learning and discovery opportunities through extensive laboratory study.
