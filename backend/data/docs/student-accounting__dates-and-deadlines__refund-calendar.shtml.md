# Refund Calendar

## Refund Calendar

### Winter 2026

* 100% Refund: Drop all classes before the semester begins
* Prorated Refund: Drop all classes after the start but before the prorated deadline
* No Refund: After the prorated refund deadline
* Key Refund Dates
* Winter 2026 4 Weeks Session– 100%: December 21 | Prorated: January 5, 2026
* Winter 2026  2 Weeks Session– 100%: January 1, 2026 | Prorated: January 8, 2026
* Refunds are processed within 3–4 weeks and returned to the original payer.

### Spring 2026

* 100% Refund: Drop all classes before the semester begins
* Prorated Refund: Drop all classes after the start but before the prorated deadline
* No Refund: After the prorated refund deadline
* Key Refund Dates
* Spring 2026 – 100%: January 16 | Prorated: March 25, 2026
* Refunds are processed within 3–4 weeks and returned to the original payer.
