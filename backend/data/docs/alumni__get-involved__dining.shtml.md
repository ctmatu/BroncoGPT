# Alumni & External Relations

## Dining with Broncos

**February 21-February 28**

Dining with Broncos is a fun, networking event allowing alumni and students to connect over a meal. Individual alumni, chapters or groups can host students in their homes, at a restaurant or on campus. Dinners are paid for by hosts and students attend the meal for free. Students are able to sign up to attend any dinner based on major, industry, employer, proximity, or availability.  Alumni can designate how many students they would like to host, what food will be provided, the time of the dinner and if they choose to have any theme for the night! Students are required to attend a brief orientation prior to attending the dinner. Host signups for 2026 will begin in October 2025. Student sign ups will begin in early January 2026. If you have any questions or need additional information, please contact alumni@cpp.edu.

## Student Registration

Student sign ups will begin in early January 2026. If you have any questions or need additional information, please contact alumni@cpp.edu.
