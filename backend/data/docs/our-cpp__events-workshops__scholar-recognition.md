## OUR Presents 2024 Scholar Recognition

The Scholar Recognition Ceremony is an annual event hosted by the [Office of Undergraduate Research (OUR)](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/index.shtml)[.](https://www.cpp.edu/learndiscovery/) Join us as we celebrate student and faculty engaged in research & creative activities at Cal Poly Pomona!
