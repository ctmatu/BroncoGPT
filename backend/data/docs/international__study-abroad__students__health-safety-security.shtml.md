# Health, Safety, and Security

### The Office of Study Abroad, along with the [Office of Strategic Enterprise Risk Management](https://www.cpp.edu/rms/index.shtml) (SERM), supports the health, safety, and security of all CPP-sponsored study abroad travelers.

 In partnership with our office, SERM prepares students and faculty going abroad with health insurance, pre-departure orientations, emergency and crisis management protocols, travel guidelines, and the best health, safety and security practices.
