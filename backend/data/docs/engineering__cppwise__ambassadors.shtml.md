# Would you like to be a part of WiSE Community?

Current students can join the CPP WiSE Affiliate Group by completing the **[CPP WiSE Affiliates Group Form](https://forms.gle/GVffEUTxqs7QSTZ96)**.

![CPP WiSE Group Photo](img/cpp-we-group-photo.jpg)

## Calling All STEM Majors!

**ALL STEM students are invited to apply to be a CPP WiSE Affiliate Member.**

The Maximizing Engineering Potential (MEP) and Women in Science and Engineering (WiSE) Affiliates Program provides students with connection to a variety of engineering and science industry connections (via our U-Hour workshops—offered Tuesdays/Thursdays 12:00pm–12:50pm) and academic enrichment support (via Tutoring and Supplemental Instruction). Some of our events also focus on post-baccalaureate preparation and others, community building. There is no commitment; and you can participate at your own pace.

There are no fees associated with being part of the program.
