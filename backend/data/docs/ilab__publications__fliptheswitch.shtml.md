# Flip the Switch Podcast

Flip the switch is designed to open the opportunity to hear out students talk about their experiences starting a business while attending college. Flip the Switch empowers students from flipping the switch from a student to an entrepreneur, innovator, inventor. This podcast is proudly fueled by the Student Innovation Idea Lab.
