## Welcome to the College of Professional and Global Education

**![CPGE Students in a classroom](../img/about-cpge.jpg)**

**At the College of Professional and Global Education (CPGE),** we open doors for learners at every stage of life. Whether you're returning to finish a degree, upskilling for a promotion, or preparing to enter a new career field, our programs are designed to move you forward.

We specialize in flexible, career-focused education, offering professional certificates, online degrees, Open University enrollment, and global learning opportunities. Courses are taught by faculty and industry professionals who understand today’s workforce and tomorrow’s challenges.

What sets CPGE apart? We meet you where you are and help you become who you're meant to be through practical, high-impact learning and a supportive culture at Cal Poly Pomona.

**Welcome to your next chapter.**

## Our Approach

### Who We Serve

CPGE supports a diverse range of learners, including working professionals, career changers, international students, and those returning to complete their degrees.

### Campus Collaboration

CPGE partners with Cal Poly Pomona’s academic colleges to deliver innovative graduate programs. We support operations, enrollment management, and marketing strategy.

### Global Perspective

From multinational professionals to students exploring Study Abroad, CPGE fosters cross-cultural learning and global collaboration.
