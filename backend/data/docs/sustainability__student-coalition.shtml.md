# Student Sustainability Coalition

![](img/tabling2023.jpg)

## Collaborating for Sustainability Elevating Student Voices

The Student Sustainability Coalition collaborates with the Office of Sustainability and other sustainability-related clubs/organizations in assisting and creating programming that provides unity among the affiliated student organizations in order to approach sustainability at Cal Poly Pomona with a holistic perspective. The Student Sustainability Coalition serves as a multidisciplinary hub for clubs and organizations that address sustainability, so that general body members become aware of all the different opportunities to get involved with sustainability on campus.
