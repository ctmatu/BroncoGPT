# Printers

## Recommended Printers

Printing, scanning, and copying requirements vary widely amongst the campus community.  Please [contact IT&IP](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp) for any special requests or printing consultation.

**IT&IP recommends the use of large enterprise multi-functional copiers (MFC)** to centralize departmental printing needs, reduce operational costs, and standard support, training, and repair activities.  Please [contact IT&IP](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp) for MFC device recommendations and leasing agreement reviews.  In general, Xerox and Ricoh models are recommended brands for large-volume multi-feature printing devices.

For smaller office printing needs, please refer to the following list of recommended printers.  The printing devices below should be available from various campus resellers, including the [CSUBUY portal](https://ds.calstate.edu/?svc=csubuy), the [Bronco Bookstore](https://www.broncobookstore.com/), and other vendors.

**IMPORTANT: Printer Support Information**

* Printer devices that differ from the recommended models listed above or are purchased without the explicit approval of IT&IP will not be supported by the Client Services department.  In such cases, IT&IP may only provide a "best effort" approach in supporting non-standard printing equipment.  To ensure your printer continues to operate within the campus client standard environment, please consult with IT&IP before purchasing a new printer.
* Ink-jet or non-laser printers are non-standard devices and therefore, not supported by IT&IP.  Such devices are not typically designed for enterprise environments as well.  Therefore, ink-jet and non-laser printers should only be connected to computers via USB cables.  Networking cables and campus network ports are not to be used by such non-standard printing devices.
