# CHARLES FREDRICK RETROSPECTIVE

## Charles Fredrick Retrospective

**Date:** November 07, 2013 to December 21, 2013
**Time:** 3:00pm to 12:00am
**Location:** Kellogg University Art Gallery

![Artwork by Charles Fredrick](charles_fredrick_retrospective.jpg)This exhibition ***Charles Fredrick: A Retrospective***comprises over 100 works in the media of drawing, painting, and sculpture, as well as an artist's journal. The collection spans four decades of work, from 1974 until 2013, in an expansive and comprehensive retrospective of this distinguished faculty member who is retiring after 35 years at Cal Poly Pomona.
