## Cal Poly Pomona Instructors & Teaching Associates

BioTrek supports a number of campus courses in a range of departments. BioTrek welcomes instructors to utilize the areas of BioTrek to enhance student learning, but instructors should complete a   [Use of Facility Reservation Form](/biotrek/planning-your-visit/facilities-reservation.shtml) request to ensure minimal time conflicts for your self-guided visit. The training material below is available for your use in tailoring your self-guided visit to suit your course needs. Also see:   [Cal Poly Pomona Class Visits](/biotrek/planning-your-visit/cpp-class-visits.shtml)

## BioTrek Service-Learning Docents

Our K-12 program would not be possible without our service-learning docents! Working with the  [Center for Community Engagement](http://www.cpp.edu/~cce/)  at Cal Poly Pomona, BioTrek is helping to advance a culture of meaningful engagement by promoting service-learning, volunteering and cooperative education. Undergraduate biology students make a commitment to learn-by-doing by applying their biological knowledge in service to the community. Our students are trained to use interpretive techniques to facilitate connections between visitors and the natural environment. The training material below is available for BIO488S Interpretation of Science docents in preparation of their service-learning commitment.

Note to docents: These materials are available for you to review at any time. Remember that slight differences in content may be observed for different age groups we serve, and you should always consult the   [age-appropriate content](/biotrek/educational-resources/k-12-resources.shtml).
