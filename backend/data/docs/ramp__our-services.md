# Our Services

## Services List

RAMP offers a variety of services to participating students at Cal Poly Pomona. From computer use to on- and off-campus events, RAMP aims to promote more than retention and academic success. Personal growth, professional development, and financial literacy, to name a few, are qualities RAMP aspires to cultivate in participants.

The following services are available to RAMP participants:

* [Reading Tutorials](tutorials.shtml)
* [CPU 1050A Course](cpu1050-course.shtml)  (Reading & Study Skills Development)
* [Supplemental Academic & Financial Aid Advising](advising.shtml)
* [Peer Mentoring](peer-mentoring.shtml)
* Academic Success Workshops
* Financial Aid & Literacy Workshops
* [Book Club](book-club.shtml)
* First-Generation College Student Meetings and Panels
* Finals Week Study Space
* [Graduate School Test Preparation](exam-preparations.shtml)
* [Grant Aid](grant-aid.shtml) (freshman and sophomores receiving a Pell grant are eligible to apply)
* [On-campus Theatre, Art, & Music Events](https://www.cpp.edu/class/theatre-new-dance/production-season/index.shtml)
* Off-campus Cultural Events
* [Professional Spotlight](professional-spotlight.shtml)
