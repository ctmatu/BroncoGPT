## Master's Degrees

The College of Professional and Global Education offers Master's Degrees that are designed for working professionals.

[View Master's Degrees](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/degree-programs/index.shtml%20 "View Master's Degrees. ")

## Open University

Through Open University, people not currently admitted to Cal Poly Pomona may enroll in undergraduate and graduate-level classes. Registration is provided on a space-available basis.

[Apply for Open University](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/open-university/index.shtml%20 "Apply for Open University. ")

![](img/international-students.jpg)

Global Campus at the College of Professional and Global Education offers programs for international students, scholars, faculty exchange, and study abroad.
