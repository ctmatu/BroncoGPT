August 1, 2025

![detail shot of "Geodynamics" by Andy Moses that will be featured in the exhibit.](../exhibitions/2025-color-and-quirk/img/moses-art/crop-geodynamics-701.jpg)

## "Color & Quirk" | Closing Campus Reception Nov 18, 2025 (4PM - 6PM)

Don’t miss the Closing Campus Reception for *Color & Quirk* at the Kellogg University Art Gallery, happening on Tuesday, November 18, 2025, from 4 to 6 p.m.

Join us as we celebrate the final week of this bold and visually striking exhibition with the campus community. Enjoy one last opportunity to experience the vibrant works on view, connect with fellow attendees, and reflect on the unique perspectives showcased throughout the show.

[CLICK HERE](../exhibitions/2025-color-and-quirk/index.shtml) for more information on ***Color & Quirk***.
