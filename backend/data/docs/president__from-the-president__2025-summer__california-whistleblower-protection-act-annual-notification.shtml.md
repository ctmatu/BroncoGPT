# California Whistleblower Protection Act Annual Notification

## June 25, 2025

Dear Campus Community,

The stewardship and care of the university’s finances and people are paramount to our ability to enrich lives and propel graduates into meaningful and fulfilling life-long careers. Students, families and the residents of California depend on our careful consideration and use of the funds and resources we steward. To continually ensure our campus is doing its part to safeguard the university and university property, we routinely provide you with information about reporting improper activities of state agencies or employees involving any fraud, waste or abuse.

The annual California State Auditor’s Whistleblower Notice includes information on what to report, where to report, how to report, a statement on protection from retaliation for whistleblowing and a statement from the California State Auditor’s Office regarding the activities of that agency.

For your convenience, the notice and the poster from the California State Auditor, along with Cal Poly Pomona’s Whistleblower reporting procedures, are available through the following links:

[California State Auditor’s Whistleblower Notice](https://www.auditor.ca.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/whistleblower_brochure.pdf "https://www.auditor.ca.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/whistleblower_brochure.pdf")
[California State Auditor’s Whistleblower Poster](https://www.auditor.ca.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/whistleblower_letter.pdf "https://www.auditor.ca.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/whistleblower_letter.pdf")[CPP Whistleblower website](https://www.cpp.edu/president/compliance/whistleblower-and-retaliation-complaints.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/president/compliance/whistleblower-and-retaliation-complaints.shtml")

Campus Contact:

Cal Poly Pomona’s designee to receive reports of allegations of suspected improper governmental activities is Jon Saclolo, Director of Strategy Management. If you have any questions regarding the California Whistleblower Protection Act or related campus procedures, please contact Jon or any of the offices listed on the Whistleblower Notice.
