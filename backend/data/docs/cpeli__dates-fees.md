Contact Program Staff - For payments to be made by personal check, or cash

To purchase the insurance plans, please click [HERE](https://www.cpp.edu/cpeli/current-students/index.shtml "CPELI Insurance puchase").

## Refund Policy

* Full tuition refund given only before the first day of class.
* 50% refund given if withdrawal occurs within the first 5 days of class.
* No refund is given after the first 5 days of class.
* Refund paid only to the person who paid the tuition.
* Application fee is nonrefundable.
