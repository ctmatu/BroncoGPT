## About Me

Dr. Travis J. Miller is a Personality Psychologist whose research focuses on goal setting and sport psychology. Dr. Miller received his B.A. in Psychology and M.A. in General Experimental Psychology from CSU, Northridge. He then earned his Ph.D. in Social and Personality Psychology from UC Riverside. Before coming to CPP Dr. Miller was a Visiting Assistant Professor at Tulane University and an Assistant Professor at Menlo College.
