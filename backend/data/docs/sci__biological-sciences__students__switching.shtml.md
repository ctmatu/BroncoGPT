# Should I switch to semester curriculum?

#### **Who will be automatically switched to semester curriculum?**

* Automatic switch will apply ONLY to **2017 Freshman** in these majors:
  + Biotechnology
  + Environmental Biology
* All other majors and years will have the option to switch. Please follow the advice below to make your decision.

#### **Should I stay on quarters or switch to semesters?**

Stay on quarter curriculum IF:

* You plan on graduating by Spring 2019, Fall 2019, Spring 2020, or Fall 2020.
* You have completed BIO 211/L
* You have taken core courses towards your Option
* You have approved petitions
* You want to keep the Option in your diploma

Switching to semesters IF:

* You plan on graduating after 2021
* You have not started courses beyond the BIO series
* You do not have approved petitions

**Warning!**IF you switch,

* You will have to re-do ALL your petitions
* DPR will be ‘red’ & may take months to fix
