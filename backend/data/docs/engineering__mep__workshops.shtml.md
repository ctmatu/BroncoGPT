# MEP Workshops

![MEP workshop](/engineering/mep/img/workshops.jpg)

MEP offers twice weekly one-hour workshops on a variety of soft skills learning and engineering industry presentations/sessions. Students can register to participate. Lunch and/or refreshment will be served during the MEP workshops.

## Schedule of MEP Workshops

See the [Calendar of Events](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/mep/calendar.shtml) for an updated list of workshops this semester.
