## REACH Journal

The Office of Undergraduate Research and Bronco Scholar at the University Library are proud to present Reach – Inspiring Creativity, Discovery, and Innovation, an online compilation of undergraduate research at Cal Poly Pomona. Reach showcases outstanding scholarship, creative works, and research conducted by undergraduate students under the mentorship of faculty members.

[View Reach Journals](reach/index.shtml)

## Scholar Recognition 2021

Scholar Recognition is presented by the Office of Undergraduate Research (OUR) and the Learn Through Discovery (LTD) Initiative to recognize research & creative activities at Cal Poly Pomona. This digital magazine highlights students in the LTD Projects Hatchery and those receiving the Research Distinction in 2021. The magazine also celebrates faculty mentors who have gone above and beyond in mentoring students.

[View 2021 Magazine](../events-workshops/scholar-recognition/magazine.shtml)
