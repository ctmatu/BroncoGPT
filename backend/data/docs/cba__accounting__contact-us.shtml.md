# Contact Us

The Accounting Department is open during normal business hours Monday through Friday from 8 am – 5 pm.   We recommend scheduling an appointment with the Department for in-person meeting since we have limited in-person office hours.  We are continuing to offer virtual/remote services as well.  To schedule an appointment, please email Accounting Department Administrative Support Coordinator, Ms. Ellie Foo at afoo@cpp.edu.

Additional health and safety information can be found on [Safer Return to Campus](https://www.cpp.edu/safer-return/index.shtml).

## Permission Numbers:

**For permission numbers**, please email your request to  **cba\_accounting@cpp.edu**  **.**

Remember to include your **Name, BroncoNumber, Course Code, Section#, Class#, and proof of the completed prerequisite** such as below:

* Copy/Screenshot of unofficial transcript (your name and transcript date must be visible)
* Approved Petition
* Request from advisor, who has verified prerequisite

Your email will be responded within **one to two business days**.

Please note, the department does not generally allow prerequisites to be taken as co-requisites.  Exceptions must be approved by the department chair.
