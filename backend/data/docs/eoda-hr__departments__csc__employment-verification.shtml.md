## Employment Verifications

When an employee applies for a loan, home mortgage, new job, and/or rental qualification, any requests for verification of your employment and income can be done by contacting The Work Number® from Equifax. Please note that employees may sign up for the State Controller's Office Cal Employee Connect website to view and download their paycheck earnings statements and W-2 forms.

## Contact The Work Number® from Equifax

Cal Poly Pomona has partnered with The Work Number® from Equifax, which is a simple, quick, and more private way to help get your employment and income information to companies when needed. It's available 24/7 so that you can get the decisions you need when you need them.

From now on when asked for proof of employment or income, just send them to:

* [www.theworknumber.com](http://www.theworknumber.com "Opens in a new window")
* 800-367-5690 M-F 8:00 am to 8:00 pm (ET)
* member@equifax.com

To learn more about The Work Number® from Equifax and how to access it, click [Work Number Instructions](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/csc/work-number.shtml "Opens in a new window").

Please Note: This service is available to all state-side employees and employees who have multiple positions.

## Public Service Loan Forgiveness Program

### Public Service Loan Forgiveness Program Description

The Public Service Loan Forgiveness (PSLF) Program is a federal program created for those in public service jobs, offering the opportunity to have their federal loan balances forgiven after 120 qualifying monthly payments.

### How To Qualify for the Public Service Loan Forgiveness (PSLF) Program

Use the [PSLF Help Tool](https://studentaid.gov/pslf/ "Opens in a new window") to confirm you are eligible, fill out, and submit your PSLF form.

### Verify Employment for the Public Service Loan Forgiveness (PSLF) Program

The Human Resources Customer Service Center will complete Sections 3 and 4 of the PSLF document to verify that employment is with a public service organization. [Employment Certification forms](https://studentaid.gov/manage-loans/forgiveness-cancellation/public-service/public-service-loan-forgiveness-application "Opens in a new window") may be submitted:

* In-person to our office in [Student Services Building 121-West-2700](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130 "Opens in a new window").
* Through our [HR Customer Service Request Form](https://cpp.service-now.com/employee_services?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=faea65dd1b3ef0908e3ca602b24bcbcc&sysparm_category=e516b490dbe9b090f0eed2e3ca9619bf%20 "Opens in a new window"). Please be sure to not include any sensitive information (Social Security number) in the form.

It is the responsibility of the PSLF applicant to submit the application, and all required paperwork, to the U.S. Department of Education in order to qualify for the PSLF Program. The U.S. Department of Education, not the CSU, reviews the applications and makes the determination of eligibility for the PSLF Program.
