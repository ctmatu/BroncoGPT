# Heating Unit Out of Service in Section of Darlene May Gym

**Summary of disruption:** Heating to some offices and classrooms in Darlene May Gym (Building 41) has been disrupted because of a faulty heating unit. A new heating unit is being ordered and notification will be sent when the unit is operational.

**The affected offices are:** 6, 7, 9, 11, 12, 13.

**The affected classrooms are:** 10 and 10A.

**Reason for disruption:** A faulty heating unit requires replacement.

**How does this affect me?** Affected offices and classrooms will be without heat until a new heating unit is installed.

**What do I need to do?** Please share this information with others in the building who may not have received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:** Facilities Customer Service at Ext. 3030.
