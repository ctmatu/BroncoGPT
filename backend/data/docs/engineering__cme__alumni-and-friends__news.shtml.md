**What's Happening in CME?**

Alumni Newsletter
Twice a year the Chemical & Materials Engineering Alumni Newsletter *FLOW* is published for Cal Poly Pomona grads. We are moving toward sending a copy of *FLOW* electronically (pdf). If you are not currently receiving your copy, please e-mail cme@cpp.edu with both your e-mail address and home address so that you may receive your copy while we work on updating our data:
