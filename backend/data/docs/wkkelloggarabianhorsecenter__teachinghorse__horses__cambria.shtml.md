# Cambria

## "Minnie"

Minnie is a beautiful, dark bay, mare who was born and raised here at CPP. She has raised many of her own babies, and now plays an important role giving riding lessons to students and participating in our monthly Sunday Shows. Her confident and quiet temperament makes her a good teacher to all.
