# CP Heir About Her

## "Felicity"

Felicity loves people and her calm energy makes her a perfect partner for many of our hands on-activities with horses here at the AHC. She was bred and born here, went on to become a successful show horse, and now has returned to us to help teach the next generation of horse enthusiasts.
