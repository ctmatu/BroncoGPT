# CBA Innovation Symposium

## **Ideas that spark innovation in business, AI, and society.**

---

### This annual College of Business Administration event brings together students, alumni, faculty, and industry to explore emerging technologies, business transformation, and innovation strategy. MHCDI plays a key role in organizing and presenting student projects that apply AI and digital tools to real-world problems.

---

#### **Features:**

* #### Student and faculty panels
* #### AI and business intelligence topics
* #### Industry guest speakers and keynotes
* #### Project showcases and networking opportunities

#### *👉 [**Learn more about the symposium**](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/symposium.shtml)*
