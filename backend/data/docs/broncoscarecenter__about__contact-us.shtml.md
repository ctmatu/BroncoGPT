# About Us

## Our Mission and Vision

### Our Mission

The Care Center is a welcoming first stop for students of concern and any student seeking support. We connect students with basic needs resources, wellness services, and advocacy to help them overcome challenges and stay on track toward their personal and academic goals.

### Our Vision

We envision a community where seeking help is normalized and every student feels supported. By fostering a culture of care, we aim to ensure all students have the tools, resources, and connections they need to thrive personally and academically.
