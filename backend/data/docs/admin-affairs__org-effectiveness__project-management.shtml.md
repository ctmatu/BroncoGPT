# Project Management

![](pictures/pmfgvy.jpg)**The OE Team's Project Management service goals include:**

* Creating strategic alignment between all stakeholders involved in any given initiative.
* Providing the leadership needed to take an idea from conception through development and on to final delivery.
* Designing clear, focused objectives and goals, and achieving buy-in from all relevant stakeholders.
* Developing a realistic resource plan that can achieve achieving the project’s objectives on time, under budget, and within scope.
* Ensuring that the final delivery meets the expectations of the organization and/or the client.

**Remember to check in with the OE team prior to starting a project so we can help equip you with everything you'll need!**
