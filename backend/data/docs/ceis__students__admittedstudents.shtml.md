Welcome to the CEIS Community! Congratulations on your acceptance to Cal Poly Pomona and the College of Education & Integrative Studies. As a Bronco, you are joining a learning community of students, scholars, and professionals. We are dedicated to the advancement and development of knowledges, interdisciplinary and student-centered education, equity, and community engagement. We look forward to seeing you at Bronco Bound, Summer Orientation, and Greeting you in the Fall! Go Broncos!
