## Be Well

As a JED campus, Cal Poly Pomona receives custom support to build upon our existing student mental health, substance use and suicide prevention efforts.

The JED Foundation works to protect emotional health and prevent suicide for America's teens and young adults. CPP joins over 450 universities across the nation as an official member. This hub is managed by the [Division of Student Affairs.](https://www.cpp.edu/student-affairs)

[Learn More About JED Campus](https://www.jedcampus.org/)
