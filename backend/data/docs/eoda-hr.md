## About Us

We support Cal Poly Pomona’s mission by enhancing the employee experience through data-informed HR services, leadership development, and programs that foster a positive, inclusive workplace. From recruitment to training, we’re here to support your success and share in the university’s purpose - to help anyone who dreams of success achieve it.
