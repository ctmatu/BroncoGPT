# Summer Research Seminars

![Summer Seminar Series banner with blue triangles as a background](../../img/summer/summer_research_seminar_general.jpg)

In collaboration with colleges and departments on campus, the Office of Undergraduate Research will host a weekly Summer Research Seminar Series. The Seminar Series is open to the public.

The Summer Research Seminars are all-inclusive of a dynamic range of topics and offer the opportunity for students to learn beyond their field. Each week, a new speaker will present their research and participants include: Cal Poly Pomona students and faculty working on campus over the summer, students from programs including the McNair Scholars Program, STARS, ENGAGE, as well as students who are part of the Summer Undergraduate Research Experience Program. The goal of the seminar series is to enrich students and faculty participating in summer programs on campus.
