## ECE Alumni & Friends

The Electrical and Computer Engineering Department graduated its first class in 1963, and now has over 5000 alumni. We would like to add their names and current status to our alumni web pages. Please e-mail us your information if you like to be in the list.

### Industrial Advisory Council

We have Industrial Advisory Council (IAC) meeting in every quarter to discuss how we can improve our program. If you like to be a member of IAC please e-mail your information to ece@cpp.edu or call 909-869-2511.
