## Getting Here

[### Driving Directions

The Collins College of Hospitality Management is located on the northern edge of the Cal Poly Pomona campus, just off University Drive at the top of Horse Hill. You'll find us in a scenic, hillside setting overlooking the main campus.](#modal-0 "Driving Directions")

### Driving Directions

×

**From the 210 Freeway traveling** **east** **and the 57 South:**

Take the 210 Freeway to the 57 South; exit on Temple Avenue (make a right). Proceed west on Temple Avenue to University Drive. Turn right on University Drive; proceed north. At Horse Hill Drive, turn right and proceed up to The Collins College.

**From the 57 Freeway** **traveling north****:**

Exit on Temple Avenue (make a left). Proceed west on Temple Avenue to University Drive. Turn right on University Drive; proceed north. At Horse Hill Drive, turn right and proceed up to The Collins College.

**From the 10 Freeway traveling** **west** **or** **east****:**

Take the 10 Freeway to the 57 South; exit on Temple Avenue (make a right). Proceed west on Temple Avenue to University Drive. Turn right on University Drive; proceed north. At Horse Hill Drive, turn right and proceed up to The Collins College.
