# Ambassadors Circle

## Edward (Ned) F. Snavely

### Retired Hotel Executive

Mr. Snavely is a 40-year veteran of the hospitality industry. His early responsibilities included property and regional sales and marketing positions. The latter half of his career had him serving as General Manager of suburban, convention and resort hotel properties. Employers were Marriott International and Montage Hotels and Resort. He retired in February 2010.

Civic duties have played an integral role in Mr. Snavely's life, and with the support of the hotel industry, he has assisted many organizations. He has also been a member, past and present, of various community organizations.

Mr. Snavely is a graduate of Pennsylvania State University.
