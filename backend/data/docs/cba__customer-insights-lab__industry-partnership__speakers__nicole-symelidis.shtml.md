# Expert Speaker Nicole Symelidis

|  | The *Center for Customer Insights and Digital Marketing* presents Nicole Symelidis, who introduced students to the Insights Association: an organization dedicated to spreading the message about consumer insights and its necessity in all industry segments. In this video, she delves into IA's mission, values (or pillars), membership benefits, and Insights IDEAtors (IA's diversity and inclusion program). The Insights Association not only aims to protect the customer insights and analytics industry, but it also seeks to support the growth of young professionals through education and training content, networking events, professional development programs, and more!    **Check below to view the video!** | ![](../../img/nicole-expert.png) |
