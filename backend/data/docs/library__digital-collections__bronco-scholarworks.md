ScholarWorks is the Cal State's institutional repository. Bronco ScholarWorks, an initiative of the Cal Poly Pomona University Library, provides persistent and reliable access to the scholarly and creative outputs of Cal Poly Pomona students, faculty, and staff by storing content in the Scholar platform. The digital content housed in this repository is accessible, durable, and permanent. Content available in Scholar supports the instruction, research, scholarship, and creative activities of those associated with Cal Poly Pomona and the Cal State system. For more information on how to deposit your work, policies, and collections see our FAQ.

## Cal Poly Pomona Collections

The CPP collections contain scholarly and creative outputs of CPP students, faculty, and staff including: theses, dissertations, presentations, posters, research papers, journal articles, essays, learning objects, conference proceedings, papers, presentations, grant-funded research reports and data sets with open access requirements, and more.

[Cal Poly Pomona Collections](https://scholarworks.calstate.edu/catalog?f[campus_sim][]=Pomona "Cal Poly Pomona Collections. ")

## CSU Collections

Visit the general CSU ScholarWorks platform to view and search collections from the Cal State University (CSU) system in addition to CPP collections. Here you can find: theses, dissertations, presentations, posters, research papers, journal articles, essays, learning objects, conference proceedings, papers, presentations, grant-funded research reports and data sets with open access requirements, and more.

[CSU Collections](https://scholarworks.calstate.edu/ "CSU Collections . ")
