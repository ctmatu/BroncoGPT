## About Me

Sarah is an adjunct lecturer at Cal Poly Pomona, where she teaches 2nd- and 3rd-year design studios. She is also an Associate and Senior Designer at the award-winning sports and entertainment firm POPULOUS, specializing in performance-driven façade systems.

Her 15+ years of industry experience include design work at Morphosis Architects, SOM, and NBBJ, spanning both domestic and international projects. Notable works include the Kolon Future Research Park in Seoul, the Hanking Tower in Shenzhen, the Montage Ohana Health Campus in Monterey, and the Seattle Opera at the Center headquarters.

Sarah received her Bachelor of Science in Architecture with honors from the University of Virginia and her Master of Architecture from Washington University in St. Louis. In 2020, she was invited to join the Scientific Committee for ACADIA (Association for Computer Aided Design in Architecture), where she continues to contribute to conference peer reviews.
