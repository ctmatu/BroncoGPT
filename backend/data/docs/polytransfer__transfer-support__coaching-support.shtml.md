# Coaching and Support

## Speak directly with dedicated professional staff and student assistants!

![Provost Gomez poses with transfer students on Engineering steps](../img/pt-steps.jpg)

## Making An Appointment

* Log into [CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml)
* Click on the Make an Appointment or View Drop-In Availability button

## Select the following options as answers to the questions:

* For Type of Appointment: Student Support
* For Office: PolyTransfer Program
* For Reason: Academic Support/Resource
* Select “Schedule” (Blue Button, Bottom Right)
* Identify Time/Date for the Meeting

Confirm your appointment and you’re all set!
