# Professional Development and Support

## Professional Development and Support

Professional development, education, and capacity building are essential in advancing student success, cultivating a sense of belonging, and ensuring staff are well-equipped with the tools and resources necessary to meet the ever-changing needs of our community. To fulfill this mission, the Division of Student Affairs(DSA) offers a variety of professional development opportunities grounded in evidence-based strategies and best practices.

These initiatives are designed to foster a culture of care and empower staff to serve students holistically, addressing their diverse needs with empathy, innovation, and excellence.
