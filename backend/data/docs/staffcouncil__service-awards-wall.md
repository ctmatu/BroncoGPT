# CPP Years of Service 2024-25 Recognition Wall

## A Message from President Coley

At Cal Poly Pomona, our people are our most valuable resource. Through the dedication of our faculty and staff, Cal Poly Pomona continues to serve as a model for a modern and inclusive polytechnic university. By virtue of their years of service, our Service Award honorees demonstrate the commitment essential to our success and also serve as role models to the rest of the campus.

On behalf of the entire Cal Poly Pomona community, I thank and congratulate this year’s honorees for your service. For more than 80 years, Cal Poly Pomona has been providing world-class educational opportunities and exploring the frontiers of research, scholarship, and creativity. Thanks to your hard work and expertise, that legacy continues today.

---

## A Message from Staff Council

Congratulations to the Service Award recipients. Cal Poly Pomona flourishes with your continuous commitment and devotion to our campus community. Your accomplishments are acknowledged and honored with this annual recognition. Thank you!

-Staff Council
