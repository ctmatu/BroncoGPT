# How to Get Involved

#### Professor for a Day

Share Your Knowledge with Students at [Professor for a Day.](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/get-involved/professor.shtml)

![photos of alumni participating in professor for a day](img/pfad_comp.jpg)

Alumni return to campus to share their career experience, rather than teach a course; however, a lot of learning takes place when alumni share how they applied their education to their career.

The week-long campus-wide event takes place in the spring semester. Check the [Professor for a Day web page](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/get-involved/professor.shtml) for dates and details.

## Bronco Mentoring Program

With just a little bit of your time, you can give students the confidence to go after the career they want.

You’re invited to join the CPP Bronco Mentoring Network, our career-mentoring and advice-sharing network that makes it easy for you to connect with others in the Cal Poly Pomona community. The goal of the program is to increase student success by connecting students with alumni who have expertise in their industry, major, or future career.

[Become a Mentor](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/get-involved/mentoring.shtml "Become a Mentor. ")

## Participate in Giving Day

Giving Day takes place in the spring. We're always looking for Giving Day Ambassadors who are willing to reach out to their networks via email, text messages, and social media. We're also looking for matching gifts to kick off the event. Please contact Bill Burrows for more information.

[Giving Day](https://givingday.cpp.edu "Giving Day. ")

## Dining with Broncos

Alumni host dinner for a group of students in their homes or at a restaurant. The dinner can be as formal or informal as you like. Discussion is nonspecific, but dinners may be organized based on various characteristics including but not limited to major, career field/interest, hobby, or geographic proximity.

[Learn More](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/get-involved/dining.shtml "Learn More. ")

## Estate Planning Resources for Alumni & Friends

Our new planned giving website includes valuable personal, financial and estate planning information and resources. You’ll learn about the best tax saving strategies when planning gifts to your family and Cal Poly Pomona. Discover how to receive supplemental retirement income from making a gift to the university. Download the complimentary copy of our Estate Planning Guide. Sign up for our FREE Estate Planning Newsletter, and learn how to join the Kellogg Voorhis Legacy Society.
