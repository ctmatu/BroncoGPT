# CPP Budget 2019-2020

## Annual Meeting on the Budget

In light of the urgency and importance regarding the state budget situation, the fall semester's Campus Conversation was combined with the Annual Meeting on the CPP Budget. The meeting took place on October 29, 2020. President Soraya Coley was joined by Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs Sylvia Alva and Associate Vice President, Finance & Administrative Services & Interim CFO Joe Simoneschi as part of a comprehenisve update and campus discussion.

A video of the campus conversation, as well as a PDF of the presentation, are available for download below (authentication required).
