# Amazon Career Choice at CPP

## Let Amazon Career Choice help you reach your educational goals and become a Bronco at Cal Poly Pomona.

**A message from Amazon:**Career Choice is an education benefit that empowers employees to learn new skills for career success at Amazon or elsewhere. We meet individual learners where they are on their education journey through a variety of education and upskilling opportunities including full college tuition, industry certifications designed to lead to in-demand jobs, and foundational skills.

![](../img/amazon-career-choice-website-banner.png)

![Amazon Career Choice](../img/amazon_cc_logo.jpg)

## No Cost to You!

Through Career Choice, Amazon will pre-pay 100% of tuition, fees, and textbooks up to the employees’ maximum eligibility.
