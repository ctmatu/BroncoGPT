# Environmental Biology Markers, Milestones and Flexibility

## Environmental Biology

#### Marker Courses

* BIO 211/L
* any upper division core course
* completed Organic Chemistry series

#### Milestones-Remain in the quarter system if the student:

* will be graduating by Spring 2020
* or, has taken an upper division elective course listed under cluster 1 or 2

#### Flexibility in Quarter Curriculum

* Students who have not taken STA 120 or BIO 211/211L: take STA 1300 (semester), and can petition for extra lecture units to count for BIO 211L
* Students who have not taken BIO 325/L: take BIO 3250 (semester) and can petition for extra lecture units to count for BIO 325L
* Only one Biochemistry lecture course is required (CHM 3210 or 3270). Biochemistry lab unit can be replaced with extra semester lecture units.
* Only 1 unit of BIO 4410 required (equals 1.5 quarter units). Use any upper div spare units to replace the missing 0.5 quarter unit.
