## Continuing and Incoming Students:

## Registration

**Continuing students** registration for courses begins in April for the fall term, October for a winter or spring term, and March for a summer term. The registration windows for eligible continuing students are available online via BroncoDirect  before the start of the registration period. All registration holds must be cleared before registration.

**New undergraduate students** will register for classes as part of their participation in mandatory orientation.

![Students studying](../img/students_study.jpg)

## Continuing Students ~

## Registration Window

* Registration windows are assigned based on class level (Senior, Junior, Sophomore, Freshmen) as well as units earned (not including currently enrolled units). If a group of students has the same number of units, registration windows are assigned randomly to ensure equity.
* Registration windows are fixed and cannot be changed. If you believe your registration window was assigned in error, you should contact the Registrar’s Office at registrar@cpp.edu.
* Unit limitations apply to each of the registration periods. These total unit limits include enrolled and waitlisted classes. See the chart below for unit limits.
* You may waitlist up to a maximum of 10 units for Fall or Spring term. Waitlisted units count toward the total allowable unit limit. For detailed information, visit [Waitlisting](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/registration/waitlisting.shtml).

For more information, visit [Registering for Courses](https://www.cpp.edu/~studentsuccess/guides/registration/register.shtml).

Please review your specific registration window online in your [BroncoDirect](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/p/broncodirect.ctf1/max/render.uP) - Student Center.

## Incoming Undergraduate Students ~

## Registration Window

New freshman and transfer students will be able to register for their first term of classes at the [orientation session](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/) that they attend. Students will be able to access [BroncoDirect](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/p/broncodirect.ctf1/max/render.uP) at designated periods during their orientation session. To ensure a trouble-free registration, new students are advised to submit documents required by the [Office of Admissions](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/index.shtml)  as early as possible before their scheduled orientation session.

![Students walking](../img/students_walking.jpg)

## Incoming Graduate Students ~

## Registration Window

The Registrar’s Office assigns fall and spring term incoming graduate students' registration window as reflected below.  To view your registration window, log in to your [BroncoDirect](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/p/broncodirect.ctf1/max/render.uP) account and click on Student Center under the Student Self Services menu. Please watch this [Enrollment Window how-to video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AEnASjuvZEU "Enrollment Window how-to video") to check your registration window. Also, you may view:  [How to register for classes](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/guides/registration/register.shtml). If you have questions about which courses you should enroll in, please do not hesitate to contact your [Department Graduate Coordinators](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/prospective-students/explore-our-grad-programs.shtml). To further assist you in your CPP journey, click on [resources and next steps](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/current-students/new-graduate-students.shtml) for more information. Check the [Class Search](https://schedule.cpp.edu/) for Course Offerings. If you miss this general registration window, the next opportunity to register for classes will be during the term Add/Drop Period reflected below.

Registration Windows are assigned to new incoming graduate students typically on the first week of November for the Spring semester and the first week of May for the Summer (applicable to Credential and EDD programs only) and Fall semester. You may visit the [Graduate Studies](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/index.shtml#:~:text=GRADUATE%20STUDENT%20WELCOME%20ORIENTATION%20FOR%20FALL%202022&text=Please%20save%20the%20date%3A%20Saturday,Fall%202022%20Virtual%20Welcome%20Event.) website for more information.
