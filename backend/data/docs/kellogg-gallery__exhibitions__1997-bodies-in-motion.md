# Bodies in Motion

## Bodies in Motion

Oct 30, 1997 to Nov 29, 1997

**Location:** Kellogg University Art Gallery

Press the tab key to view the content. Use the down arrow key to move to the next tab and up arrow key to move to the previous.

[Program Schedule](#tab-2)

A digital art exhibition with technology time line.

First in film, then video and now combined with computer technologies, artists have the means to explore new realms of human locomotion and moving forms.

The works selected for this show demonstrate a variety of visual research dealing with human locomotion and moving forms using video and computer technoloigies.

Participating artists: Rebecca Allen, Ana Maria Alvaro, Aladino Villanueva Debert, Ken Fields, Nathan Frietas, Peter Greenstone, Helvetica Jones, Paras Kaul, Robert Nideffer, Audri Phillips, Victoria Vesna, Michael Wright, and Joy Zellen.
