# Facilities Management

Diverse units work together to maintain a clean, safe and comfortable environment for students, faculty and staff that is also conducive to learning.

Facilities Management proactively and progressively manages the maintenance of the capital assets of the university and provides exceptional customer service to the campus community. This is accomplished by individuals from varied backgrounds who share a common goal and ensure campus stakeholders that “We'll take care of it.”

Facilities Management works closely with the other departments and organizations on campus, and helps set up and clean up various functions such as Commencement, Founders Day, Career Day, Bronco Fest, athletic events and other university events that allow students to embrace the college experience.
