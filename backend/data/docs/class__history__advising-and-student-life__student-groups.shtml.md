# Get Involved

![Two CPP History students (class of 2025)](../img/club-general.jpg)

## Student Groups

There are two dedicated student-run organizations for History students at Cal Poly Pomona: History Club and Phi Alpha Theta. Some of our majors are also at the core of another new group, the Student Initiative for Justice. Click on the cards below for a brief description and social media links for these groups!

## Club Details

[![History Club](../img/history-club.jpg)

### History Club

History Club is open to all CPP students interested in History. It exists to offer students and faculty a positive and creative forum to openly discuss historical events, ideas, and related topics while promoting career pathways in fields related to the study of history. Click here for more!](https://www.instagram.com/cpp_historyclub/%20 "opens a new window")

[![Phi Alpha Theta](../img/pat.jpg)

### Phi Alpha Theta

Phi Alpha Theta is the national History student honor society. Our CPP chapter of Phi Alpha Theta puts on an active program of events throughout the year! Click here for our Instagram!](https://www.instagram.com/phialphatheta_cpp/%20 "opens a new window")

[![SIJ Logo](../img/sijlogo.jpg)

### Student Initiative for Justice

Affiliated with the Ahimsa Center for Non-Violence in Thought and Action, the Student Initiative for Justice brings students, faculty, staff and others beyond the CPP together for conversations and actions concerning social justice, democracy and history. Click here to catch up with all we're doing on Instagram!](https://www.instagram.com/cpp.sij/%20 "opens a new window")
