# Physics and Astronomy Students Graduate!

May 17, 2025

*On May 17th, 2025, the College of Science held it's graduation ceremony which included our wonderful Physics and Astronomy department! We are all incredibly proud of all our graduating students. They've put in the hard work and have the diploma to show it! We wish them all the best in all that they do. Congratulations to our 2025 graduates!*
