# About

University Police is dedicated to providing professional police services to the campus and partnering with our community to support the academic mission of the university. University Police is responsible for law enforcement, security and emergency response. **The department is operational 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, year-round**.

All campus telephones are connected to a 9-1-1 emergency system, which is located in the 24-hour Police Communications Center. Staffed by certified police dispatchers, the Communications Center provides telephone and two-way radio contact for emergency personnel, and also serves as an after-hours contact for students, faculty and staff.

Responding to thousands of calls for service annually, **we are here to assist you.**
