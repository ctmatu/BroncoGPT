## Let Us Help You

Our office is here to support you in your role as a counselor, and we are excited to work together to help your students achieve their dreams of success. Find our counselor resources and events below, or reach out to our team to find more ways to get connected.

## CSU Counselor Conference

The CSU Counselor Conference brings together high school and community college counselors, educators, community-based organizations and representatives from all 23 CSU campuses.

[View Schedule of Events](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/counselor-resources/counselor-conferences/%20)

## Contact Us

We are available Monday - Friday from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. to answer your questions and get you connected to what you need. We look forward to hearing from you!
