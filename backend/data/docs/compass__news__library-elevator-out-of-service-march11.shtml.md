# Elevator and Escalator in University Library Out of Service

**Summary of disruption:** Elevator No. 2 and the escalator in the University Library (Building 15) are out of service and repairs are underway. The elevator and escalator were affected by the brief morning power interruption. Service will be restored as soon as repairs are completed.

**Reason for disruption:** A morning power interruption affected the operation of the elevator and escalator.

**What do I need to do?** Use other elevators or the stairs in the library Please share this information with others who may not have received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:** Facilities Customer Service (Ext. 3030).
