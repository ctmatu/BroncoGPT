# Master's Degree Program

## Welcome!

The Master of Arts in Education is an advanced degree offered at Cal Poly Pomona. A minimum of 31 semester units of acceptable graduate level work must be completed to earn the degree. Twenty-two (22) units of course work must be taken in residency. There are three emphases available within the program: Curriculum & Instruction; Special Education; and Educational Leadership. Explore our programs and courses by clicking on the menu above labeled "Master's Degree Program."

Graduate Coordinator
