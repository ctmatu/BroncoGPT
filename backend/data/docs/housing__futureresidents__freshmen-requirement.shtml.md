# First Year Student Residential Requirement

## Freshmen Requirement

**No First-Year Student Residential Requirement for 2025-2026 Academic Year**

University Housing Services is pausing the first-year student residential requirement for the 2025-2026 academic year. Incoming first-year students will not be required to complete the housing application process unless they choose or need to live on campus.  Please note that submitting a housing application does not guarantee a housing assignment. We strongly encourage students interested in living on campus to apply as early as possible.  The Academic Year 2025-2026 UHS application opens on Thursday, February 27, 2025, in the [Housing Portal](https://cpp.starrezhousing.com/StarRezPortal/).

Living on campus during the first year offers significant advantages that contribute to student success at Cal Poly Pomona. Research consistently shows that first-year, full-time students who live on campus achieve higher GPAs, retain at higher rates, and graduate more efficiently.
