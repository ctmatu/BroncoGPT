## ETS and the Polytechnic Approach

Educational Talent Search (ETS) is dedicated to the Cal Poly Pomona’s polytechnic spirit of learning by doing, and service to the surrounding communities. Cal Poly Pomona students (both undergraduate and graduate) are hired during the academic year as tutors/peer advisors for the after school tutoring sessions, workshop facilitators, and peer advisors providing academic advising. Volunteer, leadership, and/or community service opportunities are available throughout the academic year and summer months for Cal Poly Pomona students desiring service hours for clubs, fraternities/sororities, or professional organizations. Interested Cal Poly students, or faculty/staff should contact the project director for any of the above opportunities.
