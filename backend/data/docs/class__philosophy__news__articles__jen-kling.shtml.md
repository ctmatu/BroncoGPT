# Jennifer Kling on Zoom

October 9, 2020

[![Photo of Jennifer Kling giving a speech](../../img/jen-kling.jpg)Jennifer Kling](https://blog.apaonline.org/2019/07/26/apa-member-interview-jennifer-kling/#https://blog.apaonline.org/2019/07/26/apa-member-interview-jennifer-kling/) (University of Colorado, Colorado Springs) gave a Zoom presentation, "Who's Responsible for Refugee Justice?" on October 9th 2020, 1pm-2:30pm.

Dr. Kling is Assistant Professor of Philosophy and Director of the Center for Legal Studies. Her research focuses on moral and political philosophy, particularly issues in war and peace, self- and other-defense, international relations, and feminism. She is the author of [*War Refugees: Risk, Justice, and Moral Responsibility*](https://rowman.com/ISBN/9781498562485/War-Refugees-Risk-Justice-and-Moral-Responsibility) (Lexington Books, 2019).
