# Battle of the Bands

November 10, 2023

###### ![igebattle23](../../img/billys-botb-flyer.jpg)

IGESO (IGE Student Organization) officers MCed the Billy's Battle of the Bands that IGESO co-hosted with the ASI Beat team on Thursday, November 9th, 7-10 P.M. at Ursa Major. For the music filled night were student artists performed and completed in an "Amercian Idol" style competition, IGESO officers collaborated with the ASI Beat Team to help recruit performers, organize and promote the university-wide community building event.
