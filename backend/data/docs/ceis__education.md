## Welcome to the Education Department Applications now being accepted.

The Education Department strives to cultivate successful education professionals in a diverse community that practices innovation, discovery, and experiential student-centered learning. Our vision is to be the model for preparing highly-qualified education professionals by inspiring creativity and innovation, embracing local and global challenges, and transforming lives through the integration of teaching, learning, scholarship, and creative activities.

## Programs We Offer

![](img/bachelors-program_vanessa.png)

### Bachelor's Degree Programs

The Integrated Teacher Education Program is for students who wish to complete both their bachelor’s degree and teaching credential simultaneously. Currently, we offer the ITEP program for the Education Specialist program through Liberal Studies or Early Childhood Studies.

[More on Bachelor's Degree Programs](/ceis/itep/ "More on Bachelor's Degree Programs. ")

![](img/credential-programs.jpg)

### Credential Programs

Cal Poly Pomona's post-baccalaureate credential program prepares teacher candidates to work with TK-12 students from diverse backgrounds and to become teacher leaders within the schools and community. Our programs are fully accredited by the California Commission on Teacher Credentialing and meet all state teacher preparation standards.

[More About Credential Programs](/ceis/credential-program/index.shtml "More About Credential Programs. ")

![](img/masters-program.jpg)

### Master's Degree Program

The master’s program offers an advanced degree in the Master’s of Arts in Education with options in Curriculum & Instruction and Special Education. The program offers students the opportunity to pursue both the credential and master’s degree simultaneously in a combined program.

[More on Master's Degree Program](/ceis/education/masters-programs/index.shtml "More on Master's Degree Program. ")

![](img/credential-services.jpg)

### Teacher Intern Program

When qualified teachers are not available, school districts often search for an individual who has either partially completed a credential program and shows great promise of being highly successful or an individual who has the subject matter competence, experience and maturity to learn the craft of teaching while being paid as the teacher of record for a class.

[More on Teacher Intern Program](/ceis/credential-program/teacher-intern.shtml "More on Teacher Intern Program. ")
