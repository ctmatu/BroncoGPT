# Resources

## Connect with Us

The Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships is open for business to serve you!  We are committed to providing you the service and support you need to excel in your academic endeavors. Check out our  [online resources](campus-resources.shtml)  and create your  [Financial Aid Forms account](https://cpp.verifymyfafsa.com)  to avoid missing important notifications. Still can’t find what you need? Contact us via one of the methods below. Please be aware we are experiencing unusually high call and email volumes.

Questions about charges to your account, status of refunds, or direct deposit setup should be directed to Student Accounting and Cashiering Services at  *sacs@cpp.edu**.*For answers to the most commonly asked questions please see our [Frequently Asked Questions page.](faqs.shtml)

If you have any feedback regarding the quality of our service, [let us know](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtALfAHtQJlFIq-EkKDpEQDFUQlc4OFBPVkhSMkMyMzJPUzcwT0xHNFRSUiQlQCN0PWcu).

## Correspond With Us

### Email

When you email us, please include your Full Name and Bronco ID number in your messages so that we can provide you with accurate information that is specific to you.  When possible, please use your official @cpp.edu email address.

* **Financial Aid Questions:** finaid@cpp.edu
* **Scholarship****-****specific Questions:** scholarships@cpp.edu

Our team will respond to your inquiry within 2 to 3 business days. We appreciate your patience during this time.

### **Sending a Scholarship Check?**

In order to have your outside scholarship funding applied to your student account, make sure that the donor / agency sends the funding to the correct office.  All scholarship checks should be sent to:

California State Polytechnic University, Pomona
**Student****Accounting & Cashiering****Services**3801 W. Temple Avenue
Pomona, CA  91768

Make sure to have your donor / agency make the checks payable to **California State****Polytechnic University, Pomona**, and include your name and Cal Poly Pomona Bronco ID Number on the check's memo line. The donor should also note the term for which you or they would like the funds applied (Fall, Spring, or Fall/Spring) and any enrollment requirements (full-time, half-time).
