# The Red Chador: Close Encounters

January 9, 2025

![a promo image for "The Rainbow Brigade" live performance featuring the aforementioned performers.](../exhibitions/2025-the-red-chador-close-encounters/img/approved-marketing-images/02_03_2025_theredchador_instagram_performance_eb.jpg)

## Durational Performance Rainbow Brigade

Don’t miss the Southern California debut of *The Red Chador* and the *Rainbow Brigade* on the campus of Cal Poly Pomona! In this performance, internationally acclaimed artist **Anida Yoeu Ali** is joined by the *Rainbow Brigade*, featuring seven fully veiled performers selected from the CPP campus community. Dressed in sparkling shades of the rainbow, they will meander through and join activites around the Cal Poly Pomona campus.

The performance will take place on **Thursday, March 20, 2025**, from **12:00 PM to 3:00 PM**. It will begin at the **Cal Poly Pomona University Quad "Pancakes"** (the steps located next to Building 6 and the Campus Center Marketplace), with stops at **various sites across campus** before concluding at the **Kellogg University Art Gallery (Building 35A)**.
