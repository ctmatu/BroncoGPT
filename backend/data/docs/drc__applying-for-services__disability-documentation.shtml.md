# Disability Documentation

## Guidelines

As part of the application process, please provide any available documentation that indicates a history of disability diagnosis. Please review our **[General Guidance for Disability Documentation (DOCX)](../documents/general-documentation-guidelines-may20.docx)** for more information.

DRC recognizes barriers exist and can create hardship with regard to presenting third-party documentation. Please do not delay submitting an application for services out of concern for not having appropriate paperwork. Any information/documentation that students can readily share during or before the first meeting is helpful and can save the need for follow-up meetings.

If you're having difficulty obtaining documentation, please have your health care provider, complete our **[DRC Certification of Disability Form (PDF).](../documents/certification-of-disability-oct18.pdf)**

\***IMPORTANT\***

**For students with learning disabilities**, please refer to specific [Documentation Guidelines for Learning Disabilities (DOCX)](../documents/documentation-information-ld.docx) in accordance with California State University (CSU) policy AA-2014-08.

**For students requesting an accommodation for Emotional Support Animal**, student are required have their qualified health care provider complete our [ESA Support Animal Request Form (PDF)](../documents/esa_request_form.pdf). Once the form is completed the student must submit directly to the DRC.
