## Incremental Budget Plan

Our incremental budget plan refers to the amount of revenues and expenses above prior year’s amounts.  Before accounting for average savings such as employee turnover, we show a $37.9M funding gap.

The FY25-26 Budget is preliminary and is based on the Governor’s January 2025 budget proposal.
