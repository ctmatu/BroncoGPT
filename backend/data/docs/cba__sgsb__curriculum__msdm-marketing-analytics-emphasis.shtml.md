# MSDM | Marketing Analytics Emphasis

## Marketing Analytics Emphasis

The MS in Digital Marketing offers a flexible curriculum with the required courses and electives from either the [Digital Marketing Strategy Emphasis](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/sgsb/curriculum/msdm-digital-marketing-strategy-emphasis.shtml) or the [Marketing Analytics Emphasis](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/sgsb/curriculum/msdm-marketing-analytics-emphasis.shtml) to tailor the curriculum to their career goals. Go to the [Digital Marketing Strategy Emphasis](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/sgsb/curriculum/msdm-digital-marketing-strategy-emphasis.shtml) page to learn about its completion roadmap.

## Intended Jobs for Marketing Analytics Emphasis

Companies use various titles for various digital marketing or marketing jobs. Pay varies depending on job positions and roles:

* Marketing Data Analyst: $92,811 (Zip Recruiter)
* Marketing Data Scientist: $122,738 nationally (Zip recruiter)
* Marketing Data Scientist: $165,018 in the LA area (Zip recruiter)

Below are some of the job titles that digital marketing emphasis is intended to support the most:

* Marketing Analytics Manager
* Digital Marketing Analytics Manager
* (Digital) Marketing Data Analyst
* SEO Analyst
* Marketing Data Scientist
* Social Media & Web Analyst
* Email Marketing Analyst
* Marketing Strategy Analyst
* Market Research Associate or Director

## Curriculum for Marketing Analytics Emphasis

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Items** | **Marketing Analytics Emphasis** |
| **Major Required** (20 units) | IBM 5910 Strategic Data Wrangling and Visualization (3)  IBM 6010 - Digital Marketing (3)  IBM 6200 - Online Consumer Psychology and Behaviors (3)  IBM 6400 - Current Issues in Digital Marketing (1)  IBM 6500 - Customer Insights Methods and Survey Research (3)  IBM 6510 - Foundations of Customer Analytics (3)  IBM 6800 - Data-Driven Digital Marketing Strategy I (2)  IBM 6950 - Data-Driven Digital Marketing Strategy II (2) |
| **Major Electives**:  *Emphasis Recommended* (10 units) | IBM 6520 - Market Forecasting (2)  IBM 6530 - Marketing Analytics (3)  IBM 6540 - Applied Machine Learning in MKTG (3)  IBM 6600 - Marketing Text Analytics with LLMs and AI (2) |
| **Major Electives**: *Emphasis Other* (4 units) | IBM 6700 - Marketing Data Management (2)  *(Choose another course below)*  IBM \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_  OR  GBA 6420 – Optimization Methods (3) |

Note: The following courses will not be available for a 1-year roadmap for AY 2025-2026: IBM 6250, IBM 6450, and IBM 6700; for this reason, a 1-year track’s curriculum will be “Balanced” in AY 2025-2026.

The 2-year roadmap will have those courses in AY 2026-2027. Ten units of Emphasis recommended or four units of Emphasis Other are the minimum required credits, so going over the units is fine.

## Factors to Consider when you choose a 2-year roadmap vs. a 1-year roadmap

We provide a two-year roadmap as well as a one-year roadmap for students entering the program in the fall semester. Those who enter the program in the spring semester will follow a two-year completion roadmap, completing the curriculum in 1.5 years.
