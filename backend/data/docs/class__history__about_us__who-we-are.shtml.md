## Innovative Programs

The History Department at Cal Poly Pomona was one of the earliest pioneers across the country in emphasizing global history. Our B.A. and M.A. students take a wide range of courses that help them develop broad content knowledge, and provide hands-on training in historical research. Our undergraduate majors write a capstone senior thesis based on original primary and secondary source research; they also take at least one class in digital research methods that encompasses everything from large text analysis to mapping to web design and podcasting!

### Global History

The faculty members in the department include experts in the history of the United States (including California), the ancient and modern Mediterranean and Middle East, South Asia, East Asia, Latin America, Africa, and Europe.

![Aishwary Kumar](../img/kumar-aishwary400x240.jpg)

### Grants and Awards

Dr. Aishwary Kumar launched The Democracy Institute in 2024, with funding from the Teagle Foundation. Other faculty have received scholarships and awards from Fulbright, Guggenheim, National Science Foundation, and the National Endowment for the Humanities.

![John Lloyd on the bus](../img/lloydfoothiltransit.jpg)

### Connecting Past and Present

Dr. John Lloyd's Environmental History class focuses on the history of transportation, and aligns with his work to improve active transportation at CPP. Other engaging thematic courses include the History of Science, the History of Capitalism and the History of Sports!

![Gina Risetter and Ira Pemstein](../img/wang-pemstein-risetter.jpg)

## Career Preparation

History majors take a wide range of courses at CPP to sharpen their writing and analytical skills and to develop their content knowledge. Our majors have won prestigious internships and fellowships across the country, and go on to careers in secondary teaching, museum studies and archives, law, government, business and many other fields!
