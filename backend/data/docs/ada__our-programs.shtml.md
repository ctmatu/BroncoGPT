# Our Programs

Throughout the year, the AdA presents educational programming and hosts events aimed at increasing awareness and encouraging the idea that a disability should not be seen as a limitation, but rather as a sign of perseverance, strength, and determination. Our programs are open to and benefit the entire Cal Poly Pomona community.

## Diversity Champions

Each year, AdA selects 2 [diversity champions](diversity-champions.shtml) who have demonstrated a commitment to increasing disability awareness and to promoting access and equity for individuals with disabilities throughout the Cal Poly Pomona campus community.

## Disability Awareness Programming

Each year AdA offers a disability awareness program consisting of several events. To view previous year's programs, visit the [archive section](/Archived/program-archive) of our web site.

*Future Disability Pride Week information coming soon!*

## Ability Ally Program

All members of the campus community are invited to participate in the [Ability ally Training](ability-ally-training.shtml) to increase understanding of and demystify beliefs about people with disabilities.

Once the training has been completed, you are invited to become an [Ability Ally](ability-ally-training.shtml) to promote disability awareness, support persons with disabilities, and maintain resources for working and interacting with people with disabilities.

## AdA Scholarship Program

[Financial scholarships](scholarships.shtml) are awarded to Cal Poly Pomona students who have taken an active role in promoting disability awareness and serving as an advocate for people with disabilities.
