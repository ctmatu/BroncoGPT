# Apply for the CLASS Cap and Gown Program for Spring 2026’s commencement ceremony by February 12th.

January 16, 2026

![Students holding up their Graduation Caps in a circle](../img/news/grad-caps.jpg)

The CLASS Cap and Gown Program aims to support graduating seniors in financial need with funding for their cap and gown to participate in the Spring 2026 commencement ceremony. Students who meet the criteria below should apply. Applications will be reviewed by a committee, and students who are selected will be notified by March 3rd, 2026, via CPP email. For any questions, please email class-advising@cpp.edu .

Criteria for eligibility:

* Must be a College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences (CLASS) undergraduate major
* Must have applied for Fall 2025, Spring 2026, or Summer 2026 graduation
* Must have met with a CLASS staff advisor to confirm graduation
* Must demonstrate a financial need
* Must complete application by Thursday, February 12, 2026.

[**Apply Today!**](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_7R5Ty5TeFyFEiI6)
