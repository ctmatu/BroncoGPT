# Asphalt Sealing Project on Two Roads Postponed

**Update on project:**Inclement weather has forced the postponement of asphalt sealing on two recently repaired roads. Asphalt sealing will be rescheduled, and notification will be sent before the start of the work. The sealing project was originally scheduled for March 10 on Mansion Lane from University Drive to Kellogg House and a section of the road in front of Police and Parking Services (Building 109).

**Reason for project:** Allow contractor crews to apply a coat of asphalt sealant.

**What do I need to do?** Please share this information with others who may have not received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:**Stan Suh at Ext. 6706 or sjsuh@cpp.edu.
