# Office Toolkit

## Office Toolkit

![New branded business cards with name, college and address.](../img/business-cards25.jpg)

### Business Cards

Official Cal Poly Pomona business cards may be ordered through Graphic Communication Services. Email graphics@cpp.edu for more information.

[![email signature example](../img/sc_emailsig-mockup-v2.png)

### Email Signature

Consistent style reinforces Cal Poly Pomona’s brand identity. Faculty and staff are encouraged to use the official email signature.](../email-signature.shtml)

[![Word Doc Template](../img/word-doc.jpg)

### Word Doc Template

Faculty and staff can download a branded Word template for digital letters, memos and general communication.](https://cpp.canto.com/v/brand/%20)

![Cal Poly Pomona branded stationery set with letterhead, envelope, business cards and more.](../img/stationery-letterhead-env.jpg)

### Letterhead and Envelopes

Custom print and digital letterhead and envelopes may be ordered through Graphic Communication Services. Email graphics@cpp.edu for more information.

![Billy Bronco, University Mascot](../img/nametag-toolkit25-2.jpg)

### Nametags

Official Cal Poly Pomona name tags are produced by an external vendor and may be ordered through Graphic Communication Services. Email graphics@cpp.edu for more information.

[![PowerPoint template on a computer screen](../img/powerpoint.jpg)

### PowerPoint Template

Download the branded PowerPoint template through Canto, our digital asset management system.](https://cpp.canto.com/v/brand/%20)

![Front and back design of Presentation Folder.](../img/presentation-folders21.jpg)

### Presentation Folders

Presentation folders may be ordered from an external vendor in multiples of 500. There are two design styles to choose from and can be customized with your department name. Email kurban@inlandgroup.com to order.

[![zoom background on a computer](../img/zoombgthumb.jpg)

### Zoom Backgrounds

Show your Cal Poly Pomona pride during your Zoom meetings. Download a branded Zoom background from Canto, our digital asset management system.](https://cpp.canto.com/v/brand/album/PMPAV?viewIndex=2&display=curatedView )
