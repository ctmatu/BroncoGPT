## **If You Are Interested in the Combined Master's and Credential Program:**

CPP teacher credential students may begin taking master’s courses upon acceptance to the credential program. Teacher credential candidates may transfer up to three post-baccalaureate teacher credential (EDU) courses towards the master’s degree. The posting of the master’s degree is contingent upon the recommendation/posting of the teaching credential. View more [information](https://www.cpp.edu/ceis/education/masters-programs/combined-credential-and-masters-program.shtml) for this combined master's and credential option.
