# Facilities & Equipment

![Dr. Corcoran in a lab](../img/corcoran.jpg)

The departments modern instrumentation includes:

* Single Crystal X-ray Diffractometer (Bruker Venture D8 with Kappa goniometer)
* Ion Chromatographs
* Vacuum Ovens
* Thermogravimetric Analyzers
* Differential Scanning Calorimeters
* Isothermal Titration Calorimeters
* Raman Spectrometers
* Glove Boxes
* 400 MHz NMR Spectrometer (Nuclear Magnetic Resonance)
* X-band EPR with liquid nitrogen cooling
* Protein purification liquid chromatography instrument (ÄKTA pure)
* FT-IRs, GC-MS and LC-MS instruments
* Stopped Flow Spectrophotometer
* High pressure liquid and gas chromatographs
* Circular dichroism spectrometer
* Fluorimeter
* Capillary Electrophoresis
* Atomic absorption spectrometers
* Light and fluorescence microscopes
* Stereomicroscopes
* PCR machines
* Centrifuges
* Lasers
* Electrochemical workstation

The department also has shared instrumentation in the **Center for Macromolecular Modeling and Material Design** (CM3D), including an Atomic Force Microscope for imaging materials.
