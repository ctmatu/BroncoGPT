# AMA Business Conference!

February 22, 2025

![amaconf](../../img/ama-conference.jpg)This past Saturday's AMA Business Conference on campus brought together aspiring students and industry professionals for a day of networking and career insights. The event kicked off with great icebreakers that helped the attendees make meaningful connections and meet new people, followed by a Q&A session with our special guest speakers, Laura R. Orellana Sanchez, Henry Ong and Justin Nankee, who shared their career journeys and provided practical advice on professional development. Between these sessions, attendees enjoyed a well-earned lunch and took advantage of our professional headshot photo booth. Concluding with the Speed Networking session the conference proved to be a valuable platform for fostering connections between members and professionals.
