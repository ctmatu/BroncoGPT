## Visit the Music Department and Experience CPP Firsthand

Experience the vibrant campus life and well-equipped facilities of Cal Poly Pomona by scheduling a tour of the Music Department.

Campus tours provide you and your families an opportunity to explore our beautiful campus, learn about our academic programs, and see firsthand the resources available to our music students.

[### Tour Highlights](#modal-2 "Tour Highlights")

### Tour Highlights

×

**Performance Spaces**

* Recital Hall
  + An intimate venue for solo and chamber performances
* University Theatre
  + Designed for large ensemble performances, featuring advanced acoustics and lighting

**Practice and Rehearsal Rooms**

* Individual Practice Rooms
  + Equipped with pianos and soundproofing
* Ensemble Rehearsal Rooms
  + Spacious rooms for various ensemble groups

**Recording Studios and Technology Labs**

* Modern Recording Studios
  + Equipped with the latest digital and analog recording technology
* Music Technology Labs
  + Access to the latest music software and hardware

**Community Spaces**

* The Couches
  + A place for students to relax and collaborate
* Conference Room
  + Available for student organizations and group projects

**Meet Our Faculty and Students**

* Faculty Interaction
  + Opportunity to meet our accomplished faculty members
* Student Ambassadors
  + Hear from current students about their experiences and life at Cal Poly Pomona
