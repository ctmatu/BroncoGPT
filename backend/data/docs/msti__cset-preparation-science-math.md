# CSET & CBEST Preparation for Science/Math

## CSET Math Subtests I & II Resources

San Jose State offers free online videos for the Math Subtests I & II in additional to other resources and testing strategies.

[MSTI - San Jose State](https://rfdr266.wixsite.com/matheducation "MSTI - San Jose State. ")

## CSET Courses/Workshops for Single Subject Mathematics and Science Teachers

California State University, Fresno sponsors free online courses/workshops which review subject matter content included on the CSET subtests for math and science to help prepare future teachers to meet subject matter competency.

[MSTI - Fresno State](https://fresnostate.edu/kremen/about/centers-projects/teachmathscience/index.html/documents/AMTE_2010_MSTI_Hsu.ppt "MSTI - Fresno State. ")

## CSET Courses/Workshops for Single Subject Mathematics and Science Teachers

California State University, Northridge is offering free CSET Preparation Workshops in Math-1, Math-2, Math-3, General Science, Physics-2, and Chemistry-2.

[MSTI - CSUN](https://sites.google.com/site/csunmsti/#TOC-GENERAL-SCIENCE---Subtest-I-CSET-215- "MSTI - CSUN. ")

## CSET - Preparation Resources

The University of California, Irvine offers free math and science online resources to assist prospective secondary math and science teachers to pass the qualifying exams.

[MSTI - UCI](https://open.uci.edu/collections/california_subject_examination_for_teachers__preparation_resources.html "MSTI - UCI. ")
