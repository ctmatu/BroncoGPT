# PI Requirements

## Qualifications for Principal Investigator Status

please see [CPP Policy 1206](https://www.cpp.edu/academic-manual/1200-1299-research-and-instructional-support/1200-1299/1206-minimum-qualification.pdf)

## Critical Deadlines

* Registering an online [Intent-to-Submit](intent-to-submit.shtml) with ORSP: AT LEAST 30 days prior to the sponsoring agency submission deadline.
  + \*\*Please note that if the submission is as a subaward, this 30-day requirement applies to the date for documents required by the Prime institution which normally precedes the Sponsor deadline.
* Submitting documents for approval routing: 2 weeks (10 business days) prior to the submission deadline.  Documents include:
  + final budget (including budgets for any subcontracts)
  + final budget justification
  + draft proposal abstract
  + statement of work (SOW)
* Final proposal documents: 1 week (5 business days) prior to submission deadline
