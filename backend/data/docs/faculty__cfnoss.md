## Content here

Clay Noss is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Environmental Science, Policy and Management at the University of California - Berkeley. He uses a combination of observational and experimental approaches to address fundamental questions in community ecology, with a specialty in desert ecology and herpetology.
