## South Korea - BUS

According to global research, conducted by the Korean Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism in 2022, entertainment content from Korea ranked 1st place among international content, including movies, TV series (K-Drama), and music (K-pop) through various media channels. The purpose of this faculty-led program in Korea is to investigate factors that contribute to the success of K-contents and help interested students explore career opportunities in the future. This faculty-led study abroad program will include visiting entertainment agencies, broadcasting studios, and concert venues (attend a concert if schedule permits), meeting and interviewing key personnel in the entertainment businesses, and experiencing various Korean culture, including: Korean food, landmarks of Seoul metropolitan area, and the life of Seoulite. Note: This faculty-led study abroad course will be accessible to all Cal Poly Pomona students since this class will be open to non-business majors.

**Program Dates: Summer 2026.**

Please contact Faculty Coordinator Professor Anthony Kim for more information: hongbumkim@cpp.edu
