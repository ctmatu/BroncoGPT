## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

MyLifeMatters by Empathia offers staff, faculty, and their households individual assistance and support services.

* [LifeMatters EAP Brochure (PDF)](../docs/lifematters-eap-brochure-ep.pdf)
* [LifeMatters EAP Employee Guide (PDF)](../docs/employee_guide_complete_pacific-customize.pdf)
* ­[LifeMatters Website](http://www.mylifematters.com)

  + Our company password is available for use for the services covered by Empathia. Please reach out to our department at benefits@cpp.edu for the company password.

Empathia is offering two new programs:

* [Digidoc (PDF)](../docs/lm_digidocpromo_csu.pdf): Access LifeMatters resources through your phone
* [OnTopic with Empathia (PDF)](../docs/ontopicpromo.pdf): Podcast about overcoming challenges in times of uncertainty. New episodes every other Wednesday.
