# SJAC to Host Hispanic Heritage Month Event

September 15, 2022

![SJAC](../img/sjac-22.png)Celebrate Hispanic Heritage Month and support Chicana/o/x and Latina/o/x higher education access by attending the Social Justice and Action Committee's (SJAC) student-led resource fair on Thursday, Sept. 29 from 11:30 a.m to 1 p.m. The event will feature free snacks, prizes and an opportunity to connect with Broncos Care, the Cultural Centers, RAMP and more. RSVP on [Eventbrite](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/cpp-equitable-access-resource-event-hispanic-heritage-month-tickets-420800063317%20).
