## About LTD Projects Hatchery

The Cal Poly Pomona Learn Through Discovery Program (LTD) Projects Hatchery promotes student success through undergraduate research, innovation, and community engagement. Visit the [LTD Projects Hatchery website](https://www.cpp.edu/hatchery/index.shtml) to learn more.
