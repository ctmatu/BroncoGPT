### About

If you believe you have a truly revolutionary idea and would like to take full advantage of all of the services and guidance that the iLab offers, apply to have your startup registered with the iLab.

Benefits of being a startup team with the iLab:

* Free advising/consulting from iLab staff: Prototyping, Graphic Design, Website Development, Marketing & Sales, Business Plan
* Access to 50+ professional mentors/advisors from different industries
* Reserve iLab for private meetings
* Priority use of iLab equipment
