# MEP Calendar of Events

![MEP Engineering Rocks 2015](/engineering/mep/img/calendar.jpg)

MEP offers several activities and events throughout the year. MEP offers twice weekly one-hour workshops, large "milestone" events once per quarter, and field trips via "Freshmen Fridays."

**Events and workshops are posted to the [MEP-WiSE Calendar](https://livecsupomona.sharepoint.com/%3Ax%3A/s/mep_program/EWej59GPwn1OluIV86L8oDgBNA1HK7AyThMU5YRcsRHyjw).**
