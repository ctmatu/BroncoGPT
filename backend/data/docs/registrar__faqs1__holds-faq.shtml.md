# Holds FAQs

## Holds

### What is the "Grad App Hold"?

A Graduation Application Hold is placed on your account after submitting a graduation application via BroncoDirect.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to registrar@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### What will the "Grad App Hold" prevent?

The Grad App Hold will prevent students from enrolling in future courses due to the fact that they will complete all requirements at the end of the semester. **Note:** The Registrar's Office will remove the Grad App Hold for undergrad students who will be continuing in a graduate program closer to their registration window.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to registrar@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### What is a Title IX hold and how do I remove it?

Each year you are emailed with a requirement to complete training for sexual misconduct. A Title IX hold is placed when you do not complete the annual training by the deadline shown in the email. The Title IX hold will prevent you from registering for classes.

The hold will be removed 24-48 hours after completing the training. For more information regarding the Title IX hold or removing it ASAP, please email officeofequity@cpp.edu and include your Bronco ID.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to registrar@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### What types of holds might prevent me from registering?

Some additional holds that may restrict you from enrolling in classes are: advising holds, financial holds, GWT holds, and more. Note: The department that has placed a hold on your account is the only department that may release the hold for you.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to registrar@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.
