# Preliminary Thesis Formatting Review

## **Get a Head Start on Your Thesis Formatting**

The Graduate Studies Office now offers **Preliminary Formatting Reviews** to help you prepare your thesis for official formatting approval.

This service is designed to give you early feedback and personalized guidance from our **Graduate Student Assistants,**who will walk you through the university’s formatting requirements and help you identify areas for improvement.

By participating in a preliminary review, you can ensure your thesis is on the right track — saving time and reducing revisions when you submit for your official format review.

## **What to Expect**

During your preliminary review, a Graduate Student Assistant will:

* Review your document for alignment with university formatting guidelines
* Provide feedback on layout, margins, page numbering, headings, and other key formatting elements
* Offer tips and resources to help you make necessary corrections before your official review

## **How to Schedule**

To schedule a Preliminary Formatting Review appointment, please **send an email to** **formatreview@cpp.edu**

In your email, include:

* Your full name
* Bronco ID number
* A PDF copy of your thesis
* Preferred appointment dates and times

Appointments are available on a first-come, first-served basis and must be scheduled in advance.

### **Hours of Availability – Fall 2025**

Preliminary formatting review appointments are available during the **Fall 2025 semester:**

* Mondays – 9:30 AM – 2 PM & 4:15 PM – 6:45 PM
* Tuesdays – 1:45 PM – 6:15 PM
* Wednesdays – 9:30 AM – 4:45 PM & 5:45 PM – 7:45 PM
* Thursdays – 9:15 AM – 6:15 PM
* Fridays – 1:15 PM – 4:45 PM

The **last day** to schedule a preliminary appointment is **December 5, 2025**.

After this date, the preliminary review service will no longer be offered.

## **After December 5, 2025**

Following the end of the preliminary review period, students should carefully review the **official thesis formatting guides** available on the [Graduate Studies website](formatting-guidelines.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/project-and-thesis/formatting-guidelines.shtml").

Once you have successfully formatted your thesis to meet all requirements, you may email **formatreview@cpp.edu** to request your **official formatting review and approval.**

## **Questions?**

For more information, please contact the Graduate Studies Office at **gradstudies@cpp.edu****.**
