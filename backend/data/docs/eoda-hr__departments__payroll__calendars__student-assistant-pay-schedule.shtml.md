## 2026 Student Assistant Pay Schedule

Student Assistant Pay Schedule

| Pay Period | Begin Date | End Date | Last Day Students Can Enter Time in PeopleSoft | **Last Day Departments Can Enter & Approve Time in PeopleSoft** | Positive Payday\* |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| December 2025 | December 02 | December 31 | January 05, 2026 | January 07, 2026 | January 15, 2025 |
| January 2026 | January 01 | January 29 | February 02 | February 06 | February 16 |
| February 2026 | January 30 | February 28 | February 28 | March 05 | March 16 |
| March 2026\*\* | March 01 | March 31 | April 05 | April 05 | April 15 |
| April 2026 | April 01 | April 30 | May 05 | May 05 | May 15 |
| May 2026 | May 01 | May 31 | June 05 | June 05 | June 15 |
| June 2026 | June 01 | June 30 | July 05 | July 05 | July 15 |
| July 2026 | July 01 | July 30 | August 04 | August 04 | August 14 |
| August 2026 | July 31 | August 31 | September 05 | September 05 | September 15 |
| September 2026 | September 01 | September 30 | October 05 | October 05 | October 15 |
| October 2026 | October 01 | October 31 | November 05 | November 05 | November 16 |
| November 2026 | November 01 | December 01 | December 06 | December 06 | December 15 |
| December 2026 | December 02 | December 31 | January 05, 2027 | January 05, 2027 | January 15, 2027 |

\*Paychecks are available after 9:00 a.m. in your department. Paychecks will be mailed to students working off-campus.

\*\*Effective with the March 2026 pay period, the last day to enter and approve time in PeopleSoft (CHRS) will be five (5) calendar days after the pay period ends, including weekends and holidays.
