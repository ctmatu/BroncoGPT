# About the Alumni Association

## Welcome to the Alumni Association!

Dear Fellow Bronco,

I am proud to serve as the President of Cal Poly Pomona’s Alumni Association.  We are dedicated to creating opportunities for engagement, networking, and for fostering the pride we have for our alma mater. Our initiatives include career readiness programs, community volunteer days, professional development opportunities, chapter events, and various other ways to connect with the Bronco family. The opportunities to get involved are numerous.

Your alumni membership signifies pride in our institution and advocacy for both current students and future alumni. Benefits of membership include invitations to exclusive events, support for student scholarships, networking opportunities, and access to unique resources and benefits.

We extend our gratitude for your part in Cal Poly Pomona’s distinguished history, enriched by its dynamic students, dedicated faculty, and remarkable alumni. As the campus continues to expand, students benefit from the learn-by-doing environment. We look forward to seeing you at an upcoming event, reading about your accomplishments on the We Are CPP site, or viewing your profile on LinkedIn.

Helene Galang

’96 Biology

President, Alumni Association
