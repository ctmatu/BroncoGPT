# Faculty Reserve Request Form

The following form is for use by faculty wishing to submit requests for Course Reserves in the Library.

Course Reserves requests will be processed as soon as possible depending on staff availability and resources. For materials to be ready for students to use the first day of class. The estimated turnaround time is **2-3 working days** to process the materials.

To submit personal copies, come to the Library Circulation/Course Reserves Desk, 2nd floor, Bldg. 15 with your material and completed form.

(\*These fields are required to process your request.)
