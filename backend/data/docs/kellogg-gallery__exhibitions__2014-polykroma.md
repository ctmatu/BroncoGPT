**Location:** Kellogg University Art Gallery

Polykroma](img/pk13.png) *Pol******y******Kr******om******a***   ***2014*****,** celebrates the Fine Arts, Art History & Graphic Design on campus with the  ***2014 Fine Art Senior Show*** , AND  ***Industry***   ***Night***  *-* the senior Graphic Design exhibition, presented concurrently at the **Kellogg University Art Gallery**. These exhibitions feature the "best of" Cal Poly Pomona's Fine Arts and Graphic Design Seniors. **Fine Art Seniors** are given the opportunity to exhibit a series of their artwork created during their university experience, while exposed to a gallery setting, and gaining professional art world practices in installing, exhibiting and pricing their artwork in a "real world" environment.  ***Industry Night*** is a special invitation to outside industry professionals to view graduating seniors' graphic design portfolios, projects and motion graphics presentations with exposure to potential job opportunities being offered. These Fine Arts & Graphic Design events celebrate the legacy of the arts on campus in conjunction with the **University's 75th Anniversary.**
