# McGoldrick Award Open for Nominations

March 3, 2022

![Mcgoldrick Award Image](../../img/news/mcgoldrick-award-image.png)

The McGoldrick Award for the College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences (CLASS) recognizes seniors who have faced significant challenges but excelled academically and have stayed involved in campus or community activities.

The McGoldrick Award is open to all CLASS seniors who plan to graduate in Spring 2022. Nominations can be made by faculty or student self-nominations by completing this form: [https://cpp.formstack.com/forms/mcgoldrick\_award](https://cpp.formstack.com/forms/mcgoldrick_award "McGoldrick Award Nomination Form")

* Nominations must provide a brief (no longer than 500 words) explanation of how the nominee has shown the strength of character and perseverance despite the challenge(s) they have overcome during their college experience.

 The deadline to submit nominations is March 31, 2022. Up to two awardees will be selected to receive a minimum of $750.00 each.
