# Faculty Research

Our Faculty are scholars with a commitment to advancing sociological research. In addition to scientific and scholarly publications, our faculty also engage in disseminating their research to a wider non-academic audience. Faculty members in the department carry out research projects in which they involve interested students, facilitating student growth and learning beyond the classroom. To learn more about the research areas of faculty in the department, check the cards below.
