# Parent & Family Engagement Program

The Maximizing Engineering Potential (MEP) Center for Gender, Diversity & Student Excellence is committed to helping you participate in your student’s experience at Cal Poly Pomona. We believe an informed and supportive family plays an integral role in a student’s educational success.

Knowing this, we want you to be informed and involved.

We are excited to share information about our partnership with [Peachjar](https://accounts.peachjar.com/login) to help us unite parents and families with MEP in our collective mission to elevate student outcomes! Peachjar’s platform streamlines school-to-home communication by distributing Cal Poly Pomona, College of Engineering and MEP announcements directly to families as digital flyers. By enabling MEP to go paperless, Peachjar helps us save thousands of trees each year.
