### **Parking**

Parking permits for [Parking Structure 2](https://maps.app.goo.gl/bQknH6xDUYgGMKg88) are provided for session dates. Daily parking passes can be purchased for $8 using [the campus pay app](https://www.cpp.edu/parking/permits/pay-stations.shtml)/stations. Please ensure that your parking permit is visible from your dashboard, as parking is strict, and the MDC cannot appeal citations.

### **MDC Location**

The MDC is located in the Kellogg Gym (building 43) in room 146. [See [Cam](https://www.cpp.edu/maps?id=1130#!m/276316?share)[pus Map](https://www.cpp.edu/maps?id=1130#!m/276316?share)].

It takes approximately 5 – 10 minutes to walk from Parking Structure 2 to the MDC.
