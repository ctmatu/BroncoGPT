# Femineer Summit April 7, 2017

April 7, 2017

![femineer summit april 7, 2017](../../img/femineer-summit-april-7,-2017.jpg)

Over 500 people attended this year's FemineerTM Summit on April 7, 2017. Hundreds of young FemineersTM from various schools came together to showcase their robotic creations and programming feats. These young ladies spent months learning to program and build their projects, and in our eyes, they did fantastic work. This is a definite demonstration of the future engineers we hope to see here one day! We want to thank all the volunteers who dedicated their time and effort towards making this a successful event, and for their encouragement and support of the FemineerTM program.
