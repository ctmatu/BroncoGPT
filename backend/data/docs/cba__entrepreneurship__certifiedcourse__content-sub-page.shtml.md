# Certified Course

## Executive Certificate in Accelerated Restaurant Growth and Nasdaq IPO

This executive program provides an intensive, three-day immersion into restaurant franchising, growth strategy, technology integration, private equity, and Nasdaq IPO readiness, combining academic rigor with real-world industry insight.

Participants learn directly from leading executives and faculty through case studies, guest lectures, and guided tours of Cal Poly Pomona’s restaurant and innovation ecosystem. The course equips rising leaders with the strategic tools and capital-market understanding needed to scale restaurant enterprises in today’s competitive global landscape.
