# ENV Emergency Assistance Program

![jumbo-eap.jpg](../img/jumbo-eap.jpg)

During this [unprecedented time](https://www.cpp.edu/safety/coronavirus/), the College of Environmental Design (ENV) is committed to the success of our students while keeping the safety and well-being of our community a top priority.

To address the unforeseen hardship that students face in their academic, professional and personal lives, the College has established the **ENV Emergency Assistance Program**, which awards deserving students a one-time $500 stipend. To qualify, they must be enrolled in a major within ENV during the academic year the application is filed and be able to demonstrate an urgent financial need with documentation.

A small committee comprised of ENV faculty and staff will review applications. All information will remain strictly confidential. (Note: Applicants will be asked to sign in via the CPP portal to securely access and submit the application.)

[Apply for the ENV Emergency Assistance Program](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtKELD74qR-ZAg1cJzw6R4XtUN0FIWjY3MENLQ0pEUVpCNkMwMFJVWjdaNSQlQCN0PWcu)

## Broncos Care

ENV is working together with    **[CPP Broncos Care](https://www.cpp.edu/basicneeds/about/give.shtml),** a program that provides critical services including emergency housing, hotel vouchers, meal cards, virtual care coordination, counseling and psychological services. Like the   **ENV Emergency Assistance Program**, Broncos Care Emergency grants have been established to help meet the rising needs of students facing unexpected challenges during this time.
