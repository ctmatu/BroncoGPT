## Resume Reviews

Talent Acquisition is dedicated to supporting the professional growth and internal mobility of our campus community. Whether you’re looking to advance in your current role or explore new opportunities within our campus, our team offers personalized and confidential resume reviews designed to help you stand out as a candidate in recruitments across campus.

**Why use our resume review service?**

Our team will provide tailored feedback to help you showcase your skills, align with the role you’re applying for, and position yourself as a top candidate for recruitments across campus. When you sign up, you get:

* Personalized and confidential 1:1 feedback on your resume
* Insights on showcasing achievements effectively
* Tips for standing out for campus-specific roles

**Who can access this service?**

This service is exclusively available to current employees (stateside or auxiliary), who are interested in pursing new staff position on campus (stateside).

If you are currently a CPP student, please visit the [CPP Career Center](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/) for resume support.
