# Mission Statement

## Mission Statement

The mission of the Cal Poly English Language Institute (CPELI) is to provide exemplary instruction for students with diverse cultural backgrounds and linguistic abilities in English with exposure to American culture that advances academic, professional, and personal goals.

## CEA Accreditation

Cal Poly English Language Institute (CPELI) is accredited by the Commission on English Language Program Accreditation for August 2020 through August 2029 and agrees to uphold the CEA Standards for English Language Programs and Institutions.
