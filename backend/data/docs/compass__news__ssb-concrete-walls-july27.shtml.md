# Concrete Walls Around SSB to Be Refurbished Starting on Monday, July 27

**Summary of disruption:** The exterior finish of the low concrete walls that surround the SSB (Building 121) will be replaced starting on Monday, July 27. Contractor crews will perform the work weekdays from 6 a.m. to 4 p.m. The work is scheduled to be completed by Wednesday, Aug. 5.

**Reason for disruption:** Allow contractor crews to replace the exterior finish on the walls.

**How does this affect me?**The work should not interfere with access to the SSB or pose disruptions to building occupants.

**What do I need to do?** The work areas will be cordoned off. Please share this information with others in the building who may have not received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:**Bruyn Bevans, Senior Project Manager, at Ext 5517 or at (951) 202-4036.
