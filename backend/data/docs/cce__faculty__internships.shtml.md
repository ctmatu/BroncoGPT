## Academic Internship Information for Faculty

---

### **OUR GOALS**

* Provide collaboration and operational support for faculty and students engaged in high-impact practices of for-credit, academic internships
* Guide sustainable existing and new university-community partnerships related to for-credit internships.

### **ACADEMIC INTERNSHIP POLICIES**

* Executive Order: Student Internships ([EO 1064](https://documentcloud.adobe.com/link/track?uri=urn:aaid:scds:US:626a9245-7180-4195-a7f8-dd494158cdac))
* S. Department of Labor - [Fact Sheet #71: Internship Programs Under The Fair Labor Standards Act](https://www.dol.gov/agencies/whd/fact-sheets/71-flsa-internships)
* [Cal Poly Pomona Academic Internship Policy (DOC)](https://www.cpp.edu/~senate/documents/packets/2016-17/04.26.17/AA-001-167_Report_First_Reading_Internship_Policy.docx)

### **FACULTY RESPONSIBILITIES**

---

* Develop syllabus, educate/guide students during internship.
* Assign a grade for the academic internship course.
* Collect the following Academic Internship forms from students:
  + [Academic Internship Learning Plan](https://app.calstates4.com/cpp/node/335540)
  + [Emergency form](https://app.calstates4.com/cpp/emergency-contact-information)
  + [Release of liability form](https://app.calstates4.com/cpp/node/335379)

* Submit the following form to CCE for review and approval:
  + [Request to Initiate Partnership](https://app.calstates4.com/cpp/node/470663) (New community partner ONLY)
  + [Academic Internship Learning Plan](https://app.calstates4.com/cpp/node/335540)

* If you required timesheet for your student, please [view downloadable timesheet](https://documentcloud.adobe.com/link/track?uri=urn:aaid:scds:US:7f6e8801-75f8-42b4-9186-ca5c36419aea).

For more information, see the [frequently asked questions](internships-facultyfaqs.shtml) for faculty.

---

For additional information and/or questions about the Academic Internship Program, please contact the Engaged Learning Coordinator at 909.869.4269.
