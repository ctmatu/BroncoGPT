## Facilities, Pictures & Maps

### Building 2

The department is based in the Huntley College of Agriculture in Building 2. Tenure-track faculty and the Student Success Center have their offices here.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276180?share  "opens a new window")

### Building 94 (University Office Building)

Lecturers have offices in Building 94 on the northeast corner of the University Quad, along South University Drive.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276180?share  "opens a new window")

### Cattle Unit

Our livestock at the Cattle Unit includes a breeding herd of Angus and Angus Crossbred cattle.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276194?share  "opens a new window")

[![A hog at the Cal Poly Pomona swine unit.](../img/2019-swine-unit-920x702.jpg)

### Swine Unit

We have various commercial breeds of swine in our animal units.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276194?share  "opens a new window")

[![Sheep eat hay from a feeder at the Cal Poly Pomona sheep unit.](../img/2023-sheep-unit-feeders-920x702.jpg)

### Sheep Unit

Our sheep unit includes goats and flocks of purebred Rambouillet and Suffolk sheep.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276485?share  "opens a new window")

[![Photo of the Building 67 exterior](../img/2018-building-67-920x702.jpg)

### Building 67

Building 67 is home to our animal health science program, which trains students to become veterinary technicians - the equivalent of nurses in veterinary medicine.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276535?share  "opens a new window")

[![Four female students pose with a horse at the W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center.](../img/2023-horse-center-920x702.jpg)

### W. K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center (Building 29)

Learn about equine science or watch a Sunday Horse Show here!](/maps?id=1130#!m/276485?share  "opens a new window")

[![Students uses a tractor to plant crops at Cal Poly Pomona's Spadra Farm.](../img/2024-spadra-farm-920x702.jpg)

### Spadra Farm

For more than 60 years, Cal Poly Pomona students have grown crops at Spadra Farm. The farm spans more than 150 acres, including a 10-acre section for organic crops. Produce from the farm is sold in the Cal Poly Pomona Farm Store.](/maps?id=1130#!m/299991?share  "opens a new window")

![A male student harvests grapes from the Huntley Vineyard at Cal Poly Pomona.](../img/2018-vineyard-920x702.jpg)

### The Huntley Vineyard

The largest of three grape-growing areas on campus is at the corner of Kellogg and University Drive. It was made possible through a donation by Don B. Huntley ('60, animal husbandry). Grapes from the vineyard are used to make Horsehill Vineyards wine, which is sold in the Farm Store.

[![A student tends to the Microfarm.](../img/2022-micro-farm-920x702.jpg)

### Horsehill Microfarm

Adjacent to the Restaurant at Kellogg Ranch, the Microfarm allows students to learn small-scale farming techniques.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276540?share  "opens a new window")

![A student works with a plant in the Cal Poly Pomona nursery](../img/2023-nursery-920x702.jpg)

### Nursery Greenhouse

The greenhouse is part of the AGRIscapes complex, adjacent to the Farm Store. The greenhouse grows plants to sell at the Farm Store, and also serves as a lab for classes. It includes hydroponic and aquaponic growing systems.

[![A female employee sets out kale at the Cal Poly Pomona Farm Store](../img/2024-farm-store-kale-920x702.jpg)

### Cal Poly Pomona Farm Store

Since 2001, the Farm Store has sold produce grown on Spadra Farm, including freshly-squeezed orange juice. It also sells Horsehill Vineyards wine, and CPP Farms ice cream and salsa.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276449?share  "opens a new window")
