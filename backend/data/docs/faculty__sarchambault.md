## About Me

Steven Archambault is an associate professor in the Department of Agribusiness and Food Industry Management at the Huntley College of Agriculture. He teaches at RS 5250 (Foundations in Regeneration) at the Lyle Center.

His areas of research are in the food, agriculture, and energy sectors, with an emphasis on analyzing and managing large data sets.

Archambault received his doctorate in economics from the University of New Mexico, where he taught as an assistant professor from 2012-2016. He graduated with his master's degree in environmental management and policy from Lunds Universitet, and completed his undergraduate studies in biology/biological sciences at UNM. He was also the World Food Programme's 2003-04 Hunger Center Fellow.
