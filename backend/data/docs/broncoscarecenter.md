## About the Care Center

The Care Center is a welcoming first stop for students of concern and any student seeking support. We connect students with basic needs resources, wellness services, and advocacy to help them overcome challenges and stay on track toward their personal and academic goals.

[Refer a Student of Concern](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=7)

**The Bronco Wellness Center is Now at the Care Center**

Services include one-on-one coaching sessions with peer health educators, toolkits, and other programs and services such as Narcan and Fentanyl test strip distribution.

## Our Location

The Care Center is located on the first floor of the Campus Center Marketplace (Bldg 97), next to the Career Center. Stop by anytime Monday through Friday from 8:00 am - 5:00 pm.
