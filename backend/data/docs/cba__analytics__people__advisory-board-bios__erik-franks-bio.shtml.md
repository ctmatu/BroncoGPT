# Erik Franks

John Burns Real Estate Consulting - Senior Vice President, Information Management

Erik manages the integrity of data for retainer and custom consulting clients. His dedication to
delivering accurate and timely data through customized reports helps clients understand the
current state of the housing industry and the economy, both nationally, and in their specific
markets.

Before joining John Burns Real Estate Consulting in 2006, Erik served as a partner in a Northern
California branch of a mortgage lender and as a branch manager for a residential home
improvement company.

Erik holds a B.A. in Business and Communications from the University of California, Davis, and is
based in the Sacramento office.
He enjoys traveling, photography, and competes in various distance triathlons, including the
2011 Ironman Switzerland.
