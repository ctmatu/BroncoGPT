# Restrooms on Second Through Fourth Floors of Library Reopened

**Update on disruption:** Restrooms on the second through fourth floors in the south side of the University Library (Building 15) have reopened. Restrooms on the first floor will be back in service after work is completed.

**Reason for disruption:** Allow campus crews to clear a sewer blockage and restore restroom service.

**What do I need to do?** Use restrooms on the second through fourth floors of the library. Restrooms in nearby buildings such as the Bronco Student Center and University Plaza also are available. Please share this information with others in the building who may not have received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:** Facilities Customer Service at Ext. 3030.
