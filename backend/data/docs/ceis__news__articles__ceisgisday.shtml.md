# GIS in Education: A Tool to Increase Social Justice Advocacy and Action

November 17, 2023

![gis24ceis](../../img/gis24ceis.jpg)

Cal Poly Pomona and Mt. San Antonio College will be partnering in Geographic Information System (GIS) Day: Bridges to the Future.  This day will bring together students, faculty, staff, local schools, communities, organizations, and public and private industries to celebrate the amazing work happening in GIS.
