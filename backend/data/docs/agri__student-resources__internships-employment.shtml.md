# Student Internships & Employment Resources

## Internship Info

**![A U.S. Forest Service rep speaks to students at a career fair.](../img/2019-ag-career-day-usfs-920x570.jpg)**

**Information for Students**

Students can gain valuable experience and knowledge working in an internship.

Current Cal Poly Pomona students can search for internship opportunities at **Handshake**, the university **[Career Center](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/)**'s job and internship posting board. You must register and create an account there.

[Sign into Handshake](https://cpp.joinhandshake.com/login)

You can also check out information about internships with the **USDA's Natural Resources Conservation Service** (NRCS):

[NRCS Internships](/agri/student-resources/nrcs-internships.shtml)

**Information for Employers**

Employers can post internships and job opportunities on Handshake. You also must register and create an account there.

[Sign into Handshake](https://cpp.joinhandshake.com/login)

[Handshake Reference Guide for Employers (PDF)](/agri/docs/2024-handshake-employers-guide-accessible.pdf)

## Student Employment

**Information for Students**

Students looking for job opportunities should check Handshake, the primary career services platform at Cal Poly Pomona.

[Sign into Handshake](https://cpp.joinhandshake.com/login)

Many of our learn-by-doing majors hire students to assist technicians in laboratories, faculty members with teaching and/or research activities, and department staff with department office business.

Students **can inquire at their major's departmental office** to see if their program hires student assistants and what types they hire.

**[AGRIscapes](https://www.cpp.edu/agriscapes/index.shtml)** also hires students to work seasonally for the [**annual Pumpkin Festival**](https://www.cpp.edu/pumpkinfestival/index.shtml), and fall and spring field trips. Jobs are posted on the Cal Poly Pomona Enterprises (formerly known as the Cal Poly Pomona Foundation Inc.) website.

[Employment Opportunities at Cal Poly Pomona Enterprises](https://secure6.saashr.com/ta/6041387.careers?CareersSearch=&lang=EN)

The Associated Students Inc. (ASI) also employs students.

[Employment Opportunities at ASI](https://asi.cpp.edu/about-us/the-corporation/employment/)

In addition, many administrative offices on campus hire students in a variety of capacities. Students have succeeded in balancing work on campus with their classes and found academic success. All these positions are advertised through the **[Cal Poly Pomona Career Center](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/),**but can only be seen by current students.

[Sign up for a Career Counseling appointment at CPP Connect](/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml)

**Information for Alumni**

Alumni can access Handshake for one year after their graduation. They can register for Career Center support through the [Alumni Services Program](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/alumni/).

**Information for Employers**

Employers can:

* [participate in on-campus career fairs](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/career-fairs/)
* [conduct on-campus recruitment](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/recruit-a-bronco/)
* [become a partner with the Career Center](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/corporate-partners-program/)
* [post job and internship opportunities on Handshake](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/post-a-job/), the **Cal Poly Pomona Career Center**'s job board.
* [refer to the Handshake Reference Guide for Employers (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/agri/docs/2024-handshake-employers-guide-accessible.pdf)
