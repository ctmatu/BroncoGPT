# Hallie Huang

## Hallie Huang

## **Web Analyst & Text Mining Specialist**

## **Research Assistant**

**Major:** Computer Information Systems with an emphasis in Business Intelligence

**Interest:** Data Analytics, Data Science, Social Media, Digital Marketing

**Skills:** Java, SQL, Adobe Photoshop, Canva, Microsoft Suite

**Short Bio:**Hallie is a 3rd year Computer Information Systems student with an emphasis in Business Intelligence. During Spring 2021, she joined the Center of Customer Insights and Digital Marketing as a Research Assistant and also served as the Director of Membership and Recruitment for the Analytics and Marketing Insights club. She competed in the RoamEV team for the GfK NextGen Data Science Hackathon competition 2021, placing as a finalist. In Summer 2021, she started working as the Web Analytics and Text Mining Specialist. Along with Jeffrey Hsu and Emily Harrison, she is assisting the “COVID-19 Hospitality” team which aims to research the impact of culture on hygiene protocols. She is expected to graduate in Spring 2023.
