# Special Event Insurance

## Insurance for Events

For events, other than club events with only club members in attendance, public liability insurance from a carrier with a rating of **"A" or better**and a financial rating of **"7" or better**by AM Best in an amount not less than $1,000,000 for injuries, including accidental death, to any one person, subject to the same minimum for each person, in an amount not less than $1,000,000 for each occurrence, $2,000,000 aggregate and property damage insurance in an amount not less than $500,000.

Lessee shall furnish the University a certificate and endorsement of insurance by the due date listed on the lease. Certificates of insurance are acceptable as long as they are followed by an endorsement no less than a minimum of 14 days prior to the event.

## Certificate and Endorsement of Insurance

The certificate and endorsement will include the following as additional insured: **"The State of California, the Trustees of the California State University, the University and the employees, officers, and agents of each of them are included as additional insured."**For information on Special Event Insurance, contact the Strategic Enterprise Risk Management at (909) 869-2495.
