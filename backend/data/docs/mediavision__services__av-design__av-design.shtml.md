# AV Design

## What We Do

AV Design is a full-service consulting service to help you create technology installations in any campus space to meet your and our student, faculty, and staff's needs. We work with you through review, design, approval, implementation, and coordination with our various campus partners in creating technology-enhanced collaborative spaces. Collaborative spaces include classrooms, labs, conference rooms, group study rooms, and open areas. MediaVision's AV Design group also assists with the implementation of digital signage, and a myriad of other deployments facilitating the university's vision.

## Ready for a consultation?

Once you have all the required information the process for submitting a project request can be done through ServiceNow.

Please be prepared to have authorization from the approving official: (VP/AVP, Academic resources, Department Head) to request services.

Should you need assistance with completing the request form email mvdesign@cpp.edu
