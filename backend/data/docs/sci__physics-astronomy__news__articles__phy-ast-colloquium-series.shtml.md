# Physics and Astronomy Colloquium

September 25, 2025

The Physics and Astronomy Department continue their Research speaker series, now renamed as Colloquium. The department invites everyone to join our speakers for fascinating topics. Please come and enjoy light refreshments as well! Schedule is posted around the department and below for the Fall 2025 semester. Light refreshments are served at 10:45am and speakers begin at 11:00am. If you have any questions, please let us know.
