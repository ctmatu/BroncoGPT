## About Me

Lydia Chen Shah is a leader in strategy, sustainability, brand/product management, integrated marketing communications, and organizational culture. As a professor at Cal Poly Pomona in the International Business and Marketing department, she has collaborated with numerous community and industry partners where students gain real-world polytechnic experience. Deeply committed to education of the whole person and service learning, Lydia was selected by CPP’s Center for Community Engagement as a Faculty Fellow in 2015 and by Campus Compact as an Engaged Scholar in 2019.

As a consultant, Lydia has worked with clients in social impact, education, lifestyle, and technology sectors. Her professional experience spans from startups to Fortune 100 companies. She also serves on the executive committee for Cal Poly Pomona’s Alumni Association board of directors.

With a passion for equity-driven education, Lydia focuses on providing students the best possible experiences in and outside of the classroom. This includes real-world application of knowledge and developing relevant skills desired by employers. She is a leader in strategy, sustainability, brand/product management, integrated marketing communications, and organizational culture. Lydia's industry experience ranging from startups to Fortune companies plus community-based organizations turns diverse perspectives into actionable plans. She fosters excellence in others through a culture of collaboration. Lydia also serves on Cal Poly Pomona’s Alumni Association board of directors and as a Faculty Associate for CPP's Career Center.
