## Exceptional Spaces to Learn, Rehearse, and Create

The Cal Poly Pomona Music Department boasts modern facilities and resources designed to support and enhance your educational experience.
Our facilities provide hands-on opportunities necessary to excel in your musical studies and prepare you for professional careers.

### Performance Spaces: Where Your Music Comes to Life

**Recital Hall**
An intimate venue with high-quality acoustics and seating, ideal for solo and chamber performances.

**University Theatre**
A larger venue designed for orchestral, band, and choral performances, featuring advanced lighting and sound systems.

### Practice Rooms: Perfect Your Skills in Our Soundproof Spaces

**Individual Practice Rooms**
Equipped with pianos and soundproofing, these rooms offer a quiet space to practice and perfect your skills.

**Ensemble Rehearsal Rooms**
Spacious rooms designed to accommodate various ensembles, from small chamber groups to large orchestras and bands.

### Recording Studios: Hands-On Experience with Cutting Edge Technology

**Modern Recording Facilities**
Featuring the latest in digital and analog recording technology, our studios provide hands-on experience in music production and recording techniques.

**Editing Suites**
Equipped with professional software and hardware for post-production work.

### Technology and Labs: Innovate with the Latest Music Software and Hardware

**Music Technology Lab**
Access the latest music software and hardware, allowing you to learn and experiment with music composition, production, and sound design.

**Computer Labs**
Equipped with software for music notation, composition, and analysis.

### Specialized Resources: Instruments, Library, and Archives at Your Fingertips

**Instrument Collection**
A wide variety of instruments available for student use, including classical, contemporary, and ethnic instruments.

**Library and Archives**
An extensive collection of music scores, recording, and research materials available for student and faculty use.

**Pro Tools HD Lab**
High-definition digital audio workstations for advanced recording and mixing projects.

### Community Spaces: Collaborate and Connect with Your Peers

**The Couches**
A comfortable space for you to relax, collaborate, and engage with peers outside of class and rehearsals.

**Meeting and Conference Rooms**
Available for student organizations, faculty members, and collaborative projects.

## Need A Space for Your Event?

### Rent one of ours

[OFF CAMPUS USERS] Start by connecting with the License of Facilities Office. Our coordinators are here to guide you in finding the right space to match your event’s needs. A facilities use agreement and proof of liability insurance that meets university standards are required. Once you share the details of your event, we’ll prepare a cost estimate to help you plan with confidence.
[ON CAMPUS USERS] Email Dalia Puentes at dpuentes@cpp.edu and she will prepare a cost estimate and help you plan with confidence.
