# Donate to the Department

![Donate Clip Art](/class/economics/img/donate.png)

You can support our department, its research, and our students by making a one time or a recurrent donation. We have two options:

* **[Economics Excelence](https://cpp.thankyou4caring.org/donation-form2?fid=duamxiFb%2fe4%3d&fdesc=10Cy3TNXSdiuaPoyBAbfXTIydpJ7cIsZo3hb7gEm8Jw%3d)** to support the departments efforts in research and student support.
* **[Economics Scholarship](https://cpp.thankyou4caring.org/donation-form2?fid=fjun0%2bf9pj0%3d&fdesc=10Cy3TNXSdiuaPoyBAbfXVJcm%2frmGrL%2f5lfn9as34nXkwIdj%2fDoHxQ%3d%3d "Donate now to Economics Scholarship")** to provide sholarships to individual student.

Even a small donations helps. If you like to discuss donations with our Department Chair please feel free to contact us anytime (bbrown@cpp.edu).
