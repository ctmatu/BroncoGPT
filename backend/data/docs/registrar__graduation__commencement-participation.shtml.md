## Graduating Applicants:

Cal Poly Pomona’s Commencement ceremonies will include participants from the following terms:

**Fall 2025 graduates**

**Spring 2026 graduation applicants**

**Summer 2026 graduation applicants**

To be eligible to participate in the 2026 Commencement ceremonies, both Spring 2026 and Summer 2026 students need to **apply for graduation by March 13, 2026.**Please refer to our [Graduation Application Timeline](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/graduation/applying-for-graduation.shtml).

**Please note:**  There is no separate application to participate in the Commencement ceremonies.  Students who apply for graduation for Spring 2026 and Summer 2026 by the **deadline of March 13, 2026**, will automatically be included.

For information about the Commencement Event, visit the Commencement website at [Commencement 2026](https://www.cpp.edu/commencement/).
