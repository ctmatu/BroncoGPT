# College of Science Commencement

## 2026

![various shots of students from the 2025 commencement ceremonies](../img-2026/pics-for-26.jpg)

The 2026 College of Science Commencement ceremonies are scheduled for **Saturday, May 16 at 4:00 and 7:30 p.m.** Please check the university's [Commencement page](https://www.cpp.edu/commencement/) for updates.

**4:00 p.m., College of Science #1**

* **Computer Science**
* **Geological Sciences**
* **Mathematics & Statistics**
* **Physics & Astronomy**

**7:30 p.m., College of Science #2**

* **Biological Sciences**
* **Chemistry & Biochemistry**
* **Kinesiology & Health Promotion**

Grad Fair is an opportunity for grads to try on gowns, get honor cords, and take advantage of special offers. [Visit the Bookstore's Grad Fair web page](https://www.broncobookstore.com/gradcenter.asp) for more information.

For Faculty regalia [Visit the Bookstore's faculty regalia web page.](https://www.jostens.com/apps/store/productBrowse/1006222/Cal-Poly-Pomona/Graduation-Caps-and-Gowns/col_greg_1006222/CATALOG_COLLEGE?rcrdu=395&jrid=1769733334586)

Faculty planning to attend need to RSVP online to receive a ticket. Faculty tickets are required and each ceremony has its own ticket so if you are attending both ceremonies you will need a ticket for each.

[**FACULTY RSVP**](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtKTxV2vk_WBMuMOA7aquhllUNjJIM0ROVTkxOEJaOTY5NDhZS0NOMklZVi4u)
