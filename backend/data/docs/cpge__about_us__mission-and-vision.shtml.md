## OUR MISSION

### Extending Opportunity Through Education and Innovation

The College of Professional and Global Education at Cal Poly Pomona provides accessible, real-world learning opportunities that enable individuals to grow, lead, and thrive. We partner with academic colleges, industries, and communities to deliver flexible programs that meet today’s workforce needs and tomorrow’s global challenges. Whether you're advancing your career, completing a degree, or exploring something new, CPGE is here to support your journey, locally, nationally, and around the world. Rooted in Cal Poly Pomona’s polytechnic spirit, we’re committed to innovation, collaboration, and helping every learner become by doing.

## OUR VISION

### Shaping What’s Next in Global Education

To be a global leader in flexible, career-focused education that empowers learners at every stage of life to grow, lead, and thrive. We envision a future where innovation, accessibility, and hands-on learning open new doors for working adults, returning students, and international communities wherever they are in the world.
