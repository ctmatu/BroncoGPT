## Admissions Information

*Students in high school, with or without college credit; or high school graduates who have not attended college are considered first time freshmen.*

Cal Poly Pomona is #2 in the west for public universities and #1 in the nation for economic mobility amongst polytechnic universities.  Our caring and inclusive campus community is committed to helping anyone who dreams of success achieve it. At CPP, you can explore different career paths, apply your knowledge to real-world situations, and gain the experience you need to succeed and stand out in your field from day one.
