# Study abroad opportunities

The IBM department supports several study abroad opportunities.

## Traditional study abroad

CPP currently offers over 100 study abroad programs to over 60 countries all around the world. Programs are academically  focused with credit that counts toward  graduation and your degree. Program descriptions can be found throughout each individual program type.

Click here for [more information](https://www.cpp.edu/international/study-abroad/index.shtml).

## Virtual study abroad

For students who want an international experience but are not able to commit to a traditional study abroad program, virtual study abroad are online learning experiences allow them to interact with international instructors and students, explore global issues, and collaborate on unique projects.

Click here for more information.
