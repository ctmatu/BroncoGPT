# Donations

## Donations can be made to the Motor Development Clinic as follows:

To make a donation online:

1. Please click:
   [Donate](https://cpp.thankyou4caring.org/donate)
2. Please click "others" under Designation and specify "Motor Development Clinic“

For assistance or information on how to support the Motor Development Clinic, please contact Bill Burrows, Director of Development at (909) 869-4160 or by e-mail at **bdburrows@cpp.edu**.

### Specific Equipment needs:

Adapted or Regular

* Scooters
* Rollerblades
* Skateboards
* Bikes

Additional Needs

* Helmets (youth or adult)
* Protective gear (knee pads, elbow pads, wrist guards)
* Adapted Sport gear
* Wheelchairs
* Standards/Gait Trainers
* Walkers
