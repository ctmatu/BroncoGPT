# Alumni & External Relations

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Who can join the Alumni Association?

Alumni, parents, students, and friends can all [join the Alumni Association](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/membership/) and receive the benefits of being a member. Non-alumni members will be entered into the system as associates.

### What are the benefits of joining the Alumni Association?

For a full list of benefits please visit our member benefits website.

### I am not receiving email updates about alumni events. How do I make sure I receive these?

Please update your information to receive information about alumni and university events.

### How can I get involved with my alma mater?

There are several ways alumni can give back and get involved with Cal Poly Pomona! Visit the Get Involved webpage to learn more about upcoming programs and events. Please also fill out the volunteer form so that we can contact you when new events become available.

### How can I keep my CPP email after I graduate?

At this time the university does not allow students to retain their email addresses. Your email will expire soon after your last semester enrolled in classes. We do not offer an alumni email at this time.

### How do I request transcripts?

Visit the [Registrar’s office website](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/student-records/transcripts.shtml) to learn more about requesting transcripts.
