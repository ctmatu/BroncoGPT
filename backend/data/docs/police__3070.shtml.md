# Emergency on Campus - Call (909) 869-3070

![Emergency on Campus banner](img/2023/emergency_on_campus_webbanner.png)

![Police Front Desk](img/2023/police-dept-front-desk4.jpg)

## Why (909) 869-3070?

When you dial 9-1-1 from a cell phone on campus, it gets routed to the California Highway Patrol (CHP), possibly delaying service. Calling (909) 869-3070 from a cell phone routes the call to CPP police immediately.

***Note for faculty/staff: Dialing 9-1-1 from a landline campus phone will also route directly to CPP Police and avoid delays.***

**Be Proactive - Save the 3070 Number as a Contact on your Cell Phone**

**Step One: Open the 'Contacts' app on your phone**

**Step Two: Create a new contact named 'CPP Emergency'**

**Step Three: Save 909-869-3070 as the phone number**

**Step Four: 'Favorite' the contact so it's readily accessible**

**Doing this now will help in case of an on-campus emergency.**

## For Faculty: Language to Include in your Syllabus

**To-Do:** Calling 9-1-1 from a cell phone on campus could delay service. Instead, call CPP Police directly at (909) 869-3070. Take out your cell phone now and create a new contact named ‘CPP Emergency’ with the (909) 869-3070 number. Favorite it for easy access so you’re prepared if you see an emergency on campus. To learn more, please visit <https://www.cpp.edu/police/3070.shtml>
