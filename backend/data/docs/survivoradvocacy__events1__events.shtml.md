## Stay Up To Date

Survivor Advocacy Services wants to work with clubs, organizations, and groups to help spread awareness educate members of the CPP community to create a culture of respect on campus. This starts with learning about important topics like consent, healthy and unhealthy relationships, plus how to recognize and prevent harm before it happens. When we take the time to educate ourselves and support one another, we help build a safer, more caring campus community for everyone.

* SAS offers free workshops on a range of topics related to preventing harm and promoting healthy relationships. CPP students, faculty, and staff are welcome to request a workshop or presentation.

Interested? Email us at survivoradvocacy@cpp.edu.
