# MEP Organizations

MEP supports four cultural affinity student organizations:

* [American Indian Science and Engineering Society (AISES)](https://calpolypomona.collegiatelink.net/organization/aises) | [FaceBook](https://www.facebook.com/CPPAISES "AISES on Facebook")
* [National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE)](https://calpolypomona.collegiatelink.net/organization/nsbe)
* [Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE)](https://calpolypomona.collegiatelink.net/organization/shpe): | [FaceBook](https://www.facebook.com/pages/The-Society-of-Hispanic-Professional-Engineers-SHPE/150049398370158 "SHPE on Facebook")
* [Society of Women Engineers (SWE)](http://www.cpp.edu/~swe/) | [FaceBook](https://www.facebook.com/CPPSWE) | [Twitter](https://twitter.com/swecpp) | [Instagram](https://instagram.com/swecpp)
