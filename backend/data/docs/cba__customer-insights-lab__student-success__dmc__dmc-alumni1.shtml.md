The *Center for Customer Insights and Digital Marketing* initiates the ***Digital Marketing Consultancy (DMC)*** Program as part of its signature Polytechnic [Student Success Programs](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/student-success/index.shtml). Students who have taken our client consulting classes have been hired by world-class market research companies.
