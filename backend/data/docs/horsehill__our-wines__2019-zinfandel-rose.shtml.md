# 2019 Zinfandel Rosé

![Horsehill Vineyards](https://www.cpp.edu/horsehill/img/horsehill-2019-rose-banner.jpg)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/horsehill/img/2019-rose-bottle.png)Zinfandel is a versatile and wonderful varietal not just for the red wine, but rosé as well. These grapes were early season picked and lightly pressed to extract the delicate berry aromas and flavors, while getting just enough skin color to give a faint pink hue to the wine. Fruity and crisp, with all the flavors of summer, this rosé is a great way to end the day.
