# Student Research Supervised by Faculty

For all students interested in enrolling in an independent study course or senior project (PHY/AST 2000, 4000, 4160 or 4162):

1. Consult with your faculty supervisor about your project interests.
2. Once you have an agreement to engage in a research project, fill out the [Supervised Research Agreement Form](supervisory-agreement-form.pdf "Supervised Research Agreement Form"). *Both you and your faculty supervisor need to sign this form.*
3. Submit the signed form to the [Physics and Astronomy Department office](../contact.shtml) (emailed form with electronic signatures is fine).
