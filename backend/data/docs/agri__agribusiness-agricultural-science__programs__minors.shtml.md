# Minors

## Grow by Exploring

![A student worker talks with customers about avocados at a farmers market. ](../img/2010-farmers-market-1000x600.jpg)

There are more fields than you can imagine in agriculture. It’s a huge global industry, accounting for more than $50 billion in revenue in California alone, according to the state Department of Food & Agriculture.

If you're interested in knowing more about the business side of agriculture, you can take our **agribusiness and food industry management** minor, or the **international agricultural business management** minor.

In these minors, you can combine your animal-raising and crop-growing expertise with the skills to run a business in this industry. You’ll learn about accounting, marketing, retailing, wholesaling, personnel management, and regulations – maybe even make an ag-related career in these fields.

Find out more about the courses related to these minors in the University Catalog:

[Agribusiness & Food Industry Management](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19585&returnto=5890)

[International Agricultural Business Management](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19587&returnto=5890)
