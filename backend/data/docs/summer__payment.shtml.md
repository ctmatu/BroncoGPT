# Payment and Refunds

## Current Cal Poly Pomona Students\*

Current CPP Students will be entitled to a full refund of course fees and educational support fees ONLY if they cancel their registration or drop ALL classes PRIOR to the first day of instruction for the given session. Refund requests on or after the first day of instruction will be pro-rata.

**Other Fees:** $25 late registration fee
                      $35 administrative fee for the installment payment plan

Complete information regarding campus fees, payment, and refund policies can be found at [Student Accounting & Cashiering Services.](https://www.cpp.edu/student-accounting/)

\*Cal Poly Pomona campus fee and refund policies apply to those registering through BroncoDirect (e.g., current Cal Poly Pomona students).

## College of Professional and Global Education/Visiting Students/Entering Cal Poly Pomona Students\*

Students registering via the College of Professional and Global Education will be entitled to a full refund if student cancels PRIOR to the start date for the given session.

**Other Fees:** $25 late fee for payments made after the due date
                      $35 administrative fee for the installation payment plan

Complete information regarding Open University payment and refund policies can be found at the [College of Professional and Global Education.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/)

\*Open University fee and refund policies apply to those registering through the College of Professional and Global Education (e.g., visiting students, entering Cal Poly Pomona students, and former Cal Poly Pomona students).
