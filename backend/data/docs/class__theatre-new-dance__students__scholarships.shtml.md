### Fund Your Theatre Education

There are many opportunities for scholarships! You can use the keyword search field to search for “Theatre” or “Dance” and that will narrow it down to the three Department-related scholarships. However, there may be many other opportunities for which you are eligible, so be sure to check out the whole offering! If you think you might fit the description, APPLY.

[Check out all opportunities HERE.](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/users/sign_in%20)

## Past and Present Scholarships

* **Morse Family Scholarship:** In honor of former chair, William Morse II, awarded to a student who has excelled in design or technical theatre.
* **Terry Gullo Scholarship:** In honor of former Costume Shop Supervisor, Terry Gullo, for an exceptional student in Costume Design.
* **Sharon Endowment for Community Arts Scholarship:** In honor of Sharon and Megan Daseler, for a student who is actively engaged in creative work in the Pomona community.

*Additional excellence awards are offered determined on a yearly basis, along with awards to support students’ scholarship, research and creative activities.*
