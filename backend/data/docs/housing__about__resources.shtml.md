### Protecting Your Personal Property (Renters Insurance)

As noted in the Student Housing License Agreement, Cal Poly Pomona assumes no responsibility and provides no insurance or financial protection for your personal property.  We strongly recommend that you get renters insurance coverage.  As an on-campus UHS resident, you’re eligible to purchase coverage through GradGuard’s student renters insurance program.  CPP does not receive compensation for providing this student benefit.  GradGuard’s Renters Insurance is underwritten by Marked American Insurance Company.

Claims and coverage are subject to language, limits and exclusions, and policy.  Visit [GradGuard.com/renters](https://gradguard.com/renters/cpp) to get more information about their coverage and a quote.

### Off Campus Housing Information

University Housing Services (UHS) at Cal Poly Pomona does not provide or manage off-campus housing options. However, there are various resources available to assist in your search.

Online listing platforms such as Zillow, Apartments.com, and other rental websites allow you to explore available housing options in the area. These sites offer search filters to help narrow results based on location, price, number of bedrooms, and other preferences.

To search for housing near campus, use **"Cal Poly Pomona"** or the university’s address:
 **3801 West Temple Ave., Pomona, CA 91768**.

Students are strongly encouraged to conduct thorough research, verify references, and review feedback from previous tenants when considering off-campus housing. Cal Poly Pomona does not manage, oversee, or vet listings on non-affiliated websites, social media platforms, or any local landlords or property management companies.
