# Academic Support

## Reach Out for Support

Achieving your graduation goal will include challenges that will help you develop your critical thinking and problem-solving skills. These challenges can be best met with the support of faculty, advisors and learning support staff who are committed to your academic and personal success.

You may face a number of challenging situations, including building your class schedule and academic plan, passing a difficult course, or trying to move your grade from a B to an A.

Cal Poly Pomona offers a comprehensive [Student Success Central](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/)page that provides online support and points you to other help resources such as the [Bronco Advising Center](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/bronco-advising-center/index.shtml). Information about additional academic support options is available at [Academic & Support Resources](http://www.cpp.edu/~academics/academic-resources/index.shtml).

Reach out for academic help early and often.
