# Leaderboard

The STARS faculty mentors listed below have been awarded a tier based on the number of students they have mentored through the Research Distinction as of August 2024. If you are a student interested in getting involved in research at Cal Poly Pomona, these faculty can provide you with more information about how to get involved.

To read about featured STARS mentors, [visit the STARS profiles page](../../about/profiles/stars-profiles.shtml) to learn more!
