## Spring 2026 Open University Calendar

This calendar outlines key dates and deadlines for students enrolling in Spring 2026 Open University courses. Please review all dates carefully before registering.

---

## Getting Started

### December 15

Open University account activation begins
(Required each term before you can register)

### January 5

January 5 (9:00 a.m.) – January 30 (4:30 p.m.) Open University petitions accepted

### January 17

Classes begin

### Final Exams

May 9 – May 15. Final examinations

---

### **Installment Payment Plan Due Dates**

Students enrolled in a payment plan must submit payments by the following deadlines:

* **Payment 1:** Due when the Open University petition is submitted
* **Payment 2:** March 13, 2026
* **Payment 3:** April 17, 2026

---

### **Refund Timeline**

Refund eligibility is based on the official withdrawal date, not the date payment was submitted.

Refund Timeline

| Withdrawal Date | Refund Amount |
| --- | --- |
| Before January 17 | 100% |
| January 17 – January 22 | 90% |
| January 23 – January 29 | 80% |
| January 30 – February 5 | 70% |
| February 6 – End of Term | No refund |
