# Experiences of Users with Disabilities

Check out the experiences of some students with disabilities and how they interact with technology.

## [Paul Chiou](https://polycentric.cpp.edu/2017/09/computer-science-student-receives-top-csu-award/)

We Are Cal Poly Pomona features computer science alumni, Paul Chiou, who hopes to one day create more mainstream technology that offers people with disabilities the ability to excel.

## [Kelvin Crosby](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qOmNUlWMcEs)

San Diego State student is deaf/blind and shares his experience in the classroom and information on accessible instructional materials for the classroom.

## [Working Together: People with Disabilities and Computer Technology](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X92Cd6jicko)

Working Together: People with Disability and Computer Technology video shows students with various types of disabilities using different adaptive technologies in the academic environment.

## [The Disability Collection](https://www.gettyimages.com/collections/thedisabilitycollection)

The Disability Collection is a dedicated collection of images that break stereotypes and authentically portray people with disabilities in everyday life from Oath, the National Disability Leadership Alliance and Getty Images.
