### ACT-Residency Fellowship $35,000

## CSUN/LAUSD Residency Grant

The Accelerated Collaborative Teacher Residency Program at California State University, Northridge (CSUN), is pleased to announce a grant obtained by the Commission on Teacher Credentialing, in partnership with the Los Angeles Unified School District (LAUSD). Selected candidates will receive a stipend of $35,000 while completing a teacher credential program and a one-year residency, with an assigned mentor teacher (Fall 2025-Spring 2026). Candidates must commit to teach in LAUSD for a minimum of four years.

**Application Deadline:  July 1, 2025 or until all scholarships have been awarded**

[Appy Here](https://www.csun.edu/eisner-education/accelerated-collaborative-teacher-preparation-program/csunlausd-residency-grant)
