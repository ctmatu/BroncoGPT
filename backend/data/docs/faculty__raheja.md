## About Me

I am a Professor in the [Department of Computer Science](http://www.cpp.edu/~cs) in the [College of Science](http://sci.cpp.edu/) at [California State Polytechnic University, Pomona](http://www.cpp.edu/).

My research interests are in the area of image processing, computer vision and machine learning. My research focuses on interdisciplinary applications of AI techniques.

I am a member of the [IEEE](http://www.ieee.org/) Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society, Computer Society and the [ACM](http://www.acm.org/).
