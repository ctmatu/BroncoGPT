![BroncoSERV Logo](../img/learn/broncoserv.jpg)

## BroncoSERV

BroncoSERV is an online placement tracking software system created by the CSU Chancellor's Office. This system allows students to complete required risk management forms online and provides data of where students are being placed. It is required by the Academic Senate policy that BroncoSERV be used for all service-learning and academic internship courses.

[LOGIN](https://app.calstates4.com/)

(ATTENTION STUDENT: For academic internship/service learning course, you will NOT be able to access BroncoSERV for your specific course until the start of the semester.)

## Frequently Asked Questions

Expand/Collapse All Frequently Asked Questions

### What is BroncoSERV and why is my professor asking me to use it?

BroncoSERV is an online placement tracking software system created by the CSU Chancellor's Office. This system allows students to complete required risk management forms online and provides data of where students are being placed.  It is required by the Academic Senate policy that BroncoSERV be used for all service-learning and academic internship courses.

### How do I log into BroncoSERV?

Students and faculty log into BroncoSERV using your CPP Bronco ID and password.

### I'm logged in but don't know what to do?

Follow the [step-by-step guidelines](https://documentcloud.adobe.com/link/track?uri=urn:aaid:scds:US:25b97bf2-526d-4493-8228-c0e711caecd1) to complete your placement.

### I just registered for an academic internship/service learning course, but I do not see my course nor have access to the forms?

If you have just registered for an academic internship/service learning course, you will NOT be able to access BroncoSERV for your specific course until the start of the semester.

For example, if you're registering during Spring term for a course in Summer term; your access to BroncoSERV will not start until the beginning of the Summer term.

### My professor is asking me to do the timelog for my service-learning course, how do I do this?

Watch the Youtube video to learn how to navigate the BroncoSERV timelog feature for **service-learning course** that student will require to complete.

[Watch Me!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3QOptlxdWQs)

### How about academic internship course, what form of timelog is required?

Timesheet maybe required for **academic internship course** to complete. However, please confirm with your instructor or course syllabus on how timesheet will be submitted. Note that your instructor or placement site may have their own timesheet. If not, please view [downloadad timesheet](https://documentcloud.adobe.com/link/track?uri=urn:aaid:scds:US:7f6e8801-75f8-42b4-9186-ca5c36419aea).

### The system is still confusing to me. How can I get help?

Contact cce@cpp.edu with your issue. We can masquerade as your account and assist you with completing the forms.

### What is a service-learning course?

Service-learning is an academic course where a service project or service experience is part of the overall grade. In the spirit of learning-by-doing, Cal Poly Pomona faculty, staff and students actively support and engage in service-learning activities that build meaningful and sustainable community partnerships. For more information visit our [Service-Learning website](../students/service-learning.shtml).

### What is an academic internship?

An academic internship is where the student intern receives academic credit for their internship experience. The academic internship course will include significant involvement of student and their supervised faculty in planning, processing and evaluating the learning results from their experiences. For more information visit our [Academic Internship website.](../students/internships.shtml)
