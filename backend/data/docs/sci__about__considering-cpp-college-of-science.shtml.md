# Considering Cal Poly Pomona College of Science?

## The greatest scientific breakthroughs begin with a single question, a spark of curiosity that ignites a journey of discovery.

![CPP students engaged in research activities](img/comp-students-6.jpg)

At Cal Poly Pomona’s College of Science, curiosity isn’t just encouraged — it’s what drives us. It's the catalyst that transforms inquisitive minds into pioneering scientists. Through immersive research experiences, hands-on laboratory exploration and close collaboration with dedicated faculty mentors, we create an environment where students don’t just study science—they live it. Our approach combines a rigorous academic foundation with real-world investigation, allowing students to tackle scientific challenges alongside experienced researchers who share their passion for discovery and innovation.

At Cal Poly Pomona, you don’t just learn by doing. You become by doing.
