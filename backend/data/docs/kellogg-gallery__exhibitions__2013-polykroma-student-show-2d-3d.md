# Poly-Kroma 2013:

## Student Show & 2D-3D

Jun 7, 2013 to Jun 17, 2013

**Location:** LA County Fairplex

***![Polykroma](poly_kroma.png)Pol******y******-­******Kr******om******a***showcases Cal Poly Pomona Art and Design excellence in order to promote, network & recruit in the spirit of academia and community. The exhibition showcases the work of Graphic Design, Fine Art, Art History students and alumni, a high school student outreach event and VIP event, as well as CalPoly Pomona’s annual ***2D/3D Student Art Jurored Exhibition***. (This year’s event held at the LA County Fairplex due to double-­booking in the Kellogg Gallery).
