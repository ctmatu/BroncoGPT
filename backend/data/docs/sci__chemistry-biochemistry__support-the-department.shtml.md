# Support the Department

We strive to provide the highest quality education to our students in diverse areas within the Chemistry and Biochemistry Department.

Generous gifts from alumni, friends, and donors allow the Chemistry and Biochemistry Department to enhance the quality of our educational programs by providing highly valuable experiential learning opportunities to our students. These experiences reach far beyond the classroom and leave lasting impressions on the intellectual and professional growth of our students. Previous gifts have been used to support student research projects, scholarships, and equipment.

**You Can Support the Chemistry and Biochemistry Department by Giving Today**
