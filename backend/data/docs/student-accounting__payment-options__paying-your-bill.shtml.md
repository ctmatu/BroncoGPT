# Paying Your Bill

## Overview

Paying your bill is an important part of managing your student account. Below, you’ll find all available payment methods, online and in person, with step-by-step info to help you choose the one that works best for you.

## How to Pay

### Explore your payment options by method.

[Card 1 of 7

### Credit Card Payment (Transact)

Pay with American Express, Discover, VISA, or MasterCard. A 2.65% service fee applies.](https://my.cpp.edu)
[Card 2 of 7

### Pay Online with E-Check

No fee when paying from a personal checking or savings account through BroncoDirect.](https://my.cpp.edu)
[Card 3 of 7

### International Payment (FlyWire)

Pay from your home country in your local currency through FlyWire.](international-payments.shtml)

Card 4 of 7

### International Payment (PayMyTuition)

Pay from your home country in your local currency through PayMyTuition.

Card 5 of 7

### 529 College Savings Plan

Use your college savings plan to pay online through BroncoDirect.

Card 6 of 7

### Check, Cashier’s Check, or Money Order

Pay by mail, in person, or drop box. Make checks payable to Cal Poly Pomona.

[Card 7 of 7

### Third-Party or Sponsor Payment

Includes military, employer, or agency sponsors. Email: sacs3rd@cpp.edu](third_party_billing.shtml)

## Helpful Reminders

* Never place cash in the drop box.
* Check operating hours before coming in person.
* Allow 10 business days for mailed checks to process.
* Always include your Bronco ID on mailed payments.
* For full payment deadlines, visit: [Tuition & Fees Page](../tuition/mandatory-auxiliary-fees.shtml "Tuition & Fees Page")
* Double-check your payment confirmation in MyCPP (BroncoDirect) after submitting.
