## Other Fellowships, Scholarships, and Internships

### General

* [Cornell Graduate School's Website for Graduate Fellowships](https://gradschool.cornell.edu/financial-support/fellowships/)
* [National Hispanic Scholarship Fund](http://www.nhsf.org/): Provides grants up to $1,000 for Hispanic undergraduate and graduate students.
* [National Physical Science Consortium Graduate Fellowship for Minorities and Women](http://www.npsc.org/): For women or African American, Hispanic, or American Indian students pursuing a doctoral degree.
* [UCLA Graduate and Postdoctoral Extramural Support (GRAPES) Database](http://www.gdnet.ucla.edu/grpinst.htm): Search over 500 privately and publicly funded awards, fellowships, and internships.

### Arts, Humanities & Social Sciences

* [Jacob K. Javits Fellowships](http://www.ed.gov/programs/jacobjavits/applicant.html): For individuals pursuing the doctorate in the arts, humanities, or social sciences.
* [National Science Foundation Graduate Fellowships:](http://www.nsf.gov/funding/pgm_summ.jsp?pims_id=13454&org=NSF&from=fund) Provides fellowships of $14,000 plus fees/tuition for students in engineering, natural or social sciences.
* [American Psychological Association Minority Fellowship Program For doctoral students in psychology](http://www.apa.org/about/awards/index.aspx)

### Engineering

* [GEM Fellowships in Engineering, Physical, and Life Sciences](http://www.gemfellowship.org/)
* [National Defense Science and Engineering Graduate Fellowships](https://www.asee.org/ndseg/): Three-year fellowships for graduate study in science and engineering disciplines with military importance
* [National Science Foundation Graduate Fellowships:](http://www.nsf.gov/funding/pgm_summ.jsp?pims_id=13454&org=NSF&from=fund) Provides fellowships of $14,000 plus fees/tuition for students in engineering, natural or social sciences.
* [UNCF/MERCK Graduate Science Research Dissertation Fellowships](http://umsi.uncf.org/index.php): Provides science research dissertation fellowships for African Americans enrolled full-time in the life sciences or physical sciences
* [Zonta International Foundation Amelia Earhart Fellowship Awards](http://www.zonta.org/): For women pursuing graduate study in aerospace-related sciences or engineering.

### Life Sciences

* [Howard Hughes Medical Institute Predoctoral Fellowships in the Biological Sciences](http://www.hhmi.org/grants/): For students pursuing a doctoral degree in the life sciences.
* [National Medical Fellowships](http://www.nmfonline.org/): For minority students in first and second year medical studies.

### Physical Sciences

* [Fannie and John Hertz Foundation Graduate Fellowships](http://www.hertzfoundation.org/)
* [GEM Fellowships in Engineering, Physical, and Life Sciences](http://www.gemfellowship.org/)
