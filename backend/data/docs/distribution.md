## Distribution Services

operates under the direction of the Director of Procurement Services and responsible for the receipt (or acceptance) and distribution of materials delivered to the shipping and receiving area, United States Postal Services mail, courier delivered items, inter-campus mail and the preparation and mailing of United States Postal Services mail.

## Our Services

consists of the preparation and distribution of materials, the content of which relates to the official business of the university. Distribution Services, according to federal regulations and university guidelines, is not be used for commercial purposes or personal mail of university employees.
