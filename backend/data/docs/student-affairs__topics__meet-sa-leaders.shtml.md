# Meet Student Affairs Leadership

## The Division of Student Affairs serves you.

We provide intentional, student-centered programs and support services, creating opportunities that facilitate your development and enhance your sense of belonging at Cal Poly Pomona.

Scroll down to learn more about the Student Affairs leadership team! Remember, you can reach out at any time.

## Leadership

### Christina Gonzales, VPSA

Vice President Christina Gonzales leads the departments and units that make up the Division of Student Affairs, and provides leadership for a wide-range of programs and services that support your transition to Cal Poly Pomona and deepen your out-of-class experiences. She also serves as a member of the President's cabinet.

### Dr. Jonathan Grady

Senior AVP for Equity and Belonging. As the Senior Associate Vice President for Equity and Belonging, Dr. Grady leads areas including Student Support and Equity Programs, the Veterans Resource Center, Counseling and Psychological Services and Cultural and Identity Initiatives.

### Dr. Megan Stang

AVP for Student Experience. As the AVP for Student Experience, Dr. Stang oversees departments such as University Housing Services, the famous Rose Float Program, Orientation, the Bronco Leadership Center, and the Healht Center. She works closely with divisional leadership to create and grow partnerships to promote student learning and success.

### Hallie K. Lewis

AVP for Student Wellbeing and Support. As the AVP for Wellbeing and Support, Hallie oversees departments such as the Care Center. She works across campus to ensure the health and wellbeing of students.

### Brian Swanson

Director, Intercollegiate Athletics. As the Director for Intercollegiate Athletics, Brian oversees all ten athletic programs competing at the NCAA DII level, including baseball, men's and women's basketball, men's and women's cross country, men's and women's track and field, mens and women's soccer, and volleyball.

### Dr. Liz Roosa Millar

Executive Director, Associated Students, Inc. As the Executive Director for Associated Students, Inc., Dr. Roosa Millar is responsible for its overall management and administration, which includes financial services and budget matters, human resources, marketing, design and public relations and the operations of the BSC, BRIC and Children’s Center.
