# Summer Orientation 2024

June 10, 2024

2024 Summer Orientation has begun and has brought a new group of students to the CEIS Community.

CEIS will be hosting 85 first-time freshmen and 151 transfer students. Orientation is hosted by the Student Success Center with presentations from Liberal Studies, IGE, and Early Childhood Studies. CEIS Staff, Faculty, and Orientation Leaders are available for support and guidance as the students register for their classes. Special Thanks to Academic Advisors Berlinda Crawford and Marlene Melinte for all the behind the scences work and prep that is done to make this process as stress-free for students.
