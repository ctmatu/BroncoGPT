# The Lyle Center Presents:

## Virtual Tour of Santa Monica City Services Building

![](../../)

**Date:** November 02, 2021
**Time:** 12:00pm to 01:00pm
**Location:** Virtual

![Santa Monica City Services Building exterior shot](../../img/santa-monica-city-services-building.jpg)

The Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies will host a virtual tour of [Santa Monica City Hall East](https://www.smgov.net/Departments/OSE/Categories/Green_Building/City_Hall_East_Living_Building.aspx). Designated as a Living Building Certified structure, it is intended to be a model of ecological resilience, self-sufficiency and zero-waste generation.

The virtual tour will be led by **Amber Richane**, Senior Design Manager at the City of Santa Monica; and **Joseph Coriarty**, managing partner at [Frederick Fisher & Partners](https://fisherpartners.net/).

**Lyle Center Presents: Virtual Tour of Santa Monica City Services Building**

Tuesday, Nov. 2 | 12 p.m. (PST)

Email crs@cpp.edu to receive Zoom link
