## Spain

Join us for an unforgettable 3.5-week study abroad program in Pamplona, Spain, where you'll explore Spanish food systems through cultural immersion, hands-on learning, and personalized language instruction (no prior Spanish required!). Dive into the rich food culture of Spain, studying food processing, nutrition, and sensory evaluation of “Denominación de Origen Controlada” foods, all while enjoying excursions to Pamplona and surrounding cities. This unique program, in collaboration with the Public University of Navarre (UPNA), offers an exceptional opportunity for students to gain academic and cultural insights, enhancing both your education and global perspective.

**Program Dates: Summer 2026.  Please contact Professors Gabriel Davidov Pardo at gdavidov@cpp.edu or Jasmin Ilkay at jilkay@cpp.edu for more information.**
