# PolyTransfer Study Lounge

## Study up in PolyTransfer's very own lounge!

![](study-lounge.png)

The PolyTransfer Study Lounge is located on the first floor of the Student Services Building (Building 121). Just head straight through the sliding glass doors and proceed towards the counter where you pick up your Bronco ID Card. It is the room located immediately to the left. The lounge is available to students during normal business hours.

The Study Lounge is available for all transfer students as a place to comfortably study, meet other transfer students, and relax from a long day of classes. We will do our best to ensure this lounge is always stocked with healthy drinks and snacks that will support your learning, so we hope you will stop by when you get a chance!

Some lounge resources include:

* Refrigerator/Microwave
* Television w/ HDMI Cord
* Study Tables/Chairs
* Computer
* Whiteboard/Corkboard
