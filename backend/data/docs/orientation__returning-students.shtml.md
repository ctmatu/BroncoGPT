# Returning Students

## Welcome to Cal Poly Pomona!

We look forward welcoming you back to CPP as you resume your studies. Regardless of when you last attended Cal Poly Pomona or went through orientation, all returning students are required to go through the orientation experience to re-acquaint themselves with CPP and the resources and programs that are available to you. Returning students should make an orientation reservation by the stated deadlines for the term they are returning to CPP. Returning students should attend a transfer orientation session.
