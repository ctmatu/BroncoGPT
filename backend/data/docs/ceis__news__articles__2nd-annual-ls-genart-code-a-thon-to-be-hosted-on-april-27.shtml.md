# 2nd Annual LS GenArt Code-A-Thon to be Hosted on April 27

April 18, 2023

![GenArt](../../img/genart.png)The Cal Poly Pomona community is invited to attend the 2nd Annual Liberal Studies [GenArt PolyX Code-A-Thon](https://thepolypost.com/arts-and-culture/2022/05/10/student-organized-code-a-thon-teaches-students-how-to-create-genart/ "https://thepolypost.com/arts-and-culture/2022/05/10/student-organized-code-a-thon-teaches-students-how-to-create-genart/"), which will be held on April 27 from 4-5:15 p.m. through Zoom. During the event, students will learn how to code within the context of the visual arts, while building equity-minded STEAM and computer science (CS) pathways in an inclusive, accessible environment. No experience is necessary. Please RSVP through [Eventbrite](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/ls-genart-polyx-code-a-thon-by-students-for-students-tickets-597227181577 "https://www.eventbrite.com/e/ls-genart-polyx-code-a-thon-by-students-for-students-tickets-597227181577"). For more information, contact Liberal Studies Assistant Professor Maya Stovall, Jackie Barrettor Cristal Ramirez.
