# Accommodation Information for Students

## Requesting Accommodations

The first step to using accommodations **each term** is to submit accommodation **requests for specific classes.** Accommodations do not go into effect until they have been **requested** for a class. Class schedules should appear in myDRC within 24-48 hours after registering for the class. DRC recommends students submit accommodation requests as soon as possible after registration. Do not forget to submit requests, because accommodations cannot be applied retroactively.

*If classes have already started and you are unable to submit your requests, please contact DRC for assistance at drc@cpp.edu or 909.869.3333.*

Please review the [How-To Request Accommodations](https://sway.office.com/VaTDbDFI9XOQJTt9?ref=Link) tutorial to learn how to request your accommodations.

**IMPORTANT:** Some accommodations require students to take additional steps after submitting their accommodation request. It is important for students to understand their responsibilities for each accommodation. Use the links below to learn more about your accommodations.

## Accommodation Eligibility

### Not sure what accommodations you are eligible for?

DRC Students should familiarize themselves with each of their approved accommodations. If you are unsure of what accommodations you are approved for, log-in to [myDRC](https://cascade.accessiblelearning.com/CPP) click on the "My Eligibility" under the "My Dashboard" menu.
