# Unidos

## Our Mission

To support the academic and professional development of Latino faculty, staff and students as a means to enhance their leadership skills, and to celebrate and promote the Latino cultural heritage with the campus community.

## Your Impact

Unidos plays a vital role in advocating for the Latin community on campus, promoting cultural awareness, and fostering meaningful connections. Be a part of this growing community and help make a lasting impact.

We are launching a **fundraiser to raise money for our students at CPP to reach their academic goals**. Whether you’re able to donate—no matter the amount—or simply share and repost our fundraiser, we’re deeply grateful for your support.
