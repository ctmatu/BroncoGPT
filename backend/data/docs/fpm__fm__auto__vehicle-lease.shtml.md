# Vehicle Rentals

Cal Poly Pomona faculty and staff members who need a vehicle for university business can rent cars from Enterprise Rent-A-Car or National Car Rental. The partnership with Enterprise allows for free pickup and delivery service at the start and end of any rental period.

All university employees will receive a special government rate when renting vehicles for both business and personal use. The rate includes full damage waiver and supplemental liability insurance when using the vehicle for university business. University employees will not receive insurance coverage when using vehicles for personal use.

The base rate for both Enterprise and National is $35.01 per day.

For detailed information, go to the Rental Cars section of the [University Accounting Services](https://www.cpp.edu/fas/university-accounting-services/travel/automobile-travel.shtml) page.
