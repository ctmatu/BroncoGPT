# Captioning

## Need to get your content captioned?

[Request captioning services](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=ce5441846fcdce0025ce9e4eae3ee4db)

* We recommend submitting requests 5 days in advance to help ensure support
* MediaVision provides captioning at no cost to any content we produce that is used as part of course materials.
* MediaVision works with the Disability Resource Center (DRC) to provide captioning at no cost for course materials where students have an accommodation need.
* For all other content we provide the syncing of captioning at no cost; however, we are unable to generate a transcript of your event.

  + If you do not have a transcript then there is a $99/hour ($1.65/min) charge for transcription as we send this out to a third-party company.
  + If you provide us with a transcript of the event we can provide the captions at no charge.
* We can only provide captions for the following formats:

  + Online content hosted by the university (e.g. streaming.cpp.edu)
  + Public YouTube and Vimeo content through Amara.org
