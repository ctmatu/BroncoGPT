# Conference Presentation Award

---

## Conference Presentation Award

The Conference Presentation Award recognizes students whose research has been accepted for a conference presentation. The award is intended to cover conference registration fees. The award amounts are

* $300 for regional or student conferences
* $600 for national conferences
* $800 for international conferences

Presentations at SCCUR, the CPP Student RSCA Conference, the CPP CARS, and CSU Research Competition are not eligible for this award.

For more information or questions regarding the Conference Presentation Award, please contact Hosne Afrin at hafrin@cpp.edu.
