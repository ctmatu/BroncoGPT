# Asian & Pacific Islander Student Center

![An APISC tabling event](img/apisc-sideshow-pic.jpeg)

## Mission Statement

The Asian & Pacific Islander Student Center promotes social justice, equity, and student success through education and community engagement, affirming and celebrating cultural identities within the Asian, Pacific Islander, and Desi diaspora.

The APISC warmly welcomes any person, regardless of how they identify. It is also a space for people of all backgrounds to explore, learn, connect and build coalition.

We celebrate diversity in all its forms and are committed to providing a respectful and inclusive environment for every individual.
