# Google Analytics for Web Development

## Google Analytics for Generating Customer Insights for Digital Marketing

This workshop prepared by Sarah Borsaly, Web Analytics and Text Mining Specialist at the Center for Customer Insights and Digital Marketing at Cal Poly Pomona, will teach individuals how to connect Google Analytics to a website, what Google Analytics can be used for, and an introduction on how to navigate and analyze the dashboards.
