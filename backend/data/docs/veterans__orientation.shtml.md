# Orientation for Undergraduate Veterans

## Orientation

As part of your transition to Cal Poly Pomona, you will be participating in an orientation program that will prepare you for academic success and a meaningful university experience.

Because of the unique needs and services of veterans, your orientation will include an on-campus and an online orientation specifically for veterans and military personnel. You will also be introduced to our Veterans Resource Center, which will provide you information and services as you progress towards completing your degree.

For more information, go to [Freshman Orientation for Veterans and Military Personnel](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/military-veterans/freshmen-orientation.shtml) if you are entering as a first-time freshman or to [Transfer Orientation for Veterans and Military Personnel](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/military-veterans/transfer-orientation.shtml)if you are transferring to Cal Poly Pomona from another college or university.
