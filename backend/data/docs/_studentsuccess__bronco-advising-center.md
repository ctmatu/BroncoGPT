Our office is in the Student Services Building, First Floor (Blue Counter). We are open weekdays 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.

### Email Us

Email us at bac@cpp.edu. Please email us from your CPP email address and include your Bronco ID number in your message.

## About the Bronco Advising Center

We are Cal Poly Pomona's general academic advising center, available to all students. We know a lot about Cal Poly Pomona, so even if your question falls out of our scope, **we’ll get you the answers you need** – no need to travel across campus or call multiple departments.

**The BAC Has Your Back!**
