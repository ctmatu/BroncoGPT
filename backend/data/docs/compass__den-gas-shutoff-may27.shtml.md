# Vista Market and The Den to Undergo Natural Gas Shutoff on Friday, May 27

**Summary of disruption:** The natural gas supply to Vista Market and The Den (Building 52) will be shut off from approximately 8 a.m. to 2 p.m. on Friday, May 27.

**Reason for disruption:** Allow campus crews to repair leaks in gas lines leading to the building.

**How does this affect me?** Hot water and heating to the building will not be available while repairs are being completed.

**What do I need to do?** Please share this information with others who may have not received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:** Facilities Customer Service at Ext. 3030.
