# Advising

The College of Science Advising Center has advisors who are assigned specifically to assist physics majors. **You are also strongly encouraged meet with a faculty advisor every semester to make sure you are on the right track.** *Physics and Astronomy faculty are your best resource to discuss your study plans, your career plans, and options for post-graduate study.* Please feel free to contact any [tenure-track faculty member](../faculty-staff/tenure-track-faculty.shtml) for an advising appointment. If you are uncertain who you would like to meet with, [contact](../contact.shtml) the Department Chair or Associate Chair for assistance.

**Our upper-division courses are not offered every semester.** To help you plan ahead, look at our [Schedule of Planned Upper-Division Course Offerings](https://livecsupomona-my.sharepoint.com/%3Aw%3A/g/personal/mspovich_cpp_edu/IQCvXeHv15d-QJUJnWA-8WtzASNH4XZ-kdRb-osZxND1b6c?e=x82DUG) for the next few academic years, broken down by semester.

**Overall Advising Tips/Reminders:**

* Your specific registration date and time will be posted to your BroncoDirect account before registration begins.
* Keep checking your CPP email to stay in the know!
* Please refer to theCollege of Science Advising Centerfor the latest information on advising policies and procedures.
