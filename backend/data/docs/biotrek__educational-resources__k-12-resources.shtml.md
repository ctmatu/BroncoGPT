These resources are available to visiting K-12 teachers for educational use only. BioTrek is a dynamic educational experience, and there may be rotation of featured plants, animals, and stations during a visit. If there is a specific emphasis you wish your students to experience, please mention it when booking a tour.

## Additional Teacher Resources

During BioTrek field trips, we address the   [K-12 California Content Standards](http://www.cde.ca.gov/be/st/ss/)  for science and history-social science where appropriate.  A typical two-hour tour will have multiple stops that incorporate specific environmental themes.

A collection of additional pre-curriculum activities is available for:

* [Grades K-5](https://www.cpp.edu/biotrek/docs/BioTrek_K-5_Pre-Curriculum.pdf)
* [Grades 6-12](https://www.cpp.edu/biotrek/docs/BioTrek_6-12_Pre-Curriculum.pdf)

A collection of additional post-curriculum activities is available for:

* [Grades K-5](https://www.cpp.edu/biotrek/docs/BioTrek_K-5_Post-Curriculum.pdf)
* [Grades 6-12](https://www.cpp.edu/biotrek/docs/BioTrek_6-12_Post-Curriculum.pdf)
