## Tools to Help You Succeed!

RAMP maintains a library of materials for use during tutorial sessions and for graduate entrance exam preparation. The program maintains a carefully selected array of fiction and non-fiction books that participants can check out to enrich their reading experience. RAMP tutor/mentors use a variety of workbooks and anthologies to help participants strengthen reading comprehension and practice active and critical reading strategies. Additionally, computer programs such as WordSmart and Progressive Reader help participants build vocabulary and improve reading speed. Graduate entrance exam materials are updated regularly.
