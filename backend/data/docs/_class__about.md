# The CLASS Experience

## Innovation through Interdisciplinary Learning

The College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences (CLASS) at Cal Poly Pomona enriches communities through the creation and application of knowledge in the humanities, performing arts, and social sciences. At CLASS, students learn facts and figures, and, more importantly, learn how to gauge ideas and form their own opinions to communicate with their peers and beyond. Our graduates have the skills and knowledge to solve current and future challenges. Our graduates have the skills and knowledge to solve today’s and tomorrow’s challenges.

Encompassing the humanities, performing arts, and social sciences, CLASS offers a diverse range of programs that empower students to explore the human experience through inclusive academic opportunities and hands-on learning. Here, we practice hands-on humanities and arts:  from practicing in Ethics Bowl competitions to creating podcasts focused on pressing contemporary issues, to hosting performances that speak to community experiences.

Our 12 departments and 19 majors reflect the diversity of our nearly 5,000 students, with 65% being first-generation college students. Through our programs, students develop critical thinking, creativity, and analysis skills, as well as the empathy essential for thriving in their chosen professions and making meaningful contributions to society.

CLASS provides [signature experiences](../student/poly-course.shtml "signature experiences") including Model United Nations, student-run news media, judicial internships, music ensembles, and specialized centers like the California Center for Ethics and Policy and The Democracy Institute. Our [Student Success Center](https://www.cpp.edu/class/student-success-center/index.shtml " Student Success Center") offers professional advisors who help students chart paths to success and navigate obstacles.

With 60 [scholarships](../student/scholarships.shtml "scholarships"), 20+ [clubs](../student/clubs.shtml "clubs"), and connections to top employers like Amazon, Disneyland Resort, and Kaiser Permanente, our goal is to equip students with fundamental skills for both personal growth and career success, while fostering a community that enriches the world around us.

## Discover CLASS

### Mission

We cultivate one's intellectual development, ethical reasoning, and aesthetic sensibility to support creative and critical thinking in a diverse world of competing challenges.

### Vision

We will be a supportive, engaged, and diverse community of teachers, learners, and scholars committed to improving the human condition and to bettering the world.

## Values

We the faculty, staff, and students of CLASS **value**:

- Theoretical and experimental approaches in developing critical thinkers and creative problem solvers,
- Discovery through research, scholarly, and creative activities,
- Transparency, equity, empathy, integrity, diversity, and
- Working towards a just, humane, and inclusive world.
