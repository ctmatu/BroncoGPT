# Alumni & Friends

### Cal Poly Pomona Alumni Association

Want to get more updates from your alma mater? Wonder how your classmates are doing? Please join the [Cal Poly Pomona Alumni Association](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/index.shtml) for alumni activities!

### College of Engineering Alumni

The College of Engineering is the 17th largest engineering school in the nation. In over fifty years, the College of Engineering has successly produced over 30,000 engineers and engineering technologists!

Following the [College of Engineering Alumni webpage](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/alumni/index.shtml) for more activities!
