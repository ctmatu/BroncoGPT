# About the Department

![Geological Sciences students and professors](../img/on-the-field-nbbsc-02.jpg)

Through hands-on learning methods, faculty-mentored research, and exposure to current technology, our students acquire skills applicable to careers in the Geosciences and related disciplines. The Department’s programs provide a global perspective for problem solving, decision making and leadership roles in a changing world. Our [**Geology BSc program**](degrees-and-classes.shtml "GeologyBScurriculum") has three different tracks: Geology, Geophysics/Earth Exploration and Environmental Resources (see also the **Students** or **Movies and Pictures** tabs at the left). Our [**Geology MSc program**](../masters-program/curriculum.shtml "MScurriculum") prepares graduates for employment in industry and teaching. Please click on the **Master's Program** tab at the left for more details.
