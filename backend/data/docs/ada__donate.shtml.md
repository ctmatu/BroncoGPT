# Donate

## Payroll Deduction

Cal Poly Pomona staff and faculty may donate to the AdA through payroll deduction, simply:

1. [Complete the Faculty/Staff Giving form (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/forms/faculty-staff-giving.pdf)
2. Send your completed forms to AdA Presidents, Shayda Kafai or Katie Kinkopf for AdA tracking and processing. Your donation will then be processed by University Advancement.

## One-time Tax Deductible Donation

A one-time tax deductible donation may be made via personal or corporate check:

1. Make your check out to Cal Poly Pomona Access & disABILITY Alliance
2. *(Please contact us at skafai@cpp.edu for current mailing address)*
3. Upon receipt of your check, the Treasurer will issue you a receipt.

Any questions regarding making a donation may be referred to AdA Presidents, Shayda Kafai at skafai@cpp.edu or Katie Kinkopf at kmkinkopf@cpp.edu.

**We greatly appreciate your donations which will be used to support AdA programming and events.**
