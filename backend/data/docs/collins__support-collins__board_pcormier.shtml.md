# Dean’s Advisory Board

## Patrick Cormier

### Regional Vice President of Venues at Encore Global

![Portrait of Patrick Cormier](../img/board-of-advisors-pics/patrick-cormier-bio-picture.png)

With a dynamic career in the hospitality and events industry, Patrick Cormier currently serves as the Regional Vice President of Venues, where he plays a pivotal role in driving business operational and technical excellence across the Los Angeles region. At the heart of his responsibilities lies the commitment to bolstering the company's growth and profitability, all while elevating the quality of service and strengthening relationships with venue partners and customers alike.

Patrick is an operations leader with more than 20 years of executive experience working in the hospitality industry. Patrick relocating to Los Angeles from NYC to serve as Director of Operations at Malibu Beach Inn. He has also held executive leadership positions in some of the most lauded and recognized names in hospitality, including The Greenwich Hotel, ACE Hotel New York, Thomas Keller Restaurant Group, and Cipriani. Patrick’s approach to leadership reflects enduring respect for the art of hospitality and the cultural impact of its traditions.

Patrick attended the European School of Economics where he earned his bachelor’s degree in Finance in New York City in 2009. He maintains his interest in academics through continuing education courses and certifications in his spare time.
