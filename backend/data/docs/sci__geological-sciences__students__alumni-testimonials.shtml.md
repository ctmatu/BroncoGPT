# Alumni Testimonials

[![Kacie Wellington](../img/kacie-field-shot-alaska.jpg)

## Kacie Wellington

Kacie Wellington - 2012 Kacie works as an exploration geologist for Alaska Earth Sciences, Inc., a company that contracts projectswith major gold and copper mining exploration firms in Alaska.](/sci/geological-sciences/students/alumni/kacie-wellington.shtml)

[![Randal Burns](../img/alumni-testimonials/rb1.jpg)

## Randal Burns

Randal Burns - 2006 Randal is employed by Robinson Nevada Mining Company, a subsidiary of Quadra Limited, in Ruth, Nevada. Ruth is located in White Pine County in northeastern Nevada. The Robinson Mine produces copper with by-product gold and molybdenum.](/sci/geological-sciences/students/alumni/randal-burns.shtml)

[![Lauren Carey](../img/alumni-testimonials/LaurenFlowMeter.jpg)

## Lauren Carey

Lauren Carey - 2009 has taken a new position modeling groundwater flow systems for Geoscience Support Services, Inc. Lauren's previous achievements include the valedictorian award from the College of Science (2009) and recipient of a Master's degree in Hydrology from University of Idaho (2011).](/sci/geological-sciences/students/alumni/lauren-carey.shtml)

[![Jeremy Lancaster](../img/alumni-testimonials/jlancaster.jpg)

## Jeremy Lancaster

Jeremy Lancaster - 2000 Jeremy graduated in 2000 after completing a senior thesis investigation of a landslide in Chino Hills State Park. After working several years as a geologist for CalTrans, Jeremy earned his Professional Geologist and Engineering Geologist certification from the State of California.](/sci/geological-sciences/students/alumni/jeremy-lancaster.shtml)
