## About me

Dr. Tang is a Professor in the [Computer Science Department](http://www.cpp.edu/~cs/) at [California State Polytechnic University - Pomona](http://www.cpp.edu/) (Cal Poly Pomona). She joined Cal Poly Pomona in Fall 2006 as an Assistant Professor and was promoted to Associate Professor in 2012 and Professor in 2017. She served as the Chair of the CS department from 2017-2025.

Dr. Tang received her Ph.D. degree in computer science in 2006 from [The University of Tennessee - Knoxville (UTK)](http://www.utk.edu/), performing her research on multi-robot systems. She received her M.S. degree in computer science from UTK in 2003, and her B.S. degree in computer science from [Sichuan University](http://www.scu.edu.cn/), Chengdu, China, in 2000.
