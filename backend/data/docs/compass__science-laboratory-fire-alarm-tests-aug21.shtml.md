# Fire Alarm Tests Scheduled in Science Laboratory on Friday, Aug. 21

**Update on disruption:** Fire alarm testing in the Science Laboratory (Building 3) is scheduled on Friday, Aug. 21, from approximately 7 a.m. to 1 p.m.

**Reason for disruption:** Allow the university to conduct state-mandated annual testing of the fire alarm system in each building.

**How does this affect me?** Loud horns and flashing strobe lights will be activated during testing. The HVAC system will undergo interruptions. Elevators will not be affected.

**What do I need to do?** Avoid being in the buildings during testing. Please share this information with others who may have not received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:** Facilities Customer Service at Ext. 3030.
