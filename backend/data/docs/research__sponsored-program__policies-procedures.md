### Misconduct in Research Policy

Cal Poly Pomona promotes the highest ethical standards of conduct in research and creative activities. Despite being extremely rare, when it occurs misconduct in research can have a significant impact on the reputation and credibility of the University, its faculty and students and cannot be tolerated.

The [Misconduct in Research Policy 1207 (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/academic-manual/1200-1299-research-and-instructional-support/1200-1299/policy-1207_revision_misconduct_research-1.pdf) provides a set of procedures for investigating and reporting instances of alleged or apparent misconduct in research and creative activity.

### ICR (Indirect Cost Recovery) Distribution to Principal Investigators

The distribution of ICR to each Principal Investigator is based upon the PI’s grant expenditures and the IDC rate for the individual grants.  The Foundation charges a 4.5% administrative fee for every dollar expended, and this fee is taken from the indirect costs.

IDC Available = ($$ Expended x IDC rate) - 4.5% Foundation Administrative Fee on expenditures\*

\*Note that there will be no IDC available if the IDC rate is less than the 4.5% Foundation Administrative Fee

IDC Return (ICR) to PI = 11% of the IDC Available

The remainder of the IDC is apportioned to the Office of Research, Innovation, & Economic Development and to the colleges.  Some colleges keep their revenue at the college level.  Others share the revenue with the departments.

If the ICR for the PI is a small amount it will be combined with that returned to the college.

Distributions will be made quarterly or biannually depending upon the circumstances.

### Retention of Records

The CSU has a [policy on the retention of records.](https://calstate.policystat.com/policy/18018729/latest/?showchanges=true)

* For unfunded proposals, documents should be maintained electronically for 5 years.
* For awards, 3 years after completion of the project is the minimum time for retention.
* For conflict of interest documents, retention time is 7 years.
* For IRB and IACUC records, documents should be maintained for 3 years
* For Research Misconduct proceedings, documents need to be maintained for 7 years.
