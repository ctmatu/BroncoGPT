# Contacting CAPS

## How to Reach CAPS

![](../img/banner-telephone.jpg)

If you are a student seeking support services, or resources from **Counseling and Psychological Services**, please let us know by telephone at (909) 869-3220 during our normal business hours.  Have your Bronco ID number ready when calling.

For TTY service dial 7 - 1 - 1 first to connect with a free service who will then contact the various numbers below for you.

**Contact Information**

* Appointments and information:  (909) 869-3220
* After-Hours Crisis Support:  (909) 869-3220, press Option 2
  + NOT for appointment or general service inquiries
* General Fax:  (909) 869-6775
* E-mail:  caps@cpp.edu
  + For general inquiries or comments
  + NOT for appointment use
  + NOT for emergencies or crisis use

**Hours of Operation**

* In Person
  + Monday thru Friday
    - In Person:  8:00 a.m. thru 4:30 p.m.
    - Telephone:  8:00 a.m. thru 5:00 p.m.
* After-Hours Crisis Support
  + Monday thru Friday
    - Telephone:  5:00 p.m. thru 8:00 a.m.
  + Saturday and Sunday
    - Telephone:  all day
  + Campus Holidays and Breaks
    - Telephone:  all day

**Location**

Building 66 Room 116 (Bookstore building)
