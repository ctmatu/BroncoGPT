## Bio

Dr. Yuka Nishikawa is an Assistant Professor of Finance at Cal Poly Pomona. Her research interests are corporate finance, corporate governance, behavioral finance, and fintech. Her research has been published in Production and Operations Management, Journal of Behavioral Finance, Financial Review, Finance Research Letters, Applied Economics, and Managerial Finance. Her teaching interests include financial markets and institutions and corporate finance theory. Before joining Cal Poly Pomona, she taught at Florida International University and the College of Charleston. She currently serves on the Board of Directors of the Southwestern Finance Association (SWFA). In her spare time, she enjoys going to the beach and jogging along the coast.
