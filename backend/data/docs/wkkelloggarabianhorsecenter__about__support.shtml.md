[Donate](../giving/cpp.shtml)

The W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center relies on the generosity and support of friends, alumni, and the Arabian horse community. Donations of products and services are always greatly appreciated and put to good use. Monetary donations may be made to the W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center by following the link below. Be sure to designate The W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center as the recipient. All donations are tax deductible.

[Donate to the W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center](../giving/cpp.shtml)

If you are interested in donations of items or services, please contact the front desk at (909) 869-4988 or arabianhorse@cpp.edu.
