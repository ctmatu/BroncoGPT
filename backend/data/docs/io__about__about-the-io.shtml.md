# About the IO

The Innovation Orchard includes an open-space environment for the startup companies within the space to![image of io lab](../img/about/27.jpg) disperse ideas, energy, and collaborative power to our entrepreneurial ecosystem. These companies have access to many of the amenities a business needs during its growth, including a conference room and general office space. The IO will also house equipment for prototyping and product creation.
