## Accessibility Assistance

The Liberal Studies Department values the abilities of our diverse communities and recognizes the accessibility limitations of the website communication format.
Should you experience an accessibility issue or need an alternative accessible format, please contact us at accessibility@cpp.edu for help with your concern.
You may also contact the IT Service Desk at 909-869-6776 during business hours.
