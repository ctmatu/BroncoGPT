# Minor in CS

The CS department offers a minor in computer science. Please check the [university catalog](https://catalog.cpp.edu/index.php) for the detailed requirements.

* [Computer Science Minor](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=65&poid=17143&hl=%22cs+3310%22&returnto=search) (29 units) (Current Catalog)
* [CS Minor Flowchart](2023-24-cs-minor.pdf) (updated Oct. 2023)

Our lower division CS courses are open to all non-CS majors. You can sign up for the CS courses if you meet the prerequisites. I would recommend you to start with CS 1300 and CS 1400 if you are eligible for MAT 1140 (Calculus I).

> How to [declare a minor in CS](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/directories/declaring-a-minor.shtml)?
