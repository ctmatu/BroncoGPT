## Application Workshop Sessions

These Application Workshops are designed to help you approach your graduate business application with clarity and confidence.

Whether you are preparing to apply to one of our eight graduate business programs or are already working on your application, this virtual workshop will guide you through the admissions process and highlight what you need to complete your submission.

During these sessions, we will cover application requirements, timelines, and best practices, along with program-specific considerations. You will also have the opportunity to ask questions and receive practical guidance directly from our admissions team.

We look forward to supporting you as you take the next step in your graduate journey.
