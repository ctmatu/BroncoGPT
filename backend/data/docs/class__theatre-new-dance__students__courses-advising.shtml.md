## We are here for you!

### Advising for Theatre & Dance Students

All Department Majors and Minors are required to meet with their designated Advisor in the area of their option.

![Two students perform in the department production of Skin of Our Teeth](../img/courses/courses1.png)

## How To Find An Advisor

General information about Advising can be found on the [Department’s Canvas site](https://canvas.cpp.edu/courses/112605), which is accessible to majors and minors. Students who are not yet a major or minor can contact the Department Chair, Sarah Krainin, to learn more.

Advisors for the Theatre & Dance Department: Bernardo Solano, Sarah Krainin, Jesse Portillo, Brian Newman, Joshua Truett, Sayda Trujillo and Laura Epperson. [Visit the Faculty Contact](../about/faculty-staff.shtml) Page to reach out.
