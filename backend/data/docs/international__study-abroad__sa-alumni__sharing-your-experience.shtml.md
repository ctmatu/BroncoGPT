# Sharing Your Experience

### Broncos Abroad

Welcome back to Pomona! As you start to unpack your abroad experience, know that you have opportunities to stay connected with the Office for Study Abroad and to continue sharing your story.

## Stay Connected With the Office of Study Abroad

### Tell us your story

As participants return from their experiences abroad, the Office of Study Abroad invites students to share their stories and photos based on their program. First-person accounts include topics ranging from unique events and encounters with the local cultures to the surprising personal and professional benefits they obtained as a result of their program. The stories we share are amazing, inspirational, and provide a wide variety of perspectives. Do you have a story to tell?

### Photo Contest

The Office for Study abroad holds annual photo, essay, and video contests.

[Learn more about our photo contest](photo-contest.shtml)

### Study Abroad Conferences (Coming Soon)

The Lessons From Abroad Conference features presentations from Study Abroad returnees and professionals.

### Global Ambassadors

CPP Global Ambassadors is a CPP student organization created to connect returning students with future participants. GA’s also provide resources (unpacking workshop, diversity series, etc.) for study abroad returnees that allow them to continue their study abroad experience when they return to CPP.

[Learn more about our Global Ambassadors](global-ambassadors.shtml)
