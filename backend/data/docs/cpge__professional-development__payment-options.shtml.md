# Payment Options

## Payment Options

Payment for professional development courses is done at the time of registration. Students may pay for classes with a credit/debit card or pay by e-check using their bank account.

Credit cards accepted include: VISA, MasterCard, American Express.

## **Discounts**

A 10% discount is offered on all non-credit courses for the following individuals:

* Current members of the Cal Poly Pomona Alumni Association (with valid ID).\*
* Current Cal Poly Pomona Faculty/Staff (with valid ID).\*

Discounts and promotions are not applicable for courses offered through our partner organizations (Ed2Go, Cengage Learning, etc).

**\*Tuition discounts and fee waivers cannot be extended to children or other family members.**
