# Give to Cal Poly Pomona

## Support Student Affairs

The [Division of Student Affairs](http://www.cpp.edu/~student-affairs/) supports the university's educational mission through comprehensive programs and services that promote student learning and success while fostering an inclusive and vibrant campus community. We partner with other stakeholders to enroll a talented and diverse student body, enhance student engagement and development, promote the safety and general welfare of the university community, and prepare students to be contributing members of a democratic society.

**Funding Priorities**

* Investment in the [Renaissance Scholars program](https://www.cpp.edu/ssep/renscholars.shtml)
* Creation of a new [Rose Float lab](http://www.cpp.edu/~rosefloatlab/index.shtml)
* Investment in the [Cultural Centers](https://www.cpp.edu/tree/) program
* Investment in the [Career Center](http://www.cpp.edu/~career/) program
* Investment in the [Veterans Resource Center](http://www.cpp.edu/veterans)
* Investment in the [Bronco Cares Basic Needs](https://www.cpp.edu/broncoscarecenter/) program
* Investment in [Educational Opportunities Program (EOP)](https://www.cpp.edu/ssep/eop-home.shtml)
