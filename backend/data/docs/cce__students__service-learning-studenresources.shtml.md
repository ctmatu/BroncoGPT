## Enrolling in a Service-Learning Course

Service-Learning brings to life the subject matter of a course by involving students in some "real world" activity directly related to what is being learned. Several courses at CPP include Service-Learning as part of their course curriculum.

Contact your academic advisor learn more about course requirements, prerequisites, supplemental department forms/processes, recommended internship placement sites, major-specific internship credit limitations, and other pertinent information.

Service Learning Policy: [COVID-19 Experiental Learning Summer 2021/Fall2021 Guidlines in Accordance with EO #88315133](https://documentcloud.adobe.com/link/review?uri=urn:aaid:scds:US:727f2dd1-5507-4f24-a1d5-260608805937)

## Searching for a Service-Learning Site

Upon the start of the course your instructor will provide you with a syllabus and may have specific sites preselected for you. For additional information regarding these preselected sites log into [BroncoSERV](../learn-by-doing-good/broncoserv.shtml) Database.

Service-Learning can only be performed at sites which have an active agreement and are in our database. All approved sites are reflected on the BroncoSERV Database.

* Therefore, students must select a Service-Learning site from the database.
* Also, please remember that although all sites in the database are reviewed by our office your Instructor has the final approval on if a service-learning site is appropriate for the course.
* After you have registered for the service-learning course, you will have access to [BroncoSERV](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/learn-by-doing-good/broncoserv.shtml) database at the beginning of the term of your service-learing course. You will now be able to view your course, search for your service-learning site and once you have found your service-learning site you can now “place” and complete the required student forms.

## Completing a Placement for my Course

If you are in a service-learning course, you will need to create an service-learning placement. For guidance on how to create a placement please see the resources below:

* Follow the [step-by-step](https://documentcloud.adobe.com/link/track?uri=urn:aaid:scds:US:25b97bf2-526d-4493-8228-c0e711caecd1) guidelines to complete your placement.
* Review the Service-Learning [Student Handbook](https://documentcloud.adobe.com/link/track?uri=urn:aaid:scds:US:35630d14-4aa1-4d74-be1f-25deb4d48568).

Also see: [Frequently Asked Questions](service-learning-faqs.shtml).
