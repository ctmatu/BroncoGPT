### Start-Up Expo

**Start-Up Expo is designed to offer students the opportunity to discover and interact with small startup companies while at the same time allowing students that are starting their own startup to perform market research. The overall mission of the Startup Expo to raise awareness and enlight the entrepreneurial ecosystem! Come and check out our student Innovators & Entrepreneurs!**

[Learn More](https://www.cpp.edu/~ilab/launch/startupexpo/index.shtml)

![Startup expo](../img/launch/startupexpo/2019/startupexpo.jpg)

### Startup Summer Boot Camp

**The Startup Summer Boot Camp is an 8-week program for CPP Startup teams to further develop their startup concept/venture. The boot camp concludes with Demo Day, which provides a platform for the startup teams to showcase their ventures, pitch their startup concepts, and receive constructive feedback from a panel of experts.**

[Learn More](http://www.cpp.edu/~ilab/launch/summer-boot-camp/index.shtml)

![Summer Boot Camp Banner](../img/landing-page/summer-accelerator-landing.JPG)
