# Centers

## Mitchell C. Hill Center for Digital Innovation

The Mitchell C. Hill Memorial Endowment will support higher education initiatives and the Mitchell C. Hill Center for Advanced Business Information Technology. The endowment has a primary goal of raising $2.5 million to honor Mitch’s global impact integrating technology solutions into the modern business world.

### Center Purpose

1. Provide a unique, student-run, high technology center where great ideas, diversity and learning through experimentation are highly valued.
2. Offer competition support to the cyber education community, research support to faculty and interesting projects for CPP students.
3. Engage industry partners in experimental projects.

[Visit Mitchell C. Hill Center for Digital Innovation Page](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/digital-innovation/index.shtml)
