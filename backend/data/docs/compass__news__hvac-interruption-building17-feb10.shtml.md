# HVAC Interruption on Second Floor of Building 17

**Summary of Disruption:**The HVAC (heating ventilation and air conditioning) system that serves the west wing of the second floor of Building 17 (Engineering Laboratories) is temporarily out of service. Repairs are underway and the HVAC system will be restored as soon as possible.

**Reason for Disruption:**Contractor crews are repairing supply fans that serve the second floor.

**What do I need to do?** Please share this information with others in the building who may not have received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:**Facilities Customer Service (Ext. 3030).
