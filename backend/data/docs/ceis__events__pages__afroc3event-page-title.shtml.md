![Group of students holding up their hands standing and smiling for a group photo. ](../img3/cohort1vertical1.jpg)

## Where Imagination, Equity, and Innovation Meet

The CEIS Afrofuturism Initiative at Cal Poly Pomona intentionally welcomes participants regardless of race, sex, color, ethnicity, or national origin and is designed for students passionate about advancing equity and reimagining the future. It empowers Student Fellows to create visionary projects that explore liberation, belonging, creativity, innovation, and systems change in education.
