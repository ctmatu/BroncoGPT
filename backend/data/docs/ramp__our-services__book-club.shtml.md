# Book Club

## Exploring Books, Building Community

![2017 RAMP Book Club members](../img/f17bookclub.jpg)

Book Club provides a forum for interesting conversations about good books. Book Club meets four times per term. The first meeting of the term welcomes new and returning members and introduces the current reading selection. The remaining three meetings are open group discussions focused on specific pages of the book.

Book Club reads a different book each term. The fall book is selected in collaboration with [CPP Common Read](https://www.cpp.edu/~studentsuccess/oss/fye/common-read-events.shtml). The spring book is selected by vote during the last meeting of fall term.

RAMP provides a limited number of books on a first-come, first-serve basis to program participants who plan on attending Book Club meetings. If you have any book suggestions, please feel free to share them with us!

For more information or to be added to the Book Club email list, please email [ramp@cpp.edu](https://mail.google.com/mail/?view=cm&fs=1&tf=1&to=ramp@cpp.edu).
