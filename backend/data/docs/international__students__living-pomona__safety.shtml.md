# Safety

## University Police Department

Being safe is an important part of having a successful study experience at Cal Poly Pomona. The [University Police Department](https://www.cpp.edu/police/index.shtml) at CPP is a full-service, sworn police agency that operates 24 hours a day, year round. The department provides law enforcement, emergency response, conducts criminal investigations, offers crime prevention and educational programs, support for special events and a range of other services.

### Public Safety is a Shared Responsibility

We all play a role in maintaining a safe campus by taking responsibility for our own safety and looking out for one another. Report any suspicious activity to the University Police Department right away. "If you see something, say something".

## Who to Contact

For **non-urgent safety** related questions, please call the University Police's
 [non-emergency number](https://www.cpp.edu/police/contact.shtml).

For **Police, Medical, Fire or other emergencies**, Dial **9-1-1**from any campus or blue emergency phone. If using a cell phone, dial **(909) 869-3070**. Program this number in your cell phone for easy use in an emergency.

## Definition of an Emergency

An emergency is a serious, unexpected, and sometimes dangerous situation requiring immediate action. Some examples of emergencies:

* A person with a medical condition requiring immediate attention
* Someone experiencing a severe mental health episode
* Robbery or theft
* Physical or sexual assault
* Fire

If you are unsure, or if someone or something is making you feel unsafe and scared for your well-being, please call University Police Department and they will help you determine the next steps.
