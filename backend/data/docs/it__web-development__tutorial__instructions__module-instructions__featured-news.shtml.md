# News Module Tutorial

## Instructions

In this News Module, a display module is required to show the news list. If your department doesn't already have one, please contact the [Web Team](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=80cc3358db951c50e00f785e0f961996&sysparm_category=d571b2f26f84820025ce9e4eae3ee482) to request it.

***Page View***

![](../img/news-shtml.jpg)This entire section (called Featured News) and the section after it (called Featured Events) are optional and have identical requirements. If you choose to use one or both, you will need:

 · 1 Image
 . 1 Heading
 · 1 Description
 · 1 Link
 . A minimum of 1 and a maximum of 3 small images, titles, subtitles and links
