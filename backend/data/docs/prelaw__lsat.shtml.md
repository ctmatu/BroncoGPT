## LSAT Fee Waiver

The Law School Admissions Council fee waiver program is designed for law school candidates who are financially underresourced, with the goal of increasing equity and access to legal education.
For additional information about the fee waiver program, eligibility requirements, deadlines, and how to apply visit the LSAC fee waiver program page.

[the LSAC Fee Waiver Program Page](https://www.lsac.org/lsat/register-lsat/lsat-cas-fees/fee-waiver%20 "opens a new window")
