## General Admission Requirements

The following admissions requirements outline the academic preparation, prerequisites, and application criteria for the Online Master of Science in Dietetics at Cal Poly Pomona. To be admitted to the **Online Master of Science in Dietetics** program, applicants must:

* hold a baccalaureate degree from an institution accredited by a regional accrediting association or have completed equivalent academic preparation as determined by an appropriate campus authority
* have attained a grade point average of at least 2.5 or higher in the last 60 semester units (90 quarter units) attempted; and have been in good standing at the last college attended
* Applicants must provide two letters of recommendation

## Admission Requirements for International Students

Due to the program being fully online, applicants from outside the United States should note that Cal Poly Pomona cannot sponsor the F-1 visa for international students.

In addition, all applicants must have completed all the prerequisite courses and submit a verification statement from an accredited university that has been approved by ACEND in order to apply.

## Prerequisites Courses

We will accept a Verification Statement from an Accreditation Council for Education in Nutrition and Dietetics (ACEND) accredited Didactic Program in Dietetics (DPD) from anywhere in the USA in lieu of the following California State Polytechnic University Pomona - Department of Nutrition and Food Science Classes:

* Biochemistry
* Microbiology
* Food and Culture
* Nutrition of the Life Cycle
* Nutrition Education and Counseling
* Institutional Food Service
* Food Service Management/Administration
* Advanced Nutrient Metabolism
* Medical Nutrition Therapy
* Community Nutrition

OR a DPD verification statement. **Learn about our [Prerequisite Courses (PDF)](../pdfs/prerequisite-classes-accessible.pdf "Prerequisite Courses").**
