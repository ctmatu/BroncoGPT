## About Me

David has a 30-year career in operations, supply chain, and logistics leadership, serving in regional and national management positions with Panasonic North America, Toshiba America, as well the third-party logistics sector. He serves on the Board of Directors for the Council of Supply Chain Management Professionals (CSCMP), Southern California Round Table, and on the Operations and Supply Chain Management Advisory Board at CSU, Fullerton. Education includes an MBA in International Business from Texas A&M, and a B.A. in Business/Accounting from CSU, Fullerton. In addition, David is a licensed U.S. Customs Broker, as well as Lean Six Sigma Green Belt certified. Currently, David is serving as an Adjunct Professor of Business with both CSU, Fullerton, and Cal Poly Pomona.
