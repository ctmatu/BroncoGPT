# Contact Information

The CFA-Pomona office is located in the Library, building 15, 2nd floor, room 2334.

**General Inquiries:** contact the office via email.

**Faculty Rights Questions or Concerns:** contact the Faculty Rights Committee via email.

**Membership Questions**: contact the Membership Committee via email.

**CFA Statewide**: [visit our chapter website.](https://www.calfac.org/contact-us/)

**Department Representatives:**Your department may have a representative to our chapter of the CFA. You may contact them with any questions or concerns you may have.
