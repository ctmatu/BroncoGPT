# Document Resource Center

The center maintains, files and stores campus construction blueprints, specifications, utility infrastructure and electronic architectural media. The files contain more than 7,000 pages of construction drawings dating to 1927 along with copies of the original drawings of the W.K. Kellogg mansion. The center provides blueprints or copies of existing buildings and utilities plans to faculty, students, staff, outside contractors and engineering consultants.

For further information, call (909) 869-2988 or email fpmdrc@cpp.edu
