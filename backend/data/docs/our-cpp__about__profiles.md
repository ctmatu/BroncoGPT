# OUR Research Profiles

---

## The Office of Undergraduate Research (OUR) creates profiles to foster an interconnected community of research, scholarly, and creative activities participants at Cal Poly Pomona.

---

> The goal of creating both the faculty and student profiles is to showcase the diverse selection of contributors in research within our campus. Research embodies the Cal Poly Pomona ideology of “Learning by Doing". Whether faculty mentors or student researchers, we aim to give more visibility to the extensive network of research involvement at Cal Poly Pomona. We aspire to provide inspiration for research engagement as more individuals gain a better understanding of current or past research, scholarly, and creative activities.

## ASP Researcher Spotlight

The ASP Research Spotlight provides examples of exciting new research conducted by undergraduate students in the [Achieve Scholars Program](../../students/asp/index.shtml). These students are paving the way for the future by being actively involved in high-impact practices and sharing their success with other students, representing the university by presenting at conferences, and mentoring other students.

## STARS Profiles

The Faculty Mentor Research Stars ([STARS](../../mentors/stars/index.shtml)) recognizes faculty members who have engaged students seeking a Learning-by-Doing experience at Cal Poly Pomona. These faculty members have helped mentor their students to conduct, present, and publish research; all the requirements for the [Student Research Distinction](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/programs-awards/research-distinction/index.shtml).
