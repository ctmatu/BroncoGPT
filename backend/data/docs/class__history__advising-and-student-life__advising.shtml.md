## Special History Advising

*Department Chair*

History majors should meet with the History Department chair (Dr. Rob Lewis – rwlewis@cpp.edu) in all of the following cases:

* You are a new student, or would like to switch to the History major
* Your department advisor is on leave
* You have received a notification that your academic standing is any of the following: Academic Warning, Academic Notice, Academic Notice with Contract, Administrative Academic Notice, or Disqualification
* You are part of the Four-Year Pledge Program, receive Veterans benefits, or are part of the ROTC Program
* You are part of the Kellogg Honors College

*Pre-Credential Advisor*

All students who have selected the Pre-Credential Option of the major should consult with Dr. Michael Slaughter (maslaughter@cpp.edu).

*Graduate advisor*

History graduate students should meet with Dr. David DeVore (ddevore@cpp.edu)
