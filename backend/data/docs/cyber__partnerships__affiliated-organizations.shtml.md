# Affiliated Organizations

Cal Poly Pomona offers a breadth of partnership opportunities for organizations and companies to support the Cyber Collaborative. This includes ensuring our students' success through scholarships, internship and job offers, and project opportunities that allow students to work on projects and present to company executives on solutions to real issues.

Current partnerships (partial list):

* Avanade
* Boeing
* DHS
* Intel
* Juniper
* Microsoft
* National Science Foundation
* National Security Agency
* Northrop Grumman
* Sandia National Laboratories
