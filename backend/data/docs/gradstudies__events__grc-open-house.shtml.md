# GRADUATE RESOURCE CENTER OPEN HOUSE

The Graduate Studies Office hosted the Graduate Resource Center Center Open House, which took place on February 28, 2019, from 5:30-6:30 pm, in the Faculty Center Reading Room. Faculty and students were greeted with evening snacks and received a warm welcome from Dr. Salomon Oldak, our Director of Graduate Studies, and Dr. Laura Massa, AVP of Academic Programs.  Students were informed of tutoring services available to them for their projects and theses. This was followed by a demo explaining the usage of statistics to theses by Dr. Carsten Lange, professor in Economics.
