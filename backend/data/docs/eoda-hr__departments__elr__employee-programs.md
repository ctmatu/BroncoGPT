## Employee Programs

### Represented Staff

Represented staff are employees who are represented by a collective bargaining agreement (CBA).

* [Collective Bargaining Agreements](https://www.calstate.edu/csu-system/faculty-staff/labor-and-employee-relations/Pages/collective-bargaining-agreements.aspx)
  + Unit 1 - (UAPD) Union of American Physicians and Dentists
  + Unit 2, 5, 7 & 9 - (CSUEU) California State University Employee Union
  + Unit 4 - (APC) Academic Professionals of California
  + Unit 6 - Teamsters Local 2010
  + Unit 8 - (SUPA) Statewide University Police Association
* [Bargaining Updates](https://www2.calstate.edu/csu-system/faculty-staff/labor-and-employee-relations/Pages/bargaining-updates.aspx)
* [Tentative Agreements](https://www2.calstate.edu/csu-system/faculty-staff/labor-and-employee-relations/Pages/tentative-agreements.aspx)
* [Campus Union Representatives](campus-union-rep.shtml)

Please note that [Faculty Affairs](https://www.cpp.edu/faculty-affairs/index.shtml) handles all contract matters and grievances related to Academic Personnel, which includes [Faculty (Unit 3)](https://www.calstate.edu/csu-system/faculty-staff/labor-and-employee-relations/Pages/unit3-cfa.aspx) and [Academic Student Employees (Unit 11)](https://www.calstate.edu/csu-system/faculty-staff/labor-and-employee-relations/Pages/unit11-uaw.aspx).

### Non-Represented Staff

Non-Represented staff are employees who are not represented by an exclusive bargaining representative.

* [MPP (Management Personnel Plan)](https://calstate.policystat.com/policy/14430159/latest/)
* [Confidentials (C99)](https://csyou.calstate.edu/Policies/HRPolicies/HR2018-06.pdf)
