## Admissions Information

*International students are foreign non-resident students who require an F-1, J-1 or other non-immigrant visa to study in the United States.*

California State Polytechnic University, Pomona (Cal Poly Pomona) is 1 of 23 universities in the CSU System and 1 of 3 polytechnic universities in California.  The cornerstone of Cal Poly Pomona’s strong academic tradition is the polytechnic, “learn-by-doing” philosophy. We are the academic home to hundreds of international students from over 30 different countries!
