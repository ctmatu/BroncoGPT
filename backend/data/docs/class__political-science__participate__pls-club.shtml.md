# Political Science Club

## Background and Information

The Political Science Club represents majors and those who enjoy politics. The club publicizes career opportunities for majors, offers post-graduate advising, and contribute to the campus and surrounding communities. The club holds weekly meetings where they discuss these opportunities and offer members the opportunity to discuss current events.

[Visit the PLS Club Instagram!](https://www.instagram.com/calpolisci "Visit the PLS Club Instagram")
