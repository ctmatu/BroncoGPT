# Jennifer Morton on Zoom

September 18, 2020

![Photo of Jennifer Morton](../../img/jennifer-morton.jpg)[Jennifer Morton](http://jennifermmorton.com/) (University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill) speaks about the Ethical Costs of Upward Mobility on September 18th from 1pm-2:30pm.

Dr. Morton is associate professor of philosophy and a senior fellow at the [Center for Ethics and Education at the University of Wisconsin-Madison](http://ethicsandeducation.wceruw.org/). Her areas of research are philosophy of action, moral philosophy, philosophy of education, and political philosophy. She is the author of [*Moving Up Without Losing Your Way: The Ethical Costs of Upward Mobility* (Princeton 2019).](https://press.princeton.edu/books/hardcover/9780691179230/moving-up-without-losing-your-way)

Email Alex Madva for the Zoom link.
