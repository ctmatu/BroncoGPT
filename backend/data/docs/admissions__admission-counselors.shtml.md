# Admissions Counselors' Corner

## Welcome Future Broncos!

Admission Counselors are here to help support you after you have applied to Cal Poly Pomona, with the returning student process or international advising. We offer an array of opportunities to connect with us via counseling appointments, webinars and e-mail.
Domestic prospective applicants seeking pre-admission advising should make an appointment with an Outreach Counselor.

[make an appointment with an Outreach Counselor](https://engage.cpp.edu/portal/outreach_appointments%20)
