## Program Overview

The Online Master of Interior Architecture (M. INT. ARCH.) is offered by the College of Environmental Design and the College of Professional and Global Education and is housed in the Department of Architecture at Cal Poly Pomona. The primary goal of the Master’s program is to provide an opportunity for individuals with a minimum of a bachelor’s degree to pursue a rigorous program of part-time study that prepares them to enter the field of interior design incorporating the highest standards of professional practice. The program particularly serves career-change students seeking to fulfill a lifelong dream of becoming interior design professionals.
