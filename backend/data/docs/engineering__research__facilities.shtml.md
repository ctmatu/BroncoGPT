## Off-Campus Resources

**California Aerospace Technologies Institute of Excellence (CATIE)**

A research collaboration with Cal Poly Pomona and [California Aerospace Technologies Institute of Excellence (CATIE)](https://catie.avc.edu/catie) and aerospace [industry partners](https://catie.avc.edu/catie/industry-partners), which includes the Air Force. Cal Poly Pomona's faculty and research members team up with CATIE for grants and funding opportunities as well as to participate in joint publications and public educational seminars. CATIE collaborates with the US Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL), other government agencies and laboratories, universities, and industry to promote leading research and technology development in space exploration, aeronautics and science.
