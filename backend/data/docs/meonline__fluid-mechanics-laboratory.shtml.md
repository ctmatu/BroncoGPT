# Thermal-Fluids Laboratory (ME 3131L)

![balance](/meonline/img/me313l-3_20160305_111637.jpg)

In this course, students get hands-on experience with pumps, flowrate measurement devices, and other equipment used in industry. Below are instructional videos for some of the experimental procedures.
