# AQMD Survey

All State faculty, staff, student assistants (who work more than 4 or more hours this week only), Foundation, and ASI employees at Cal Poly Pomona are required to complete an Employee Commute Survey.  The survey keeps the campus in compliance with Rule 2202 of the South Coast Air Quality Management District.  This survey tracks how employees travel to campus over a five-day (Monday through Friday) period.  The survey yields an Average Vehicle Ridership number and measures the effectiveness of Cal Poly Pomona’s Rideshare Program.

The survey takes approximately 2 minutes to complete.

If you have any questions or comments about the survey please contact your department representative or rideshare@cpp.edu. This survey must be completed even if you are not currently in the Rideshare Program. If you would like to know more about the Rideshare Program please contact Dave Flores.

Thank you for your participation. Your feedback will assist us in improving our campus Rideshare Program and incentives.
