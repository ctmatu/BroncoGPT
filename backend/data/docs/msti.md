# MSTI Programs

MSTI seeks to encourage talented Science and Mathematics majors who are considering secondary teaching. Under the MSTI Program there are several options for support: awards, CSET preparation, additional credential support, [monthly seminars](monthly-seminars/index.shtml), and student learning assistant opportunities.

[## MSTI Scholar Award

For Cal Poly Pomona Students

Award: up to $10,000 ($2,500 per semester)

To support Science and Mathematics majors who are seeking a single subject credential in Biology, Chemistry, Geology, Mathematics, and Physics.](https://www.cpp.edu/msti/awards-and-programs/msti-scholar.shtml)

[## MSTI STEM Authorization Award

For Cal Poly Pomona Students

Award: up to $1,000 for Math or Science Supplementary Authorizations (CPP students)

To support students to obtain their Supplementary or Subject Matter Authorizations in  Math or Science.](https://www.cpp.edu/msti/awards-and-programs/msti-stem.shtml)

[## Partners in Education

For Cal Poly Pomona Students

Award: up to $5,000 per year

The organization raises money to support teacher candidates while completing their Clinical Practice.](https://www.cpp.edu/msti/awards-and-programs/pie.shtml)

[## Foundational Level General Science Credential

For Cal Poly Pomona Students and Multiple Subject Credentialed Teachers

For students who are interested in obtaining their Foundational Level General Science Credential.](https://www.cpp.edu/msti/awards-and-programs/flgs.shtml)
