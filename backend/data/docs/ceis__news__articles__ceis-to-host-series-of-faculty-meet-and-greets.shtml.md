# CEIS to Host Series of Faculty Meet and Greets

September 28, 2021

![two professors](../img/meet-and-greets.jpg)The College of Education and Integrative Studies will kick off its virtual faculty meet and greets program on **Thursday, Sept. 30** at noon with Department of Liberal Studies Assistant Professor Maya Stovall.

"Who’s Who in CEIS” aims to provide students with an opportunity to meet faculty and learn about their journey into higher education, research projects underway, favorite campus events, strategies for success and more. Each session will be moderated by CEIS Associate Dean Joanne Van Boxtel.

"I want to thank the advising team and our outstanding faculty for this collaborative effort,” said Van Boxtel. "I am looking forward to facilitating a way for students and faculty to connect on a more personal level.”

The schedule is as follows:

**Sept. 30** – Assistant Professor Maya Stovall, Department of Liberal Studies

**Oct. 14** – Associate Professor Jose Aguilar-Hernández

**Oct. 28** – Rosanne Welch, lecturer, Department of Interdisciplinary General Education

**Nov. 18** – Associate Professor Giselle Navarro-Cruz, Department of Early Childhood Studies

**Dec. 2** – Assistant Professor Jeff Roy, Department of Liberal Studies

The program will also include opportunity drawings at each event. To register and receive a meeting link, please visit CEIS Advising’s [Eventbrite page](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.eventbrite.com%2Fe%2Fwhos-who-in-ceis-tickets-173820912027%3Faff%3Dceis&data=04%7C01%7Cashleyjones%40cpp.edu%7C88a1bed694664cf3f36d08d9820f235d%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C637683823788271240%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C1000&sdata=E4N%2FqwR1%2FkmEenZii9t7F%2FtZD0oBLoMu0SdZ5Opq5IM%3D&reserved=0).

"In addition to supporting our students as they make progress toward their degrees, it’s also important to help them build a holistic sense of fulfillment,” said CEIS Dean Hend Gilli-Elewy. "We are proud to host these series of events to support our mission of placing student success and well-being at the center of what we do."
