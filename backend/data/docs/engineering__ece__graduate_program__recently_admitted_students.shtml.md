# Recently Admitted Students

## Conditionally Admitted Students

Students who are conditionally admitted need to select a total of 13 units worth of classes by filling out the attached form. After completing this requirement, the students will be granted full MSEE status. Thus, they will need to select classes from the link below and also see the corresponding page fully admitted MSEE students.

[MSEE Program Classes](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=65&poid=17057&returnto=5344)

[Incoming Conditional Form](../documents/msee-program/incoming_conditional_form.pdf)

### Unconditionally Admitted Students

Students who are unconditionally admitted need to select classes from the link below and according to schedule.

[MSEE Program Classes and Requirements](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=65&poid=17057&returnto=5344)
