# The Teagle Foundation’s Cornerstone Award for “The Faces Of Freedom”

## CLASS to Launch The Democracy Institute and GIFT Project, Expanding Global Justice and Civic Leadership Programs

![Ahimsa Center Director Dr. Aishwary Kumar speaking at the Inaugural Symposium of The Democracy Institute in Spring 2023](../img/news-images/the-faces-of-freedom-1.jpg)

CLASS will be launching [The Democracy Institute](https://www.thedemocracyinstitute.org/ "The Democracy Institute") and its award-winning GIFT Project (Global Inquiries in Freedom and Tyranny) in Fall 2024 with 10 CLASS faculty, including Ahimsa Center affiliates, offering the GIFT flagship seminar on *The Faces of Freedom*. The work of the Institute is made possible by The Teagle Foundation's Cornerstone Award given to Ahimsa Center Director Dr. Aishwary Kumar’s blueprint for *Democratic Visions: The American Institutions Common Core.*The project marks the beginning of the new degree pathway in Global Justice and the establishment of the Liberal Strategies Leadership Initiative at Cal Poly Pomona.
