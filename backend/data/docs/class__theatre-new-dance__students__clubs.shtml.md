## Be part of student-produced theatre!

### Actors Collaborating with Technicians (ACT)

A.C.T. is a club that aims to create a stronger bond between actors and technicians in order to create beautiful art! Open to all, A.C.T. welcomes all majors and minors and hopes they will find a home and family in the environment the club creates. The club puts on an annual musical which anyone can audition for in order to give those who have or may have a passion for acting, dancing, or music...

## January 30-February 6, 2026

### A.C.T's Seussical: The Musical

## [![Suessical Poster](../img/show-posters/suessicalposter-actclub.jpeg)](https://www.tix.com/ticket-sales/tix/3530)

***Seussical*** is a joyful celebration of imagination, brought to life by the Actors Collaborating with Technicians Club. This fully student-run production is a vibrant, family-friendly musical that brings the magical world of Dr. Seuss to life. It features the Cat in the Hat, the Grinch, and Horton the Elephant as he protects the tiny planet of Whoville! The show is filled with colorful characters, high-energy music, and playful storytelling. It explores themes of kindness, loyalty, and speaking up for what is right.
