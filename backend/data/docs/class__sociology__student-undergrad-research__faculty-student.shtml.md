# Faculty-Student Research

The department offers students the opportunity to do research and work individually or in a student-group with faculty. Current and ongoing research projects are listed below by the areas/emphasis offered in the department. Students interested in becoming involved in Faculty-Student research/projects are asked to commit at least one full semester (depending on the research tasks). Engaging in Faculty-Student research is particularly valuable for students who wish to go to graduate school or students who wish to improve their research skills.
