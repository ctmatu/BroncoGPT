# MSA Requirements

## Admission to this program is based on a holistic view of the following criteria:

1. ### Resume

   (upload it to Cal State Apply website)
2. ### Personal Statement

   (upload it to Cal State Apply website)
3. ### 4-year Bachelor's Degree (2.7 or higher GPA). Upload your transcripts to Cal State Apply website and send your Official Transcripts in one of the following three ways:

   If sending official transcripts in PDF format, please indicate the recipient email is verify@cpp.edu
   If your sending institution does not offer electronic transcripts, we recommend that students use the following services:
   National Student Clearinghouse
   Credential Solutions
   Should these services not be available for your sending intuition, please mail to:
   California State Polytechnic University, Pomona
   Admissions & Enrollment Planning
   3801 West Temple Avenue
   Pomona, CA 91768
4. ### GMAT/GRE Scores

   GMAT or GRE is not required for Fall 2025 and Spring 2026 admission.
5. ### English Proficiency Test only for applicants with prior degrees not conducted in English

   TOEFL (if required) 213 Computer Based, 550 Paper Based, 80 Internet Based, or
   IELTS 6.5, or
   Graduate Minimum Duolingo Score: 105 (MA English requires 120)
   Cal Poly Pomona English Language Institute (CPELI) offers English learning programs. CPELI level 6 is acceptable for satisfying CPP English proficiency requirement of SGSB graduate program admission purpose.

## Follow Up

Once you have submitted your application online and paid the fee, you should receive correspondence from [Admissions & Enrollment Planning](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions) with a Bronco ID number. You can use that ID number to login to your Bronco Direct account and check on the status of your application. **It is the applicant’s responsibility to check his/her account and follow up** with both [Admissions & Enrollment Planning](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions) and the Graduate Business Administration office to see that all required documents are received prior to the deadline dates.

## General Admissions Requirements for International Students

For international applicants, the same general admissions requirements as stated above apply. However, international applicants are also required to provide the following information in addition to the CSU application:
1) Affidavit of Financial Support
2) Agreement to Purchase Cal Poly Pomona Group Health Insurance
3) Supplementary Information of International (VISA) applicants
Please contact [Admissions & Enrollment](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/) for more information on international admissions.

For more information about graduate student application requirements, follow the link to the [Main Admissions office](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/graduate/masters-requirements.shtml).

[Apply](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/graduate)

## Accounting Foundation Courses

Students must earn a "C" or better in each of the following (or their equivalent) with a minimum overall GPA for the prerequisite courses of 3.0. The waiver decision will be made by the Program Coordinator for the Accelerated MSA Program.

Primarily for students with non-accounting undergraduate degrees. These courses can be waived.

* GBA 5500 Fundamentals of Accounting and Finance
* ACC 5122 Financial Accounting and Analysis (3) (Can be replaced by taking both ACC 3110 Intermediate Accounting (3) and ACC 3120 Intermediate Accounting II (3)
* ACC 5310 Introduction to Taxation (3)
* ACC 5510 Accounting Information Systems (3)
* ACC 5810 Auditing (3)
