## About The Office of Equity and Compliance (OEC)

The Office of Equity and Compliance (OEC) oversees the University's compliance with  [Federal and State laws and regulations and CSU System policies](discrimination-harassment-retaliation/policy-dhr.shtml). Part of this compliance includes overseeing reports and investigations of prohibited conduct, including:

* Discrimination, including harassment, because of any [protected status (PDF)](documents/Protected%20Categories.pdf), (i.e. age, disability, gender, genetic information, gender identity or expression, nationality, marital status, pregnancy, race or ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, and veteran or military status;)
* Retaliation for exercising rights under non-discrimination policies, opposing discrimination or harassment because of a protected status, or for participating in any manner in any related investigation or proceeding;
* Sexual misconduct which includes any sexual activity engaged in without affirmative consent,  dating and domestic violence, and stalking.
* Sexual exploitation

Other aspects of compliance that the OEC oversees includes:

* [Religious accommodation](discrimination-harassment-retaliation/religious-accom1.shtml) requests from students and employees
* [Consensual Relationship Policy](titleix/terms-definitions.shtml)
* [Pregnancy/Parental and nursing accommodation](resources/pregnancy-nursing-parenting.shtml) requests
* [Employee](gender-identity/employee-training.shtml) and [Student](gender-identity/student-training.shtml) trainings

## Title IX Training Holds

Holds due to assigned Title IX Training will be removed within 24 hours. For urgent hold requests, please email us at officeofequity@cpp.edu. In your message please note that you have completed your training and would like your hold to be removed. Please include your full name and bronco ID.
