# Announcement

**Summer Residential Programs Rescheduled for June 2022**

We are pleased to announce that our Migrant Education Summer Residential Programs will resume in June 2022, dependent upon COVID restrictions at that time. Our exciting new curriculum includes courses such as Create Your Own Virtual Reality, Coding with Minecraft Education, and Coding with Finch. We look forward to welcoming middle and high school students back to Cal Poly Pomona where they can benefit from the college life experience while meeting new friends and gaining new STEM skills.

In the meantime, we are offering year-round Virtual STEM & Coding Programs for Migrant Education and English Learner students in K-12th grades. Details are listed below.

[Click here](summer-residential-programs.shtml) for more information on our Summer Residential Programs.

**Exciting Virtual STEM Programs Available Year-Round!**

The College of the Extended University now brings exciting virtual technology and math programs into the homes and classrooms of our students year-round!

We have transitioned key portions of our in-person residential program into a Virtual STEM & Coding Programs for Migrant Education and English Learner students, allowing participants to gain new high-tech skills and develop personalized hands-on projects.

Our live online standards-based learning is available for K–12th grade students in fully virtual or hybrid formats. All course materials are provided and instruction is offered in English and Spanish.

[Click here](virtual-stem-program.shtml) for more information on our Virtual STEM & Coding Programs
