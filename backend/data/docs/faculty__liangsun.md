## Bio

* Dr. Liang Sun is an Assistant Professor of Finance in the College of Business Administration at Cal Poly Pomona. Her research focuses on empirical corporate finance and mutual funds. Liang’s most recent research shows that pilot CEOs are associated with higher intensity of FinTech adoption. Her other papers cover a wide range of topics, from board structure to mutual fund side-by-side management.
* Prior to joining Cal Poly Pomona, Liang worked at Illinois Wesleyan University and earned her Ph.D. in Finance from the University of North Texas.
