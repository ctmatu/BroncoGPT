# Support Undergraduate Research

Undergraduate Research is an important part of the learn-by-doing education at CPP. Support this effort by sharing your stories of success related to undergraduate research. If you are interested in volunteering opportunities and other ways to support the office of Undergraduate Research, please email us at our@cpp.edu.

---

## To Make a Cash Gift

You may send a check payable to **Cal Poly Pomona Foundation** and mail to the following address:

**Cal Poly Pomona Foundation**
3801 W. Temple Ave.
Pomona, CA 91768

***Please note:***

* You must designate the program for which you are giving in the “memo” section of the check, Office of Undergraduate Research.

---

## To Make a Gift Online

You may make a gift online by using the campus’ secure online form, by clicking the [Cal Poly Pomona Advancement site](https://cpp.thankyou4caring.org/donate "Cal Poly Advancement"). Under the “Additional Information” section toward the bottom of the page, be sure to designate the Office of Undergraduate Research to receive your gift.
