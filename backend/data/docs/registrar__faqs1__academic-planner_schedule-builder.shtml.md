# Academic Planner & Schedule Builder FAQ

## FAQs

### Where can I find the Academic Planner and Schedule Builder?

For access, link [MyCPP](https://my.cpp.edu) or [Student Success Central](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/advising/planner.shtml).

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to registrar@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### How do I use my academic planner and schedule builder?

Click the link for the [New Student Registration Guide](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/guides/new-student-registration.shtml). You may also access all Academic Planner tools through [MyCPP](https://my.cpp.edu) or [Student Success Central](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/advising/planner.shtml).
