# Cal Poly Pomona Class Visits

BioTrek is an educational resource used by a number of disciplines on-campus. The BioTrek facilities accommodate instructor-led visits, but we ask that you complete a [Use of Facilities Reservation Form](facilities-reservation.shtml) prior to your self-guided visit. If you are interested in training and information about access to our facilities prior to your walkthrough, please contact the BioTrek Curator at biotrek@cpp.edu.
