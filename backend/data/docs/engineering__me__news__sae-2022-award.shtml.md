# CPP Formula SAE team win 1st place in the 2022 SoCal Shootout

![The CPP Formula SAE team ](sae_2022_win_big.jpg)

The CPP Formula SAE team

The CPP Formula SAE team won 1st place at the 3rd Annual 2022 SoCal Shootout, organized by Adams Motorsports Park. The competition focused on overall track performance. The team outperformed UC Berkeley, University of Southern California, Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, UC San Diego, UC Los Angeles, Cal State Fullerton. The team was able to validate performance as well as obtain valuable test data for future international competitions. The team is co-advised by Mechanical Engineering professors Carlos Castro and Brian Ramirez.

(Posted 10-17-2022)
