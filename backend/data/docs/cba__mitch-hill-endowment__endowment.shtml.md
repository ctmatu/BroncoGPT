# About the Endowment

The Mitchell C. Hill Memorial Endowment will support higher education initiatives and the Mitchell C. Hill Center for Advanced Business Information Technology. The endowment has a primary goal of raising $2.5 million to honor Mitch’s global impact integrating technology solutions into the modern business world.

## The Mitchell C. Hill Memorial Endowment will:

* Provide access for students to market relevant teaching in applied business information technology
* Create and support the creation of a polytechnic style curriculum
* Support research and outreach activities that promote and honor the living memory of Mitch Hill
* Provide targeted support for industry partnerships and conference presentations
* Promote involvement of women and underrepresented minorities in business information technology careers

## Mitchell C. Hill Memorial Endowment Values:

* Focus on the student and their learning experience
* Hands-on education
* Applied technology within a business context, "Get Things Done"
* Market ready, move with the pace of technology change and innovation
* Providing an environment where passionate students can realize their potential
* Provide access to women and minorities
