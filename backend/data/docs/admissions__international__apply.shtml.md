## Discover Cal Poly Pomona

California State Polytechnic University, Pomona (Cal Poly Pomona) is where careers take shape, ideas thrive, and leaders learn to lead. As one of 23 campuses in the CSU system and the No. 1 polytechnic university for economic mobility, CPP blends academic excellence with a proud tradition of “learn-by-doing.” We welcome international students to turn knowledge into real-world impact.

## Application FAQs By Student Type

Cal Poly Pomona defines international students as anyone who is a foreign non-resident student who requires an F-1, J-1 or other non-immigrant visa to study in the United States. Please note that if you attended a U.S. school with a different primary instructional language than English, regardless of citizenship, you will need to submit proof of English proficiency.

### First Time Freshmen Internationals (from U.S. high schools)

You have not previously studied at a university and are from a high school **inside** of the U.S.

[View More](freshmen.shtml "International Freshman")

### First Time Freshmen Internationals (from non-U.S. high schools)

You have not previously studied at a university and are from a high school **outside** of the U.S.

[View More](freshmen.shtml "International Freshmen")

### First Time Freshmen (U.S. Citizens from non-US high schools)

You do not need an F1 or J1 visa and have attended a high school **outside** of the U.S.

[View More](freshmen.shtml "International Freshmen")

### Transfers (Internationals from U.S. colleges/ universities)

You are currently studying at a college or university **inside** the U.S. and have at least 60 semester credits (approximately 2 years).

[View More](transfer.shtml "International Transfer")

### Transfers (Internationals from colleges/ universities outside of the U.S.)

You are currently studying at a college or university **outside** the U.S. and have at least 60 semester credits (approximately 2 years).

[View More](transfer.shtml "International Transfer")

### Masters/Graduate

You have completed, or will complete, your bachelors degree.

[View More](graduate.shtml "International Graduate")
