# Student Life and Involvement

## Student Life and Involvement

The Division of Student Affairs supports a comprehensive co-curricular experience throughout your time at CPP. Students can get involved with one of the more than 300 clubs and organizations on campus, run for ASI Student Government, join a fraternity or sorority, be part of an intramural sports league, discover their inner leader, support a cause or movement and so much more!
