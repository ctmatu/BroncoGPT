### Pirüli Design

Our goal is to remove plastic waste from our ocean by collecting and recycling. We repurpose that plastic into pellets that allow us to create sustainable streetwear that ignites conversation through fashion and expression. Our clothing is now available in the Bronco Bookstore!

[Visit Pirüli Design Website](https://pirulidesign.com/ "Opens in new window")

![](../img/startups/quantsbakery.png)

### Quants Bakery

Vegans can enjoy delicious treats with regular doorstep delivery thanks to Quants. Whether it be for convenience or nutrition, subscription-based meal delivery services are surging in popularity. Quants looks to fill a market void with its boxed vegan bakery.

[Visit Quants Bakery Website](http://quantsdesserts.com/ "Opens in new window")

![](../img/startups/azuza-bev.png)

### AZUZA Beverage

A breakthrough for lovers of craft beer wanting to enjoy their favorite brew without the spirits, AZUZA is a Brew Rx system that strips beer of the alcohol while preserving the hoppy aroma and malt backbone. In essence, beer with all the taste and none of the alcohol!

[Visit AZUZA Beverage Website](https://www.azuzabev.com/ "Open in new window")

![](../img/startups/trusted-connect.png)

### Trusted Connect

Trusted Connect is a website platform that allows you to easily upload the loss of your loved one’s utility bills and subscription information so that we can contact them for you and have them closed. We notify others of your loss, so that you can have a peace of mind and more time to grieve.

[Visit Trusted Connect Website](http://www.mytrustedconnect.com/ "Opens in new window")

![](../img/startups/fromb2c.jpg)

### FromB2C

The last mile problem is experienced by individuals who use urban transit, bus, or car to travel to their primary destination, but must still walk that last mile before reaching their final destination. B2C works to solve this problem by creating an electric scooter that will allow users to travel the last mile. By designing the product in a compact way, the scooter can easily be transported when not in use.
