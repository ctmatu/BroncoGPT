## About Me

Allyn Polancic is a registered architect and Digital Practice Leader at Arup, specializing in BIM Management, automation, complex geometric modeling, and data-driven technologies. She holds a Bachelor of Architecture from Cal Poly Pomona and has been teaching digital tools courses since 2018.
