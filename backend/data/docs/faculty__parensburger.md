## About Me

I'm a bioinformatician, I use computer science methods to answer biological questions. My students and I focus on analyzing large biological datasets (such as whole genomes) and looking for patterns. CPP students interested in participating in our group can read more about it in the Research tab. Below is an example of the type of data my lab generates.

![genome_coverag.png](genome_coverage.png)

This plot was generated by the lab when we assembled the chicken genome from sequencing data. Each row is a chromosome of the chicken and show how much of it was covered by our assembly
