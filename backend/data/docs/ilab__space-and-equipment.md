# Space & Equipment

![prototyping station](../img/space-and-equipment/prototypingstationbanner.png)

The original rapid prototyping station for Cal Poly Pomona. Utilize one of our 3D printers to see a scale model of your design. Create a 2D design to make stickers or decals for custom t-shirt designs.  You can also design a custom button to help market your next student club or department event

![](../img/space-and-equipment/prototypingstationimage.jpeg)

## Equipment

### 3D Printers

3D printing, or additive manufacturing, is the process of making a physical object from a three-dimensional digital model. This is typically done by fusing material such as plastics, liquids or powder grains together layer by layer until an object is formed.

---

**Monoprice Select Mini v2**
*Build Volume: 120 x 120 x 120 mm
Max Resolution: 100microns (1mm layer height)
Materials Provided: PLA*

**Ultimaker Extended 2+**
*Build Volume: 223 x 223 x 305 mm
Max Resolution: 20microns (.02mm layer height)
Materials Provided: PLA, ABS, PETG, TPU*

*![Monoprice printer](../img/3dprint.jpg)*

*![ultimaker printer](../img/ultimaker.jpg)*

### Button Maker

Want to make a button? All you need is to come in with a design, fix it to fit in our 2" button makers, and cut yours in the iLab today! We will provide you with all of the material. Just bring your own printed designs!

---

**Button Maker:**
*Makes 2" Diameter round pin back buttons*

*![Monoprice printer](../img/space-and-equipment/buttonmaker.jpg)*

### Heat Press

The heat press is a temperature controlled heating clamp used to apply heat transfer vinyl (HTV) to shirts, cloth bags, basically any flat object you can think of that won't melt under the heat.

---

**enduraPRESS Heat Press:**
*Heat Press Area:* 11.75" x 9" flat

*Max Temp:* 400 F

*Default Temp:* 320 F

Includes a timer

*![Monoprice printer](../img/space-and-equipment/heatpress.jpg)*
