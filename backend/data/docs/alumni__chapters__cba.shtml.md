## Mission Statement

With more than 600 active members, the College of Business Administration Alumni Chapter comprises the largest alumni chapter at Cal Poly Pomona. Our members are some of the most successful people in Southern California and beyond. The CBA Alumni Chapter provides social networking engagement opportunities and career advancement workshops.
