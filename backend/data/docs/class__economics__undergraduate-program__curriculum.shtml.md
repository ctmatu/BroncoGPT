# Curriculum

![Curriculum Clip Art](/class/economics/img/curriculum.png)

The Economics Department offers Three Options:

1. General
2. Applied
3. Quantitative

Courses required of all Economics Majors, in any of the three Options include:

* STA 1200 – Statistics with Applications (3 units)
* ACC 2070 – Financial Accounting (3 units)
* EC 2201 – Principles of Microeconomics (3 units)
* EC 2202 – Principles of Macroeconomics (3 units)
* EC 3322 – Economic Statistics (3 units)
* EC 4401 – Intermediate Microeconomics (3 units)
* EC 4403 – Intermediate Macroeconomics (3 units)
* EC 4620 – Senior Seminar (3 units)

Each of the three options have additional required courses.  See the [University Catalog](https://catalog.cpp.edu) for current requirements.
