# Donate

Our department offers uniquely strong connections to industry that benefits students by placing them in internships and jobs directly after earning their B.S. degrees in physics! To support these activities, please consider making a charitable give to **[The Alexander R. Small Student Development Fund for Careers in Industry](https://crowdfund.cpp.edu/project/45318).**

We can work together to ensure that the high standards of student-centered teaching and research provided by the Department of Physics and Astronomy established will continue. For more information on how you can support Cal Poly Pomona teaching and research, please contact either the [College of Science Development Office](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/giving/index.shtml), or the [Physics and Astronomy Department Office](contact.shtml). Thank you.
