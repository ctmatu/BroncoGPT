## Policy Statement:

The California State Polytechnic University, Pomona adopts as campus policy the California State University Additional Employment Policy contained in Coded Memorandum HR 2002-05 including any subsequent revisions.

## Implementation:

Diversity, Human Resource and Payroll Services, Organizational Development and Training, and Risk Management
