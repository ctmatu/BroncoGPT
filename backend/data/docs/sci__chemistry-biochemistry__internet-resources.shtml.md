# Internet Resources

## Useful Internet Sites for Chemists

* [What's That Stuff](http://pubs.acs.org/cen/whatstuff/stuff.html "Interesting Chemistry facts.")
* [Online C&E News](http://pubs.acs.org/cen/)
* [Material Safety Data Sheet](http://www.ilpi.com/msds/index.html "Great listing of where to find Material Safety Data Sheets, compiled by the University of Kentucky")
* [ACS Publications Division](http://pubs.acs.org/ "Magazines, Research Journals, Directories, Professional Services.")
* [Chemical Abstracts Service](http://www.cas.org/)
* [American Chemical Society](http://www.acs.org/ "Chemists worldwide rate our site as their Number 1 source for what's new in the world of science & chemistry.")

## Teaching and Learning Resources

* [Central Science LIVE](http://www.prenhall.com/brown/ "It is intended for use with T. L. Brown's Chemistry: The Central Science, Seventh Edition, Prentice Hall. The first two chapters are ")
* [WBT Systems (formerly WEST)](http://www.wbtsystems.com/ "Desktop Education Server by WBT Systems (formerly WEST) for web-based instruction.")
* [Fair Use of Copyrighted Materials](http://www.cetus.org/ "Fair use of copyrighted materials booklet, put together by CETUS (Consortium for Educational Technology in University Systems).")
* [Infrared (IR) Spectroscopy Websites](http://www.dq.fct.unl.pt/qoa/jas/ir.html)
* Chemistry Textbooks in Print Archive
* [Journal of Chemical Education Online](http://jchemed.chem.wisc.edu/ "...currently a summary is given for each article in an issue along with full text of a few general pieces; we envision a day when subscribers will be able to download all articles directly from the Internet.")
* [ChemCAI: Instructional Software for Chemistry](http://www.chem1.com/chemed/ "ChemCAI: Instructional Software for Chemistry From Simon Fraser University: Information for teachers and course designers with lots of great links to instructional resources. ")
