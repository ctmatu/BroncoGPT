## About me

Dr. Zahra Sotoudeh’s areas of expertise are computational structural dynamics (CSD) and aeroelasticity, and statistical energy analysis. Zahra joined aerospace engineering department in Fall 2016. Zahra is an associate fellow of AIAA.
