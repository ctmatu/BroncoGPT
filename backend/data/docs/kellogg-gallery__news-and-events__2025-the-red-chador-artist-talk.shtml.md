January 8, 2025

## Artist Keynote Talk

Join Performance Artist **Anida Yoeu Ali** for an **artist keynote talk** about her latest works, currently on view in the exhibition, *The Red Chador: Close Encounters*.

The talk will take place at **Ursa Major** in the **Bronco Student Center (Building 35)** on **Wednesday, March 19, 2025, from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM**.
