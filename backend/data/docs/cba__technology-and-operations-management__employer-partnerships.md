# Employer Partnerships

![](/cba/technology-and-operations-management/img/employer-partnerships-banner.jpg)

At the Technology and Operations Management department (TOM), we are committed to providing our students with real-world opportunities to grow and succeed in their careers. If your company is interested in partnering with us to hire talented students from our department, we invite you to fill out the form below. By becoming an employer partner, you can tap into a pool of skilled, motivated, and job-ready students, eager to apply their knowledge and skills to make an impact in your organization.

Whether you’re looking to hire interns, recent graduates, or part-time employees, we’re here to connect you with the right candidates.
