# About Our Department

## Cal Poly Pomona

The Department of Political Science offers a [Bachelor of Arts Degree in Political Science](../undergraduate/index.shtml) and a [Master's of Public Administration](https://www.cpp.edu/class/mpa/index.shtml). Our department also offers a [minor in Political Science](../undergraduate/minor.shtml). Although Cal Poly Pomona enrolls over 20,000 undergraduates, our department is proud to maintain small classes for both our undergraduate and graduate courses. Check out our:

* [Mission and Vision](https://www.cpp.edu/class/political-science/about/mission-and-vision.shtml)
* [Learning Outcomes](learning-outcomes.shtml)
* [Rankings](ranking.shtml)

Courses include basic training in political science, but our students engage in [multiple subfields](../undergraduate/lower-division-courses.shtml) in over [50 undergraduate courses](../undergraduate/index.shtml). Our undergraduates work with faculty members to write senior theses or intern with a local government or administrative agency. Our Master's students work with faculty members to write a Master's Thesis or complete a project with a local public service organization.

Students who are interested in the political science major should contact a member of our [faculty](../faculty-staff/index.shtml). Undergraduates should work closely with faculty to develop a coherent program of study.  For more information, see the [Advising](../advising/index.shtml) link.
