## EH&S Purchase Approval

EH&S Purchase Approval Required Before Ordering Certain Items

## Information

To ensure safety and regulatory compliance, **EH&S pre-approval is required before purchasing specific chemicals, materials, and equipment** listed in the EH&S Pre-Approval List (PDF). This includes purchases made through **Purchase Order (PO), P-Card, and CPP Foundation/Enterprise Accounts**.

Before placing an order:

* Review the[EH&S Pre-Approval List (PDF)](../all-pdfs/additional-resources/ehs-pre-approval-list.pdf).
* If your item is listed, submit a request using the [EH&S Purchase Approval Request Form](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/e47bbfc711594443a0a8e801c2861403 "https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/e47bbfc711594443a0a8e801c2861403").

If approved, EH&S will issue a **Purchase Pre-Approval Confirmation Sheet** required by [CPP Procurement](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/55ac9b05706f429caa2e8bd69c2ef8a8 "https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/55ac9b05706f429caa2e8bd69c2ef8a8").

For questions, contact EH&S at **ehs@cpp.edu** or **(909) 869-4697**.
