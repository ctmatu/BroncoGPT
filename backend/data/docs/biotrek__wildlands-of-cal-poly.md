BioTrek’s mission aims to inspire a sustainable relationship with the environment. We value the native and natural areas that remain on our campus that facilitate educational opportunities for visitors of all ages. As you visit the BioTrek facilities, take a moment to look to the wild places nearby. They reveal the story of our campus' past and highlight the importance of conserving our local environments.

* [Voorhis Ecological Reserve](/biotrek/wildlands-of-cal-poly/voorhis-ecological-reserve.shtml)  - Learn more about the coastal sage scrub that dominates our hillsides.
* [California Walnut Woodlands](/biotrek/wildlands-of-cal-poly/california-walnut-woodlands.shtml)  - Learn about the remaining walnut woodland that still exists today.
