## What is a Digital Badge?

A digital badge is a visual representation of skills or knowledge you have gained and learned during a course or activity. You earn badges each time you successfully attend, participate in, and/or complete one of Cal Poly Pomona’s courses or activities that awards digital badges. By taking these types of courses or participating in these types of activities, you are able to build a set of digital credentials to demonstrate your own learning. Badges earned through Cal Poly Pomona are organized in a hierarchy that you can build upon.

[View Digital Badges](https://cpp.badgr.com/public/organization/badges%C2%A0%C2%A0)

## Why earn a Digital Badge?

By earning a badge, you are providing employers with a quick and convenient way to digitally verify your acquired skills and knowledge at a glance. Digital badges provide a unique benefit during job searches because they allow prospective employers to discover qualified employees like you based specifically on your professionally relevant skills and experience. This can assist you in moving one step ahead of the competition during the hiring process.

## What is a Pathway?

What is a pathway? Pathways were created to illustrate a specific learning journey. Pathways help learners see what they’ve done and what to do next. Pathways provide the ability for badge program administrators to map out badged curriculum in a format that is easy-to-follow for learners.

[View Digital Badge Pathways](https://cpp.badgr.com/public/organization/badges)

![](../img/digital-badging/pathwaysrose.png)

![](../img/digital-badging/pathwayscheckgif.gif)

## Get Started on a Pathway!

Admins can track group and individual progress on the pathway and award milestone badges automatically. Learners can track their own progress and look ahead to plan the next steps in their learning journey.

[Log in to start a Pathway](https://cpp.badgr.com/auth/login)

## FYE Badge

The First Year Experience (FYE) program badge is centered on opportunities to engage in learning, knowledge sharing, and discovering around topics related to first-year college students.

## CEL Badge

The Community Engaged Learning (CEL) program badge is centered on inclusive excellence within the field of community engagement. Faculty and staff earners design and plan for inclusive excellence.
