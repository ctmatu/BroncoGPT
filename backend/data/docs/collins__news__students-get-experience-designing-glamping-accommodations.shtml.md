# Students Get Experience Designing Glamping Accommodations

December 12, 2025

![](../img/glamping-thumbnnail.png)

Hospitality management students are combining design creativity with business analysis to envision new glamping accommodations for standalone sites and RV resorts through a cross-college studio and micro-internship experience.

Read how their research, field trips and collaboration with industry experts are shaping real-world glamping concepts and potential investment insights.

[Read the full article](https://www.cpp.edu/news/content/2025/12/students-get-experience-designing-glamping-accommodations/index.shtml)
