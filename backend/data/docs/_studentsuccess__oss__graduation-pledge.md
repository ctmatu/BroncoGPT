## About the Program

The Graduation Pledge programs at Cal Poly Pomona can help you stay on track towards a timely graduation. The programs support incoming students in earning a bachelor's degree within two (for transfer students) or four years (for freshmen).

![Graduate cheering with diploma in hand.](img/grad-home-feature.png)

## Participating in the Program

CPP offers Through in Two (for transfer students) and Finish in Four (for first-time freshmen) as our graduation pledge program, which is [a legislatively-mandated program](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billNavClient.xhtml?bill_id=202320240AB1205). Students who are eligible for the program promise to balance school, work and personal responsibilities and stay committed to their education. CPP will provide an effective and coordinated advising program to help students register for the appropriate classes and stay on track to graduate. Graduation Pledge students receive priority registration appointments for the fall, winter, spring and summer terms.

The program does not provide tuition assistance or tuition reimbursement.

## Eligibility and Next Steps

Incoming students who are eligible for the program will receive an invitation to apply. Learn more about our [2-year](2-year-info.shtml) and [4-year](4-year-info.shtml) programs.
