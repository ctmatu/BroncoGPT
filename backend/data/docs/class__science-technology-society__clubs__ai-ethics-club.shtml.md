# AI Ethics and Policy Club

## Starting Fall 2025!

**![AI Ethics and Policy Club logo](../img/ai-ethics-and-policy-club-logo.jpg)**

The AI Ethics and Policy Club is a student-led organization committed to exploring the ethical, social, and philosophical dimensions of artificial intelligence. Our mission is to create a space where students from all disciplines can critically engage with how AI is shaping our world in education, industry, and society.

We host regular events including discussions, guest speakers, and collaborative projects that examine the real-world impact of AI. Students can expect opportunities to share their perspectives, investigate emerging technologies, and connect with others who are passionate about responsible innovation.

Starting this semester, we will also be launching a monthly newsletter featuring:

* Student-written investigative pieces on any topic related to AI and society
* Reflections on how AI is influencing students’ educational experiences
* Perspectives on AI in their prospective professional fields and academic majors

We welcome all students who are curious about the intersection of technology and ethics. No technical background is required.
