# Submitting the Final Version of your Thesis for Format Review

Once you have reviewed the [Master's Thesis Formatting Guidelines](formatting-guidelines.shtml) and made sure your paper matches the [Thesis Sample Guide (PDF)](../documents/thesis-template-2025.pdf), submit an email request that includes your thesis as a PDF (.pdf) attachment to formatreview@cpp.edu. At this point your paper must be finalized, defended, and approved by your committee members before you contact the Format Review Team.

If the reviewer determines that your paper has **NOT**been formatted according to the standards outlined in the [Thesis Formatting Checklist (PDF)](../formatting-checklist-2025.pdf), then they may ask you to revise your submission according to the [sample guide (pdf)](../documents/thesis-template-2025.pdf) and resubmit. If this occurs, the format reviewer will respond to your initial request with an email including a [Thesis Formatting Checklist (PDF)](../formatting-checklist-2025.pdf) of steps to be completed before you seek formatting approval again. Once you have updated the paper, please contact the format reviewer again for approval.

Once a reviewer has officially approved the formatting of your thesis, you will receive an email confirmation from the Graduate Studies Office.
