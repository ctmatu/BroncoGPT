# Escalator in University Library Shut Down for Repairs

**Summary of disruption:** The escalator in the University Library (Building 15) has been shut down. Parts needed for repair work have been ordered. The escalator shutdown was initiated on Friday, March 6, and is expected to last two weeks.

**Reason for disruption:** Repairs on the escalator will commence as soon as parts arrive.

**What do I need to do?** Use the first-floor elevator or the stairs. Please share this information with others who may not have received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:** Facilities Customer Service (Ext. 3030).
