# Annual CPP Reports

## Annual CPP Reports

### Title IX Annual Reports

In an effort to create transparency and accountability across the CSU system, standardized data is collected and aggregated from each university and the Chancellor’s Office through an Annual Report Survey. The Annual Report Survey compiles data on the number of reports, complaints, investigations and outcomes. The Annual Report Survey provides the Chancellor’s Office and each university with critical data necessary to identify and track emerging patterns and trends, and highlights opportunities for additional prevention education and training.
