# Ralp W. Ames Distinguished Research Award Honoring Chantal Stieber

August 29, 2023

Join us in honoring Associate Professor Chantal Stieber who is receiving the Ralph W. Ames Distinguished Research Award on Thursday, October 5th at 12pm in room 4-2-314.

Stieber will make a presentation on her research which has a long-term goal of removing pollutants from the environment and using them as feedstock in chemical synthesis.
