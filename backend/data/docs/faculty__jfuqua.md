## About Me

Dr. Juliana Lynn Fuqua is a Professor of Psychology. She teaches multiple courses in psychology. She has a mixed methods background in quantitative and qualitative research methods, including survey research and focus groups. Her doctoral dissertation work was an evaluation of the process and the outcomes of an NIH-funded transdisciplinary scientific collaborative project among university research investigators focused on the problem of tobacco use. Her current interests include evaluation of educational innovations that are designed to improve student online and/or in-person learning among college students. Dr. Fuqua enjoys collaborative research and has published multiple peer-reviewed papers and many conference presentations, with other students and faculty.
