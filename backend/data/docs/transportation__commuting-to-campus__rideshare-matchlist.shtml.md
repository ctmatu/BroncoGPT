# Rideshare Matchlist

Looking for a carpool partner?  Contact the Rideshare Office.  We can send you a list of Cal Poly Pomona Faculty/Staff members who live in your area who you can contact to rideshare.

Faculty/Staff may contact the rideshare office directly or may send an email to rideshare@cpp.edu with the following information:
**In the subject line of the email please write, "Faculty/Staff Matchlist Requested"**

Name
Email Address (preferably a Cal Poly Pomona email address)
Home City
Home Zip Code

We may already have some rideshare members in our database who are already participating in our program.  If this is the case, we will forward a list to you via email.  The Rideshare office will also include some tips to get your carpool rolling!

For more information, please contact the Rideshare office.
