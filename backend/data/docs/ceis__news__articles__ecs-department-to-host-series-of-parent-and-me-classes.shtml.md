# ECS Department to Host Series of Parent and Me Classes

March 9, 2023

![Reading](../../img/reading.png)The Department of Early Childhood Studies in CEIS invites the Cal Poly Pomona community to attend Parent and Me Classes on March 10 and 24 at the Broncos Family Space in the University Library on the third floor from 9-11 a.m. Bring your infants and toddlers as we read, play, share and learn about the significance of the first three years of life. Additional classes will be held on April 7, April 21 and May 5. For more information, email Assoc. Prof. Giselle Navarro-Cruz at gnavarrocruz@cpp.edu.
