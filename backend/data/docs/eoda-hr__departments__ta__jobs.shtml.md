## Career Opportunities

Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) is the #1 polytechnic university for diversity and economic mobility. Our commitment to mentorship and a culture of care supports students, staff, and faculty from all backgrounds as they forge their own unique paths to success.

With benefits ranging from CalPERS retirement to a tuition fee waiver program, we have what you need to develop professionally and retire comfortably. Enjoy the beauty of our campus and be a part of a mission-driven community of educators, innovators and leaders.

Join our mission to help those who dream of success achieve it. Discover your next rewarding career with CPP!

[CPP Careers](https://www.cpp.edu/jobs/index.shtml)

\*If you are a CPP employee interested in new career opportunities on campus, we strongly encourage you to create an account with your CPP email to easily identify you as an internal candidate.

If you’re a CPP employee (stateside or auxiliary) looking to advance in your current role or explore new opportunities on campus, we encourage you to take advantage of our internal resume review service.
