# Contact Us

### For Current CPP Engineering Students

All inquiries about the program should be sent to egrlead@cpp.edu.

### Invitation to Give Back

For our alumni and industry partners, we seek your input for this program. Many of you have significant experience in professional practice where you gained knowledge, skills, and attitudes to help you succeed.  Many of our newer alumni and industry partners may have experienced leadership moments even though they do not have positional leadership.  Regardless of the number of experiences, we would like to hear your story.  We invite you to be a guest speaker or lecturer or help us create the next leadership lesson.  If you are interested, please contact us at egrlead@cpp.edu.
