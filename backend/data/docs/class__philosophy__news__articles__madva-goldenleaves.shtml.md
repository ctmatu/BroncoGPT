# Dr. Madva honored with Golden Leaves author award

April 26, 2022

The Golden Leaves program celebrates those members of the Cal Poly Pomona campus community who have authored or edited a book in the preceding year.  Dr. Madva was honored this year for his work co-editing *The Movement for Black Lives: Philosophical Perspectives*. Oxford University Press, 2021.
