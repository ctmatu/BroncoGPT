# Summer Program

![Decorative Banner](../../img/banner.png)

This immersive program is thoughtfully structured to provide a challenging curriculum and a supportive learning environment, while fostering personal growth and exploration. The campus and dormitory setting further facilitates adaptation to college life, independence, and social connection.

Throughout the six weeks in residence, students will engage in four academic courses, some taught by college professors/lecturers, in preparation for their next academic year in high school and academic demands at their future college/university.

Additionally, students will particpate in exciting recreational activities, including enriching field trips to colleges/universities, museums, concerts, community events, and theme parks.

Students depart equipped with valuable academic insights, newfound confidence, and a deeper understanding of themselves, ready to thrive in their educational journey.
