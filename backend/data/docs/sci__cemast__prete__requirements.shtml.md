# Requirements

## The application period is now open!

## Application deadline is September 15, 2024.

![](../img/checklist-icon.png)

## Minimum Requirements

* Must be an undergraduate STEM, Liberal Studies, or Education major with at least 25 semester units completed or a STEM graduate student with a least 12 units completed.
* Must be enrolled as a full-time student (at least 12 units for undergraduate students and 6 units for graduate students) during the fall and spring semesters.
* Must have an overall GPA of 3.0.
* Selected applicants will have a background check/live scan and a current negative TB test free of charge.
