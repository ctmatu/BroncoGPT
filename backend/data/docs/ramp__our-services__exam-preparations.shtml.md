## Preparing You for What’s Next

Students who are interested or planning on pursuing a graduate degree, may request tutorials that assist in preparing for the various graduate entrance exams, including CBEST, GRE, GMAT, MCAT, OAT, and LSAT.
In addition to using practice exams and problems to prepare for the substantive material tested on the exam, program tutor/mentors help each participant develop a study and time management plan for maximizing the effectiveness of time invested in preparing for the entrance exam.
