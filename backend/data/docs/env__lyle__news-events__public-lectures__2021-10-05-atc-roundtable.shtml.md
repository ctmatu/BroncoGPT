# The Lyle Center Presents:

## Alternative Transportation Roundtable

![](../../)

**Date:** October 05, 2021
**Time:** 12:00pm to 01:00pm
**Location:** Virtual

The Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies and the Department of Urban and Regional Planning are co-hosting the Alternative Transportation Committee Roundtable to discuss the its [involvement](https://polycentric.cpp.edu/2020/02/new-transit-initiatives-to-encourage-use-of-alternative-transportation/) in projects such as the University's new Class Pass Program, [Valley Boulevard Bikeway](https://la.streetsblog.org/2021/07/02/eyes-on-the-street-pomona-bikeways-on-valley-boulevard-and-kellogg-drive/), Silver Streak, and the Bronco Mobility Hub. The event is free and open to the public.

**Alternative Transportation Roundtable**

Tuesday, Oct. 5 | 12-1 p.m. (PST)

RSVP to crs@cpp.edu to receive Zoom link

The conversation will be led by Professor **Gwen Urey**; and ATC co-chairs **Danny Wu** ('95, urban and regional planning), Cal Poly Pomona Executive Director of Transportation and Planning, and Associate Professor **John Lloyd**from the Department of History.
