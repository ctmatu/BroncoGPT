## Who We Are

[### Mission

We cultivate success through a diverse culture of experiential learning, discovery and innovation.](mission-vision-values.shtml)

[### Vision

Cal Poly Pomona will be the model for an inclusive polytechnic university that inspires creativity and innovation, embraces local and global challenges and transforms lives.](https://www-test.cpp.edu/strategicplan/mission-vision-values.shtml#vision)

[### Values

Cal Poly Pomona’s values are academic excellence, experiential learning, inclusivity, student learning and success, community engagement, and social and environmental responsibility.](https://www-test.cpp.edu/strategicplan/mission-vision-values.shtml#values)
