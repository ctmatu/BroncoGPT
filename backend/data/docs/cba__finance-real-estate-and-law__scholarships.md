# FRL Scholarships

The Finance, Real Estate and Law Department 2018 Scholarship Applications

## Boeing Company Scholarship ($1,000)

The FRL – [Boeing Company Scholarship](https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcsupomona.academicworks.com%2Fopportunities%2F8217&data=02%7C01%7C%7Cc21ef79370ef4d47b08e08d5b5d67c35%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636614855155409169&sdata=5pCM6zQSGTuhSp%2F0xCbxvsDGMujpawreRWsmz2o41TM%3D&reserved=0) is for freshmen with a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.5 and sophomores, juniors and seniors with a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 who are in the College of Business Administration studying Finance, Real Estate & Law. Applicants must be US citizens or eligible non-citizens. In order to qualify for this scholarship you must be enrolled full-time (12 or more units)\* for both semesters of the upcoming academic year.

## FRL - David Parry Real Estate Scholarship ($1,000)

The [David Parry Real Estate Scholarship](https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcsupomona.academicworks.com%2Fopportunities%2F8058&data=02%7C01%7C%7Cc21ef79370ef4d47b08e08d5b5d67c35%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636614855155409169&sdata=KA%2FAlNAhYAbrhjx%2Fss72w2lxWhwj2L9B8Mc441p5mE4%3D&reserved=0) is for full-time Cal Poly Pomona students currently in their sophomore, junior, or senior year and majoring in Real Estate. Applicants must have a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0. In order to qualify for this scholarship you   **must be enrolled full-time (12 or more units)**  for both semesters of the   **upcoming academic year.**

## FRL - Tomkoria Scholarship (3, $1,500 each)

The [Tomkoria Scholarship](https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcsupomona.academicworks.com%2Fopportunities%2F8218&data=02%7C01%7C%7Cc21ef79370ef4d47b08e08d5b5d67c35%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636614855155409169&sdata=JPuvOluoTC5PJsGz3dgh1UDAN0mkA5%2FwWJoiynxUrtg%3D&reserved=0) is for full-time Cal Poly Pomona students currently in their sophomore, junior, or senior year and majoring in Finance, Real Estate, and Law. Applicants must have a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.25. In order to qualify for this scholarship you   **must be enrolled full-time (12 or more units)**  for both semesters of the   **upcoming academic year.**
