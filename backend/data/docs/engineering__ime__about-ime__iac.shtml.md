# Industry Advisors Council (IAC)

The IAC is a diverse team of industry representatives supporting the CPP IME Department for industry insights, best practices, and cutting-edge technologies that shape the future of industrial and manufacturing engineering field.

Mission and Vision

Mission Statement:

The Industry Advisory Council at CPP IME Department provides strategic guidance and resources to help the department achieve its goals of producing world-class engineers and fulfilling the technological needs of industry.

Vision Statement:

To foster a collaborative partnership between IME Department and industry to advance education, research, and innovation in the fields of Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering.
