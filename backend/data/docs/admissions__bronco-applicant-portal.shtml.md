# Bronco Applicant Portal

## New Bronco Applicant Portal!

As a Fall 2026 undergraduate applicant, you can:

* **Monitor your application status**
* **Review your** **To Do List** for any required documents
* **Find financial aid information**
* Stay informed on**upcoming events**
* Access important **next steps** for your application

 For steps on how to activate your BroncoAccount, view our [BroncoAccount Activation for Undergraduate Applicants](https://www.cpp.edu/it/2step/bronco-account-activation-for-undergraduate-applicants.shtml) page.

**Access the [Bronco Applicant Portal](https://applicant.cpp.edu/) now!![](../img/bap-home.png)**
