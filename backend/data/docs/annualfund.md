# Annual Giving & Stewardship

![CPP graduates during Commencement](img/25env-grad_48.jpg)

## Your impact starts here.

Philanthropy at Cal Poly Pomona creates opportunity and changes lives. Your generosity helps empower future innovators and the next generation of leaders by advancing career-readiness initiatives, emerging technology programs and modern campus spaces. The Annual Giving & Stewardship team connects donors to impact through crowdfunding, tribute gifts and student-led efforts. We’re here to celebrate your role in this community and support you in your philanthropic journey.

## Spark Meaningful Change

[### Bronco Fund

Support Cal Poly Pomona’s greatest needs through a flexible, high-impact fund that powers hands-on learning and student success across campus.](bronco-fund/index.shtml)

[### Bronco Launchpad

Our fall crowdfunding campaign empowers students, faculty and staff to raise funds for the projects, programs and scholarships they care about most.](bronco-launchpad/index.shtml)

[### Broncos Give

CPP’s high-energy, 36-hour giving campaign unites the campus community each spring to uplift students and expand opportunities through collective generosity.](broncos-give/index.shtml)

[### Faculty & Staff Philanthropy

Campus educators, mentors and leaders shape the Bronco experience — and their involvement in philanthropy helps strengthen our campus community.](faculty-and-staff-philanthropy/index.shtml)

[### Giving Societies

Our giving societies honor donors whose annual, planned or lifetime generosity transforms lives and shapes Cal Poly Pomona’s future.](giving-societies/index.shtml)

[### Memorial & Honorary Giving

Honor a loved one or celebrate a meaningful milestone with a tribute gift that reflects their impact and continues their story at Cal Poly Pomona.](memorial-and-honorary-giving/index.shtml)

[### Student Philanthropy

Cal Poly Pomona students lead with generosity and pride — creating a culture of giving that supports their peers and future Broncos.](student-philanthropy/index.shtml)

[### Contact

We’re here to answer questions, support your giving and help you make a difference at Cal Poly Pomona.](contact/index.shtml)
