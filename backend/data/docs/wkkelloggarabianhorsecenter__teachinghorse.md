# TeachingHorse

## Effective Leadership for an Uncertain Future

![TeachingHorse Facilitator Michelle Elrod and an Arabian Horse](../img/teachinghorse/equine-leadership-training-elrod.png)

Horses have been leading through uncertainty for millions of years. They use their roles within the herd to safely move the herd from one place to another. They have confidence in the roles of each leader and recognize that leadership must be shared. They do not know; they notice.

In these times of profound change and uncertainty, effective leaders who understand how to notice, set direction and pace for decisions and actions, and align their actions with their inner confidence will be successful in guiding organizations through change and into the future.

TeachingHorse, the premier provider of equine leadership training globally, and Cal Poly Pomona, known for our polytechnic “learn by doing” excellence, have partnered to provide world-class leadership development programs that define how leaders and teams will impact the future.

TeachingHorse at Cal Poly Pomona provides an innovative, experiential learning environment for leaders at all levels to become more agile, human-centered and future ready.

## Our Vision

To provide a premier model of equine experiential leadership development.

## Program Goals and Outcomes

### Program Goals

* Enhanced Leadership Skills
* Stronger Team Culture
* Team Alignment
* Increased Productivity
* Reinvention of Leadership Development & Talent Management

### Program Outcomes

* Practice noticing yourself, those around you and the environment
* Elevate agile decision making based on what you notice
* Move at a pace that reflects the current reality
* Align your inner and outer motivations to elevate confidence in your leadership
