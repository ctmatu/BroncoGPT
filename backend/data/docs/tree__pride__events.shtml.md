# Signature Events

## Annual Signature Events

Card 1 of 6

### Q-Camp

A Back to School event highlighting the services and resources we provide to the LGBTQIA+ community.

Card 2 of 6

### Pride Formal

An evening with food and entertainment

Card 3 of 6

### Queer Chats

Queer Chats is a dynamic discussion series, creating a safe and inclusive space for exploring LGBTQIA+ topics, fostering dialogue, and building community.

Card 4 of 6

### National Coming Out Day

Join us as we celebrate this day and the power that comes with owning who you are.

Card 5 of 6

### LGBTQIA+ History Month

Held every October on campus.

Card 6 of 6

### Lavender Graduation Celebration

The annual celebration held in May for graduating students.
