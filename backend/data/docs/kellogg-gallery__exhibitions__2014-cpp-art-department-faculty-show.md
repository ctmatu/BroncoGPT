## Cal Poly Pomona Art Department Faculty Show

**Date:** November 10, 2014
**Location:** Kellogg University Art Gallery

![Cal Poly Pomona Art Faculty Show, November 10 - December 13](img/artfaculty14.jpg)This exhibition showcases art and graphic work by the **Cal Poly Pomona Art Department Faculty.**This year's participating artists include: Anthony Acock, Gina Lawson-Egan, Melissa Flicker, Joyce Hesselgrave, Raymond Kampf, Alyssa Lang, Crystal Yachin Lee, Babette Mayor, Sarah Meyer, Ann Phong, Deane Swick and Barbara Thomason.

Many of the artist's themes coincide as critiques of the world's social, technological and environmental issues that affect our world today.
