# Programs and Services

The Credential Services Office is the main resource on campus for candidates seeking information and services related to credentialing. All credential programs offered are accredited by the California Commission on Teacher Credentialing (CTC).

Please see all services we provide in the boxes below and feel welcome to reach out with any questions.

Instead, please make every effort to submit all required documents to complete your application so it can be recommended for the CTC verification.
