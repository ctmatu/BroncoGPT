# Supply Chain Operations Technology (SCOT) Lab

![SCOT lab with 2 students studying](../img/scot-lab-kao.jpg)

## The SCOT Lab Offers:

* Quiet environment to complete homework and study
* Collaborate with other TOM/EBZ students
* Certification study books for testing in CPIM, ASQ, PMBOK
* CSCMP Membership information and materials

Interested in certification study materials? Click the links to learn more information and what benefits these certifications provide! If you would like to see what materials are available for rental, stop by the SCOT Lab.
