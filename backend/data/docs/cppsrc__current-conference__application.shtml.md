## Submit Application

Submit your application to present at the 14th Annual Research, Scholarship, and Creative Activities (RSCA) Conference. Each project team should submit only one application. The list below details information you will need to have to complete your application. Consult with your faculty mentor on the proper format for your abstract and presentation. There is a “[Presentation Guide](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/research-guide.shtml)” that provides general recommendations on preparing your application and an overview of the presentation formats for the conference.

1. Bronco IDs for all Cal Poly Pomona Students
2. Full Name of each group member (please check spelling)
3. Program Name or Lab Assignment for both Cal Poly Pomona and non–Cal Poly Pomona Students
4. Abstract (250 words or less)
5. Faculty Mentor's name and email address
6. Institutional Review Board (IRB) or Animal Use and Care Committee (ACUC) Protocol ID (if applicable)

The application has now closed. If you have missed the deadline, please consider applying for the Creative Activities and Research Symposium in August!

**![Step 2](https://i.imgur.com/EQXdtp3.png)**

## Faculty Mentor Approval

Each presentation requires approval from a faculty mentor to be considered for the RSCA.

Faculty Mentors will each receive a link to the approval form via email after the student submits their application. If you do not receive an email, submit the form below using the Unique Submission provided by your student.

\*A maximum of 4 teams may participate in the competitive session for each faculty advisor. Please provide the Unique Submission ID for each team in your application\*

Please submit the approval form within 2 business days of your student's initial submission.

**![Step 3 on a blue background](https://i.imgur.com/3jXJ0Vl.png)**

## RSVP for the RSCA

Presenters are welcome to invite their friends and family to participate in the RSCA. All presenters' guests who RSVP will get updates with the final event schedule.

Presenters must RSVP for the event. Presenters who do not RSVP will not be included in the event program or schedule.
