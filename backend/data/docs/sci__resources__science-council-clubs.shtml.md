# Science Council/Clubs

## [Science Council / Science Clubs](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/resources/science-council-clubs.shtml)

There are over 20 science clubs within the College of Science. Clubs offer opportunities for students to meet others with similar interests, participate in college activities, learn about career options, and gain leadership skills.

The Science Council allocates ASI funding to science clubs with the intent of promoting student involvement in campus activities. The objective of the Science Council is to establish a community which fosters student success through encouraging collaborative leadership, providing resources, and demonstrating the enjoyable aspects of university life.

**For a list of clubs:**[Clubs Listed on MyBar](https://mybar.cpp.edu/organizations?categories=4815)
