# Cal Poly Pomona Undergraduate Research Journal

---

The Office of Undergraduate Research and Bronco Scholar at the University Library are proud to present Reach – Inspiring Creativity, Discovery, and Innovation, an online compilation of undergraduate research at Cal Poly Pomona. Reach showcases outstanding scholarship, creative works, and research conducted by undergraduate students under the mentorship of faculty members.

To be considered for inclusion in Reach, students should submit a full-length, faculty-approved paper or creative work to Bronco Scholar. The submission must clearly explain the background and motivation for the work and a thorough discussion of the results and significance of the research or final product. Reach encourages submission from students and teams of students from all disciplines.
