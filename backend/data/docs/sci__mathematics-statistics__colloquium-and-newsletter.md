# Colloquium Schedule

Math and Stats Colloquium will be in person for this academic year; however, some of the talks will also be accessible via Zoom. All the information for each session, including Zoom ID and password when it corresponds, will be emailed to the CPP math community a few days before the talk. If you are interested in presenting at the Colloquium or being added to the Colloquium mailing list to receive regular invitations, please contact any committee member: Prof. Anne Cawley, Prof. Fernando Lopez-Garcia (Spring 2026 only), Prof. Briana Foster-Greenwood, or Prof. He Jiang.
