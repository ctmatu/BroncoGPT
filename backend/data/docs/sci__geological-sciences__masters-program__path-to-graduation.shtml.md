* Stop-Out Policy:
  + ***Only one semester of non-enrollment (not including summer term) is permitted. Graduate students with two semesters or more of non-enrollment are automatically dropped from the MS Geology program and will need to re-apply. If you anticipate a delay in your studies, please apply for a Leave of Absence* .**    [**Link to E-form**](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/forms.shtml "Registrar's Office E-forms").
