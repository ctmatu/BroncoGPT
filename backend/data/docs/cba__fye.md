# First Year Experience

![first year experience welcome image](img/FYE_WelcomeBanner.jpg)

Welcome to the website created for College of Business Administration (CBA) students taking the First Year Experience (FYE) course BUS 101. Click the links on the left to access our library of videos for each of the CBA departments and our own Student Success Center. We hope you learn more about the College of Business Administration.
