# Contacts - Master's Projects and Theses

## Library format reviews

In order to support the submission process, the University Library will work with students to review their document. Prior to submitting their final thesis or project to Bronco ScholarWorks students are required to have it reviewed for formatting errors and to ensure that it meets the University's standards for publication in the institutional repository. Please review this [sample guide](https://libguides.library.cpp.edu/ld.php?content_id=58869851) and [formatting checklist](https://libguides.library.cpp.edu/ld.php?content_id=65530405) BEFORE requesting a formatting review appointment.

Once you have formatted your work, submit an email request that includes your thesis or project as a PDF (.pdf) or Word (.docx) attachment to [libraryformat@cpp.edu](#mailto:libraryformat@cpp.edu). Appointments are required and should be scheduled before the thesis/project deadline.
