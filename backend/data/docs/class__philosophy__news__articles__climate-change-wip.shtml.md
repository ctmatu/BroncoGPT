# Work in Progress Talk on March 4th

March 1, 2021

![Climate change talk poster](../../img/climate-change-wip-poster.jpg)

Please join us this Thursday for a work-in-progress talk, featuring CPP Philosophy’s own Dr. Alex Madva:

**“Individualism, Structuralism, and Climate Change”**

Dr. Michael Brownstein (John Jay College and the Graduate Center, CUNY), Dr. Alex Madva (CPP), and Dr. Daniel Kelly (Purdue)

U-hour (12:00 to 12:50 p.m. PT)

Thursday, March 4

**Register for Zoom information at**: [climate-wip.eventbrite.com](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fclimate-wip.eventbrite.com%2F&data=04%7C01%7Cbkim%40cpp.edu%7Cfd0a6dca34d44fbf3d8108d8dcce0443%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C637502125140066237%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C1000&sdata=QS6y8oglbhx%2BfJvy8Shf4rv5VwY6%2FOgq7lTtl7%2BHFwU%3D&reserved=0).  Please share this invitation and the attached poster with anyone who might be interested in the talk—all are welcome!  Also, I have attached a draft of the paper that will be presented, in case you would like to read it ahead of time.
