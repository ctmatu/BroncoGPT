# Foundational Level General Science (FLGS) Credential

The MSTI Program supports Cal Poly Students and Multiple Subject Credentialed Teachers currently working in the classroom who are interested in obtaining their Foundational Level General Science Credential. CSET online courses are provided free for those who have a strong interest in teaching science.

###### Requirements for obtaining a FLGS Credential for current credentialed teachers:

* Pass the CSET Science Subtest I (test code 215) or have an academic degree major in any type of engineering or any degree major offered by a college or university science department
* Complete a secondary science teaching methods course
