# HVAC Service Restored on Second Floor of Building 17

**Update on Disruption:** Repairs to fans have been completed and HVAC (heating ventilation and air conditioning) service has been restored to the west wing of the second floor of Building 17 (Engineering Laboratories).

**Reason for Disruption:**Contractor crews repaired ventilation fans that serve the second floor.

**What do I need to do?**Please share this information with others in the building who may not have received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:**Facilities Customer Service (Ext. 3030).
