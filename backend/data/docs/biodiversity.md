# Biodiversity

Cal Poly Pomona is one of the most biologically diverse campuses in the California State University. These pages explore the [factors responsible for this diversity](about/index.shtml), the four [ecological communities](communities/index.shtml) in the undeveloped parts of campus, and the [impacts](impacts/index.shtml) to diversity. Lists of the species of [amphibians](species/amphibians.shtml), [reptiles](species/reptiles.shtml), [birds](species/birds.shtml), [mammals](species/mammals.shtml), and [vascular plants](species/vascular-plants.shtml) found on campus are also provided.
