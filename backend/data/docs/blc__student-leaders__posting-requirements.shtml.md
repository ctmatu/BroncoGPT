# Flyers, Posters, and Banners

## Requirements

For the complete list of posting policies, please see the [CPP Addendum (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/free-speech/img/cpp-addendum-2024-august-15.pdf) to the CSU Time, Place and Manner policy. You can also read the [CSU Time, Place and Manner policy](https://www.cpp.edu/free-speech/policies.shtml).

**Document Requirements**

For the Bronco Leadership Center to approve a posting, the following information **must be included** on the posting:

* Sponsoring club/org name or department name
* Date and location of event
* Contact info email or phone number

Failure to include the above will result in the stamp request being rejected.

**Posting sizes**

* Posters cannot be larger than 11”x17”
* Staking flyers cannot be larger than 8.5”x11”

**How to Request A Posting Stamp**

* [Request a stamp for your flyer, poster, or banner (adobe.com).](https://na1.documents.adobe.com/account/customComposeJs?workflowid=CBJCHBCAABAAi38syL7r85FISolzhoy14O3im5AmnY7k)
* Log into Adobe Sign with your CPP credentials
* Add the Organization Name at the bottom of the first Adobe Sign page
* Complete the form on the next page with the last date of posting, type of posting, and upload your document. Digitally sign the form.
* The BLC will receive your request and send you back a digital copy of a stamp. Place that stamp on your document before printing your posters.

**Stamp Request Expectations**

The stamp will provide the end date of your posting. The end date is the last day your posting can be up on campus. All postings must be removed by the date on the stamp. The type of event your hosting determines how long your posting can be up.

* Event-specific Signage - (in-person or virtual events with a set date)
  + End date - the day after the event or for thirty (30) days, whichever occurs first

* General signage (weekly club meetings, on-campus job applications, deadlines to join a group, etc.)
  + End date - may be posted for up to thirty (30) days

* These timelines apply to:
  + Clubs & organizations
  + Departments
  + ASI Elections Flyers (dated event- day after elections close)
  + IRB Research
  + IRB postings also require the IRB approval number on flyer

* The BLC may grant special longer stamps for certain campus departments that advertise for a semester
  + ASI Elections, Kellogg Gallery, Common Read, University Library, Athletics, Theater Department

* Postings may only be placed in authorized areas
  + Staking locations can be found on the [CPP Staking Locations Map (PDF)](../docs/staking-locations.pdf)

**Approved Posting Locations**

* General use public bulletin boards
* Department boards with department permission

**Prohibited Posting Locations**

* Doors
* Trees
* Light posts
* Trash cans
* Walls
* Campus department owned A-frames
* Glass windows or glass walls

\*\*Please note: The campus Facilities Planning & Management department may grant special permission to certain campus departments & registered student organizations; however, proof of special permission **must be emailed to** **leadership@cpp.edu** **to ensure postings are not removed**.
