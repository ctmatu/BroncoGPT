# APISC Mentor Program (AMP)

## Learn More

![Students at an AMP event](../img/2events.jpg)

Are you looking for community and mentorship to navigate Cal Poly’s campus? Come to the APISC and ask about our APISC Mentor Program! Whether you want to be mentored or be a mentor yourself, come by and get connected to others in the community through our peer mentorship program.

* Utilize campus resources and services to optimize academic success
* Transition into university life
* Get connected and take full advantage of academic resources on campus
* Stay on track with coursework academic requirements
* Participate in workshops to support your college and personal success
* Get support from current students
