## Bio

Dr. Kang received her Ph.D. in Accounting from the University of California, Irvine, and she is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA). She teaches Auditing and Cost Accounting. Her research interests are in the areas of auditing and corporate governance. She has been published in Accounting Horizons, Accounting Research Journal, Auditing: A Journal of Practice & Theory, Business and Society Review, Managerial Auditing Journal, and other high-influence accounting and auditing journals.
