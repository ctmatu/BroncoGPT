## About Me

In this role, I support research, professional development, and programmatic growth across the college by facilitating external funding and strategic partnerships. My work advances initiatives related to academic programs, pilot plants and facilities, infrastructure, new courses, and innovative teaching and research activities. The goal is to strengthen both existing and emerging efforts that contribute to the college’s long-term impact and success.
