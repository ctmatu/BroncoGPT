# CPP WiSE Events & Tours

## **WiSE Events**

[Events](https://livecsupomona.sharepoint.com/%3Ax%3A/s/mep_program/EWej59GPwn1OluIV86L8oDgBNA1HK7AyThMU5YRcsRHyjw) are open to all current students in the Colleges of Engineering and Science in collaboration with [Maximizing Engineering Potential (MEP)](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/mep/index.shtml). Register as a WiSE Affiliate to receive information on upcoming events and workshops by completing the [online form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeb_Iir1hKaFHnrwVpBpAbaCmchPfC-Mwzd9MpWHViVxjd_2A/viewform).

## **WiSE Tours**

School groups who are interested in a WiSE presentation or activity during their CPP visit can contact wise@cpp.edu after the campus tour is booked through [Outreach, Recruitment & Educational Partnerships](https://www.cpp.edu/outreach/tours.shtml).  Presentations and activities are on a first-come, first-serve basis, and is not guaranteed.
