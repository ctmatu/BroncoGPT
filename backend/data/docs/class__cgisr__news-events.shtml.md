## GIS Day 2025

Date: November 19, 2025 \_\_\_ Time: 10 am - 2 pm \_\_\_ Location: Bronco Student Center - Ursa Major \_\_\_\_\_ Cal Poly Pomona announces the hosting of GIS (Geographic Information System) Day as an opportunity for CPP and community members to come together and celebrate the ideas and technology involved in the world of GIS. Register to experience and learn about the amazing work being done in this field.
