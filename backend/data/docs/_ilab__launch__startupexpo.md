## About

Start-up Expo is designed to offer students the opportunity to discover and interact with small start-up companies while at the same time allowing students that are starting their own start-up to perform market research. The overall mission of the Start-up Expo is to raise awareness and enlighten the entrepreneurial ecosystem! Come and check out our student innovators and entrepreneurs!
