# Events & Workshops

Workshops are held every Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday during the academic year.

### Study Abroad 101 (Virtual)

**Tuesday, 12 pm - 12:30 pm**

This is the first step for Broncos to Study Abroad! Learn about types of programs, course pre-approval, how credit applies to your DPR, Financial Aid & Scholarships, getting connected with a Global Ambassador, and more! SA101 is a 30-minute total presentation (20-minute presentation followed by a 10-minute Q&A)

[RSVP via mybar.cpp.edu](https://mybar.cpp.edu/ "RSVP via mybar.cpp.edu. ")
