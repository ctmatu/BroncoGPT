## Welcome to the 11th Annual Creative Activities and Research Symposium!

The Cal Poly Pomona Office of Undergraduate Research is excited to invite students, faculty, families, and supporters to our annual Creative Activities and Research Symposium. This event serves as a platform for students to present the research projects they have worked on in STEM, humanities, and social science disciplines over the summer. These projects help students deepen their knowledge and develop skills essential for their future careers. Sharing their hard work with the wider community is the highlight of their PolyX experience. We take great pride in hosting this symposium for the Cal Poly Pomona and community college students and warmly welcome everyone to join in this celebration of summer research.
