### How to Schedule Your Audition: Easy Steps to Follow

×

1. **Submit Your Application**
   * Make sure you [submit your Cal Poly Pomona application through CSU Apply](https://www.cpp.edu/apply/index.shtml)
2. **Register for an Audition**
   * After submitting your application, [fill out the Cal Poly Pomona Music Department Supplemental Application](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtHsyie5YOi9LrxijHDAIMllUMzlBSTJMUDRCTzlXSlNPRFVHWUpJVkFMNy4u)
   * The Cal Poly Pomona Music Department Supplemental Application must be filled out by Wednesday, 1/14/26.
   * The earlier you fill out the application, the bigger the chance of your first choice audition slot being accommodated.
3. **Prepare Your Materials**
   * Gather and prepare all required audition materials, including your performance pieces and any additional documentation needed.
4. **Attend Your Audition**
   * Arrive at your scheduled audition date - **on time** - prepared to perform and possibly participate in an interview.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Auditions

### When should I get started preparing for my audition?

You already have been!

Every lesson, rehearsal, and performance you’ve done so far is part of your preparation.

Now is the time to focus that experience into choosing pieces that best showcase your strengths.

Use our repertoire guidelines to select works that match your instrument and the program you’re applying for.

### What is the deadline to sign up for an audition?

To schedule an audition:

* Apply to Cal Poly Pomona via [CSU Apply](https://www.cpp.edu/apply/) by **December 1, 2025**

  + Application open October 1, 2025
* Complete the [Music Department Supplemental Application](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtHsyie5YOi9LrxijHDAIMllUMzlBSTJMUDRCTzlXSlNPRFVHWUpJVkFMNy4u) by **January 15, 2026**

  + Application opens October 1, 2025

Once we receive both, you’ll be contacted to schedule your audition.

### Do I need to audition?

Yes.

* If you’re applying to a **Bachelor of Music (BM)** program (Composition, Pedagogy, Performance), your audition determines whether you’re admitted into that program.
* If you’re applying to a **Bachelor of Arts (BA)** program (General Music, Music Industry Studies), the audition helps us determine your placement within the program based on your skill level.

### What if I can't make it to the in-person audition date?

If you live outside the area or face extenuating circumstances, contact us to discuss your options.

We strongly encourage attending an in-person audition to meet faculty, ask questions, and experience the campus.

If the in-person date doesn’t work for you, reach out to Dalia Puentes at dpuentes@cpp.edu to make alternate arrangements.

### What happens if I do not audition?

Not submitting an audition will impact your admission and placement in the music program.

For Bachelor of Music applicants, it may mean you are not admitted to the program.

For Bachelor of Arts applicants, it limits our ability to properly place you.
