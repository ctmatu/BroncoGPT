# CEIS Council Presents IntersectEd: Building Bridges with Education

October 30, 2023

CEIS Council will be hosting an interdisciplinary panel of experts that will dive into the topic of building bridges in education. Discover innovative ideas, strategies, and success stories that are reshaping the educational landscape. This event is open to all CEIS Community. November 2nd 5-7PM BSC URSA Major.
