# Workshops

## Workshops

The iLab hosts workshop ranging from starting your business, working on teams, negotiating your first job or next promotion, and protecting your intellectual property.  All workshops held at the iLab and other maker spaces can be found by going to the [Student Innovation Idea Labs page](https://mybar.cpp.edu/organization/ilab) on MyBAR.

![](../img/workshops/workshops.jpg)

Are you looking to learn new skills, and enhance your ability to create?  You should check out one of our equipment and training workshops to learn 3D printing, laser cutting, 3D scanning, 2D and 3D digital design, and many more.  Our list of workshops changes each semester so keep coming back to see what’s new!

[Check out our workshops!](https://www.cpp.edu/siil/events/workshops.shtml "link to upcoming workshops")
