# Liberal Studies Department to Host Student Showcase

March 9, 2023

![LS showcase](../../img/ls-showcase.png)The Department of Liberal Studies invites the Cal Poly Pomona community to attend the Liberal Studies Student Showcase on Thursday, May 4 from 12-3:30 p.m. at BSC Orion. This co-curricular event celebrates the outstanding interdisciplinary work made by students in the department, centering the organizing themes of Creativity, Social Justice/Social Responsibility, Sustainability, and Wellness. The event will feature panel presentations, creative and scholarly works from the LS Student Virtual Showcase, and activities led by students for students and the greater CPP community. Attendance is free and food will be provided. The LS Student Showcase is a Signature Polytechnic Experience (PolyX). For more information, contact Assistant Professor Jeff Roy by emailing jcroy@cpp.edu.
