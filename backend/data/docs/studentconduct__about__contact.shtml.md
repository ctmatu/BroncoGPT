# Contact Us

## Contact Us

The Office of Student Conduct & Integrity is now located in the Student Services Building (SSB). SSB is Building 121 and we are located in Suite 2560. Suite 2560 is located on the 2nd floor. Look for the blue wall. Stop by and visit us. Our office will be closed on Holiday Observances. The list of Holiday Observances can be found on the [Payroll Services Holiday Observances website](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/payroll/calendars/holidays.shtml "Link to CPP Holiday Observances website").
