# Office of Academic Programs

## University Rubrics

[Analytic Rubric

### Written Communication

Students will express their ideas through the written word with an awareness of audience, purpose, and form.](protected/assessment/written_communication_rubric_2023.pdf)

[Holistic Rubric

### Written Communication

Students will express their ideas through the written word with an awareness of audience, purpose, and form.](protected/assessment/written_communication_rubric_holistic_2023.pdf)

[### Oral Communication

Students will express their ideas through acts of speech with an awareness of audience, purpose, and context.](protected/assessment/oral_communication_rubric_2023.pdf)

[### Critical Thinking

Students will engage in the logical process of inquiry to analyze information from multiple perspectives to develop reasoned arguments.](protected/assessment/critical_thinking_rubric_2023.pdf)

[### Quantitative Literacy

Students will use quantitative information to draw inferences and communicate informed arguments.](protected/assessment/quantitative-literacy-rubric-2025.pdf)

[### Information Literacy

Students will responsibly identify, locate, and critically evaluate the array of information sources and voices necessary to engage in sound inquiry.](protected/assessment/information_literacy_rubric_2024.pdf)

[### Civic Literacy

Students will apply civic knowledge, associated with historical structures of power, to self-discovery and responsibility to the community.](protected/assessment/civic-literacy-rubric-2024-1.pdf)

[### Intercultural Engagement

Students will integrate knowledge and relationships reflective of the diversity of human experience and forms of expression.](protected/assessment/intercultural-engagement-rubric-2025.pdf)
