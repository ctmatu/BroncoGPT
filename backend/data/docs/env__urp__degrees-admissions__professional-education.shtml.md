# Professional Education

The Department of Urban and Regional Planning collaborates with area sections of the American Planning Association to provide workshops for the American Institute of Certified Planners examination.

Professionals are invited to enroll in regular classes in the undergraduate or graduate programs of the department through the College of Extended University. The classes are listed in University Catalog and can be found directly on the university's website. Graduate classes are also offered in the evening.

Please refer to the [University Catalog](https://www.cpp.edu/academics/university-catalog/index.shtml "University Catalog (opens in new window)") to view additional information.
