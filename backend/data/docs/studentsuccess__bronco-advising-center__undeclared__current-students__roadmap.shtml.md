# Roadmap to Declare a Major

## What does declaring a major look like?

In the Undeclared Advising program, students work with the Undeclared Advising team to declare a major by the end of the first year. Here are some recommendations to help you find the right major during your first year:

## Fall Semester

* [Meet with your undeclared advisor](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/bronco-advising-center/undeclared/contact.shtml) to discuss major and careers of interest
* Explore academic department websites and the [Declaration of Major Tool](../major-declaration/index.shtml)
* [Take assessments](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/assessments/) such as the Strong Interest Inventory to explore potential career paths
* Meet with a Career Counselor from the [Career Center](https://www.cpp.edu/career/)
* [Research the different careers that interest you](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/resources/career-exploration/)
* [Attend major/career information sessions](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/bronco-advising-center/undeclared/resources.shtml) sponsored by the Undeclared Advising Program
* [Explore MyBAR](https://mybar.cpp.edu/) to find out about clubs and organizations related to your interests

## Spring Semester

* Meet with your undeclared advisor to discuss the majors that interest you the most and review change of major requirements
* [Meet with an academic advisor](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/directories/advising-directory.shtml) from your top majors of interest for more information
* Attend major/career information sessions sponsored by the Undeclared Advising Program and the major departments of your interests
* Once you have fulfilled the requirements to declare the major of your choice, submit the [Change of Major E-Form](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/guides/changeofmajor/apply.shtml)
