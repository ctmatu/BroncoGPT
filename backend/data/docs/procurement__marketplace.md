# Procurement Marketplace

## CSUBUY

### Procurement Marketplace

This hub provides CPP employees with quick access to purchasing tools and resources. Connect to the CSUBUY Procure-to-Pay (P2P) system, find procurement guidance, and explore CSU purchasing support to streamline buying goods and services.

[![](img/bitmap-business.jpg)

### P2P

CSUBUY Procure-to-Pay (P2P) is a comprehensive platform that streamlines the entire purchasing process—from sourcing to payment—across the CSU system. Use the P2P system for standard or custom purchases with catalog and non-catalog suppliers. You can access marketplace punchout vendors here. This is also the platform to purchase computers and laptops.](https://csubuy.calstate.edu/%20 "opens a new window")
