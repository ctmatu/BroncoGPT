# Strategic Communications

## CPP News & Stories

Submit story ideas or written stories for consideration to the Department of Strategic Communications at Cal Poly Pomona, which is available to review, edit or produce stories for [CPP News](https://www.cpp.edu/news), the official source for university news. The department will consider breaking news stories, feature stories, achievements, events and more.

Stories may also be considered for the university’s official social media channels (@calpolypomona) as well as for media news releases.

[Submit in TDX](https://cpp.teamdynamix.com/TDClient/2860/Portal/Requests/ServiceDet?ID=54029)
