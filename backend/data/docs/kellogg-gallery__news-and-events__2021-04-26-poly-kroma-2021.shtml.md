# Poly-Kroma 2021

April 27, 2021

## ![Multicolored graphic that reads: poly-kroma 2021](../img/pk-2021-banner)

## About

Poly-kroma 2021, celebrating th​e Fine Arts and Visual Communication Design work by Cal Poly Pomona students with a virtual "2d3d+ Annual Juried Student Art Exhibition", and virtual "Industry Portfolio Showcase" — featuring the Class of 2021 Visual Communication Design majors' senior portfolios. Join us for our virtual exhibition events Saturday, May 8 and Tuesday, May 11, 2021!

![multicolored graphic with text that reads: Poly-Kroma](../img/pk-2021-ig-sq)

## Upcoming Events

*Industry Day* - Saturday, May 8, 1-3pm
*2D3D+ Juried Student Art Exhibition* - Tuesday, May 11, 4-6pm
Virtual Exhibition (available by download) Saturday, May 8 - Thursday, May 20, 2021

Streaming live at [poly-kroma.com](https://poly-kroma.com/) during the listed events, or on Zoom.
