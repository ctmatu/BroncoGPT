## In Memoriam

### Toni- Mokjaetji Humber

Dr. Toni- Mokjaetji Humber The Ethnic & Women’s Studies Department was saddened to share the news of the passing of their dear professor, colleague, mentor, and friend Dr. Toni- Mokjaetji Humber on May 11, 2023. As a faculty in the Ethnic and Women’s Studies Department for 15 years, Dr. Humber taught students about African American Studies, Africa and Black culture.
