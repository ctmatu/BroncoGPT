# Mission, Vision, Values, and Goals

## Environmental Health & Safety

The Office of Environmental Health and Safety (EH&S) proactively provides services, consultation and training to foster a culture of safety and compliance within the campus community

### Vision Statement

The Office of Environmental-Occupational Health & Safety (EH&S) will be recognized in the CSU as a leader in providing a safe environment for the campus community in support of student success.

### Core Values

* Provide safe, welcoming and accessible environments that enable student success.
* Deliver best in class services and innovative solutions to the University.
* Ensure reliable service that is timely, responsive and *solutions-oriented*.

### Goals

* Model effective campus stewardship
* Cultivate collaborative communication
* Improve operational excellence
* Enhance organizational capability
