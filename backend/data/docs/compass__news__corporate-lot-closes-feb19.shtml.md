# Off-Campus Parking Lot at Corporate Center Closes

The temporary off-site parking lot on Corporate Center Drive will not be available after Feb. 19. The lease has come to an end without the possibility for an extension. As such, shuttle Route E, which serves the parking lot, will also halt its operation on Feb. 19.

Drivers are encouraged to park in the two express parking lots in Innovation Village if parking close to the main campus becomes impacted. The express lots accommodate additional cars and are directly served by Shuttle Route D, which provides direct service to and from the Student Services Building.

As a reminder, parking permits are required in all parking lots at all times. Semester permits can be purchased online, or a one-day permit can be purchased from a yellow kiosk or the information booth next to Parking Structure I. Visit the parking website for details.

There are also several alternative transportation options to reach campus:

* Foothill Transit buses make convenient stops on campus.
* The CPP Metrolink Connect shuttle provides free transportation to campus from the Pomona North Metrolink Station.
* The rideshare program offers reimbursement if you carpool, vanpool, bike, walk or ride the train.
