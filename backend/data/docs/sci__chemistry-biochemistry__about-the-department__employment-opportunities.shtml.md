# Employment Opportunities

## Current Faculty Openings

If you are interested in a tenure track faculty position in the Chemistry and Biochemistry Department at Cal Poly Pomona.

[Open positions](https://careers.cpp.edu/en-us/job/521867/chemistry-analytical-or-physical-assistant-professor "Open positions. ")

## Part-Time Faculty

We have periodic openings for part-time faculty. If you are interested, please send a letter of interest and a CV to chemistry@cpp.edu.

[Application for Academic Employment](https://www.cpp.edu/faculty-affairs/documents/acadapplication.pdf "Application for Academic Employment. ")

## Graduate Teaching Assistants

Students who are currently enrolled in the Chemistry MS program may be employed as Teaching Assistants for General Chemistry. Candidates will apply in the Summer preceding Fall semester. Please contact the department if you are interested, or send an email to chemistry@cpp.edu
