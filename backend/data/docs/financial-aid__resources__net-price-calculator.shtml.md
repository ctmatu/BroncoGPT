# Resources

## Net Price Calculator

### **Welcome to the Financial Aid Net Price & Cost Calculator!**

We are glad that you are considering joining the Cal Poly Pomona family. We understand that you and your family may have a lot of questions about how you will cover your costs of tuition, housing and other necessities. Through a variety of programs available, the Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships can help you to cover your educational costs.

To apply for financial aid, please make sure that you complete the [FAFSA](https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa) or the [CA Dream Application](https://dream.csac.ca.gov) after October 1 and **before the March 2** priority filing deadline. This will ensure that you are considered for maximum funding that may be available. In the meantime, our Net Price Calculator will allow you to generate an estimate of your cost of attendance and general financial aid eligibility. It is important to note that the more accurate the information you provide in the calculator, the more accurate the estimate provided will be.

This calculator is intended only for full-time, undergraduate students who are eligible to file a FAFSA. Dream Act applicants and graduate students should contact Financial Aid and Scholarships office for more information.

**[Access Cal Poly Pomona's Net Price Calculator!](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/npcalc/npcalculator.html)**
