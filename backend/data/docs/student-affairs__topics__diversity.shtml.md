# Student Retention and Belonging

## You Belong at CPP

Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) remains steadfast in its commitment to ensuring all members of the CPP community feel genuinely welcomed, valued and supported, with access to resources necessary to flourish, thrive and make their dreams of success a reality.

Our centers, programs and initiatives are intentionally designed to inspire exploration, deepen learning, foster meaningful connections and cultivate collaboration. At their core, they are dedicated to enhancing student retention and supporting holistic success.
