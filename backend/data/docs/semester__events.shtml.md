# Semester Conversion Events & Workshops

## ![a student success ambassador talks to two students at table](img/workshop-banner.jpg)

## Upcoming Student Workshops & Events

If you don't see a workshop for your major, contact your [academic department](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/directories/departments-az.shtml) about department-specific workshops and events.

## Student Success Ambassador Tabling

Ask the Student Success Ambassadors for help on using academic planning tools and get general information about semester conversion. Meet your [Student Success Ambassadors](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/) .
