## About Me

Roberto Sheinberg has practiced Architecture for over 25 years in the U.S. and Mexico. Former president of the LA Forum for Architecture and Urban Design and actively engaged with non-profit social enterprises and community organizations. Professional focus includes design and awareness for sustainable and innovative housing for homeless angelenos.
