# Student Account Access

## Login to Your Student Account

[### Account Login](https://cpge.cpp.edu/portal/logon.do?method=load "opens a new window")

[### Login to Your Canvas Account](https://canvas.cpp.edu/login/canvas)

## In Your Student Account, You Can:

* Update your user profile and password
* View your current schedule, order history, grades, and transcripts

![Destiny portal main screen](../img/destiny-account-main-screen.png)

**Viewing your Class Schedule**
Go to My Enrollment History -> Enrolled

**[Viewing your Grades (Sample) JPG](../img/sample-student-grade-report.jpg) / [(Download Grade Report Instructions) PDF](pdfs/download-grade-report-accessible.pdf)**
Go to My Enrollment History -> Completed

**[Viewing your Official Transcript (Sample) JPG](../img/sample-student-transcript.jpg) / [(Download Transcript Instruction) PDF](pdfs/download-transcript-accessible.pdf)**
Go to My Enrollment History -> Completed

[**Viewing your Certificate (Download Instruction) PDF**](pdfs/download-certificate-of-completion-accessible.pdf)
Go to My Certificates and Designations -> Print Certificate

**[Viewing your Receipt Sample Grade Image (PDF)](pdfs/sample-student-receipt-accessible.pdf) / [Instructions on how to Download Grade Report (PDF)](pdfs/download-receipt-accessible.pdf)**Go to My Invoices -> Outstanding Invoices
