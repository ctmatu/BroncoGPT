# Advising Resources

### Updates to Academic Standing Terminology (Effective Fall 2025)

Beginning **Fall 2025**, the following terms are used across the university:

| Previous Term | New Term (Fall 2025) |
| --- | --- |
| Early Warning / At-Risk | **Academic Warning** |
| Probation | **Academic Notice** |
| Probation with Contract | **Academic Notice with Contract** |
| Administrative Probation | **Administrative Academic Notice** |
| Disqualification | **Disqualification** |

For the most current information about academic standing, policies, and student support resources, visit **Student Success Central**:
👉 <https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/oss/academic-advising/university-policy-information/academic-standing.shtml>

**Peer Mentors**
Sometimes talking to your peers is easier than talking to your professors. We know this. The Department of Sociology Peer Mentor (PM) program is an award-winning organization dedicated to providing peer mentorship from student leaders to students in the department. The PM program provides regular workshops focused on navigating academics, graduate school preparation, and career preparation. The PMs hold weekly office hours so students can connect with them one-on-one. For more information, please follow on [Instagram](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.instagram.com%2Fcpppeermentors%2F%3Fhl%3Den&data=05%7C01%7Canarayan%40cpp.edu%7C65e37e0af35644f400a908dabcfc5f6d%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C638030089356019403%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C3000%7C%7C%7C&sdata=j0caiua3yMoUjYJo2Fh5tCD64CsnS69h5Un9d0NKH1o%3D&reserved=0).
