# Change of Minor Meetings

**Fall 2025
 IB & Marketing Minor Meetings**

Students who are interested in changing or adding a minor offered by the **IBM Department** are encouraged to attend one of the following information meetings offered by IBM minor advisor. No registration is required for these meetings.

It is important that students provide a **complete** list of proposed classes per minor curriculum requirement in the petition e-form through **MyCPP - Student Center**. Incomplete petitions will not be approved.

### 10/08/25 IBM Change of Minor Meeting

Optional Information Session: change of minor process, curriculum, Q and A

**Time: Oct 8, 2025 11:00 AM Pacific Time (US and Canada)**
<https://cpp.zoom.us/j/81088785115>
Meeting ID: 810 8878 5115

### 11/12/25 IBM Change of Minor Meeting

Optional Information Session: change of minor process, curriculum, Q and A

**Time: Nov 12, 2025 11:00 AM Pacific Time (US and Canada)**
<https://cpp.zoom.us/j/89985632427>
Meeting ID: 899 8563 2427

Dr. Jun Myers will discuss the minor requirements, the application and approval processes, and answer any questions you may have regarding changing (or adding to) your minor program. No registration is required for the following meetings.
