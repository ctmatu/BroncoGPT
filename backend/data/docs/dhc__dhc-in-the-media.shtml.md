# DHC in the Media

> The Digital Humanities Consortium (DHC) at Cal Poly Pomona has been featured in a variety of campus and community media. This page brings together articles and features that highlight our events, programs, projects, and collaborations. It offers visitors an easy way to see how DHC’s work is represented in the broader community.
