BioTrek would not be possible without the generosity of its donors. Major funding for construction and completion of the project was provided by [**Rain Bird Corporation**](http://www.rainbird.com/), the leading manufacturer of irrigation equipment, in their continuing commitment to “The Intelligent Use of Water.” Global electronic access, including these web pages, were endowed by the [**Ernest Prete Jr. Foundation**](https://www.cpp.edu/biotrek/support-our-programs/other-donors.shtml). The **[Boeing Corporation](http://www.boeing.com/)** has provided technological enhancement to the hands-on experiences of students of all ages. Many other donors of funds, goods, services, and volunteer labor helped make BioTrek possible.

**You, too, can support BioTrek.** One way is to fund an endowment for field trips that bring public school classes to BioTrek. Another is to volunteer as a docent who conducts tours of the BioTrek Learning Centers. Original research is a vital part of our mission. Individuals and organizations may find a variety of ways to become involved with faculty and student research projects.

Rainforests and other natural environments are disappearing around the world at an alarming rate. Preserving these resources is the message we are communicating to many thousands of people each year. This is made possible through your support.

Please contact us at +1 909 869 6701 for information on how you can become a part of the BioTrek team. Your involvement is critical to maintaining the quality of the educational experiences.
