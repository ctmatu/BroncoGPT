# Contact Us

We are aware that construction projects and unexpected repairs can affect your access to campus. We strive to help you get to your class or workplace.

Let us know how we can improve this website or be more responsive to your needs by sending an email to fmcustomer@cpp.edu.

To report an immediate repair, call Facilities Customer Service (909) 869-3030.
