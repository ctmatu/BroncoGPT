# Lactation Room

## Access the Room

The Lactation Room is located two doors down from the Womxn’s Resource Center in Building 95-408. For your convenience, operating hours are 7:00 am – 11:00 pm. In order to access the space, community members (students, staff, faculty) should reach out to WRC staff by completing the form below:

[Request Access](https://forms.office.com/r/txNhBNxNnH)

## Inside the Room

The WRC’s lactation space provides our community with a private, comfortable, and safe space. Our goal is to always provide an empowering atmosphere that will facilitate development of personal success at CPP.

![Lactation Room](../img/wrc3.jpg)

## Resources

The Womxn’s Resource Center understands the necessity of a lactation space for our community. We know you deserve a comfortable and clean space to pump. We are happy to accommodate your needs!
