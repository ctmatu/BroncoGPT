# CEIS Fall Welcome

October 3, 2023

![cow2](../img/cow-2.jpg)

On September 28th, CEIS Council hosted the CEIS Fall Welcome. This event was for the CEIS Community to be in community, connect, and network. Campus partners were also invited to come learn about goals, changes, and acheivements in CEIS. Students, staff, and faculty were treated to a lunch from campus catering.

CEIS departments, clubs, and campus partners were invited to host a table to share more about their organization. The organzations include: CEIS Educational Leadership, CEIS Student Success, IGETSO, Liberal Studies, Early Childhood Studies, The Credential Program, The Career Center, and Student Sustainability Coalition. Thank you all the organization who came out to participate!
