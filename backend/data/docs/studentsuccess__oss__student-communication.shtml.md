# Student Communication

## About

The Office of Student Success is responsible for several dimensions of Cal Poly Pomona student communication.

## Billy Chat

Billy Chat is the university's [artificial intelligence text-messaging robot](https://www.cpp.edu/~studentsuccess/billy-chat/).

## Ask Billy

Ask Billy is the university's [question-and-answer database](https://cpp.service-now.com/askbilly).

## Bronco Advising Center Social Media

The BAC posts explainers and reminders on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/cppbac/). It also shares information and events from other campus partners to amplify messaging.

## How-to Videos

The BAC produces [how-to videos](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCRqnPcIL-oS24GmvMwZb0KA) that explain navigating the BroncoDirect Student Center.

## BroncoBytes

The BAC writes and sends a [biweekly student newsletter](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/broncobytes/broncobytes.shtml) with announcements, important dates and deadlines, and frequently asked questions.

## Targeted Communication

The Office of Student Success manages targeted phone and email campaigns that help students stay on a path to graduation.

## Semester Conversion Student Communication

The Office of Student Success manages the student communication plan around [semester conversion](https://www.cpp.edu/~semester/).
