# Part-Time Positions

![MEP appreciation lunch](/engineering/mep/img/appreciation1.jpg)

Industry representatives and MEP partners work closely with MEP staff and the Cal Poly Pomona Career Center to maximize opportunities for employment both during and upon completion of each student's academic career.

Part-time student employment opportunities can be found on:

* Cal Poly Pomona Career Center’s job posting site, [Handshake](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/handshake-101/)
* Cal Poly Pomona [Foundation Employment Services](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsecure6.saashr.com%2Fta%2F6041387.careers%3FCareersSearch&data=04%7C01%7Chdy%40cpp.edu%7C7407016b7bca4453c64c08d8c9963ad3%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C637480994352699502%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C1000&sdata=6UYQsOrrAjkEVINPwJBwSYQbuzb08k3a%2BmQQuHdSVrE%3D&reserved=0 "Original URL: https://secure6.saashr.com/ta/6041387.careers?CareersSearch.
