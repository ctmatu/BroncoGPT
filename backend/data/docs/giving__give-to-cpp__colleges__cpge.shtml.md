# Give to Cal Poly Pomona

## Support the College of Professional and Global Education

The [College of Professional and Global Education (CPGE)](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/index1.shtml) is dedicated to providing exciting and dynamic continuing education programs in new and innovative ways to meet the needs of both working professionals and non-traditional students. The programs are designed to address not just the direct demand from professionals, but also the continuing needs of regional industry to ensure a steady supply of a competent workforce.

### **Funding Priorities**

* Professional training institute for workforce development
* Leadership academy for communities and nonprofit organizations
* International Center of Excellence
