# Fire Alarm Tests Set for Building 6 and Building 94 on Friday, July 31

**Summary of disruption:** Annual testing of fire alarms in Building 6 (College of Education and Integrative Studies) and Building 94 (University Office Building) are scheduled on Friday, July 31. Testing will begin at 8 a.m. and is expected to end at 1 p.m.

**Reason for disruption:** Allow the university to conduct state-mandated annual testing of the fire alarm system in each building.

**How does this affect me?**Loud horns and flashing strobe lights will be activated during testing. The HVAC system in each building will undergo interruptions during testing, but elevators will not be affected.

**What do I need to do?** Avoid being in the buildings during testing. Please share this information with others who may have not received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:**Facilities Customer Service at Ext. 3030.
