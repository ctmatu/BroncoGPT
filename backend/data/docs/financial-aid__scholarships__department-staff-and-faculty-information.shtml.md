## Bronco Scholarship Portal FAQ for Department Staff and Faculty

### What is the timeline of Scholarship Fest?

Scholarship Fest runs from October 1st through March 2nd – for scholarships to be awarded for the 2025 - 2026 academic year.

### What am I expected to do and what are the deadlines?

The application period for scholarships is October 1 through March 2.  After reviewing the applications and choosing the recipient(s), you must select scholarship recipients within the Bronco Scholarship Portal (BSP) no later than April 30th of every year.

### How do I open a scholarship in the Bronco Scholarship Portal?

For assistance with opening a scholarship, please email the scholarships team at scholarships@cpp.edu.

### What happens if our first choice for a scholarship does not meet the eligibility criteria?

If your first choice for a scholarship does not meet the eligibility criteria, or later becomes ineligible, you should have an alternate recipient ready so that you are prepared to award the scholarship to the next eligible student.

### What if I don't have enough eligible applicants to award all our scholarships?

Please email the scholarships team at scholarships@cpp.edu for assistance.

### Can we still award scholarships to graduating seniors for the current year?

There are few instances in which you may still award graduating seniors funds for the current school year.  However, Federal, State, and CSU Regulations & Policy require that all scholarships must be coordinated with a student's other financial aid - **regardless of when the scholarship is awarded**.  Late awarding could have a negative impact on a student's overall aid package.

Students must be enrolled during the 2025 - 2026 school year in order to receive a scholarship for the 2025 - 2026 academic year.  If a student has graduated prior to Fall 2025, they would not be eligible to receive a scholarship for 2025 - 2026.

### Our scholarship donor has specified an award period outside of the standard timeframe.  What are our options?

Work with your Director of Development to communicate to the donor the timeframe & award period for Cal Poly Pomona scholarships, and the importance of adhering to Cal Poly Pomona's timeframe.

### How do I add reviewers to a scholarship?

The Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships will help you add reviewers. Email us at scholarships@cpp.edu.

### How do I sort the applicants who qualify?

The Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships can assist you with sorting your qualified applicants.  Email us at scholarships@cpp.edu if you require assistance.

### Why do unqualified applicants appear as qualified applicants?

There are a few reasons why unqualified applicants might appear as qualified applicants. For example, the qualifications may need to be adjusted. Email the Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships for assistance at scholarships@cpp.edu.

### Why are some application fields not displaying student information?

There could be various reasons. Email the Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships for assistance at scholarships@cpp.edu.

### What steps do I follow to select recipients from the Bronco Student Portal?

Please refer to the video below, as well as the document titled [*Bronco Scholarship Portal (BSP): Steps to Read and Score Applications (PDF)*.](../forms/read-and-score-application01072021.pdf)

### Do we still need a Payment Form?

Effective Spring 2022, a payment form will no longer be routed to the department for signature if award information for scholarship recipients was entered into the scholarship portal. A payment form might be required if we are unable to locate award information for a specific scholarship or when access to the portal becomes unavailable. In those cases, you will be notified by a member of our team.

### The Bronco Scholarship Portal is challenging.  Why should we use it?

The portal provides a centralized location for students to apply for scholarships without having to visit multiple websites or locations on campus. It expedites the awarding of scholarship funds to students. Additionally, it makes it easier to track the quantity of scholarships and amount of money awarded.

This is an essential step to a strong culture of philanthropy on campus. We want to be able to share the amazing stories about scholarship recipients and donors.

Lastly, it helps contribute to the university's mission of student-centered success.

### What should I do if I have technical issues or have questions about the Bronco Scholarship Portal?

Email the Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships for assistance at scholarships@cpp.edu.

### Do you offer training opportunities specific to the Bronco Scholarship Portal for staff and faculty?

Training opportunities are available to staff and faculty by request. Email the Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships at scholarships@cpp.edu.

### Is there a simple way to review applications so I don't have to read all applicants, or re-read the same ones for different scholarships?

The number of scholarships each reviewer is assigned can be designated and reduced or increased by request from your scholarship committee chair.

### I don't believe I will have time to review all the applications assigned to me.  Who should I contact?

The number of applications to review can be reduced and reassigned to others in your committee. This should be done prior to beginning the review process. Email the Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships at scholarships@cpp.edu. Additionally, consider recruiting more reviewers to reduce the number of applications to review.
