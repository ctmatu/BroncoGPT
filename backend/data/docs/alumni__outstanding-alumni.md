## Distinguished Alumni Awards Gala

Cal Poly Pomona has countless outstanding alumni who have attained success through significant achievements in their professional fields, served their community and given back to the university. The Distinguished Alumni Awards program highlights many of our exceptional alumni who are making a difference.
