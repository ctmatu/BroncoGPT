# Toner and Inkjet Cartridge Disposal

To dispose of empty toner cartridges, put a label on the cartridges that says: **Staples — Empty Toner Cartridges for Pick Up**. Place the empty cartridges in the area where Staples makes deliveries to your work area. The Staples delivery person will take the empty cartridges for disposal.
