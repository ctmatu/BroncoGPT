# Social Media Toolkit

## Social Media Toolkit

### Canva

Strategic Communications maintains the campus' Canva site, which includes a brand toolkit and design templates for social media. There are limited user seats available for campus use. Email brand@cpp.edu for access.

[### Giphy

Show your Bronco pride with custom stickers and animated GIFs. Browse our official Giphy channel.](https://giphy.com/calpolypomona)

[### Social Media Cover Photos

Download branded cover photos for your social media accounts, including LinkedIn, Facebook and X (Twitter).](https://cpp.canto.com/v/brand/landing?viewIndex=0)
