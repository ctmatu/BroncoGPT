## History and Background

### Over 50 Years of Musical Excellence

Founded in 1963, the Cal Poly Pomona Music Department has grown into a leading institution in artistic education. Accredited by the National Association of Schools of Music (NASM), our department offers a comprehensive curriculum guided by 38 esteemed faculty members, serving over 300 majors who share a passion for music and commitment to excellence. Engage in ensembles such as the University Concert Band, Symphony Orchestra, and Jazz Band to experience the collaborative spirit of Cal Poly Pomona's "Become by Doing" philosophy. This nurturing environment, enriched by faculty mentorship and real-world job experiences, prepares you for dynamic careers in the music industry. Community engagement is a cornerstone of our mission, with nearly 70 annual events including faculty concerts, guest artist performances, and ensemble showcases. Performances by the acclaimed Mariachi ensemble, Mariachi Los Broncos y Caballeros de Cal Poly Pomona, unite the campus and surrounding areas in a shared appreciation for music. Committed to integrating modern technology and industry practices, the Music Department fosters the growth of passionate musicians and industry leaders, inspiring resilience and continual improvement in a vibrant, supportive community.

## Mission Statement

### Our Commitment to Your Growth

At the Cal Poly Pomona Music Department, we cultivate a hands-on, creative environment where you can thrive in performance, musicianship, industry, and technology.

We are dedicated to fostering diverse musical practices that enrich lives and the human experience.

By promoting ethical and innovative career paths in performance, teaching, and the music industry, we empower you to achieve your highest artistic, scholarly, and professional potential.

## Vision Statement

### Inspiring Musical Innovation and Inclusivity

Our vision is to build an inclusive and dynamic community where music brings people together, fostering resilience and adaptability.

We aim to make music education accessible to all, inspiring you to reach your full potential.

Driven by a passion for music, we strive to ***create***, ***collaborate***, and ***innovate***, ensuring that music's transformative influence enhances both individual lives and the broader community.
