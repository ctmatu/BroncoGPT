### Spring 2026

* **Tuition Payment Due**: Jan. 15, 2026 (please click [**HERE**](https://www.cpp.edu/cpeli/dates-fees/index.shtml) for options and instructions on making the payment)
* **Placement test and program orientation** (students must attend and lunch will be provided):
  + **Placement test**: 09:00a.m. to 10:45a.m. on Jan. 15, 2026
  + **Program orientation**: 11:00a.m. to 01:00p.m. on Jan. 15, 2026
  + **Location**: College of Professional and Global Education
  + **Address**: Bldg. 220A-105, 3650 West Temple Avenue, Pomona CA 91768. **Please click**[**HERE**](https://www.cpp.edu/cpeli/docs/cpeli-map.pdf) for driving and parking instructions (**parking permits** are available for pick-up in the classroom)

### **Important Forms**

For all requests please download, fill out all information and return paper to the front office.

## I-20 Extension

If your I-20 will expire soon and you want to continue studying at CPELI.

**[I-20 Extension](https://www.cpp.edu/cpeli/docs/forms/I-20-Extension-Form.pdf)**

## Vacation Request

If you have studied for 4 terms and would like to take a break for 1 term (can be in the U.S. or outside the U.S.).

**[Vacation Request](https://www.cpp.edu/cpeli/docs/forms/Vacation-Request-Form.pdf)**

## Transfer Out

If you have been accepted into another institution in the U.S. and need to transfer before you begin classes at the new institution.

**[Transfer Out](https://www.cpp.edu/cpeli/docs/forms/Transfer-Out-Form.pdf)**

## I-20 Replacement Request

If you have lost your I-20 and need to re-print a new one.

**[I-20 Replacement Request](https://www.cpp.edu/cpeli/docs/forms/I-20-Replacement-Request-Form.pdf)**

## Submitting your documents

If you need to submit documents with your request:

Please upload your additional documents [HERE](https://cpge.cpp.edu/ "DestinyOne").

Please be advised that these requests may take up to a week to create. After submitting your request to the front office, you will be contacted if more information is needed or once the request has been completed.

## Language Proficiency Test Links

Testing is a vital part of English education and University entrance. Explore the resources below to improve your chances at going to an American University and documenting your progress.

## Student Handbook

Moving to the U.S. to learn a new language can be challenging. In our student handbook, we share everything you need to know: preparing for classes, international student visa information, adjusting to life in America, places around Pomona to shop, eat, and play, and more.
