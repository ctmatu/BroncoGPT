# Emeriti Faculty

The Emeritus Faculty designation honor those who have made an exceptional contribution to the University. Faculty who have taught at Cal Poly Pomona for a minimum of ten years of service may be honored with the Emeritus Faculty designation upon retirement, once approved by the Academic Senate. For additional information, see the [Office of the President](https://www.cpp.edu/president/honoring-excellence/emeritus-awards.shtml).
