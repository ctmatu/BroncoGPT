# Competition Awards

## The following are competition awards received by our undergraduate and graduate student teams since 2024.

* 1st place at Regional Collegiate Penetration Testing Competition (**CPTC**), Nov. 2025
* 2nd place at National Centers of Academic Excellence (**NCAE**) Competition, April 2024.
* 2nd place at National Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (**NCCDC**), April 2024.
