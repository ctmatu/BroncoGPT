# Professional Development

## Learn More

Our Professional Development Series allows us to collaborate with and highlight Native professionals in a variety of areas, particularly in STEM fields, to extend relations and opportunities to Native students. Past collaborations have included work with professionals from American Indian Science & Engineering Society, Southern California Edison’s Native American Alliance, Boeing, NASA, and Northrup Grumman.

These events provide our students with crucial connections to the workplace, individualized resume feedback, as well as internship opportunities and information on graduate school. All events are open to anyone in the campus community.
