# Transcripts FAQ

## FAQs

### How can I order my official transcript, and what do I need?

Cal Poly Pomona has authorized the National Student Clearinghouse (NSC) to process transcript orders. The transcript documents produced by NSC are official and contain all pertinent course information, as recorded by the California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. Students and alumni can use a secure, web-based system to order official transcripts 24 hours a day, 7 days a week from anywhere in the world. Students and alumni can order official transcripts online using a major credit card and track the status of their orders. Transcript processing time is indicated on the website. Official transcripts may be delivered electronically or by U.S. First Class Mail. Express mail service may be available for an additional charge.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to registrar@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### What type of official transcripts does CPP offer?

Official Transcripts are available electronically or by mail. Cal Poly Pomona has authorized the National Student Clearinghouse (NSC) to process official transcript orders. The transcript documents produced by NSC are official and contain all pertinent course information, as recorded by the California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. Students and alumni can use a secure, web-based system to order official transcripts 24 hours a day, 7 days a week from anywhere in the world. Students and alumni can order official transcripts online using a major credit card and track the status of their orders. Transcript processing time is indicated on the Registrar's Office website. Official transcripts may be delivered electronically or mailed. Note: Electronic transcripts are only available for 30 days upon opening email. Even if the transcript is saved on a computer the file will lock after the 30 days.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to registrar@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### How can I get an unofficial transcript?

An active student may access an unofficial transcript through BroncoDirect. Log on to your Student Center and click the option "Transcript: View Unofficial" near the bottom to view your unofficial transcript. For step-by-step directions please refer to the Unofficial Transcript Guide.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to registrar@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### Can I get an unofficial transcript, yet I am no longer an active Cal Poly student?

No, if you are no longer an active Cal Poly Pomona student, you can order an official transcript through the National Student Clearinghouse website. An unofficial transcript is only available to active students.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to registrar@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### Does CPP accept electronic (PDF) official transcripts taken at other institutions?

Yes, Cal Poly Pomona accepts electronic (PDF), official transcripts. Electronic Transcript Exchange (ETX) is also acceptable if the sender participates in the service.
