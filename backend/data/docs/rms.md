# Welcome!

University Risk Management coordinates the campus risk management efforts.  Our office, in conjunction with the CSU Risk Management Authority (CSURMA), works to provide a framework for risk management that promotes a deliberate, coordinated, and anticipatory approach to risk identification, evaluation, and management.

All Cal Poly Pomona  faculty, staff and students have a responsibility to identify  and manage the risks associated with their activities, and our office is a resource for risk management advice so that activities are carried out in a manner that considers safety, financial, reputational, or legal risks.  Our office has primary responsibility for oversight of insurance claims, and development of various risk and loss control programs.

Members of the campus community are encouraged to contact the University Risk Management office with any questions or concerns related to proposed activities, with respect to loss prevention,  life safety, premises liability, automobile liability, claims recovery, or any risk related matters.

Please review the [Cal Poly Pomona Risk Management Policy](forms/risk-management-updated-procedure.pdf) (PDF) for additional information and details.
