# No. 1 Polytechnic University in the U.S.

December 2, 2020

![commencement](../../img/commencement.jpg)

Cal Poly Pomona has [earned recognition](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2020/11/cal-poly-pomona-no-6-nationally-in-boosting-student-economic-mobility/)again as the highest-ranking polytechnic university in the nation on the “2020 Social Mobility Index” for propelling low-income students to financial success by graduating them into good-paying careers. In addition to leading all polytechnic institutions, CPP ranked No. 6 among all colleges and universities nationwide. The university was also [rated](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2020/11/cal-poly-pomona-earns-spot-in-top-10-most-transformative-colleges-ranking/) No. 10 in Money Magazine’s list of the “10 Most Transformative Colleges in the U.S.”
