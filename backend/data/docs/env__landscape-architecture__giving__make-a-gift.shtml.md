# Make a Gift

Landscape Architecture and the College of Environmental Design rely on the generosity of alumni and friends to help us sustain our standards of excellence as a *learn-by-doing* institution. Your investment helps:

* Support **students**with the costs of field trips, supplies, and community-building events
* Create**learning environments** that foster student, faculty and industry collaboration
* Provide**learn-by-doing**and **technical** training through service-learning and cutting-edge instruction

*We invite you to be a part of our future!*

To help fund our landscape architecture programs, [click this link](https://cpp.thankyou4caring.org/pages/env) and select one of the following options:

* Landscape Architecture Excellence: funds department events and overall student resources
* Landscape Architecture Scholarships: funds scholarships given to individual students
* First Generation Landscape Architecture Endowment: funds the Grisol Ramirez Memorial Scholarship
* Collaborative for Healthy and Inclusive Learning Env: funds student travel, student fellows, and community engagement activities for our [CHILE](https://www.cpp.edu/env/lyle/chile/index.shtml) Studios
* Other: enter your preferred use for donated funds. Use this for the B.L.A.C.K. in LA Scholarship

For more giving options, please visit the College of Environmental Design, which maintains a section with detailed information on how to **[make a gift](/env/make-a-gift/index.shtml "Make a Gift to ENV!")**.
