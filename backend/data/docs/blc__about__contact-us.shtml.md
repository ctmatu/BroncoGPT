# Contact Us

## Vision

The Bronco Leadership Center cultivates student leadership and fosters a sense of belonging to help every student feel connected to Cal Poly Pomona.

## Mission

The Bronco Leadership Center empowers students through involvement in co-curricular engagement and leadership development initiatives that encourage self-discovery and prepare them beyond Cal Poly Pomona with intentional, transformative, and culturally relevant experiences.
