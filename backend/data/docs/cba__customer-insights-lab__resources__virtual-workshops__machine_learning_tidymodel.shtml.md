# Machine Learning: Tidymodels

## Machine Learning with Tidymodels

The Center for Customer Insights and Digital Marketing presents “Machine Learning with Tidymodels” featuring Dr. Carsten Lange, a center advisor. Dr. Lange is currently a Professor in the Economics Department in the College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences, Cal Poly Pomona. He is also the Graduate Coordinator for the Economics Department. In this workshop, Dr. Lange discusses the problem of overlearning, introduces the Tidymodels package, presents 3 types of machine learning models, and explains cross-validation.
