# Art History Newsletter

The Art History program at Cal Poly Pomona is the only one of its kind in the CSU system in Southern California to offer an   *independent*  Bachelor of Arts in Art History. This gives our students due recognition for their expertise in the field when they apply for employment or to graduate programs.

The annual Art History Newsletter highlights program events, faculty, student research, and news from our current students and alumni.
