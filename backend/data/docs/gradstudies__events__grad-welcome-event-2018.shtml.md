# Graduate Students Welcome Event 2018

The Graduate Studies Office and Orientation Services partnered once again to  host  the Graduate Student Welcome Event, which took place on August 18, 2018, from 9-1 pm. Students from various disciplines were greeted with a morning breakfast and received a warm welcoming  from Dr. Laura Massa, our AVP of Academic Programs, and Dr. Salomon Oldak, our Director of Graduate Studies.  Students participated in different workshops led by faculty and staff, and the day ended with raffle giveaways and a tour of the campus.  [More information on the program (pdf)](../documents/welcome-event-program-2018.pdf) can be viewed here.
