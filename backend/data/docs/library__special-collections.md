# Special Collections and Archives

The Special Collections and Archives Reading Room is closed until further notice for maintenance. We apologize for the inconvenience. We are currently taking appointments by reservation in our Special Collections and Archives Processing and Instructional Lab (Room 4434 on the 4th floor of the University Library). Please contact speccollections@cpp.edu with any research questions.

---

Special Collections and Archives collects, organizes, preserves, and makes accessible Cal Poly Pomona’s rare books and archival collections for use by current and future researchers. These unique interdisciplinary collections center around themes relevant to the mission of the university and the history of the region. Available to researchers from the university and general community, a key part of our mission is to engage researchers of all levels and promote learning, discovery, and scholarship. Taking pride in our role of preserving the history of these areas, we actively collect and welcome all interested users including students, faculty, staff, the general research community, and scholars from around the world on an annual basis.

* [The Southern California Wine and Wine Industry Label and Bottle Collection uncorks the region’s winemaking legacy through rare bottles, historic labels, signed artifacts, and unique items dating as far back as 1812.](https://oac.cdlib.org/findaid/ark%3A/13030/c8zk5pc5)
* [The Southern California Wine and Wine Industry Grape Crate Labels Collection captures the artistry and branding of American viticulture with colorful labels from growers in California and beyond.](https://oac.cdlib.org/findaid/ark%3A/13030/c82b94mq)
* [Discover the mind of Abraham Kaplan, a distinguished Cal Poly visiting professor, through this compelling collection of philosophical writings, lectures, and personal materials.](https://www.oac.cdlib.org/findaid/ark%3A/13030/c86q1z11)
* [Raise a Glass to History: The Southern California Wine and Wine Industry Photograph Collection is now Digitized and Available for Research!](https://oac.cdlib.org/findaid/ark%3A/13030/c8hd8296/)
* [Unlock the Science of the Future: Episodes from Cal Poly Pomona’s FutureWatch are Now Available for Research](https://oac.cdlib.org/findaid/ark%3A/13030/c8mk6mxs/)
