# Dean's Advisory Board

## Bruce J. Gorelick

### Retired Hotel Executive

![Portrait of Bruce Gorelick](../img/board-of-advisors-pics/bruce-gorelick-bio-picture.png)

Bruce has an extensive background in hotel management. In his 38 years with Marriott, he has worked in most of their brands, including Ritz Carlton, Renaissance, Marriott Vacation Club, Marriott Hotels, Resorts and Suites, Courtyard by Marriott, and Residence Inns by Marriott. Bruce retired for a short time in 2017, only to rejoin the industry with Loews in late 2017. He managed the Loews Regency in San Francisco prior to returning to southern California to become Managing Director of The Lowes Hollywood Hotel.

Bruce was raised in Pasadena, California, and earned his bachelor's degree in Hotel and Restaurant Management at The Collins College of Hospitality Management, Cal Poly Pomona. Bruce and his wife Sara have four daughters: Katy (31), Hannah (26), Anekah (14), and Astrid (11).
