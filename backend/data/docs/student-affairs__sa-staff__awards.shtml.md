# Student Affairs Awards

## Horseshoe Award

The Horseshoe Award is an easy way to recognize Student Affairs colleagues who go above and beyond. Anyone can send a Horseshoe award to a DSA staff member throughout the year as an easy way to say ***thank you.***

The recipient will be sent a printable award via email, with a message of gratitude from their nominator. All Horseshoe award winners each academic year will be recognized at the DSA End of Year Celebration.

[Send a Horseshoe Award Now](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_9LGBv47AMJXyTqe)

## Year End Award

The Year End Award is given each May to two recipients per cluster, chosen by each cluster’s Associate Vice President. The award recognizes exceptional service provided to students and the university throughout the academic year.
