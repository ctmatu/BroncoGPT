## Request a Study Abroad Presentation

Faculty or departments can request a study abroad classroom presentation facilitated by the Office of Study Abroad staff. Each presentation can be adapted to cover a variety of topics based on the needs of the classroom session. Topics can include:

* Program options and requirements
* Courses and credits
* Finances and scholarships
* How to apply

Please **contact the Office of Study Abroad** to schedule a presentation.
