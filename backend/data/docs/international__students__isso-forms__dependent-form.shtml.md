# Dependent Form

International students can bring their spouse and/or child or children to the USA on an F2 Visa. In order for them to apply for an F2 Visa, they need a dependent I-20 from the student’s SEVIS record.  A spouse requires an additional $5000 and a child an additional $3000 of support to the student costs. For calculating costs, see the Student Accounting & Cashiering Services website at: [http://www.cpp.edu/~student-accounting/tuition-fees/](http://www.cpp.edu/~student-accounting/tuition-fees/ "http://www.cpp.edu/~student-accounting/tuition-fees/"). To request a dependent I-20 for a family member, please click on the link, complete the form and upload the required documents.
