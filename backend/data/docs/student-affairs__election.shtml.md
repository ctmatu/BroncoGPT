# Civic Engagement and Voter Empowerment

## Election Information and Support

![Get out the vote](img/2025/election_banner-01.png)

**Important Dates:****2026 Primary Election (June 2, 2026)**

* **April 23 - May 12:** The California Secretary of State will mail the Voter Information Guide during this period.
* **May 4:** Counties will begin mailing each registered voter their vote-by-mail ballot. Early Voting sites open.
* **May 18:** Last day to register to vote ahead of the Primary Election.
* **May 19 - June 2:** Same Day Voter Registration is available. Voters can “conditionally” register and vote at a county elections office or polling location after the voter registration deadline, up to and including Election Day.
* **June 2:** Election Day - polls are open from 7:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m.
