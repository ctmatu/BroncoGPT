# Advising

## Our staff is available to walk you through the steps of choosing an education abroad program that is right for you!

![workshop flyer](../img/sa-home-workshop.jpg)

## Step 1: Study Abroad 101 Information Meeting

Attend a Study Abroad 101 Information Meeting where you’ll learn about different types of programs, financial aid, course pre-approval, and more! Upcoming information meetings for specific programs are listed on our [events page](events-workshops.shtml).

## Step 2: Peer Advising

The Office of Study Abroad is centrally located within the International Center. It is staffed by Peer Advisers who have already participated in a program abroad and Student Assistants who have been trained to answer a variety of questions. Walk-ins are welcomed and encouraged!

International Center – Building 1, Room 104

Drop-in Hours: Tuesdays and Thursdays TBD

Email: studyabroad@cpp.edu
Phone: (909) 869-3782

You can use the Study Abroad Research Guide to help you take notes regarding different program options.

[Study Abroad Research Guide (xlsx)](/international/study-abroad/docs/study_abroad_research_guide.xlsx "Study Abroad Research Guide (xlsx). ")

## Step 3: Study Abroad Advising

Schedule an appointment via [CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml)

If this is your first Study Abroad Advising Appointment, fill out the Pre-Advising Worksheet to discuss with your Advisor.

[Pre-Advising Worksheet (pdf)](https://www.cpp.edu/international/study-abroad/study-abroad-pre-advising-worksheet-2026-2.pdf "Pre-Advising Worksheet (pdf). ")
