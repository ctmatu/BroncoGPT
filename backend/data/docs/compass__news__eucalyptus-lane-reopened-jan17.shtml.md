# Access to Parking Lot G to Be Restored on Friday, Jan. 17

**![](https://www.cpp.edu/compass/img/1-17-20-eucalyptus-lane-reopening.jpg)**

**Summary of disruption:** Vehicle access to Parking Lot G by using Eucalyptus Lane from the intersection of Kellogg Drive and Red Gum Lane will be restored on Friday, Jan. 17.

**Reasons for disruption:**The temporary closure of Eucalyptus Lane allowed contractors to complete the Pedestrian Safety Upgrade Project and adjacent sidewalks.

**How does this affect me?**Drivers will no longer be able to use Eucalyptus Lane from University Drive to access Lot G. Private vehicles will not be allowed to enter the Bronco Commons pedestrian walkway.

**What do I need to do?** Motorists will be able to use restored Eucalyptus Lane to access Lot G, the Children’s Center, the Bronco Bookstore and Foundation Administrative Offices. Please share this information with others who may have not received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:** Mooris Taylor, Senior Project Manager, at moorist@cpp.edu
