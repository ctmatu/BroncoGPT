# Faculty Progress Reports

University-wide faculty progress reports are used to provide timely interventions to support primarily undergraduate students. A partnership between faculty, the Office of Student Success and campus partners, progress reports allow faculty to share information about students who need support, resulting in early intervention, connecting students with critical resources, and supporting their academic success and wellbeing.

## Spring 2026 Progress Report Timeline

There are 2 separate progress report campaigns for Spring 2026. Instructors will receive an invitation to submit a progress report throughout the following points in the term:

### Weeks 3-5

Instructors will receive an invitation on **Monday, February 2** and the progress report campaign will close on **Sunday, February 22, at 11:59 p.m.**

### Weeks 8-11

Instructors will receive an invitation on **Thursday, March 12,** and the progress report campaign will close **Sunday, April 12, at 11:59 p.m.**

**Once a progress report campaign closes, the campaign cannot be reactivated.**
