## Incremental Budget Plan

Our incremental budget plan refers to the amount of revenues and expenses above prior year’s amounts.  After the March 2025 implementation of Voluntary Separation Incentive Program (VSIP), an ongoing gap of $19.1M remains.

The FY25-26 Budget is preliminary and is based on the Governor’s January 2025 budget proposal.
