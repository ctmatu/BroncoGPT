# Water Reports and Notices

## Campus Water Quality Testing

![](img/student-water-bottle.jpg)

Cal Poly Pomona’s drinking water has met or exceeded all drinking water health standards of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency and the State of California. The drinking water delivered to the campus community in 2024 met or exceeded the standards established by these federal and state agencies. This annual report fully complies with the regulations of the 1996 Safe Drinking Water Act. State law mandates that water quality reports be distributed to the campus community.
