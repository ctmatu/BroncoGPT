## The Office of Assessment and Program Review

### Our Role on Campus

The Office of Assessment and Program Review supports Cal Poly Pomona’s commitment to academic excellence by guiding meaningful assessment practices and facilitating program review across campus. We collaborate with faculty, staff, and leadership to ensure that educational programs remain effective, student-centered, and aligned with institutional goals and accreditation standards.
