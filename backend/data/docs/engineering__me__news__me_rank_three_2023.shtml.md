# CPP Program Ranks Third Nationally by US News, Excels in Laboratory Focus

Cal Poly Pomona's Mechanical Engineering program is ranked third in the category of “schools where doctorate not offered” by the US News and World Reports, recognized for its exceptional emphasis on laboratory exercises. The program's commitment to hands-on learning equips students with practical skills, setting it apart in engineering education. Dr. Henry Xue, the program's director, highlighted the significance of this focus in preparing students for real-world engineering challenges. The success can be attributed to “learn-by-doing” curriculum, dedicated faculty, diversified students, and influence of alumni. This achievement solidifies Cal Poly Pomona’s reputation as a leader in practical engineering education, attracting top talent and promising a world-class education for its students.

All the ranking information of Cal Poly Pomona can be found at the [US news website](https://www.usnews.com/best-colleges/california-state-polytechnic-university-pomona-1144/overall-rankings).
