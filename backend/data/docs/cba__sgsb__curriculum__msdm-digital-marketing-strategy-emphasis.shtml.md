# MSDM | Digital Marketing Strategy Emphasis

## Digital Marketing Strategy Emphasis

The MS in Digital Marketing offers a flexible curriculum with required courses and electives from either the [Digital Marketing Strategy Emphasis](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/sgsb/curriculum/msdm-digital-marketing-strategy-emphasis.shtml) or the [Marketing Analytics Emphasis](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/sgsb/curriculum/msdm-marketing-analytics-emphasis.shtml)  to tailor the curriculum to their career goals. Go to the [Marketing Analytics Emphasis](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/sgsb/curriculum/msdm-marketing-analytics-emphasis.shtml) page to learn about its completion roadmap.

## Intended Jobs for Digital Marketing Strategy Emphasis

Companies use various titles for various digital marketing or marketing jobs. Pay varies depending on job positions and roles:

* Digital Marketers: $77,381 (Zip Recruiter)
* Digital Marketers with Master’s in Digital Marketing: $87,944
* Digital Marketing Manager: $127,700 (Salary.com)
* Advertising, Promotions, and Marketing Managers: $159,660 (U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics)

Below are some of the job titles that digital marketing emphasis is intended to support the most:

* Digital Marketing Specialist/ Manager/ Director
* Growth Marketing Manager
* Social Media Marketing Specialist / Coordinator / Manager
* Social Media Marketing Specialist / Coordinator / Manager
* SEO Specialist
* PPC Specialist
* Database Marketing Specialist
* Email Marketing Specialist
* Marketing Manager
* Content Marketing Manager
* Marketing Manager

Note: \*One course can be taken from one of the courses listed under [Marketing Analytics Emphasis](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/sgsb/curriculum/msdm-marketing-analytics-emphasis.shtml#Marketing Analytics Emphasis "Marketing Analytics Emphasis").

\*\* If you prefer to take one or two courses from Marketing Analytics Emphasis instead of the courses listed under Digital Marketing Strategy Emphasis, you can do so. Just resubmit the curriculum contract.

## The Roadmaps for the Digital Marketing Strategy Emphasis

We provide a two-year roadmap as well as a one-year roadmap for students entering the program in the fall semester. Those who enter the program in the spring semester will follow a two-year completion roadmap, completing the curriculum in 1.5 years.
