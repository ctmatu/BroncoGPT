# Support the Department

We strive to provide the highest quality education to our students in diverse areas within the biological sciences including cell and molecular biology, microbiology, biotechnology, physiology, neuroscience, ecology, evolutionary biology, botany, zoology, and environmental biology.

Generous gifts from alumni, friends, and donors allow the Biological Sciences Department to enhance the quality of our educational programs by providing highly valuable experiential learning opportunities to our students. These experiences reach far beyond the classroom and leave lasting impression on the intellectual and professional growth of our students. Previous gifts have been used to support student research projects, internships, service-learning activities, and scholarships.
