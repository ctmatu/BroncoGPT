## Space Management Assistance

**Andrew Naranjo, Capital Projects Specialist** - consultative project guidance from start-up to completion.

* Planning Process:
  + Space walk-through to discuss vision, plans, requirements, and constraints
  + Help determine priorities and assess possible costs
  + Provide guidance on Facilities Planning & Management (FP&M) forms & processes
  + Provide contact information for furniture vendors
* FP&M and IT&IP work liaison:
  + Assist in communication, obtain updates, answer questions

**Myra L. Panis, Academic Resources Office Coordinator** - contact for Associate Deans on classroom chairs.

* Maintain current counts and track needs for replacements

**Lisa Rotunni, Executive Director** - contact for space data revisions, including capacity, and space type.

* Make PeopleSoft updates, coordinate with 25Live office and FP&M Planning

Find us on our **[Academic Resources Personnel Contacts](../our-team/personnel-contacts.shtml)**page.
