# Ways to Give

## Online Gift

Please complete our [online form](https://www.cpp.edu/ceis/giving/cpp.shtml). Your gift will be processed the next business day and a receipt will be mailed to you.

## Pledges

A pledge can be paid off in monthly installments. If interested, please contact Annual Fund at (909) 869-4321.

## Gifts of Securities

Gifts of securities such as stocks, mutual fund shares or other appreciated assets can be a great tax-efficient donation. For more information, contact Gift Processing at (909) 869-2914.

## Planned Gifts

If you are looking for a way to make a significant gift, a bequest is a gift made through your will or trust. It is one of the most popular and flexible ways that you can support CEIS. Visit our [Planned Giving website](https://cpp.giftlegacy.com/) for more info.

## Matching Gifts

When you give to CEIS, you can multiply the effects of your generosity by taking advantage of our matching gift program. Contact the Human Resources or Employee Benefits Office at your company or [click here](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/ways-to-give/matching-gifts.shtml) to find out whether they will match your gift.

## In-Kind Gifts

In-kind gifts include tangible personal property such as equipment, materials, crops and company products.

## Payroll Deduction

Giving to CEIS is about demonstrating your support for our programs. It is more important than ever that students, alumni and friends of CEIS know that you believe in our university and the good work that's taking place in CEIS.
