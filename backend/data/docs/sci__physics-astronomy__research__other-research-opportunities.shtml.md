# Other Research Opportunities

**Tips on getting into a research internship program**

1. Apply!
2. Do not miss the deadlines.
3. Many programs are competitive. Keep your GPA high!
4. Do research with Cal Poly Pomona Physics Faculty starting your sophomore year.

**Research Opportunities**

* [NSF REU](https://www.nsf.gov/crssprgm/reu/reu_search.cfm) (National Science Foundation, Research Experience for Undergraduates) -Searchable, organized by disciplines
* [NSF REU Physics](https://www.nsf.gov/crssprgm/reu/list_result.cfm?unitid=69)
* [NSF REU Astronomical Sciences](https://www.nsf.gov/crssprgm/reu/list_result.cfm?unitid=5045)
* [McNair Scholars Program](https://www.cpp.edu/mcnair/index.shtml)(on campus)
* [SIRI (Student Independent Research Internship)](https://siri.jpl.nasa.gov/) at JPL(Jet Propulsion Lab) - Contact Dr. Ertan Salik, faculty sponsor for SIRI prorgram
* [Summer Programs at JPL](https://education.jpl.nasa.gov/higher_ed/undergrad.html) (Jet Propulsion Lab)
* [Caltech SURF](https://www.surf.caltech.edu/) (Summer Undergraduate Research Fellowship) - Deadline February 22, 2008
* [NASA USRP](https://www.epo.usra.edu/usrp/) (Undergraduate Student Research Program) - Deadline for Fall 2008 Session is February 29, 2008
* Many research opportunities in Sciences compiled by [Sonoma State University](https://www.phys-astro.sonoma.edu/advisor/SummerScienceAvailNow.html) - Opportunities for Summer 2008
* [Research Opportunities at Sonoma State University](https://www.phys-astro.sonoma.edu/undergraduateopportunities.shtml)
* At the [University of Chicago](https://physics.uchicago.edu/page/undergraduate-research)
* Long list of Math and Mathematical Physics Opportunities from the [American Mathematical Society](https://www.ams.org/employment/reu.html)
* [Summer Research Program for Undergraduates at Delaware State Univ](https://www.creosa.desu.edu/download/summer_research.pdf)., Center for Research and Education in Optical Sciences and Applications ([CREOSA](https://www.creosa.desu.edu/)) - Deadline April 14, 2008
