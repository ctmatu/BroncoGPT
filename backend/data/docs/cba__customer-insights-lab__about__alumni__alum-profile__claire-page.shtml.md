![](../../../img/members/claire-4x6.png)

## Claire Son

## **Customer Experience Analyst**

## **Research Assistant**

**Major: Communications, Public Relations and a minor in Marketing Management**

**Interest:** Social Media Marketing, E-Marketing, Public Relations

**Skills:** Microsoft Suite, Adobe Suite, Photography, Graphic Design

**Short Bio:** Claire is a Communications major with an emphasis in Public Relations, along with a minor in Marketing Management. During her second-year at Cal Poly Pomona, she joined the Center for Customer Insights and Digital Marketing as a Research Assistant. Along with Patrick Ogaz, Sebastian Hernandez, and Andrea Escobar Vara, she is assisting the “Regulatory Foci” team which aims to research the impact of culture on social media. She is expected to graduate in Spring 2023.
