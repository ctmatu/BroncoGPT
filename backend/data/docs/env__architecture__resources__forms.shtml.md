# Forms

Visit the [Office of Student Success - Academic Forms and Petitions](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/oss/forms.shtml "Office of Student Success - Academic Forms and Petitions (opens in new window)") page to find the following forms:

* + General Academic Petition
  + Repeat Course Form
  + Schedule of Conflict Form
  + Academic Advising Worksheet

Graduate students should visit [Graduate Studies - Forms](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/forms-and-procedures/graduate-forms.shtml "Graduate Studies - Forms (opens in new window)") page to find the following forms:

* + Graduate Academic Petition
  + Report of Culminating Experience

For additional advising resources, visit the [ENV Student Success Advising Center](https://www.cpp.edu/env/student-success-advising-center/index.shtml "ENV Student Success Advising Center (opens in new window)").
